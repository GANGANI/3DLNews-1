Four benefits for you!

Club 49 members get 2 free bags when traveling, but do you know about the other perks? Not only do we have weekly fare sales and Travel Now discounts, but shipping freight is made easy with Freight for Less.

Check your status!

There is so much more to being a Club 49 member than just flying with “2 Free Bags”! The best way to maximize those benefits is to first check your status. Simply use the Alaska Airlines app, click on your profile, select “Mileage Plan Card” and check to see if it says Club 49. If it does, you’re up-to-date and good to go.

When your account is active, you’ll receive important information about Club 49—including flash sales to great destinations!
If you’re not currently enrolled, contact mileage.plan@alaskaair.com to be reinstated.

Freight for Less

Freight for Less offers Club 49 members $49* (plus tax) cargo shipping within the state of Alaska. Ship up to 100 lbs. in up to two 35-gallon totes or sturdy shipping containers that do not exceed 20”x30”x15” each. Oversized, unpackaged or hazardous materials may not be shipped. For cargo shipping details including allowable contents, please reference Alaska Air Cargo.
