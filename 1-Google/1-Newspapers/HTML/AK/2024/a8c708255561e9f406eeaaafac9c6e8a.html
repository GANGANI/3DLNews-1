Loopholes?

  • Feb 4, 2024
  • Feb 4, 2024

The late W.C. Fields (1880-1946) was a well-known entertainer. Unfortunately, he was not known for his thoughts and example for God. He had an illness that overtook him in his last days, so he opened a Bible. One of his friends was quite surprised at the sight and asked him what he was doing.

Fields replied in his characteristic style, “Looking for loopholes, my man, looking for loopholes.”

The Bible plainly teaches that God has established a path that each and every person must be on in order to have eternal life and attain Heaven. (Matthew 7:13-14) The path is founded in faith in Jesus Christ alone. (John 14:6) It does not involve any works or merits from any person; in fact, the Bible states that there are no good works that any person can do to draw God’s favor. (Ephesians 2:8-9) We, rather, fall far short of God’s sinless perfection and therefore need His salvation by simple faith and trust. (Romans 3:10,23; 6:23; 10:13)

There are no “loopholes” with God. You either come to Him as the Bible (Holy Scriptures) states, or are without Him and eternal life forever. (John 3:3,7) God created Heaven and God set the requirement for being able to dwell there, which is solely by faith in His Son Jesus Christ. Have you received God’s wonderful gift of salvation as His Word states?

Remember, there aren’t any “loopholes”! Think about it.
