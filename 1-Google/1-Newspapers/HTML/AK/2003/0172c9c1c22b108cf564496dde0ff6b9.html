ROBERTSON BUILDING REMODEL OPEN HOUSE;
ADULT EDUCATION OPEN HOUSE ANNOUNCED

 

October 07, 2003
Tuesday - 1:20 am


Ketchikan, AK - The public is invited to an open house for the newly remodeled Robertson Building, located the University of Alaska Southeast Ketchikan Technical Center, 600 Stedman Street. The event is scheduled for Friday, October 10 from 5:30 to 8:00pm.


The Robertson Building's New Look
photo by Dick Kauffman


The Ketchikan Chapter of the UAS Alumni Association is hosting the Open House. The event will feature hors d'eourves, tours throughout the evening and a brief program at 6:30pm to recognize key people in the remodeling project. The alumni association will also be selling tickets for its upcoming raffle at the end of the month. Partial proceeds of the raffle will benefit the Ketchikan chapter.

Representative Bill Williams will be recognized for his support of the University, especially during the funding process for the Robertson Building remodel.

The office of Adult Education which recently relocated to the Robertson Building will be holding an Open House in conjunction with the university event.
