MS Roald Amundsen visits Little Norway

 
  Share   Tweet 0 Comments

June 30, 2022

Chris Basinger / Petersburg Pilot

Petersburg Chamber of Commerce President Jim Floyd and Captain Svein Rune Strømnes exchanged plaques aboard the Amundsen to commemorate the ship's inaugural visit to Petersburg.

A ship named for one of Norway's most famed explorers has been visiting Petersburg for the first time this summer.

The MS Roald Amundsen operated by Hurtigruten is the world's first hybrid powered expedition ship and is currently traversing through Alaska, offering passengers a chance to participate in experiments and research among the state's natural environments.
