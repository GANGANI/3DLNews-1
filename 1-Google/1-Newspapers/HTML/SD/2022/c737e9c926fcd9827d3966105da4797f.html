Missoula Children’s Theatre’s production of “The Snow Queen” hits the stage at the Sisseton Performing Arts Center on Apr. 29

Published by admin on Fri, 05/06/2022 - 10:07am

HOBGOBLINS & STUDENT ASSISTANT DIRECTORS Front Row, Left to Right: Violet Glynn, Quinn Hanson, Danica LaCroix, Michael Payne Back Row: Jordan Just, Fallon Hawkins, Dathan Wilson, Piper Langager, Mason Gray

The Missoula Children’s Theatre (MCT) and around 60 local students took the stage at the Sisseton Performing Arts Center with their presentation of “Snow White & the Seven Dwarfs.” MCT tour actor was Linnea Connery, and MCT Tour Director was Sage Tokach. Local MCT Coordinator was Alexis Monroe. Dr. Michelle Greseth served as accompanist.
