Dorothy Bell

Posted Tuesday, February 15, 2022 10:12 am

Dorothy Jean Bell, age 93, passed away on Tuesday, February 1, 2022 at Dow Rummel Village in Sioux Falls, South Dakota. A graveside service was held at 11:30 am on Saturday, February 5, 2022 at Union Cemetery in Flandreau, SD.
Dorothy grew up on a family farm near Flandreau in Moody County, South Dakota. She attended a rural grade school and graduated from Egan High School. During her teen years, she was a very active 4-H Member, receiving many achievement awards for her sewing, baking, and raising chickens. Later she became the most devoted leader of The Happy Youngsters Club in Moody County and received the Leadership Award for her service. Dorothy also helped teach Sunday School class at 2nd Presbyterian Church in Flandreau. During those years, she worked for several local families doing childcare and home care.
Wanting to branch out, she moved to Denver where an uncle and brother lived. She found work with a family in Denver and later moved to California with them. After the children grew up, Dorothy moved to Sioux Falls where she spent many years with the Margulies family and was very dear to Craig and Todd Margulies.
Dorothy did a lot of embroidery, needlepoint and made tons of dish towels. Everyone would get a beautiful embroidered dish towel for Christmas.
Dorothy has lived at Dow Rummel Village for 16+ years. Thanks to the wonderful care she is receiving from staff and spent with new friends it has become home. She is a longtime member of First Congregational Church in Sioux Falls.
Dorothy never did anything wrong and spent her whole life for others.
God must consider her one of His Angels.
Grateful to have shared Dorothy’s life with her are her brother, Harold (Susan) Bell; her sister, Marlys Britton; and the family of Carolyn Margulies. Dorothy is preceded in death by her parents, Henry and Aileen; and three sisters.
