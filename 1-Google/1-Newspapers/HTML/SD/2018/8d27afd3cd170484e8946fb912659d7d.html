Flandreau Homecoming Week- Candidates announced

Posted Monday, September 3, 2018 11:00 pm


Flandreau Public Schools will celebrate Homecoming the week of September 10-15. The theme for the week is “Super Heroes.” Coronation and LIttle Orpheum is scheduled for Monday, September 10 at 7 p.m.
The senior class has chosen the following as candidates for royalty. Front from left, queen candidates, Madeline Hursey, Hope Brennan, Marianna Pavlis, Ashtyn Bertram and Elle Thompson. Back, king candidates, John Eng, James Auren, Nathan Kneebone, Connor LeBrun and Kyle Johnson.
Any business or organization who wishes to have an entry in Friday’s 1 p.m.  parade, should call Rick Weber at 997-3263 to join. Line up begins at 12:30 south of the Moody County Resource Center.
