Wild Wins Tri Valley Tournament

  By Colton Hall | January 28, 2016 - 12:03 am |January 28, 2016 Local
Leave a comment

The Winner Wild girls basketball team won the Tri Valley tournament on Saturday.
In the first game, Winner defeated Mitchell 35-16.  In the second game, Winner defeated West Central 28-25.  In the third game, Winner defeated Brandon Valley 33-26.

“The girls did a good job as we only had seven girls this weekend and we were tired. They played match up zone to save our legs and played well. They were excited as Brandon Valley beat us by two points two weeks ago,” said Swedlund.

Colome Girls Nipped by Centerville

  By Colton Hall | January 28, 2016 - 12:00 am |January 28, 2016 Sports
Leave a comment

Colome girls basketball team was nipped by Centerville 60-56 Monday night in Parkston.  This was a makeup game from the Snowball Classic in Winner in late December.

Callie Heath led the Cowgirls with 22 points followed by Mariah Kinzer with 14 and Taylor Hrabanek with 11.

Hrabanek pulled down 16 rebounds and Heath, 11.

Warriors Win Game over Tri Valley

  By Colton Hall | January 27, 2016 - 11:55 pm |January 27, 2016 Sports
Leave a comment

Winner boys basketball team bounced back with a win over Tri Valley in the Dakota State University Classic in Madison on Saturday.
Winner won the game 57-48.  The game was played in two 18 minute halves.

Leading the scoring were Devon Leiferman and Lattrell Terkildsen with 12 points each; Krockett Krolikowski with 10 points and Drew DeMers with 8 points.

Cameron Kuil pulled down 8 rebounds and Leiferman had 7.

Kuil had 3 steals and DeMers, 4 assists.

Wyatt Ewing had one man maker.

Harter, O’Bryan Lead Colome to Victory

  By Colton Hall | January 27, 2016 - 11:53 pm |January 27, 2016 Sports
Leave a comment

Reed Harter scored 28 points as the Colome boys basketball team defeated Jones County 68-41 on Jan. 19 in Colome.  Kelly O’Bryan added 21 points.

Jackson Kinzer pulled down 7 of Colome’s 26 rebounds. Holden Thieman and Will Cahoy each grabbed 4 rebounds.

Colome will host Platte on Jan. 29 in a boys and girls doubleheader.

Lady Warriors Roll to Victory Over Bennett Co.

  By Colton Hall | January 27, 2016 - 11:46 pm |January 27, 2016 Sports
Leave a comment

Winner Lady Warriors rolled to a 77-19 victory over Bennett County Jan. 19 in Martin.

Bailey Volmer and Gabby Kocer scored 12 points, Sydney Fritz, 10 points; Sydney Hollenbeck, 9 and Allison Cox, Chloe Bartels and Madyson Frazier, all with 8 points.
