Obituaries

Don Kraus, 77

News Staff
Wednesday, May 29, 2024
Body
In the presence of family, Don Kraus concluded a life well lived on May 20th, 2024 in the gentle, amazing care at Monument hospital in Custer. After almost 5 years of fighting metastatic cancer, the treatments, anxiety, pain and occasional moments of hope, it came down to the brutal truth that his body could no longer carry on. The family sends a heartfelt thanks to all the Doctors, Nurses and staff at Monument Health in Custer and Rapid City for the absolute best care he could have had.
