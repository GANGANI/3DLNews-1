Sisseton hosts Golf Invite

Published by admin on Fri, 05/24/2024 - 12:26pm

The Sisseton Redmen Girls Golf Team hosted the Sisseton Invite on Thursday, May 16, at the Valley View Country Club, with five teams competing – Aberdeen Roncalli, Madison, Groton, Milbank and Sisseton.

Sisseton’s Elliot Hortness took ninth place shooting a 104. Hortness was Sisseton’s only golfer.

Claire Crawford of Aberdeen Roncalli was the medalist shooting an 88.
