Lyman Raiders fill the weekend with basketball

By News Staff on Tuesday, February 13, 2024
  • Cole Kieffer (center) fights to keep the ball away from two Scottie players during the double header basketball games on Thursday, February 8th. Lyman boys lost to Philip 49-57. Photo by Connie Penny/LCH
  • Lyman’s Brooke Palmer looks to pass the ball during the Flandreau vs. Lyman DWU Classic game last Friday night. Photo by Rebecca Palmer
  • Dawsen Volmer moves to inside the paint during the Philip Scotties game on Thursday, February 8th. Photo by Connie Penny/LCH
  • First place champs in the fifth grade boys basketball division are, back row, l-r: Coach Grady Floyd, Hase Hills, Jude Miller, Trig Larson, Kane Kieffer, Tracen Floyd, Coach Tyson Floyd; front row, l-r: Colby Gerard, Will Stanley, Uriah Moran, and Jaxx Collins.
  • Bringing home first place in the fifth grade girls division are back row, l-r: Ginger McManigal, Sylvia Wermager, Averee O’Dea, Aspen Volmer, Jada Lillebo, Coach Rene Lillebo; front row, l-r: Loey Lingemann, Amber Mertens, Aaliyah Middletent, and Sophie Wolf. Not pictured: Essyance Brown.
Lyman Raiders athletes filled the weekend with basketball on Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. Thursday, February 8th, Lyman boys and girls basketball teams hosted Philip Scotties. Girls Varsity brought home the win 51-33; followed by the boys coming in a with a loss 49-57.
