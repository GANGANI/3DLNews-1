Buddy Baskets - 2020

Posted Tuesday, March 3, 2020 11:00 pm

Iroquois Elementary students, the Iroquois community and Thrivent jointly funded a drive that resulted in making 21 Buddy Baskets for homeless and needy veterans. From the left are: Janelis Klancnik, John Raether, Ervin Crater, Manuel Argueta, Alixis Hansen and Maiya Shoultz.
Buddy Baskets include pillows, blankets, cooking supplies, laundry supplies, cleaning supplies and a variety of items The baskets were presented to the Bensley-Rounds American Legion Auxiliary Unit 280 of Iroquois for distribution.
