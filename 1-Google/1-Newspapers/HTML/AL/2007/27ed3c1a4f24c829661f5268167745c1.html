Jonathan Blake

Brewer

Jonathan Blake Brewer celebrated his third birthday April 23. He is the son of Lee and Michelle Brewer of Moulton. His grandparents are Leon and Sue Parker of Houston and Gaines and Rosemary Brewer of Moulton. He is the great-grandson of Dewey Parker, Ollie Brewer and Elizabeth Adair, all of Moulton. Blake has a brother, Austin.

Hunter Joseph

Drane

Hunter Joseph Drane celebrated his first birthday April 13. He is the son of Eric and Kim Drane of Moulton. Hunter is the grandson of Tony and Lisa Lovett, Carl Drane, all of Moulton and Steve and Kim D'Agresta of Hayden. His great-grandparents are Vera Dean Terry of Courtland, Jerry Lovett of Moulton and Bernie and Ilene Shultz of Silver Springs, Fla. Hunter has one brother, Austin and one sister, Summer.

Nathan Bart

Naylor

Nathan Bart Naylor will be five years old May 3. He is the son of Bart and Melonie Naylor of Moulton. Nathan is the grandson of Joe and Virginia Jackson of Kitchens Mill and Roger and Elizabeth Naylor of Hartselle. Hazel Jackson of Danville is his great-grandmother. Nathan has two sisters, Briana Jeffreys and Brette Naylor.

Kendall Grace

Demastus

Kendall Grace Demastus, daughter of Tim and Candi Demastus of Moulton, celebrated her fourth birthday April 28. She is the granddaughter of Linda Sparks of Trinity, Jacky and Cathy Sparks of Moulton and Jim and Betty Demastus of Decatur. Kendall Grace has a sister Whitney Peppers.
