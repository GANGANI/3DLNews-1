CAREERS

Join us at Alabama Media Group – we’re hiring

We are looking for editors, reporters, video producers, digital marketers, sales leaders, creative thinkers and customer experience builders who want to be part of one of the most innovative local media companies in the country.

Our team includes more than 100 journalists dedicated to telling stories that change lives, laws and minds in the communities we serve – and our work continues to earn national recognition for its impact.  In addition, we run a digital marketing business serving clients throughout the Southeast, a film production group, and the national brands Reckon and It’s a Southern Thing.

At Alabama Media Group you’ll work with talented colleagues, and enjoy a diverse and supportive culture, excellent benefits, and development opportunities that support who you are both inside and outside the office.

Explore Opportunities

Alabama Media Group is part of Advance Local Media and owned by Advance, owners of Conde’ Nast; and shareholders in Reddit, Warner Bros. Discovery and Charter Communications.

Diversity and Inclusion

Differences are what shape our world – and we’re committed to ensuring everyone in our company is seen, heard, and feels they truly belong.

We know for Advance Local to be a home for everyone, we need to hire, develop and retain a diverse workforce where employees feel empowered to contribute their unique ideas and perspectives and feel supported in building their careers. We’re proud of the recent progress we’ve been able to make but recognize that we have more work to do as we continue to build Advance Local’s community.

Alabama Media Group and Advance Local Media are committed to creating a diverse environment and are proud to be an equal opportunity employer, encouraging applications from people of all backgrounds.
