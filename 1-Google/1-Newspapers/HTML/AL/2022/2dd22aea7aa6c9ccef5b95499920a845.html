About

Courtney Thomas, Office Manager

Courtney Thomas is from Reform, Alabama. She lives in Carrollton and graduated from Mcculley Hill Christian School in West Blockton, Alabama in 2016. She attended Shelton State Community College for her general studies. She has 3 kids Braxton 6, Xander 1, and Sawyer 1. When she is not at work she enjoys spending time with her family.

Felicity Holman, Editor

Felicity Holman is from Vicksburg, MS. She has been a resident of Pickens County since 2010. She graduated from Pickens County High School is 2017. She attended BSCC for her general studies and will be pursuing a degree in journalism. She is proud to be the new Editor of the Pickens County Herald.
