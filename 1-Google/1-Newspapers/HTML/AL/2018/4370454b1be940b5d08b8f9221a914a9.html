Print Subscription

Subscribe to the print edition!

By joining our growing family of print subscribers, you'll find all things inside that make your local newspaper a great asset to you, your community and surrounding areas.

Each issue of the Fayette Times-Record aims to include the latest in local & area news, sports, upcoming events, editorials, feature articles, obituaries, business profiles, promotional advertising and supplements plus so much more.

The Fayette Times-Record is currently published on Wednesdays each week. Typically, in and surrounding counties will experience mail delivery the day of publication but due to many variables, this is not always possible.

Subscriptions are made easy with only a few selections and then adding the item to your cart. Your billing, mailing and payment information is entered during the checkout process. Of course, we are always delighted to speak with you directly and can take your information right over the phone.

Start enjoying all the Fayette Times-Record has to offer by subscribing below or call us today at 205-932-6271 • Monday thru Friday • 8:00 A.M. til 5:00 P.M. CT., except Wednesdays when we close at noon.
