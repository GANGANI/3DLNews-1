Vol.) as amended, the Alabama Department of Environmental Management is proposing to issue a Consent Order to the Choctaw County Commission, which operated the Choctaw County Regional Landfill, a solid waste disposal facility in Toxey, Choctaw County, Alabama. The violations listed in the Order consist of: failure to maintain the run-off control system and on-site drainage structures, failure to prevent water pollution or an unauthorized discharge, failure to cover waste daily, and failure to control litter at the facility. The Department is proposing a civil penalty in the amount of $29,625.00. The Order, if issued, would require the Choctaw County Regional Landfill to correct the violations above within the prescribed timeframe, and to operate in compliance with all applicable requirements of ADEM Admin. Code div. 335-13 immediately upon the effective date of this Order and continuing each and every day thereafter.
