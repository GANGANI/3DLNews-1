WCSO deputies graduate from state's Law Enforcement Training Academy

Six Williamson County Sheriff's Office deputies recently graduated from the Tennessee Law Enforcement Training Academy in Donelson after completing 12 weeks of Basic Police School.

Earning their diplomas in June were  Deputy Trevor Hunt (Patrol Division), Deputy Josh Jeter (Patrol Division), Deputy Kyle O’Neal (Litter), Deputy Dillon Brooks (Detention Division), Deputy Andrew Riney (Detention Division), and Deputy Allan Beckloff (Detention Division).
