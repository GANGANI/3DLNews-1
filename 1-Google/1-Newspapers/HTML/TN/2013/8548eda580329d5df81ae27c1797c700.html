Cowboy James Storm returns to Lewisburg Saturday

James Storm
Posted Thursday, December 5, 2013 11:00 pm
ASW Wrestling

"Cowboy" James Storm has been one of Tennessee's best wrestling successes.

Having held multiple titles with Impact! wrestling, Storm has been one of those stories that people love to hear. But this Saturday night, Storm will be returning to the area at All-Star Wrestling in Lewisburg, Tennessee, to take care of a problem.

"The Superstar" Mikey Dunn has been running rampant at ASW and now Storm will be coming to Lewisburg to give Mikey Dunn an attitude adjustment. While Dunn has been very vocal at the House of Pain, Storm went to YouTube with his response.

"Hey Mikey Dunn, if you don't recognize the voice, it's James Storm from Impact! Wrestling! Come December 7th, I'm coming down to Lewisburg, Tennessee, and I'm going to teach you a little lesson cause my good friend Chandler told me you have been mistreating people and you've been thinking that you're better than anyone else. Well, let me tell you something son, I came from the same place that you're wrestling in. I've wrestled in Columbia; I've wrestled in Lewisburg; and I've never acted like I was any better than anyone else. So Chandler has told me he'd give me some compensation to come down there and kick your butt, but I told him he wouldn't have to do that, but he said it was for my time to come down there. So December 7th, Mikey Dunn, lace your boots up, brother, real tight, cause this butt-whoopin' is going to hurt a little bit. Sorry about your damn luck!"

So this weekend, Mikey Dunn will be facing off against "Cowboy" James Storm! Will Storm be able to finally shut Dunn up and keep peace at ASW?

Also announced for Saturday night, ASW's Heavyweight Champion Jeremiah Plunkett will be defending the title against "The Sharp Dressed Man" Lawrence. The Morton brothers will be teaming up when Shane and Steve Morton will face off against The Great Mephisto and Forsaken with Lucifer. Larry Cooter will be in singles action against Drew Haskins. "The Honeybadger" Mark Dunnavant will face off against The Masked Superstar.

Card is subject to change.

ASW Wrestling is located at the House of Pain at 2244 Mooresville Highway in Lewisburg, Tennessee. Tickets are $10 and are available at the door.

Doors will open at 6:00 p.m. with bell time at 8:00 p.m.

For more information, visit ASW on Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/aswwrestling.
