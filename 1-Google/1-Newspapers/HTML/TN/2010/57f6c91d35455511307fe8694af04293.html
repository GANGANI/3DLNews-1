Scott, Charles Houston

  • Sunday, January 31, 2010
Charles Scott

Charles Houston Scott, 70, of Rossville, died on Saturday, January 30, 2010 at his residence.

He was a member of Elizabeth Terrace Baptist Church and had worked at Chattanooga Coke and Chemical and Rockwell Industries before retiring as a contractor.

Survivors include his wife, Wilma Scott; daughters, Stacy (David) Evans, Rossville, Wendy (Rodd) Pearcy, Katy, Tx.; grandchildren, Brandon and Megan Pearcy; brothers, Butch Scott, Rossville, Bobby Scott, Knoxville; sisters, Gail Massey, Chattanooga, Ann Williams, Rossville, Kay Janeway, Johnson City, Tn., Debbie Bailey, Macon, Ga.; and several beloved nieces and nephews.

Services will be at 2 p.m. on Tuesday, Feb. 2, in the chapel with Rev. Amy Nutt and Howard Solomon officiating. Interment will follow in Tennessee – Georgia Memorial Park.

Visit www.heritagefh.com to view the memorial tribute and share words of comfort with the family.

The family will receive friends from 4–8 p.m. on Monday, Feb. 1, at Heritage Funeral Home and Crematory, Battlefield Parkway.
