Guffey, Jackie

Retired From Sears After 44 Years

  • Saturday, January 2, 2010
Jackie Guffey

Jackie Guffey, 66, of Rossville, Georgia, died on Friday, January 1, 2010 in a local hospital.

Mrs. Guffey was born in Garden Grove, Ca., and was a graduate of Rossville High School. She was retired from Sears with 44 years of service and was a member of the Battlefield Church of the Nazarene.

She was preceded in death by her parents, Cletus and Mildred McKeown, her husband, Billy Jack Guffey, and a brother, Cletus McKeown Jr.

Survivors include her son, Michael Guffey, Atlanta; daughter, Rhonda Cigainerco, Cartersville; grandchildren, Cassie Guffey, Rossville and Matthew Best, Cartersville.

Funeral services will be at 2 p.m. on Thursday in the funeral home chapel with Pastor Joe Brown officiating. Interment will follow in Lakewood Memory Gardens South.

Visit www.heritagefh.com to share words of comfort to the family and view the memorial tribute.

Family will receive friends on 4-8 p.m. on Wednesday, Jan. 6, at Heritage Funeral Home and Crematory, Battlefield Parkway.
