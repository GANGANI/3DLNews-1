About Knoxville Daily Sun


 
Jeaneane Payne, Knoxville Daily Sun Owner/Publisher/Editor

The Knoxville Daily Sun keeps pace with local news demands throughout the day.

We bring you news about community happenings, individual achievements, special events, interesting places to travel, small business news, health news, entertainment, and lifestyle.

The Knoxville Daily Sun launched on Monday, November 15, 2010. It is owned by Image Builders, and its Publisher and Editor is Jeaneane Payne.

Jeaneane is a member of the Society of Professional Journalists.

Jeaneane's Education Background:
Central High School, Knoxville
University of Tennessee - Business Administration
Northern Seminary - Certificate of Pastoral Leadership, 2020; Certificate of Ministry, 2022; Certificate of Justice & Reconciliation, 2022
