Description of Data 

Bankruptcies - All bankruptcies filed in Western District of Tennessee, including Memphis & Jackson. Dates from 1989 to most current date. Updated daily.

Trust Deeds - All Deeds of Trust filed in the Shelby County Register's office since 10/21/95. Updated daily

Warranty and Quit Claim Deeds - All Warranty & Quit Claim Deeds filed in the Shelby County Register's office since 10/21/95. Updated daily

Chancery Court Filings - All lawsuits filed in Shelby County Chancery Court since 9/1/95. Updated daily.

Circuit Court Filings - All lawsuits filed in Shelby County Circuit court since 1990. Updated daily.

Federal Court Filings - All civil lawsuits filed in U.S. District Court in Memphis since 9/1/95. Updated weekly.

Divorces - All divorce petitions filed in Chancery and Circuit courts in Memphis since 9/1/95. Updated daily.

Marriages - All marriage licenses filed in Shelby County, Tennessee since 9/1/95. Updated daily.

Building Permits -All building permits recorded in Shelby County since 1990. Files include owner, contractor, description of work, value of work,  and addresses. Includes Building, Electrical, Plumbing and Mechanical Permits. Updated daily.

Utility Connections - All MLGW new service connections since 9/18/95. Includes names, addresses, ZIP codes of people who have new utilities in their names for the first time. Updated daily.

Foreclosures - An index of foreclosure notices published in The Daily News or in other publications from January 26, 1996, to present. Indicates the start of foreclosure action against a person or company. Includes original trustee, instrument number, property address, attorney. Updated daily.

Business Licenses -All new and renewed business licenses filed in Shelby County since 1990. Includes owner's name & address. Updated daily.

Probate Court - Wills and Estates filed in Shelby County since 1970. Includes deceased's name, executor, date of filing, attorney's name. Updated daily.

Tax Liens - All federal and state tax liens filed in the Shelby County Register's office. Dates from 1990 to most current date. Files updated as recorded in Register's Office, usually once a week.

UCC Filings - All UCC filings recorded in The Shelby County Register's office. Dates from 1990 to current date. Files updated as recorded in Register's Office, usually once a week. Also all Shelby Co. UCC's from the TN Secretary of State since Jan. 1, 1997.

General Sessions - Money judgments, new case filings, FED's and garnishments from the Shelby County General Sessions Court since 2002.

Crime Reports – Reported criminal incidents from the Memphis Police Department since 8/9/1999
