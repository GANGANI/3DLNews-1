    About Us

    The Advocate & Democrat has been serving the news and advertising needs of Monroe County for nearly 150 years.

    The award-winning newspaper is published on Wednesdays. The Advocate & Democrat publishes several informative magazines about Monroe County and the surrounding area, including the Tennessee Mountain Traveler tourism publication produced three times a year.

    The Advocate & Democrat is part of Adams Publishing Company, which publishes about 150 newspapers in the U.S.

    A variety of news and other services are provided online at AdvocateAndDemocrat.com.
