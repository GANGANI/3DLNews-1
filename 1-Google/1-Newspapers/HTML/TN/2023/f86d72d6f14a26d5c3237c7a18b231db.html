Homecoming King & Queen Revealed!

  • By Campbell Carter, Staff Writer

Say hello to His and Her Majesty!

In front of 8,978 onlookers, Dominique Harris and Tred Hill were named the 2023 Homecoming Queen and King, respectively. Alongside them on the court were John Adkins, Emaan Aziz, Carmin Chambers, Cece Gragg, Tamia Caldwell, and Jalen Redditt.

Both were visually shocked and happy as they received their crowns from last year’s winners, Isaiah Owens and Delali Gadzekpo, and flowers from Chancellor Steve Angle.

“As far as students go, everyone was super supportive,” stated Dominique as she exited the field. She explained how she felt the love from the student body. “I feel like we do have a community here, and I didn’t really realize that until I was out campaigning.”

As for Tred, his time on the court provided him a chance to learn and grow. “My leadership has evolved, my community service has evolved. I’m grateful for the experience.” He also felt driven to promote diversity. “I’m representing communities that are bigger than me. Everyone should have a chance to go above and beyond.”

Congratulations to Dominique and Tred, and everyone on this year’s court!
