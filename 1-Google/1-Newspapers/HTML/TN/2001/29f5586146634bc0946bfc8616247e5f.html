About Us
1974 ad for an open house held when the "new" addition was finished.

The Shelbyville Times-Gazette is nearing its 150th year of service to Bedford County (counting from the founding of the Shelbyville Gazette in 1874). It was owned for many years by the family of the late Franklin Yates, whose legacy of journalism excellence we continue to honor today while looking toward the future.

The newspaper is published via print edition on Thursdays, and throughout the week on t-g.com and on Facebook. This website is operated by Richardson Media Group. 

We are proud to be your hometown source for all local news, sports, and lifestyles stories. For general news inquiries or submissions, please send to tgnews@t-g.com.
