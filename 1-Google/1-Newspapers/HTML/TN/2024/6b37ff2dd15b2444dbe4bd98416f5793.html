And The Theft Goes On

  • Thursday, February 15, 2024

I saw the report of yet more theft in the Chattanooga area on the local news last night.  Three men were caught on camera after using allegedly stolen cards at a local Walmart.  The cards were believed to have been taken from unlocked cars at Rainbow Lake.

Leaving valuables in unlocked cars is irresponsible and contributes to theft.  However true that may be, it does not entitle people to steal whatever they want whenever they want it.  I still wonder what sort of sense entitlement to steal is possessed by the people who apparently steal for a living. 

Personal responsibility, honesty and integrity have long ago taken a decline in the United States.  Would someone who believes he or she has a right to steal anything he or she wants please explain to me the source of that right?  I still believe that if one wants something, he or she pays for it --- outdated concept, I guess.

Tim McDonald
