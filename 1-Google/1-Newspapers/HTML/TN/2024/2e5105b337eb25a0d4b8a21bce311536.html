Docket No. EE8822

IN THE MATTER OF

Mariyah Giselle Blackmon (D.O.B. 3/22/2016

A Child under 18 years of age.

ORDER GRANTING MOTHER'S MOTION FOR SERVICE OF PETITION TO MODIFY PARENTING SCHEDULE THROUGH PUBLICATION

THIS CAUSE came to be heard before the Honorable Sheldon McCall, Magistrate of Juvenile Court in Memphis & Shelby County, Tennessee on Wednesday, February 7, 2024, upon Mother's Motion for Service of Petition to Modify Parenting Schedule through Publication, hereinafter "Motion," statements by counsel of Shaunita Lacole Callicutt (Malone), hereinafter "Mother," made in open court, the Affidavit of Diligent Search filed on behalf of Mother on January 26, 2024, and from the entire record in this cause, from all of which the Court finds as follows:

1.       The first subpoena attempted to serve Leon Michael Blackmon, Jr., hereinafter "Father," by private process seven (7) times, all unsuccessful.

2.       A second subpoena, attempted to serve Father through the Shelby County Sheriff’s Office, was also returned after several attempts, all unsuccessful.

3.        Mother's Affidavit of Diligent Search itemizes multiple attempts through various sources to locate Father, all unsuccessful.

4.      The return of service of the private process server indicates Father's automobile in the driveway on several occasions, but he never came to the door or responded to the process server's requests to contact him.

5.        Father had proper notice of Mother's Motion, set on Wednesday, February 7, 2024.

6.       As such, good cause exists to grant Mother's Motion and issue service of her Petition to Modify Parenting Schedule through Publication, as Father is intentionally evading service.

ALL OF THE ABOVE IS HEREBY ORDERED, ADJUDGED, AND DECREED.

Sheldon Y. McCall

Magistrate
