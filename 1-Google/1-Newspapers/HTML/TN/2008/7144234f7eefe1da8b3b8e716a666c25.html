WDSI FOX61 Anchor Scottie Goodman Leaving Station

  • Thursday, October 2, 2008
Scottie Goodman

Scottie Goodman is leaving her position as weeknight anchor of FOX61 First at Ten, it was announced by Tracye McCarthy, station general manager.

Ms. Goodman has been with FOX61 for a combined six years starting as a reporter and moving into the weekend anchor position before becoming co-anchor. Since 2005 she has anchored the 10 o’clock newscast solo.

Regarding her decision to leave, Ms. Goodman said, “I have thoroughly enjoyed my time at FOX61. It has been a pleasure to deliver the news to folks in the Tennessee Valley. People stop me all the time and tell me how much they appreciate being able to watch the news at 10, since it's an hour earlier and how we're often the last thing they see, before they go to sleep. That's such a compliment and I'm so thankful to all our viewers who welcome the FOX61 team into their homes at 10 p.m.

"I'll admit this is a bittersweet decision for me. After 13 years in the news business, it is definitely in my blood. But now that the boys are getting older, I think it's important for me to be home at night to be "mom" so we can have dinner together, attend Wednesday night church, and be at the ball field.

"I'll still be working, but it will be volunteer work, at the boys' school, our church and various charity organizations.”

Ms. McCarthy said, “Scottie has done a great job for us as the First at 10 anchor and we hate to lose her. However, I admire Scottie for her dedication on and off the news desk, and know she will continue to be a strong part of our community.”

Ms. Goodman will anchor FOX61 First at Ten until Wednesday.

A release regarding Scottie Goodman’s replacement will be available at a later time.
