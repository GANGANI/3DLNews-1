Sam Leman opens new location on Courtland Street

  • Aug 18, 2015
  • Aug 18, 2015

Sam Leman Chrysler Dodge Jeep of Morton recently completed the construction of their new dealership on Courtland Street. MEDC and the Morton Chamber of Commerce held a joint ribbon cutting to celebrate this major project. 

The new 40,000 square foot facility is another major investment in the retail corridor on Courtland. The move also adds an additional 12,000 square feet of space versus their previous location on Detroit Avenue.
