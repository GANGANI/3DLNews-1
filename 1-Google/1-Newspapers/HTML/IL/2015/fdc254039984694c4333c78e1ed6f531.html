Take "The Quill" Along

Paul and Barbara Corzatt of Dallas City celebrated their 67th wedding anniversary by traveling with their daughter Kathy Butler and taking "The Quill" along to visit their granddaughter Julie Butler Smith's family. On August 7th, they visited Marcy Jo's Mealhouse and a Joey and Rory concert in Pottsville, TN.

When you go on a trip, take "The Quill" along and have your picture taken with it and send it to us. Let's see where all "The Quill" can travel.
