About Us

Brighton Park-McKinley Park LIFE is a family owned and operated newspaper that has been in publication for more than 90 years. The circulation is based out of Chicago's southwest side and keeps residents current on local events and news in the community. Rain or shine, the weekly issue is delivered to doorsteps and electronically. We also deliver to local stores so that people can pick up a copy.
