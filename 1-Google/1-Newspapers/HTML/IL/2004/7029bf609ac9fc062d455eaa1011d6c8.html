Welcome to StrohmNews.com, the online hub of Strohm Newspapers, Inc., proudly serving the communities of Clark County, Illinois, since 1996.

At Strohm Newspapers, we believe that local news is not just a source of information; it’s a vital part of community life.

Our mission is simple: “It’s all about you.”

The Marshall Advocate, an independently produced newspaper with deep local roots, is your source for news that matters. Published every Tuesday and Friday, we deliver the latest updates directly to your mailbox via USPS and digitally to our subscribers’ emails.

With thirteen vending and retail locations, staying informed has never been easier. We’re dedicated to keeping you informed about the Clark County Board, Marshall City Council, Marshall School Board, Martinsville City Council, Martinsville School Board, and important public legal notices.

The Casey Westfield Reporter shares the same commitment to community and unbiased reporting. Published every Saturday, we deliver the Reporter right to your mailbox via USPS, covering the Clark County Board, Casey City Council, Casey-Westfield School Board, Westfield Village Board, and public legal notices.

Our readers and engaged community members are the heart of what we do. They care about local events, local government, and are deeply involved in area businesses, manufacturing, agriculture and many other services.

In addition to our comprehensive news coverage, we’re passionate about local sports. From football to track and field, we cover it all. Whether it’s the Marshall Lions and Lady Lions, Martinsville Bluestreaks and Lady Bluestreaks, Oblong-Palestine-Hutsonville (OPH) Wildcats, Hutsonville Tigers, or Casey-Westfield Warriors and Lady Warriors, you’ll find the latest updates on your favorite teams right here.

But we’re more than just news.

We’re a platform for community engagement. Share your life’s milestones for FREE in the Marshall Advocate and Casey Westfield Reporter! Announce engagements, weddings, births, and anniversaries with your community. Celebrate with us today!

We accept press releases from all local organizations, provide graphic/ad design and copy writing services for our advertisers, and publish informative guides like the Clark County Business Guide and event-specific guides throughout the year.

Our website is more than just a digital extension of our newspapers. It’s your gateway to community involvement. Share your photos and news release items for community events, clubs, and church groups. Explore our online edition, a password-protected, full-color PDF of the physical newspaper, with new features on the horizon.

At Strohm Newspapers, Inc., we’re dedicated to delivering relevant locally-focused news that matters to you. We welcome Letters to the Editor, Press Releases, News Tips, Classified Ads, and Display Ads. Contact Us today!

Together, we make our community stronger, more informed, and closer-knit.

Because at the end of the day, “It’s all about you.”

Thank you for being a part of our community.

Stay connected, stay informed, and let’s make a difference together.
