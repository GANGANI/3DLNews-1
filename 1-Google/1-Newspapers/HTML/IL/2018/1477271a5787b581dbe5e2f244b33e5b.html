United Methodist churches welcome new pastor

Posted Friday, October 5, 2018 7:37 am

The Forreston and Leaf River United Methodist Churches are pleased to announce that Pastor Sung-Eun Kim has been appointed to lead their ministry's.

Pastor Kim brings many gifts to the ministry of both churches including her passionate faith and experience in all aspects of ministry, especially working with children. For the past three years, she has served as the assistant to the Pastor at the North Northfield Methodist Church. She completed her undergraduate work at Methodist Theological University and attended Garrett-Evangelical Theological Seminary, Evanston, Ill.

Worship service is held at 9 a.m. at First United Methodist Church of Forreston and 10:30 a.m. at Leaf River United Methodist Church. All are welcome.
