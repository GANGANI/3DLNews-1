Sued in a Connecticut court under Connecticut law for allegedly helping a killer evade federal restrictions on short-barreled rifles, Sturm, Ruger & Co., a Connecticut-based gun manufacturer, argued it was entitled to remove the case to federal court because a “substantial federal question” was “embedded” in the state-law claims.
