Maddie L. Finch, 19, of LaHarpe, passed away Monday, Jan. 1, 2018, at her home.

She was born Sept. 23, 1998, in Macomb, the daughter of Jason and Carrie Walkington Finch.

Maddie attended grade school in LaHarpe and was a 2017 graduate of Illini West High School. She had finished her first semester at Illinois State. Her goal was to become a nurse and help children who were battling cancer. She worked at Jenni’s Ice Cream & Grill in Lomax, and R & M Market & Floral in LaHarpe.

Maddie was very artistic, loved being with her friends and watching Grey’s Anatomy. She had a big heart and had adopted her dog, Fynn, from the animal shelter.

She is survived by her parents, Jason and Carrie Finch of LaHarpe; her sister, Josie Finch of LaHarpe; grandparents, Nancy and Greg Walkington of Woodstock, Patty (Mark) Finch of LaHarpe, and Mark (Barbara) Finch of LaHarpe; aunts, Renee (Steve) Torrez of Walworth, Wis., Kristi (Mike) Newman of Woodstock, Britt Whitting of Norfolk, Va., Leanne (Mark) Schimming of Cary, and Kelli Adkisson of LaHarpe; uncles, Alex (Kristen) Finch of Lake of the Ozarks, Mo., and Joe Adkisson of LaHarpe; seven cousins, Maya, Hudson and Quincy Torrez of Walworth, Emmett, Elliot and Olive Newman of Woodstock and Clay Finch of Lake of the Ozarks; and many, many friends.

Friends called Jan. 4, 2018, at the LaHarpe Christian Church where the family met with friends.

Services were Jan. 5, 2018, at the LaHarpe Christian Church with Rev. Bruce Goettsche officiating. Burial was in the LaHarpe Cemetery.

A memorial fund has been established to help with expenses.

Banks & Beals Funeral Home in LaHarpe is assisting with the arrangements.

To sign the guest book and leave a condolence, visit her obituary at www.banksandbeals.com.
