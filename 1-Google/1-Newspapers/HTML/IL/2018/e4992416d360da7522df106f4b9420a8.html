Public Notices

Our full-service public notice solution, The Public Notice Network, handles legal notice placement across the state and manages all the details for you from point of submission until the final certificate is filed. To work with us today, call 312-644-1672 or email publicnotice@lawbulletinmedia.com.

Submit Notice

Since 1888 Chicago Daily Law Bulletin has published public notices for legal matters in Cook County, Illinois. This includes probate notices, foreclosures, adoptions, condemnations, tax deeds and other notices required by law to be published in a newspaper of general circulation. In addition to publishing the notices in Chicago Daily Law Bulletin, we upload them to chicagolawbulletin.com for quick searches and to the PublicNoticeIllinois.com, an aggregated statewide website hosted by the Illinois Press Association.
