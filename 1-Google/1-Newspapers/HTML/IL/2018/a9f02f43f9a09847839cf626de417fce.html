Our Audience

Chicago Daily Law Bulletin readers are the leading litigators and law professionals in Chicago and the collar counties. Since 1854, the legal and business communities have turned to CDLB for coverage of what’s happening in the legal community, trends, key court decisions, verdicts and settlements and much more. Our exclusive content – available in a daily print edition hand delivered in downtown Chicago every afternoon and 24/7 on its companion website – chicagolawbulletin.com, is valuable to attorneys, legal professionals, law students, managing partners, litigators, corporate lawyers and others. The CDLB publishes appellate court decisions and covers the latest developments with mergers, trends, court news and information, law schools and breaking news.
