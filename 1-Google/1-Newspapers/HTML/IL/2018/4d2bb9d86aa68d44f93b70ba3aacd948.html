HSHS Medical Group welcomes Venuti as pediatrician

By Editor | June 16, 2018 | 0

HSHS Medical Group added Guy Venuti, MD, pediatrician, to its team of providers. Venuti is now accepting patients through 21 years of age at HSHS Medical Group Pediatrics – O’Fallon, located at 670 Pierce Blvd., Suite 200 in O’Fallon, Ill.

Venuti earned his medical degree from the Uniformed Services University of Health Sciences in Bethesda, Md. He completed a pediatric residency at the University of California – Davis in Sacramento, California, in correlation with Travis Air Force Base. In his free time, Venuti enjoys European soccer, fly fishing, hunting, spending time with family and traveling.

To schedule an appointment with Venuti in O’Fallon, call (618) 206-2088. To learn more about Venuti and other HSHS Medical Group providers, visit hshsmedicalgroup.org.
