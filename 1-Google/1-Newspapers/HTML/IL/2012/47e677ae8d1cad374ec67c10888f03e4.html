Mount Carroll holds first Arts Walk

  • Apr 24, 2012
  • Apr 24, 2012 Updated Apr 24, 2012
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Print
  • Save

Mount Carroll hosts its first Arts Walk on April 19. Arts Walks will be held from 6-8 p.m. on the third Thursday of each month through October. Various artists perform their skills inside or just outside of businesses in the downtown area. The Arts Walks are sponsored by the Main Street Art Guild, the Mount Carroll Community Development Corporation, Brick Street Coffee and Photography by Elly.
