14 Ladies in News to be honored

By Rose Lane
Fourteen Ladies in the News will be honored during the Oklahoma Hospitality Club’s annual luncheon and style show at noon, March 10, at the Oklahoma City Golf and Country Club.
Tres Chic is providing the fashions. Tickets are $45. For more information, call June Bickerstaff at 722-1611. Tables will seat 10 and seating is limited.
The honorees include:
Rosemary Bachle, author, poet, businesswoman; Cindi Broaddus, author; Masie Bross, director of Whiz Kids; Anita Bryant, author, entertainer, businesswoman; Annette Drake, teacher; Amy Gardner, director, Stockyards City Main Street Program.
Also, Lori Hill, volunteer, fund raiser; Jackie Hutchinson, professional golf teacher; Kathy Kennedy, inspirational speaker; Kristi Koelsch, medical researcher, OMRF; Tina Majors, businesswoman, past president, National Women Business Owners; Vicki Miles-LaGrange, judge, United States District Court; E. Elaine Schuster, owner, Heritage Law Center; Stacy Burns Smith, CEO, Valir Rehabilitation Hospital.
— OKC FRIDAY Newspaper
