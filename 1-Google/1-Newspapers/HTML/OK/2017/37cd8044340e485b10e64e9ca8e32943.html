Print Advertising: Since 1901 businesses have been inviting people to come shop with them by advertising in The Altus Times. 

Insert Opportunities: If you are looking for another affordable way to showcase your business let us insert your pre-printed flyer in any or all of our publications. If you are needing help with printing your piece for insertion ask us about our affordable pricing.

Placing Retail Ads: To place display ads, inserts or other retail print products, contact Stacey Willmann, Director of Sales and Marketing at 888.400.1083 or email swillmann@hicks-co.com.

Placing Classified Ads: To place garage sale, automotive, real estate, personal property, employment or other classified ads, please contact the classified department, customerservice@altustimes.com or call 888.400.1083.

Classified and all regular advertising deadlines are 3 p.m. on Friday for the following Thursday edition.

Legals: Send legal notices for print in The Altus Times to customerservice@altustimes.com or call 888.400.1083.

Legal deadlines: The deadline is noon on Monday for Legals in Word document form, and noon Fridays for PDF formats.

The Altus Times now publishes weekly on Thursdays, and is delivered with your mail!
