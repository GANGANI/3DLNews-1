About Alva Review-Courier

The Alva Review-Courier is a semiweekly newspaper in Alva, Oklahoma. The Review-Courier is published Sunday and Friday. On Wednesday, we publish The Newsgram, a free newspaper distributed to all homes in a 3-county area, Woods and Alfalfa counties in Oklahoma and Barber County, Kansas. Ad deadlines are 5 p.m. Monday for the Newsgram (classifieds are accepted until noon Tuesday). Review-Courier ad deadlines are noon of the business day before publication.
