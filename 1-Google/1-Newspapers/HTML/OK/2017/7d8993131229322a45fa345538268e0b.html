Print Advertising: Businesses have been inviting people to come shop with them by advertising in The Frederick Press Leader for many years. 

Insert Opportunities: If you are looking for another affordable way to showcase your business let us insert your pre-printed flyer in any or all of our publications located in Frederick, Altus, and Mangum. If you are needing help with printing your piece for insertion ask us about our affordable pricing.

Placing Retail Ads: To place display ads, inserts or other retail print products, contact Mary Ann Callaway at 580-951-3307 or email advertising@hicksmediagroup.com

Placing Classified Ads: To place garage sale, automotive, real estate, personal property, employment or other classified ads, please contact the classified department, customerservice@altustimes.com or call 580-482-1221

Classified and all regular advertising deadlines are 3 p.m. on Friday for the following Thursday edition. Keep in mind that all of our editions are mailed through the U.S. Postal Service.

Legals: Legal deadlines: The deadline is Monday at noon for Thursdays publication. Send Legal notices to customerservice@altustimes.com

The Frederick Press publishes weekly on Thursdays, and is delivered by mail!
