McCurtain vs. Keota basketball

Wed, 12/06/2017 - 4:55pm

Above are scenes from the McCurtain-Keota basketball games on Friday.

McCurtain girls basketball players gather with Elisha Lovell (back center) after the girls game. Jason "Wally" Lovell, husband of Elisha and father of one of the McCurtain players, Ashlie Lovell, passed away Nov. 26 and his funeral was on the day of the game.

McCurtain cheerleaders perform.

Keota's Mariah Byrd (left) slaps hands with Lexi McClary (right) during player introductions.

Keota's Piper Reed bears down on McCurtain's Ashlie Lovell before Lovell goes to score.

McCurtain's Rachel Myers hits a three-pointer.

McCurtain boys basketball players charge out before the game.

Keota's Chaz Williams throws a pass.

Keota's Brandon Geer flies to the basket through contact from McCurtain's Bruce Mahnken.

McCurtain's Hagen Glenn turns to bring the ball up the floor.

McCurtain's Ethan Satterfield looks to pass.
