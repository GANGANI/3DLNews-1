FAQ
Where can I pick up copies of OKC Friday? You may pick up copies of the paper at our office located at 10801 N. Quail Plaza Drive (the SE corner of Hefner Road and May Ave.) Each copy is $1. Our office hours are Monday-Friday 9am to 5 pm. How can I submit a Letter to the Editor? You can email to Vicki at [email protected] or mail to FRIDAY Op-Ed, PO Box 20340, Oklahoma City, OK 73156. How can I submit a story idea? Where do I send a press release? Where do I send our travel photos? Please send story ideas, press releases and travel photos to our General Manager, Rose Lane at [email protected]. How do I subscribe to OKC Friday? You may call Jennifer Clark at 755-3311 or subscribe online. Just click on Services, Subscribe Now and fill out the form. We accept Visa or MasterCard through our secure site. Is the entire paper posted to the website each week? No, because our website if free and we do not have advertising on it, we only post a sampling of stories. Please subscribe or pick up a copy for the complete paper. What if I’m interested in advertising? Please contact Lovina Morgan at [email protected] or by calling 755-3311 for advertising rates and specials. For classified ads (help wanted, selling a car, etc.) please contact Rebecca Hall at [email protected]. Can I buy prints of pictures printed in the newspaper? Yes! For a 5x7, it is $10 and an 8x10 is $15. We can also save you a digital copy on a CD for $15. If the picture you would like to purchase is from the sports page, please contact the photographer that took the picture. Their name and website are usually included with the caption. If you are not sure, please give us a call. Please feel free to call us at 755-3311 with additional questions if needed.
