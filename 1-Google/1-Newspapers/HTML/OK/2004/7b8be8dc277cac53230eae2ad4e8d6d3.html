Strickland - McHugh
Carrie Kathryn McHugh, of Edmond, and Dax Andrew Strickland, of Oklahoma City, were married in a 7:30 p.m, July 31 ceremony at St. John the Baptist Catholic Church in Edmond. Father David Monahan and Father John Metzinger officiated. A reception followed at the Meinders Hall of Mirrors at the Civic Center Music Hall.
The bride is the daughter of Jon "Tucker" and Susan McHugh, of Edmond.
The groom is the son of Sharon Strickland and Ted Strickland, both of Oklahoma City. He is the grandson of Marvin and Nelda Tebow, of Oklahoma City, and Frances Strickland, also of Oklahoma City.
The bride was given in marriage by her father. Her sisters, Kelly Moody and Courtney Ehinger, were the matrons of honor.
The bride's other attendants were Erin Cooper, Candace Claro, Jenny Ferguson, Maggie Slavonic, Kari Keaton and Shanae Hilgenfeld.
The groom's brothers, Cole Strickland and Sean Strickland, were the best men. The groom's other attendants were David Luke, Matt McHugh, Brooks Walker, Justin Suiter, Clay Holmboe and Jeff Matulis.
Emily Reimer and Andrea Reimer were the flower girls. Coleman Moody and Carter Moody were the ring bearers.
The ushers were Brent Moody, Matt Ehinger, Brian Bogert, Ryan Cargill and Grant Godby. The house party included Amy Morton, Julie Cockrill and Andrea Buckley.
The bride is a graduate of Bishop McGuinness Catholic High School and the University of Oklahoma. She is manager of advancement operations at Heritage Hall School.
The groom is a graduate of Heritage Hall School and the University of Oklahoma. He is working on his MBA at Oklahoma City University. He is the training team lead at Advanced Financial Solutions.
Following a wedding trip to Negril, Jamaica, the couple will make a home in Oklahoma City.
