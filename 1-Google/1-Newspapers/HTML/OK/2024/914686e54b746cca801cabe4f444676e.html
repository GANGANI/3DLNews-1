Dr. Morris Allen Foster

3 weeks ago By News Staff
Body
Dr. Morris Allen Foster passed from this life on April 26, 2024 in Ft. Worth, Texas at the age of 92. Born and raised in Wilburton, Oklahoma he married Rosa Lou (Mickey) Pate on May 5, 1953. He proudly served in the U.S. Army from 1953-1955. After his honorable discharge, he worked his way through college at Oklahoma A&M, graduating in the last class before the institution became Oklahoma State University. He was a lifelong Cowboys fan and had an encyclopedic knowledge of OSU athletics.
