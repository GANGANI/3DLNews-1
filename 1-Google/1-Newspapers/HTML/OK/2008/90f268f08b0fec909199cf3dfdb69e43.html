Moss - Monahan
Whitney Delaune Monahan and Clayton Gene Moss, both of Oklahoma City, were married in a 5 p.m., Feb. 16 ceremony at Blue Bay Grand Esmeralda, Mayan Riviera, Mexico. Petra Maurus officiated. A reception with a surf and turf dinner and dancing followed the ceremony at the Esmeralda.
The bride is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Richard B. Turner, of Cleburne, Texas, and Boyce R. Monahan, of Fort Worth, Texas. She is the granddaughter of Edna Rose Delaune, of Fort Worth.
The groom is the son of Grenda Moss and the late Raymond E. Moss II, of Oklahoma City. He is the grandson of the late Mr. and Mrs. Gene Moss.
The bride, given in marriage by her father, chose a designer gown in ivory silk shantung from the Cymbeline Paris Collection. Her slim strapless, silhouette with roushing mermaid skirt and demi-train featured a corset back closure. The dress was from J. J. Kelly Bridal, OKC.
Maren Whitehead, sister of the bride, was the matron of honor. The bride’s other attendants were Amy Airington, Tara Beeler, Stephanie Battle, Drue Diesselhorst, Christina Hiersche, Kathryn Idoux, Jessica McIntyre, Tessa Moss, Alisa Ratzlaff, Gabriella Smith, Whittney Stauffer and Ashley Whitehill.
Travis Moss, brother of the groom, was the best man. The groom’s other attendants were Tucker Brollier, Buck Branson IV, Gavin Butcher, Philip Carey, Thomas Collins III, Paul Collins, Stephen Idoux, Cavett Lee, Quinn Leinen, Louis Pearce IV, Brooks Ratzlaff, Lee Welker and Matt Whitehead.
The ushers were Mack MacLean, cousin of the bride; Turner MacLean, cousin of the bride; and Tyler R. Monahan, brother of the bride.
Ninety guests from all United States traveled to Mexico for the ceremony.
The bride and groom selected the Destination Wedding Divas from Denver, Colo. to plan and coordinate their wedding weekend. Cary Holton Photography from OKC traveled with the wedding party to capture the eventful weekend.
The bride is a graduate of the University of Oklahoma where she was a member of Kappa Kappa Gamma Sorority. She is a publishing representative for Pearson Education.
The groom is a graduate of the University of Oklahoma where he was a member of Delta Tau Delta Fraternity. He earned an MBA from Acton School of Business. He is a commercial real estate broker for Wiggin Properties.
Following a trip to Serects Capri - Mayan Riviera, the couple is making a home in Oklahoma City.
