Americans with Disabilities Act Compliance Statement

We firmly believe that the internet should be available and accessible to anyone, and we are committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of circumstance and ability.

To fulfill this goal, we aim to adhere as closely as possible to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 (WCAG 2.1) at the AA level. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide array of disabilities. Complying with those guidelines helps provide a site that is accessible to all people.

This website is scanned regularly to confirm that the site elements and the content are in compliance with WCAG 2.1 standards.

Our goal is the support the widest array of browsers and assistive technologies possible, so users can choose the best fitting tools for them. Our goal is minimal limitations for all users.

Despite our best efforts to allow adjustment to the sites, there may still be pages or sections that are not fully accessible. We are constantly improving the accessibility of the site and the intent is to reach the optimal level of accessibility .

If you have discovered a page or element that does not allow access or have ideas that might help us improve access to the site, we would be happy to hear from you. Use the Contact link on any page or send us an email at support@etypeservices.com.
