Fleming - Diesselhorst
Matthew Michael Diesselhorst and Drue Elizabeth Fleming
  •  
Mr. and Mrs. Zane Fleming, of Edmond, have announced the engagement of their daughter, Drue Elizabeth Fleming to Matthew Michael Diesselhorst. Matt is the son of Michele Reese, of Edmond, and Dave and Sheila Diesselhorst, of Kingfisher.
Drue Fleming is a graduate of Edmond North High School. She continued her education at the University of Oklahoma, where she earned a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communications. She is currently employed as a pharmaceutical sales representative with Johnson & Johnson.
Matt Diesselhorst graduated valedictorian from Fairview High School and attended the University of Oklahoma where he received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Zoology/Biomedical Sciences. Matt is currently in his third year of medical school at the University of Oklahoma Health Sciences Center in Oklahoma City.
The two will marry at the Westminster Presbyterian Church in Oklahoma City on April 8.
