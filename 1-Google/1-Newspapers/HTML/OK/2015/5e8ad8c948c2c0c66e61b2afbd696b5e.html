Hospitality Club presents Ladies in the News for 49th time

Linda Haneborg
From left, Janie Axton, Bette Jo Hill, Carole Kenner and Mary Pointer, 2015 Ladies in the News. Photos by Linda Haneborg
Founded in 1923, the Oklahoma Hospitality Club is one of the state’s oldest and most prestigious charitable organizations.  
The annual fundraiser, Ladies in the News, gives the club the opportunity to honor outstanding Oklahoma women and to provide funds for area non-profits. This year’s grant recipients are OKC Metro Alliance, Inc/Women’s Firstep and Hearts for Hearing.  
Oklahoma City Golf and Country Club was the location for this fun-filled event complete with raffles, delicious chicken breast with brie and apricot chutney lunch, glorious singing by David Briscoe and a spectacular fashion show by Cindi Shelby’s r meyers featuring this year’s honorees. Back for another encore, mistress of ceremonies was the lovely and lively Linda Cavanaugh. She joined President Connie Weber, Chairman Marlene Beery and myself as honorary co-chair at the podium.

Edie Roodman, Evelyn McCoy, Jennifer Abney and Lisa Synar join the coveted list.


Please help me in congratulating this year’s Ladies in the News: Jennifer Abney, Janie Axton, Cathy Cummings, Bette Jo Hill, Becky Ivens, Carole Kenner, Elaine Levy, Evelyn McCoy, Mary Pointer, Lynda Powell, Edie Roodman and Lisa Synar.  
As is with any well done event, no one is an island. Hardworking President Connie Weber and Chairman Marlene Beery were joined by a determined and eager committee: Diane Strube, Sherrian Geurin, Pat Dean, Susan Howard, Dorothy Gray, Bonny Green, Jan Brown, Angela Giddens, Lynnda Berryhill, Jan Brown, Judy Perrin, Venita Springfield, Pat Grisham, Dolly Cox, Pat Patton, Carol Thomas, Becky Barr, Carol Bowman, Peggy Butcher and Lala Hatcher.
Spring fashions choreographed and provided for by r meyers were modeled by our illustrious honorees.  Made us all want to jump into spring and rush to Cindi Shelby’s fashion forward shoppe!
