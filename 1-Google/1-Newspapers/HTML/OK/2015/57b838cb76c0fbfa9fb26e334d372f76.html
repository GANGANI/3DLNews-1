Construction start set for summer Village school due $3.2 mil renovation
RIDGEVIEW Elementary is up next on the MAPS For Kids plan.
By GORDON WALKER
OKC FRIDAY
Ridgeview Elementary will be the next Fridayland school to get a facelift as part of the MAPS for Kids program.
A $3.2 million renovation project is scheduled to begin this summer at the school, located in the heart of The Village.

Oklahoma City schools officials will host a community meeting, at 6 p.m. Tuesday at Ridgeview, to allow patrons a chance to review the final designs.
