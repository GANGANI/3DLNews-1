Throwback Tulsa: City officials approve American Airlines lease for Tulsa maintenance base in 1946

  • Stephen Pingry
  • Jan 11, 2024
  • Jan 11, 2024 Updated Apr 16, 2024
  • 0

78 years ago, city officials approved American Airlines lease for the Tulsa maintenance base. The company would move its main maintenance base from New York’s LaGuardia Airport.

Since then, American Airlines has maintained the world’s largest commercial aviation maintenance facility in Tulsa. Today, Tech Ops – Tulsa is home to 22 buildings, including 3.3 million square feet of hangar and shop space positioned on 330 acres at Tulsa International Airport.

It is a hub for American’s base maintenance system, which also includes key maintenance hangars at Dallas-Fort Worth International Airport, Charlotte Douglas International Airport and Pittsburgh International Airport. American performs more maintenance work in-house than any other airline.

The company announced in February 2020 that it would pour $550 million over seven years into Tech Ops-Tulsa, making it the largest economic development investment in city history.
