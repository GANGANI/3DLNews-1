No more games to play: Crossings Christian seniors earned accolades

Jason Jewell
Crossings Christian senior Gabby Crampton (21) scored 51 goals and 28 assists in 4 years.
Editors Note: This is the next in a series of stories highlighting seniors in Fridayland schools who will not be playing this spring after schools shut down due to the coronavirus pandemic. This week, we are profiling seniors from Crossings Christian.
The following is a list of Crossings Christian seniors, arranged by sports.
