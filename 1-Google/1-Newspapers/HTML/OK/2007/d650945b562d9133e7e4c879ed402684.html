Government should be accountable to the public and
warn citizens of government actions affecting their way of life.

Printed public notice is part of our national heritage.
For hundreds of years, newspapers have been the best form of public notice. Oklahoma newspapers take this responsibility seriously and are committed to broad dissemination of notices to the public.

Now you can read the same notices on the Internet that appear in Oklahoma newspapers. It's print power with Internet ease.

Oklahoma newspapers voluntarily created this free site at no cost to government to help citizens find information that affects their way of life.

Basic Search Information

When you click the link below, you will be directed to the Legal Notice search site titled "Open". Several search options are available including searching by newspaper, city, county or Zip Codes. You can also search in a specific newspaper by clicking in the
"All Oklahoma State Newspapers" menu. Search for legals notices that have appeared in the past 180 days or use the customize date feature to narrow your search.
