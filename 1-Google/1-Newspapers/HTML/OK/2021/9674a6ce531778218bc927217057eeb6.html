Rising Star Gymnasts Medal As They Open The 2021-2022 Season

By News Staff on Wednesday, December 1, 2021
  • Level 4/5 gymnasts pictured above (from right) are Ocean Minor, Karlee Linn, Juliet Troutz, Rilynne Roberts, Kassidi Lowden, Aria Griggs, and Kashyrah Chavez. Photo provided
  • Pictured above are Platinum gymnasts Taylor Reyes, Trinity Rakestraw and Jayden Tabor. Photo provided
  • Level 3 gymnasts pictured to the left are Alivia Kohn, Emma Kohn, and Kalila Duck. Photo provided
  • Level 2 gymnasts pictured above are Alye Ramirez and Kara Robbins. Photo provided
The gymnasts at Rising Star began their 2021-2022 season with a bang! They competed Nov. 6 at Dynamo Gymnastics on Wes Hefner Blvd. in OKC. Levels 2 through Platinum competed. Clubs from Midwest City, Oklahoma City, Clinton, and Duncan attended. The Shawnee gymnasts placed in their respected levels and age groups: Level 2 – Kara Robbins 1st AA and Alye Ramirez 3rd AA, Level 3 – Kalila Duck 2nd AA…
