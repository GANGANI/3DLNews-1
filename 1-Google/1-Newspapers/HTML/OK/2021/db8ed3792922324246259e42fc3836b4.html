Weaver - Hammond
Courtney Lynn Weaver and Jeremy Matthew Hammond, both of Nashville, Tenn., will be married in an Aug. 2 ceremony at Crossings Communi-ty Church in Oklahoma City.
The bride is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. William Brian Weaver, of Edmond. She is a 2006 graduate of the University of Oklahoma and is currently pursing her Masters Degree at Vanderbilt University in Nashville.
The groom is the son of Mr. and Mrs. Gerald Waggoner, of Columbia, Tenn., and Rick Hammond, also of Columbia. He is a graduate of the University of Tennessee at Knoxville and is currently a state certified appraiser with Waggoner Appraisals, of Columbia.
