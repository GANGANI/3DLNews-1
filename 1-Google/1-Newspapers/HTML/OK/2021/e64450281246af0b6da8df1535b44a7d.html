Biscone - Hubbard
Mr. Joseph C. Biscone, II, of Mustang, Oklahoma, and Ms. Ellen Biscone of Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, are pleased to announce the engagement of their daughter, Emily Julian Biscone, to Charles Andrew Hubbard, son of Dr. W. Andrew Hubbard, of Oklahoma City, and Ms. Carolyn Hubbard, of Oklahoma City.
Emily and Andrew attended Bishop McGuinness High School, in Oklahoma City and Oklahoma State University in Stillwater. Andrew graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering. Emily earned her Juris Doctorate at Oklahoma City University.
Emily practices law with her father at the firm Biscone & Biscone in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, specializing in the areas of workers� compensation, personal injury and social security.
Andrew is a mechanical engineer and is employed by International Environmental Corporation in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.
Emily & Andrew were engaged in Ravena, N.Y., on New Years Eve, 2009,and will be married on Friday, Aug. 20, at St. Francis of Assisi Catholic Church in Oklahoma City. The wedding party consists of family and very close friends of the family. A reception to follow at the Quail Creek Golf and Country Club will follow.
