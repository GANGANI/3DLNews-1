Kaplan - Wilson
Lesa and Mat Wilson, of Edmond, announce the engagement and upcoming wedding of their son, Aaron Matthew, to Stephanie Joy Kaplan, daughter of Jamie and Glenn Kaplan, of Bryn Mawr, Penn.
Stephanie received a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology Degree from the University of Michigan. She later obtained both a Master of Arts Degree and a Master of education Degree in Psychological Counseling from Columbia University. Stephanie is a guidance counselor at the New York City public high school in Harlem.
Aaron, a graduate of Bishop McGuinness High School, earned a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and History from Williams College in Williamstown, Mass. He graduated magna cum laude and was elected to Phi Beta Kappa. He is employed at Och-Ziff Capital Management, a new York City-based alternative asset management firm, in the private equity group.
Stephanie is the granddaughter of the late Eleanor Brownstein and I.E. Brownstein, Arthur Kaplan and the late Betty Kaplan. Aaron is the great-grandson of Virginia McCann, of McAlester. He is the grandson of Pat and Virginia Fitter, of Edmond, and the late Harold Wilson and Bettie Cope.
The couple resides in New York City and is planning a June 28 wedding at the Radnor Valley Country Club in Villanova, Penn.
