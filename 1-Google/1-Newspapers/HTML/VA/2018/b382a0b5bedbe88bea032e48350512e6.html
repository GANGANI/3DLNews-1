Press Release guidelines

  • All press releases are subject to editing, available space, and run at the discretion of the editor.
  • Include a name and contact information for any questions that may arise concerning the release.
  • For statements that require attribution within the article, provide the name and position held of the person who is responsible for the statements. All press releases need attribution.
  • If a release includes a photo, include the names of all people in the photo from left to right, front to back.
  • Remember the basics: who, what, when, where, why and how. For upcoming events: name, time, date, place, sponsor, speakers, performers, topics.
  • Press releases may be sent to [email protected] by Tuesdays at noon.
