Jessica Lynn Estruch of Mineral, Va passed away July 25th, 2023. She was born Oct. 24th, 1990.

She is survived by her Mom and Dad, Al and Lisa, sister, Stephaine Addington, brother, Kenny, and son, Joel Thomas Pauley, and many uncles, aunts and cousins.

She graduated from Louisa High School and worked as a nurse’s aid. We will miss her bubbly personality and infectious smile.

Please join us for “A Celebration of Life”. The service will be held at Walton Park, Mineral, Va on Saturday, August 19th at 1 p.m.

Contributions may be made to her son for his education and his love of football. Please send contributions to: 202 Louisa Av., Mineral, VA, 23117.

We want to dedicate a poster for friends to bring photos to the service.

To plant a tree in memory of Jessica Estruch as a living tribute, please visit Tribute Store.
