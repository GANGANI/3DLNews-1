Thursday, May 30, 2024

Results of the 2022 VPA News & Advertising Contest

The Virginia Press Association announced the winners in the 2022 VPA News & Advertising Contest during the 2023 VPA Conference and Awards Banquet, held on May 6th at the Short Pump Hilton. We celebrated the great work being done across the Commonwealth and handed out awards to well deserving recipients. Congratulations to all the winners and thank you for your impactful work.
