9/11 Ceremony of Remembrance

September 9, 2019September 9, 2019 HRM Staff 9/11 Ceremony of Remembrance, Chesapeake Va
Tweet

The “9/11 Day of Remembrance” ceremony will be held in Chesapeake City Park at the Chesapeake 9/11 Memorial site to remember those who perished on 9/11, to honor those who rose in service to assist in the search and recovery efforts, and to pay tribute to those who protect and defend our country in the ongoing war against terrorism.

When/Where:

8:30 a.m. Ceremony – “9/11 Day of Remembrance”
Chesapeake City Park, 900 City Park Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23320  

8:46 a.m. Bell Tolling – Church bells/Carillons/Handbells
“Let Freedom Ring” – all faith-based communities throughout Hampton Roads are encouraged to participate.

There will also be a special performance by Symphonicity at 6 pm at Courtyard Square Park during the Food Trucks on the Square event.

Information: City of Chesapeake Expanded Public Art Committee | Chesapeake 9/11 Facebook page or 757-382-6411.
