13650 E. 11 Mile Road, Warren, MI 48089

Since 1981, C & G Newspapers has served metro Detroit residents in Macomb, Oakland and Wayne counties through its award-winning publications featuring the best local journalism. The family-owned company has 21 bi-weekly publications that reach 45 communities and are delivered to more than 571,000 homes via direct mail.
