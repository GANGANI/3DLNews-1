Cassandra Hankins, Ph.D., MA LLPC

  • The Grand Rapids Times
  • May 13th, 2022

A native of Detroit who now resides in Grand Rapids, Michigan, Cassandra serves in ministry with her husband, Gary E. Hankins Th.M., the Senior Pastor of Resurrection Fellowship Church. They have been married for 38 years, and God has blessed them with five children-- ("The 5 Heartbeats") Maggie (32), Luke (30), Titus (29), Joel (26), and Talitha "Kumi" (24), a Son-in-law, Justin, and three grandchildren. Cassandra enjoys being a wife, mother, and grandmother, but most importantly, she is a Christian who seeks to glorify God via her God-given life. She trusted Jesus Christ as her Lord and Savior at the age of 7 and has dedicated her life to serving Him.

Cassandra is a limited licensed professional counselor, teacher, and conference speaker. By God's grace, she has completed a Bachelor of Science in Health from Grand Valley State University, a Master's of Arts in Biblical Counseling from Grand Rapids Theological Seminary, and a Doctorate in Counseling and Psychological Studies at Regent University. She is a diligent worker who currently heads the Women's Ministry and the Counseling Ministry and teaches Sunday School at her church. Cassandra is a Community Health Program Specialist at Spectrum Health Hospitals and Chairman of the Board for Spectrum Health Academy of Health Sciences & Technology program Advisory Council. She develops and implements healthcare career education programs for high school students in this role. Additionally Cassandra is the Mentor Director for the Grand Rapids Theological Seminary Urban Cohort program.

She is a popular speaker at marital, singles, and women's conferences and seminars. Cassandra has a comic talent that she utilizes to help people. Cassandra employs her Godgiven talents to challenge and encourage others while speaking the truth in love.
