Al Arends recognized for 30 years with Secord Fire

By News Staff on Wednesday, December 28, 2022
  • Al Arends recognized for 30 years with Secord Fire
At the December Secord Township Board Meeting held on December 21, 2023, Al Arends (right) was recognized and awarded this plaque commemorating his 30 years of service to the Secord Township Fire Department. He achieved Fire Fighter Class MFR. Al Joined the Department in 1991. Congratulations, Al!
