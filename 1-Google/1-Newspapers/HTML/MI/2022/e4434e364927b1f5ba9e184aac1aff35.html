JOIN US: Inves­­­­t in Education ... Invest in You!

Benton Spirit News has been serving the Benton Harbor Community for 20 years.
Princella Tobias
  •  
Education is a POWERFUL TOOL! Every day people are enhancing their lives by becoming more educated!

Young and old. From books to www, people are into becoming educated.

Next week, the Benton Spirit Newspaper will celebrate 20 years of informing, enhancing, showcasing, promoting, and EDUCATING through multi-media. We are still here! Praise the Lord!

All day, every day, I thank the Father for our keeping us and being with us. I thank the many friends and supporters of the Benton Spirit. I thank the youth of Benton Harbor for their energy and passion. I thank our seniors for their prayers, wisdom, and stories. I thank our volunteers, our staff and those who have shared their stories and insights with us. I am proud that we have shown Benton Harbor and its people in their full humanity through these pages from our years of publications.

So, we celebrate these 20 years as Black History, as Woman History and as Benton Harbor History. We give God all the Glory for our 20 years.

Yet, at the same time, we also must keep it 100% real – for it has been a struggle, where I consistently must fight prejudice, injustice, inequity, racism, and low expectations from others.

For an example, take the article below, the stats of COVID and other variants in our community are low, but still we have only received one insertion order in approximately 10 years. There is a reason former Governor Jennifer Granholm’s Benton Harbor Task Force Report, named the Benton Spirit the #1 Recommendation for Communications.

The bitter makes the sweet even sweeter, so we carry on, like our ancestors did. There are more rivers to cross, more barriers lie before us.

So, we ask you to JOIN US. The Benton Spirit has always been about us, not about me. It has been my ministry for the benefit of our community. We tell your stories, and in our pages others can see you, beautiful, proud and strong. Overcomers.

JOIN US in continuing to empower, inspire and EDUCATE our great community.

Today, right now, you can join us, in keeping the Spirit of the Dream alive. It’s simply: ADVERTISE!

Ask the places you spend money, or organizations that receive grant money from your statistics, or have pledged to help Benton Harbor, or believe that Black Lives Matter and Women Lives Matter, and others, who like myself KNOW that All Lives Matter and we in this moment will make a difference … Invest in Education, Invest in our Community, Invest in our history, Invest in yourselves: ADVERTISE!

I send you my love, I give you my heart, celebrate the Spirit, celebrate you. I love you.

#BentonSpiritCelebrating20Years — Benton-Michiana Spirit News
