Breaking: Fire in Downtown Holly

  • Jun 21, 2022
  • Jun 21, 2022 Updated Jun 21, 2022
  • 0
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Print
  • Save

      Holly — At 4 p.m. on Tuesday, June 21, there were 911 calls reporting the front of the Holly Moose Lodge at 210 S. Broad St., Holly caught fire. Multiple fire departments were dispatched to the scene in downtown Holly.

      The fire quickly spread and jumped to other buildings including Battle Alley Arcade Antiques and the historic Holly Hotel on Battle Alley.

      At 4:53 p.m., it was reported that fire and police departments were seeking to have the train tracks shut down so that firefighters could run water hoses over the tracks.

      The Holly Hotel is a nationally known restaurant, listed on the United States Register of Historic Places. It has Queen Anne Victorian architecture, and a loyal following since 1891.   

      Battling the fire was difficult with strong winds and 95-degree temperatures Tuesday afternoon.

      This story will be updated as more information becomes available.
