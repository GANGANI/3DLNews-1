The Michigan Association of Register of Deeds (MARD) recently held their 108th Annual Educational Conference in the City of Grand Rapids. The conference was hosted by Kent County Clerk/Register of Deeds, Lisa Posthumus Lyons and current MARD President, Brandon Denby, Livingston County Register of Deeds.
