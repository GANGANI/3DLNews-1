FHA, Brewery Tour set for June 23

  The Frankenmuth Historical Association and Frankenmuth Brewery are collaborating to celebrate one of the community’s favorite aspects of Frankenmuth’s cultural heritage: Beer!

  This event will take place Sunday, June 23, from 4:30-6pm.

  This one-of-a-kind experience begins at the Frankenmuth Historical Museum, 613 South Main Street, with a guided tour, including an in-depth look at the original German settlers who colonized Frankenmuth in 1845. Explore an interactive journey through Frankenmuth’s rich brewing history – from past to present.

  The FHA recently completed a two-year renovation and addition to its galleries, including a large room dedicated to brewing history and breweriana. ......
