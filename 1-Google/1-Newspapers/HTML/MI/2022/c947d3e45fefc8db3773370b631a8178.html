Photos speak many words, so we'll leave just a few here.

At times, Year 2022, you were hard, unforgiving, even cruel. But you also offered moments to grow, to triumph, to celebrate.

You showed us pain ... grief ... and communities roiled in conflict. But also redemption, resurrection, resolution among neighbors.

For every tornadic storm or devastating fire, you showed us opportunities to rebuild.

For every nonsensical act of violence, you showed us hope in the warmth and resiliency of children.

For every worry over our health and the future of democracy, you showed us medical progress and peace at the polls.

You took a lot from us, 2022. But you also gave.
