Sadly, we announce the unexpected passing of our brother, William Lee Bodine, in his home (Dundee, MI) at age 58. Born July 2, 1964, he was the son of Lennon C. and Dolores R. (Goodnough) Bodine. A graduate of Dundee High School, Lee was a proprietor and worked in construction, carpentry and general contracting. He enjoyed hunting, camping, gardening, boating, riding recreational vehicles, and loved animals-especially his dog, Gage and cat, Abby.

Lee is survived by siblings: Margie (Ray) Kuehnlein, C. Dean (Mary) Bodine, Nancy (Harry) Salenbien, Bonnie (Donald) Gildersleeve, Dolores (Jerry) McMullen, Annette (Perry) Freelon, Peggy Heiden, Lori (Douglas) Brown, sister-in-law: Jackie Bodine, and multiple nieces and nephews. He was preceded in death by parents, brother: L. Timothy Bodine, and 2 nephews: Taylor Brown and Gabriel Cavasin.

To celebrate Lee’s life, arrangements have been made at Capaul Funeral Home in Ida, MI on Tuesday, February 28, from 11 am to 8 pm.

Memorial contributions may be made to the family.

To send online condolences to the family, please visit www.capaulfuneralhome.com
