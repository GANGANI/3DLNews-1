    Maryann Laurine Jessop OBITUARY

    Jessop, Maryann Laurine, age 85 of Sterling Heights, passed away January 26, 2024. Loving mother of the late Michael (Brenda) Jessop, The late Thomas (Cheryl) Jessop and Richard (Christy) Jessop, cherished grandmother of Krisssy (Chris) Wald, Jenny (Eric) Sandow, Kelly (Josh) Lewis, Tommy (Meagan) Jessop, Justin (Autumn) Jessop and Nick Jessop great grandmother of 13, dear sister of the late Sister Nadine Sheehan, Jacqueline (the late Jerry) Noonan, Sharon Crisan and Carl (Becky) Sheehan. Funeral Mass will be held Thursday, February 1, 2024, 11 a.m. with an instate time of 10:30 a.m. at St. Andrew Catholic Church, 1400 Inglewood, Rochester. The family will receive friends Wednesday, 3-8 p.m. at the Potere-Modetz Funeral Home, 339 Walnut Blvd. Rochester. Interment Mt. Avon Cemetery. In lieu of flowers memorials may be made to the American Cancer Society. Online guest book: www.modetzfuneralhomes.com
