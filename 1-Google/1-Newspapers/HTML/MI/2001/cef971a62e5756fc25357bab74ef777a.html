KIMBERLY ANN CONELY

SANDUSKY
By ohtadmin
on April 23, 2014



 

Kimberly Ann Conely, age 45, of Sandusky, passed away Sunday, April 13, 2014 at Henry Ford Hospital in Detroit.

She was born November 8, 1968 in Bakersfield, Calif., the daughter of the late Jack and Henrietta (Packard) Nelson.

She was a lifelong area resident and a member of the Full Gospel Tabernacle Church, Bad Axe.

Kimberly married David Conely on November 26, 1994.

She worked for Jerry’s Foodland for ten years and for Trelleborg Manufacturing for one year.

Kimberly loved to play golf.

She is survived by her husband, David; two sons, Jack (April) Pangburn of Madison Heights, Scott Pangburn of Farmington Hills; two daughters, Amanda Pangburn of Sandusky, Mercadez Conely of Sandusky; three sisters, Laura (Walt) McClorey of Sandusky, Melissa Nelson of North Carolina, Kelly (Jose) Villafranca of Palms; father-in-law, Owen (Joyce) Conely of Sandusky; two grandchildren, Evelyn Orroco and Carter Pangburn.

Funeral services were held at Marsh Funeral Chapel, Sandusky. Pastor Mike Beeler officiated.

Burial was in the Zion Cemetery in Sandusky.

Memorial suggestions may go to the Full Gospel Tabernacle Church, Bad Axe.

Arrangements by Marsh Funeral Chapel, Sandusky. (989) 648-2244 – www.marshfuneral.com.
