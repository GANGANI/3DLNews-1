About

In its 137 years of publication, the Reporter has maintained its vitality and importance to the Iron County, Michigan community by providing accurate and reliable local news coverage. Published each Wednesday, the Reporter reaches more than 2,400 homes in Iron County.

The Reporter also publishes, each Tuesday, the Iron County Shopper's Guide. It is a free publication which is delivered through the mail and reaches more than 11,000 homes in Iron County, Michigan and parts of Vilas, Forest and Florence counties in Wisconsin, and Gogebic, Baraga and Ontonagon counties in Michigan.
