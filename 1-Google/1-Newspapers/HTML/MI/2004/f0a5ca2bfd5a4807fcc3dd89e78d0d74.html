Straitsland Resorter

The Resorter was an independent weekly newspaper covering Indian River, Burt & Mullett Lake areas and Cheboygan County activities.
Located in downtown Indian River, Straitsland Publishing llc publishes the Indian River Resource Guide & Chamber Directory.
