Pet of the Week: Sable is an affectionate cat who would love to be your companion

  • Joe Ballor
  • PUBLISHED: July 11, 2014 at 9:10 a.m. | UPDATED: June 16, 2021 at 7:38 a.m.
  • Categories: Local News, Michigan News, National News, News, Things To Do

If you’re looking for a companion, Sable could be a great choice.

Sable is a 1-year-old domestic longhair, black-and-white cat available for adoption through New Beginnings Animal Rescue who loves being with people.

‘Sable is a real sweet cat,’ said Lisa Hill of New Beginnings. ‘She’s real laid back and she’s affectionate. She wants to be with you.

‘She loves being brushed, so her having long hair isn’t a problem. She loves being picked up and held and carried around. She’s an all-around really great little cat.’

According to Hill, Sable has a friendly personality.

‘She is very affectionate,’ Hill said. ‘She loves catnip and she loves to be brushed and held. She’d be a great addition to any family.’

Sable gets along well with other cats and also does well with dogs.

‘She’d be great as a companion for other cats in the home or as a companion for yourself as an only cat,’ Hill said.

All animals available for adoption through New Beginnings have been spayed or neutered and have all their shots. To adopt Sable, fill out an application online at nbarmichigan.org, or stop by the New Beginnings Adoption Center at 2502 Rochester Road in Royal Oak and meet her in person.

For Adoption Center hours or more information, call 248-543-4070 or email nbarmichigan@gmail.com.
