Experience the rich history of the Harbour View Inn during your stay on Mackinac Island. Located just off the beaten path, close enough to town but far enough from the hustle and bustle. We have four buildings to choose from with two distinctive styles. The Chateau LaFramboise and the B & B House offer historic French and Victorian styles with modern conveniences. The Guest House & Carriage House offer contemporary furnishings with island appeal. All our rooms are non-smoking and offer complimentary wireless internet, flat screen TV’s and our complimentary continental breakfast each morning. Enjoy the beauty of our gardens or sit on one of our many porches while you relax during your stay. We have specialty rooms with whirlpools or fireplaces that are great for your honeymoon, anniversary, or special getaway.
