Students of the Week


By ohtadmin
on November 17, 2014



 

The Brown City Lower Elementary Student of the Week is kindergartener Wyatt Fleming. He is the son of Ashley Genshaw and Dean Fleming. Teacher Beth Schopp chose him because, “Wyatt doesn’t let any obstacles stand in his way! He is a very determined little boy who comes to school every day with a contagious smile on his face. Keep up the great work Wyatt!”

The Brown City Upper Elementary Student of the Week is fifth grader Anthony DeBono. He is the son of Dan and Alanna DeBonno. Teacher Erika McClintic chose him because, “Anthony is a great kid! He really cares about his school work and about doing his best work every day. He is caring and kind to everyone at all times and is just a wonderful positive person to have in class!”

The Brown City Jr/Sr High School Student of the Week is freshman Annie Schlaud. She is the daughter of Kevin and Tammy Schlaud. Teacher Audrey Prowse chose her because, “Annie always comes to class with a smile on her face and a positive attitude. In addition, she works hard to accomplish any task that she is given. It is refreshing to have her in my algebra class. Keep up the good work, Annie!”
