Greenville schools have two bids for Black Field

Audio Article

Cory Smith | May 30, 2024

Looking to relinquish ownership of the historic Black Field property, the Greenville Public Schools Board of Education now has two bids to evaluate.  During the May 13 meeting of the board, Superintendent Wayne Roedel said the school district had received two bids to acquire Black Field, both of which were submitted during the 30-day window from when the request for bids was initially posted on the school district’s website on April 10 until the deadline of 4 p.m. on May 10. 

Ionia Co. board approves $200,000 in ARPA funds for Road Department projects

Audio Article

Tim McAllister | May 30, 2024

The Road Department's request to begin spending American Rescue Plan Act (ARPA) funds on a variety of facility improvements was approved by the Ionia County Board of Commissioners this Tuesday. Road Department Managing Director Linda Pigue provided the board with a spreadsheet listing more than $150,000 worth of projects that they requested ARPA funds to complete, along with their location and the potential cost. The spreadsheet listed seven projects at their Riverside facility and two at their Jordan Lake facility.
