Fifteen Jeff Davis High football players were named to the 2018 All Region 2-AA Team.
First team stars were sophomore tight end James Wood, junior offensive lineman Jaylen Grady, senior defensive lineman Cody Ewing and senior defensive back Emanuel Rumph.
Making the second team were junior quarterback Britt Metts, sophomore running back Austin Wood, junior fullback Jashaun Kirkby, senior offensive lineman Ty Padgett, junior offensive lineman Christian Ray and sophomore defensive back Cade Walters.
Honor mention selectees were senior offensive lineman Steven McKever, senior split end Sam Snell, senior defensive lineman Roderick Lewis, junior lineman Mitchell Jones and senior defensive back Nijai Gillis.
