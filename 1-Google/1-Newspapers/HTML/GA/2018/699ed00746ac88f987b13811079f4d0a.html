Hall woman believed to have fallen off dock, drowned in Lake Lanier
Hall County Sheriff’s Office investigators believe a Hall County woman fell off of a dock and drowned Tuesday night in Lake Lanier. Hall County Fire Services, the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Georgia Department of Natural Resources and the sheriff’s office sent personnel to Holiday Marina around 10:15 p.m. Tuesday. First responders started CPR, but the woman, Teresa Ann Graham, 51, was pronounced dead.
