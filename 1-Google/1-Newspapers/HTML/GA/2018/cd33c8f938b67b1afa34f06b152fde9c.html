Church Notes

Stockbridge First UMC
The church will host their Vacation Bible School from June 26 – 30 from 9 a.m. until noon for ages 3 years (potty trained) – incoming 5th grade. Register online at https://stockbridgefirst.org/vbs. The church is located at 4863 N Henry Blvd. in Stockbridge.

Victory Christian Ministry
The community is invited to a free Family and Friends Funday on Saturday, June 24 from 2 until 5 p.m. Music will be performed by the Rhythm and Gospel Network. There will be games with prizes for the children, a bouncy house and face painting. They will have local vendors, free food and an hourly raffle (you must be present to win). The church is located at 1320 S. Zack Hinton Parkway in McDonough.

The Ole Country Church
The community is invited to a free Southern Gospel Concert featuring Heirborn Quartet on Sunday, July 9 at 10 a.m. Join them for a Homecoming Lunch after the service. The concert is free with a love offering being taken. For more information, contact the church at 770-957-2818. The church is located at 2649 Ga. Hwy. 155 North in McDonough.

Wesley Way UMC
Sunday morning worship services are held in person at 8:30 and 10:30 a.m. Services are streamed live at 10:30 a.m. each Sunday. For more information, call the church at 770-898-3023 or visit them online at www.wesleyway.org. The church is located at 150 John Wesley Way in McDonough.

Faith Independent Baptist Church
Sunday School is held at 9:30 a.m. Sunday worship services are at 10:30 a.m. and at 5 p.m.  Mid-week worship is held on Wednesday at 7 p.m. The service can be streamed at www.fibcministries.com. For more info, call the church at 770-957-9889. The church is located at 1099 Hwy. 81 West in McDonough.

First Baptist Church Stockbridge
Sunday School is held at 9:30 a.m. and on-campus Morning Worship begins at 10:45 a.m. Online services are held on YouTube at First Baptist Church Stockbridge Georgia and www.facebook.com/emma.oforiatta/. A Sunday evening service is held on campus and online beginning at 6 p.m. Wednesday evening services begin with Children’s Choir at 6 p.m. Children’s Mission Ministry begins at 6:45 p.m. Prayer meeting and Bible study begin at 6:30 p.m. Adult Choir Practice starts at 7 p.m. Wednesday Prayer and Bible Study are available on Facebook at 6:30 p.m. For more information, call the church at 770-474-4484. The church is located at 4566 N. Henry Blvd. in Stockbridge.

Flippen United Methodist Church
The community is invited to traditional Worship each Sunday morning at 10:30 a.m. in person or on Facebook Live. Visit them online at www.flippenumc.org/. The church is located at 3707 Jodeco Road in McDonough.

Jodeco Road UMC
Sunday morning worship services are held in person at 10:30 a.m. Services are also streamed live on their Facebook page. For more information, call the church at 770-957-8300. The church is located at 1500 Jodeco Road in Stockbridge.

Henry Baptist Church
Family Groups are held each Sunday at 9:45 a.m. with a Worship Service at 11 a.m. Mid-week Services are held at 6:45 p.m. The service can be streamed on the Henry Baptist Church Facebook page. The church is located at 4035 Jodeco Road in McDonough.

Life in the Spirit Full Gospel Church
Sunday School is held at 10 a.m. Sunday worship service is at 11 a.m. Midweek worship is held on Wednesday at 7 p.m. The service can be streamed on the Life in the Spirit Full Gospel Church Facebook page. For additional information, contact Pastor Jeff or Christine Clark at 770-712-8328. Visit them online at https://litsfg.webs.com/. The church is located at 638 Daily Mill Road in McDonough.

Locust Grove Church of Christ
The community is invited to worship each week. Sunday School is at 9 a.m. and Worship Services are at 10:30 a.m. Evening Worship is at 2 p.m. Monday Bible Class is at 7 p.m. Wednesday Bible Class is held at 7 p.m. The church also has free clothing and shoes for males and females, all ages and sizes. Items are new and like new. For an appointment, call 559-232-4858. For more information, visit www.locustgrovechurchofchrist.com. Contact the church office at 678-994-4668 or 770-957-6999. The church is located at 240 Beersheba Road in Locust Grove.

Berean Baptist Church
The community is invited to worship each Sunday starting with Sunday School at 9:45 a.m. Morning Worship is held at 10:50 a.m. and Evening services are held at 6 p.m. Bible Study is held each Wednesday at 7 p.m. For more information, call the church at 256-590-7676, visit them online at www.bbcatlanta.com and on Facebook @Bereanbaptistchurch ATL. The church is located at 350 Atlanta Street in McDonough.

McDonough First United Methodist Church
The community is invited to worship in-person each Sunday at 10:30 a.m. Virtual sermons are available on YouTube or online at https://vimeo.com/mcdonoughfumc. The church is located at 151 Macon Street in McDonough. For more information, call the church at 770-957-4150, or visit www.mcdonoughfumc.com.

Open Door Church International
Worship services are held each Sunday night at 8 p.m. by calling 518-418-1065. For more information, email Pastor Mack at opendoorphonechurch@gmail.com.
