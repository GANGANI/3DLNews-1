Robert Billingsley, 70, of Carrollton, Georgia, died March 30, 2018.

Funeral services and viewing will be Friday, April 6, at A Place of Refuge Church in Carrollton. His Viewing will be from 10 a.m. until 12 p.m. and funeral services will be at 12 p.m. Burial will follow in Carrollton City Cemetery.

Walker Funeral Home, Carrollton.
