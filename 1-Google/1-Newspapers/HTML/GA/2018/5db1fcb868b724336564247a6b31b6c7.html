Next Phase Magazine

For more than 53 years, The Atlanta Voice — Atlanta’s most widely read and respected African-American newspaper — has upheld its legacy of informing, educating, inspiring, and empowering our community.

Consistent with that tradition, we are excited to present our latest supplement for junior and senior high school students to help them prepare for the NEXT PHASE of their lives. This uniquely positioned stand-alone magazine will focus on helping students identify their path, passion and future.

NEXT PHASE is a publication that will assist parents and students to be more informed about their options, whether higher education or a career path that could direct them to technical schools or the military.

For more than 58 years, The Atlanta Voice has ably provided a voice for the voiceless. It is the largest audited African American community newspaper in Georgia. Founded in 1966 by the late Ed Clayton and the late J. Lowell Ware, The Atlanta Voice has evolved and redefined its efforts to better connect with the community it serves.
