Contact

The Colonnade is the official student newspaper of Georgia College & State University and is published throughout the academic year by its student staff. The paper’s primary mission is to cover events and issues of importance to the campus community and to provide a forum for public debate and discussion. Working on the newspaper gives students an opportunity for hands-on experience in a variety of areas such as management, writing, advertising, editing, photography, design and publishing. The editors have the authority to make content decisions without censorship or advance approval. A faculty advisor, Christina Smith, is assigned to the newspaper.
