COBB ONLINE HOMEPAGE


Cobb web site answers
transportation questions and more

Cobb's newest web site offers the inside information on transportation issues throughout Cobb County. The new site includes information about McCollum Field, CCT, Silver Comet Trail and road construction updates.

Recently launched, the DOT site is already proving to be a great resource for the community.

"With bus schedules, maps of the Silver Comet Trail and live traffic cameras, we are providing needed information in a quick and convenient format," said Webmaster Mike Lee.

The site was created to answer the most common questions about transportation. The CCT section includes information for bus routes, arrival times and passenger fares.

The Silver Comet Trail page includes maps of the trail, access areas, and future routes. The status of major road projects and closings will be updated on a regular basis along with live images of road conditions in some of Cobb's busiest roadways.

The site also includes direct email access to DOT, and a comprehensive phone list of DOT contacts.

"DOT is using the website to not only get information out, but to hear back from the public as well," said Cobb DOT Director Dan Dobry. "Delivering data while receiving citizen feedback allows us to do a better job in serving Cobb's motorists."

For information, visit http://www.cobbdot.org
