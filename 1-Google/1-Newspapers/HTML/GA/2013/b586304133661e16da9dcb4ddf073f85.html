About MGI

 
For more than 30 years, the MIDDLE GEORGIA INFORMER has worked to educate readers on important issues that affect our community. We spotlight businesses and organizations in Middle Georgia's surrounding counties to give them exposure and support. And we feature hometown Heroes and Heroines — ordinary people doing extraordinary things — who make a difference in the communities in which they live and serve.
