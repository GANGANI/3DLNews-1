Car Tags/Registration

Web site: http://www.cobbtax.org/

Out-of-state tags expire 30 days after you move to Georgia, regardless of the dates shown on the tag. Only two exceptions: non-resident military members and full-time college students.
(If you are planning a trip to the tag office, our busiest days are Mondays and Fridays. Also avoid the first and last workday of the month.)

Call our 24-hour voice information system at: (770) 528-TAGS (8247). Your 24-hour detailed information source.


Main Tag Office
700 South Cobb Drive
(Near Atlanta Road just south of Lockheed)

Satellite tag offices:
East Cobb Government Service Center
4400 Lower Roswell Road
(Across from Mt. Bethel Methodist Church)

Market Square Shopping Center
2950 Canton Highway
(Across from Old Time Pottery)

South Cobb Government Service Center
4700 Austell Road
(Just north of South Cobb High School)

All offices open at 8:30 a.m. and close to walk-in customers at 4:45 p.m. Arrive before 4:45 p.m. to be assured of service. The three satellite tag offices close for lunch from 11:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. They close to walk-in traffic at 11:15 a.m. Arrive before 11:15 a.m. to be assured of service. The main tag office does not close for lunch.

Voter's Registration
You have to be a U.S. citizen and at least 18 years old to vote in any city, county, state or federal elections. You must be registered 30 days prior to an election to vote in that election. Please call the Cobb County Voter Information Line 770-528-2300 or visit the local office at 47 Waddell St. Marietta.

You may also register to vote in any one of the local Cobb County Library branches (see Libraries for all locations and hours of operation), or at the driver's license bureau.
