Welcome to Connect Savannah’s weekly podcast, “What are we doing?”. Every Wednesday, hosts Jamie Burton and Lauren Wolverton share what people in Savannah can look forward to in the week ahead. We give you a rundown of weekend happenings and introduce you to some special guests making moves in the Hostess City.

Our goal is simple: To show you all the awesome things going on in Savannah and help you answer life’s toughest questions. Where are we eating? What are we drinking? Where are we shopping? What are we listening to? What are we doing?

New episodes drop every Wednesday at 8 a.m. Listen and watch weekly on Spotify, YouTube, and right here at connectsavannah.com!
