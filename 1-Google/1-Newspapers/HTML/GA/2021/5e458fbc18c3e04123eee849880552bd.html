Camp Pinckney Baptist Church to host singer

  • Jamison Guice
  • Sep 14, 2021
  • Sep 14, 2021

Southern gospel artist Joseph Habedank is performing at Camp Pinckney Baptist Church this Saturday at 7 p.m. Come out to see this two-time Grammy nominated, Dove Award-winning Southern Gospel Artist of the Year (2020) and Gospel Music’s Favorite Soloist for four consecutive years (2017-2020) in concert.

Habedank has performed in some of the country’s greatest churches and venues, including the Brooklyn Tabernacle, Charles Stanley’s First Baptist Atlanta, The Grand Ole Opry and the historic Ryman Auditorium. Additionally, he has been featured on FoxNews.com’s Spirited Debate, TBN’s Praise the Lord and publications like “Billboard Magazine,” “CCM Magazine,” and Glenn Beck’s “The Blaze.”

Habedank’s discography includes a Quiet Reflection series which includes religious hymns and Christmas songs. Three albums make up the series: “Hymns with friends: Duets for quiet reflection,” “Take time to be holy: Hymns for quiet reflection” and “Silent night: Christmas for quiet reflection.”

Learn more about the singer at www.josephhabedank.com. For more information, contact Pastor Tim Hall at (912) 409-7334. Everyone is invited.
