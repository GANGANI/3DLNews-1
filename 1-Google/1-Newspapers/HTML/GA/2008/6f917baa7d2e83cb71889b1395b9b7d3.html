News | Peachtree City

Tree smashes golf cart; driver injured

Tue, 09/16/2008 - 8:59am
By: The Citizen

A Peachtree City resident was seriously injured when a large tree fell on a golf cart Monday afternoon, city fire officials said.

The tree, approximately 18 inches in diameter, fell across the cart, narrowly missing a direct blow to the victim, who was treated by paramedics before being flown to the trauma unit at Atlanta Medical Center by helicopter.

The incident cause the cart's roof to completely cave in.
