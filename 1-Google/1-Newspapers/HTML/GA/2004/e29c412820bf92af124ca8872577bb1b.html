Alford, Dixon join Lake Windsong builder group

Gary Alford and Bob Dixon are now among the elite group of custom home builders at Lake Windsong in Tyrone.

Dixon, a graduate of the University of Georgia, has been building homes on Atlanta's Southside for more than 30 years. Professional accolades include Builder of the Year for both the Midwest Georgia Homebuilders Association and the city of Peachtree City, and Fayette County Chamber of Commerce Business Person of the Year.

Dixon and Alford have been business affiliates for more than 20 years and partners since 1995. Alford has worked in the building business his entire adult life. In 1996 he received the Environmental Award and was recognized as Builder of the Year by the Peachtree City Building Inspection Department. Alford also is the recipient of the Customer Service Award from the Bonded Builders Home Warranty Association for being claims free.

In 2002 Alford was honored by a group of homeowners at The Point on Lake Kedron in appreciation of the "integrity, competence and enthusiasm" with which he built their homes. Dixon and Alford maintain a list of persons for whom they have built homes so that prospective home buyers may contact their previous customers for reference.

Lake Windsong is a 285 acre residential development offering impressive custom homes from the mid $300's complemented by the natural beauty of rolling wooded countryside and a picturesque 18-acre lake. The neighborhood will comprise 167 one-acre or larger homesites. Popular amenities, advanced technology including hi-speed Internet and home automation, and a desirable location near Peachtree City and I-85 add to the neighborhood's appeal.

A focal point of Lake Windsong will be The Lodge clubhouse, with cedar and stone exterior and timber frame construction. Featured are a covered porch overlooking the lake, and dramatic interior including a large open room with huge stone fireplace, and a loft which eventually will be available to homeowners wishing to take advantage of the technology and comfortable setting of this "office away from the office."

The amenity area also will include a swimming pool, tennis court, playground and boat dock. A 14-acre nature area adjacent to Line Creek with unpaved walking paths, a picnic shelter and secluded benches will enhance outdoor enjoyment. The neighborhood will have sidewalks and paved bike paths that will connect Lake Windsong to Tyrone's pathway system.

To reach Lake Windsong from Peachtree City, take Hwy. 74 North to left on Dogwood Trail, cross Senoia Road and continue on Dogwood Trail to Lake Windsong on the left.

For more information call Olivia Marquez or Jan Spell of The Home Source Realtors at 678-870-0001 or visit the Sales and Information 11 a.m.-5 p.m. daily or 1-5 p.m. Sundays.
