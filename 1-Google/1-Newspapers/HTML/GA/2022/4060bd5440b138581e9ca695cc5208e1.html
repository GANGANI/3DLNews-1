2022 Year In Review
By Editor Becky Watts

ZEBULON - Pike County Times had about 74,000 visits over the past year with a little more by computer (38,900+) than phone (35,000+). That evens out to a little over 6,000 visitors per month for a newspaper that has never been in print and began when online newspapers were not the norm.
