BREAKING NEWS: November 8, 2022 Election Results

ZEBULON - These are the results of Advance Voting and Absentee by Mail voting at 8:03 p.m. So far 5,533 votes were cast early out of 14,251 voters. This is 25% of the vote.

This is 28.65% of the vote of 171 possible votes.

UPDATE AT 9:41 p.m. Pike County had a 66.28% turnout. 9,446 votes were cast out of 14,251 possible voters. This is 100% of the unofficial and incomplete vote.

There are 4 provisional ballots that have until Monday to be resolved. These election results will be certified on Monday.

City Council Post 1 - Meansville
Jim Cairns - 58 votes with 56.86% of the vote
Ashley English - 44 votes with 43.14% of the vote
65.50% of the voters in this precinct cast their vote. Jim Cairns is the winner of this race.

City Council Post 1 - Molena
Catherine Parker - 52 votes with 35.86% of the vote
Jennifer Riggins (I) - 93 votes with 64.14% of the vote
56.23% of the voters in this precinct cast their vote. Jennifer Riggins is the winner of this race.

City Council Post 2 - Molena
Willie "Billy" McDowell - 81 votes with 51.92% of the vote
Damon Riggins (I) - 75 votes with 48.08% of the vote
56.23% of the voters in this precinct cast their vote. Billy McDowell is the winner of this race.

Exemptions from School District Taxes (Yes or No)
Yes - 8,239 votes with 89.09% of the vote
No - 1,009 votes with 10.91% of the vote
66.28% of the voters in Pike County cast their vote. Seniors will be allowed exemptions on their school taxes.

Packaged Distilled Spirits - Williamson
Yes - 181 votes with 54.52% of the vote
No - 151 votes with 45.48% of the vote
66.87% of the voters within the city limits of Williamson cast their vote. The packaged distilled spirits referendum passed in Williamson.
