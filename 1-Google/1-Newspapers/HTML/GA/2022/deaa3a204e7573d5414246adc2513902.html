However, when there is no postal delivery, such as federally recognized holidays, your newspaper will be delivered the next day there is postal delivery available.

  • Unlimited Full Access every day to savannahnow.com.
  • Breaking news alerts and updates on our tablet and smartphone apps. You can personalize your news alerts, swipe, and scroll through stories faster, and bookmark them to save and read later. To unlock unlimited access, sign into your account in the app.
  • Newsletters tailored to specific topics our readers want more of. Newsletters can be sent right to your inbox. Sign up for one (or all of them) today at savannahnow.com/newsletters.

  • Florida Residents: Florida law requires we inform you transportation costs are included in your monthly subscription. The transportation fee can be avoided if you choose to pick up your newspaper at one of our distribution centers. To arrange for pick up, please call customer service.
