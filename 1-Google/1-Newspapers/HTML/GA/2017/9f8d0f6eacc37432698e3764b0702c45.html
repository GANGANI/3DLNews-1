About Us

Welcome to the Douglas County Sentinel - we're glad you are here!

The Douglas County Sentinel serves Douglas County, Georgia as well as entrusted as the legal organ for all municipalities within the county. You can trace our roots back to 1902, but regardless of the changes both our newspaper and community have seen, we are always committed to serving the needs of Douglas County.

We invite you to contact us at any time with thoughts, questions or suggestions on how the Douglas County Sentinel can better serve you and your neighbors.

Rachael Raney
