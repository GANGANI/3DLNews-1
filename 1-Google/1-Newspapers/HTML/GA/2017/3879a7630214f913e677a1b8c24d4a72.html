Links

The City of Fitzgerald, web page is packed with information about Fitzgerald, from its founding to its resources, development opportunities and more.
The homepage for Ben Hill County is complete with links to county offices and services and includes a history of the community.
The Blue and Gray Museum celebrates Fitzgerald’s unique heritage as a colony founded by Northern and Southern veterans of the Civil War. Check here for museum hours, directions and more.
Find out about patient services, employment opportunities and more at Phoebe Dorminy Medical Center‘s website.
Wiregrass Georgia Technical College provides online resources for students and prospective students as well as an event calendar and directory of faculty and staff.
Check the Fitzgerald-Ben Hill County Chamber of Commerce for Chamber activities, programs and information on membership benefits.
Find out about utility services, billing, what to do when there’s a power outage and how to apply for energy assistance at the Fitzgerald Utilities website.
The State of Georgia website is your online connection to all state agencies, the legislature, the governor’s office and even to federal government resources.
Keep up with Ben Hill County Schools news and your child’s progress at the school system’s website.
The Fitzgerald-Ben Hill County Library lists upcoming events and news for library patrons at its website.
Fans of The Purple Hurricanes will find stats galore, team and coaching history, photos and much more at Mike Kitchens’ website.
