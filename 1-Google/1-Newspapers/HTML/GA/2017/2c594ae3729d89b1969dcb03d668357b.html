Announcements should be submitted by our deadline of 5 p.m. the Friday prior to the desired week of publication. Please do not type in ALL CAPS. Forms submitted in ALL CAPS will be rejected, possibly delaying publication of your announcement.

Wedding Information
Include name of church or facility and city.
Include name and relationship to bride.
Bride's Information
Include high school name and year graduated, college name and year graduated, etc.
Please note if one or both parents are deceased or if parents should be listed at separate addresses.
Groom's Information
Include high school name and year graduated, college name and year graduated, etc.
Please note if one or both parents are deceased or if parents should be listed at separate addresses.
