Abby Layne Hendrix

  • Jan 19, 2017
  • Jan 19, 2017 Updated Jan 19, 2017

   Abby Layne Hendrix, 15, went to her heavenly home on Wednesday, January 18, 2017 from injuries sustained in an automobile accident. Born in Statesboro, Abby had been a student at Bulloch Academy for many years and was currently a sophomore at Metter High School. In her younger years, Abby enjoyed being very involved in gymnastics. She was currently active in several clubs and activities at Metter High School, including FBLA and FCCLA. Abby loved to listen to music, ride the dirt roads, sit by the fire pit, and spend time with friends.

 She was a member of the Excelsior Baptist Church.

 Surviving are her parents, Kevin and Camille Miller Hendrix of Pulaski; her sister, Logan Hendrix of Pulaski; her maternal grandparents, Nancy and Johnny Miller of Metter; her paternal grandparents, Tom and Lynn Hendrix of Clito; and her aunts and uncles, Frieda Escobar of Metter, John Miller of Metter, Greg and Jodie Hendrix of Statesboro and Cole and Kasey Hendrix of Pulaski. Many cousins also survive.

 The family will receive visitors on Saturday from 4-7 p.m. at Joiner-Anderson Funeral Home in Statesboro.

 Funeral services will be Sunday at 2 p.m. at the Metter First Baptist Church with Rev. Justin Merritt officiating, assisted by Rev. Tom Osborne.

 The body will lie-in-state at the church one hour prior to the service.

 Interment will be in the Lake Primitive Baptist Church Cemetery in Candler County.

 Honorary Pallbearers will be members of the Sophomore Class of Metter High School and the Sophomore Class of Bulloch Academy, as well as her dear friends in the Senior Class of Metter High School.  

 Class members will assemble at the church on Sunday at 1:15 PM.

 Friends may sign the online register book at www.joineranderson.com.

   Memorial contributions may be made to The Abby Hendrix Memorial Fund at any Sea Island Bank location, or mailed to Sea Island Bank, P.O. Box 568, Statesboro, GA 30459, Attn: Kim Brannen. Online contributions may be made at https://www.youcaring.com/camillehendrix-740159.

 Joiner-Anderson Funeral Home & Crematory of Statesboro is in charge of the arrangements.
