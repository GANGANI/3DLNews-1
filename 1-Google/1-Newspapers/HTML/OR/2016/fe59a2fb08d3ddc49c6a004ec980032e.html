Israeli Actress: The Israeli Government is the Most Racist State in History

Salem-News.com Foreign Affairs

"Today the world's silence is very deadly and one day will inflict itself, just like this fire has engulfed Europe."- Israeli actress Orly Weinerman


Orly Weinerman, the Israeli actress
Courtesy: YouTube

(SALEM, Ore.) - Orly Weinerman, the Israeli actress, published an item on her Twitter page containing harsh criticism of the Israeli government and has declared the country's government as terrorist, she also used the word dictator to describe Israeli leadership, presumably Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu.

According to the Tel Aviv Times, the Maariv newspaper quoted Weinerman's Twitter page as saying that the Israeli government is the most #dictator and the most #racist state in #history.

"They easily kill children and no one protests. They bombard defenseless people's houses and human rights organizations don't say anything," Weinerman stated.

Earlier during the operation "Solid Rock" Weinerman strongly criticized the army writing: "In a country where terrorism prevails I'm a famous actress. When I participated in the struggle for those who have no right in my country, officials tried to convert me to a puppet so that I actualize their will, and when I distributed images of Palestinian children killed while playing soccer on the beach the state attacked me in the media."

She also writes: "Today the world's silence is very deadly and one day will inflict itself, just like this fire has engulfed Europe."
