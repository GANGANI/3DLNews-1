MSU golf course booked up ahead of reopening

  • Cal Brown
  • Aug 31, 2022
  • Aug 31, 2022

Ever since the Mississippi State Golf Course announced that it would be reopening on Friday, September 2, nearly all the tee times have been booked for Labor Day weekend.

With it being Labor Day weekend and also the first home football game for Mississippi State, it is no surprise to Head Golf Professional Tim Basel that the golf course will be full of customers.

To read more, see the full Wednesday, August 31st edition of the Starkville Daily News.
