    TO ALL owners, operators and persons interested in Chaparral Field,

    Wayne County, Mississippi:

    TAKE NOTICE that Petitioner, Post Hole Drilling Company, LLC, has filed

    a Petition with the Mississippi State Oil and Gas Board under Docket No.
