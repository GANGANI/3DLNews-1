Church Directory

Lawrence County Press is proud to be able to offer a local Church Directory to our visitors. Please click on the Church’s name below for more information.

To Add A New Church:
Simply click on the Add My Church button below and fill out the form.  It is quick and simple.  Once you do, the form is sent to us and we will review it and post to the Church Directory.

To Update An Existing Church:
Click on the church below that you want to update, click on the Update This Information link and make the change to the form that comes up.  Once the form is submitted, we will review it and post the changes.

Become A Church Page Sponsor:
Please join us in saying Thank You to the sponsors on this page that help make this Church Directory possible.  To learn more about how to become a Church Page Sponsor, please email us at info@lawrencecountypress.com.
