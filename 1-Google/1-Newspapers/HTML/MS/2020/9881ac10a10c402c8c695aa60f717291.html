Submit News

  • 13561 views

Have a news item from your organization or church or neighborhood or business? Do you have a story idea you'd like for us to look into? Let us help you get the word out in The Clarke County Tribune. Simply fill out the form below and a member of our staff will contact you soon.

Contact information is not for publication, but instead will be used for our internal use if we have questions about your submission. If you'd rather leave an anonymous submission, feel free to do so. However, if you'd like your information published, please be sure to include any/all contact information you would like published with your news item in the "News item" field below.

Be sure to submit a photo if you have one. Photos should be in .JPG format and should be no larger than 5MB. Additional photos may be emailed to cbaxley@clarkecountytrib.com
