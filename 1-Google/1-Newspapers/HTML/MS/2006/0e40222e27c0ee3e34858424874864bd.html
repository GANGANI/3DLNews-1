Best of Oxford, our city’s longest-running popularity poll, is back. Vote for your favorite restaurants, bars, shops, services and people in the survey below. You don’t have to submit a vote in every category, but you must complete at least 30 categories to be entered in our drawing for one of two $50 gift cards.
