Creekmore – Chrestman Will Exchange Vows

By Editor | April 26, 2017 | 0

Samuel Joshua Creekmore and Anna Lee Chrestman

Mr. and Mrs. Kim Dean Chrestman of Water Valley announce the engagement of their daughter Anna Lee, to Samuel Joshua Creekmore, IV, son of Mr. and Mrs. Samuel Joshua Creekmore, V, of New Albany. The couple will exchange vows on May 13, 2017.

The bride-elect is the granddaughter of the late Mr. and Mrs. Wilton Eldridge Chrestman of Houlka and Mr. and Mrs. James Michael Mitchell of Vardaman.

The groom is the grandson of Mr. and Mrs. Richmond Lewis Scott Poindexter, Jr. of Inverness,  and Dr. and Mrs. Samuel Joshua Creekmore, III, of New Albany.

Anna is a graduate of Mississippi State University (2016) and will receive her Master of Secondary Education from Mississippi State University in May of 2017. She will begin her teaching career at Houston High School in Houston in the fall of 2017. Josh will graduate in May, 2017, from Mississippi State University with a Bachelor of Arts in History and Business Administration. He will begin his career with Starkville Properties as a real estate agent in June. 

The couple will reside in Starkville after their marriage in May.
