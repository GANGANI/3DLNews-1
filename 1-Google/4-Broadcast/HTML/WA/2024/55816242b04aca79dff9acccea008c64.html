Local company offering a unique way to avoid Super Bowl DUIs

by Jedediah Hoyt

Sun, February 11th 2024 at 11:00 AM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
KEPR Action News

    TOPICS:

    Super BowlDUIsRideHomeServiceCommunityMothertruckers LLCSafety

One local company is offering a way to make sure both you and your ride get home safe following any planned Super Bowl festivities. They also offer the service regularly for the community.

Mothertruckers LLC. is a local transport company you may have heard of before. They offer rides home and a tow, so you don't have to worry about leaving your vehicle at a location.


The idea was inspired by tragedy affecting those around him. This weekend, Action News spoke with the owner about why the mission is so important. Benjamin Trent, with his son Jaxon Trent, tells us. "This is my home, this is my son's home, this is where we grew up. Even if you don't have all of it here and now, we don't want that to deter you from getting home safe. We want to make sure we're keeping you safe, keeping you off the road, keeping you out of jail, and keeping you out of the hospital."

Trent tells us they charge $100 for a ride, and adds you can schedule ahead by contacting them on their Facebook page below.
