Volunteer Firefighters Battling Large Storage Unit Blaze East Of Pullman

February 8, 2024 Evan Ellis News

A large storage unit building is burning East of Pullman this afternoon.

Whitman County Fire District 12 Volunteer Firefighters out of Pullman were called to the blaze at Express Storage on the Moscow-Pullman Highway around noon.  A large storage unit building on the North side of the highway is burning.  Volunteers from District 4 out of Palouse, District 14 from Colton-Uniontown and Moscow are helping battle the blaze.  The highway is still open.
