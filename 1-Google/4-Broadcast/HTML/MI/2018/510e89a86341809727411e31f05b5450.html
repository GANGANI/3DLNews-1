Kalamazoo, Portage women arrested in child porn ring investigation out of Calhoun County

by Newschannel 3

Updated Thu, August 1st 2019 at 11:43 AM

Two more people have been arrested in connection to a multi-agency child porn investigation that uncovered a plot to abduct, abuse and kill a child from a local county fair in west Michigan.

Michigan State Police investigators said the latest to be arrested in the case are Amaris Wyman and Rachel Burrell.

Detailed in a series of text messages and online accounts is where MSP Detective Kyle Bowers said investigators found sexually inappropriate images of children allegedly taken by Amaris Wyman, 31, of Kalamazoo, and Rachel Burrell, 29, of Kalamazoo.

Bowers said, “This is often hidden and its only brought to light, when somebody suspects something and reports a tip."

Records filed in Kalamazoo County 8th District Court show Wyman, 31, of Kalamazoo, is charged with sexually abusive activity, distributing or promoting child sexually abusive activity, and two counts of using a computer to commit a crime. Her bond was set at $25,000.

Bowers said, “We've found out that one was videotaping a child in a shower and then sharing it again that and then sharing it again to our suspect Matthew Toole."

Court records show that Burrell, 29, of Portage, is charged with child sexually abusive activity, using a computer to commit a crime, and second-degree criminal sexual conduct. Her bond was set at $50,000; she was booked into the Kalamazoo County Jail on Monday, Oct. 1, 2018.

Burrell is accused of taking photos of a baby in a bathtub.

Bowers said, “Rachel Burrell she was arraigned recently too, her charges are both manufacturing child sexually abusive material sharing it again with Matthew Toole."

Bowers said both women who do not know one another were caught following the investigation by MSP’s Computer Crimes Unit into the four suspects involved in the child abduction plot.

Bowers said, “Originally we started with two and from there multiple devices, cloud accounts, several different accounts, we were able obtain further information I found two others, David Bailey and Jayme Lapoint there in Athens."

Four other people have been charged in this investigation. They include David Bailey, Jayme LaPoint, Talia Furman and Matthew Toole. State police investigators said the group was plotting to abduct a child from a local county fair, torture the child, sexually assault the child and in the end, kill them.

Detectives said they suspect more predators still part of the ring.

Bowers said, “I am not done with the evidence yet, it's still ongoing."

Police and court systems in Calhoun, Branch and now Kalamazoo counties are involved in the investigation.
