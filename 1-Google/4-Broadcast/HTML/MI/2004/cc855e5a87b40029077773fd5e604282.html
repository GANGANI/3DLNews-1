Introduction

Midwest Communications, Inc. and WRIG, Inc. (“Midwest,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) have developed this Privacy Policy because your (“you” and “your” referring to any party using our internet site) privacy is important to us.  As we collect certain types of personally identifiable information (“Personal Information”), we believe that you should know how that Personal Information is used.

 Our internet site (the “Midwest Site”) is exclusively for the private non-commercial use of individuals who are at least thirteen (13) years of age or older. 

PLEASE READ THIS PRIVACY POLICY CAREFULLY BEFORE USING THIS MIDWEST SITE.  This Privacy Policy is part of this Midwest Site’s Terms of Use Agreement which is binding upon all users of this Midwest Site.  Your use of this Midwest Site signifies your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not accept all the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, you may not use the select areas and features of this Midwest Site which require you to create a registration, membership, or account (collectively, an “Account”).  As with our Terms of Use Agreement, we reserve the right, in our sole and exclusive discretion, to change, modify, add, or delete all or portions of this Privacy Policy at any time for any reason without notice to you. The last date when this Privacy Policy was revised is set forth below. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy whenever you visit this Midwest Site. 

 

Definition of Personal Information

Personal Information includes, but is not limited to, data such as your name, street address, city, state, zip code, telephone number, fax number, instant-messenger nickname, date of birth, e-mail address, any other information that identifies you or would permit someone to contact you, and information about your radio listening habits, your interests, and other demographic data.  If you have provided information about your relationship with Midwest or any of its parent(s), subsidiaries, or affiliates to any of those parties, then your Personal Information also includes that information. 

 

Collection of Personal Information

In general, you may browse and use this Midwest Site without providing any Personal Information.  However, in order to access select areas and features of this Midwest Site, you are required to create an Account.  To create an Account, you must provide us with your Personal Information. In addition, we may occasionally conduct online surveys and contests or promotions through this Midwest Site.  Those surveys and contests or promotions may ask you for contact and demographic information (collectively, “Contest Information”). 

Although Midwest may track the numbers of visitors (“Tracking Information”) to inform us on how best to update this Midwest Site and enhance the experience for all visitors, personally identifiable information is not extracted in this process. 

 

Use of Information by Midwest

The Personal Information which you provide to Midwest (as well as any information, personal or otherwise, which you create and which may be saved on or in association with this Midwest Site) is used to provide you with a better Midwest Site.  We may use the Personal Information which we obtain from you to tailor your experience on this Midwest Site, to display content we think may be of interest to you, or to let us customize what you see when you visit this Midwest Site according to your preferences.  We may use Contest Information to provide you with information about Midwest, to provide you with promotional material on behalf of some of our partners, and to contact you when necessary. We may combine Personal Information and Contest Information with other information about you that we may obtain from other sources.  We use Tracking Information on an aggregated basis to help us operate this Midwest Site, enhance and improve our service, improve the look of this Midwest Site, and display advertising and marketing information. 

By creating an Account on this Midwest Site, you understand, acknowledge, and agree that you are giving us your consent to track your activities and your use of this Midwest Site and its functions and features.  You further understand, acknowledge, and agree that Midwest has the right, for internal purposes, to monitor and edit any Personal Information about our users including, but not limited to, information about the use of website activities, website services, and the contents of users’ e-mails and messages. 

Please bear in mind that when you submit Personal Information to us to create an Account it does not guarantee that we will permit you to use any or all of the features or functions of this Midwest Site. 

 

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or lease your Personal Information to others for their use.  We do share Personal Information and Contest Information as described in this Privacy Policy. 

As set forth in this Privacy Policy, Midwest may disclose your Personal Information and/or Contest Information to Midwest’s parent(s), subsidiaries, affiliates, advertisers, sponsors, promotional partners, service providers, and other third parties, including to law enforcement agencies when necessary to do so pursuant to the terms, conditions, or requirements of this Privacy Policy or the Terms of Use Agreement. 

Midwest may share any information collected from you with our affiliates, advertisers, sponsors, promotional partners, and service providers for the purpose of extending offers of goods and services without your consent.  Midwest may provide offers of goods and services to you by mail, e-mail, and telephone. When making such offers, Midwest will do so without sharing your Personal Information with the third parties who provide these goods and services on whose behalf Midwest extends the offers.  Midwest accomplishes this by controlling the process by which the offers are extended. The third party extending the offer gets no information about you unless, and until, you respond to the offer. 

Midwest has the right to disclose your Personal Information to third parties for the purpose of administering and maintaining services on this Midwest Site.  In addition, we may share any of the information collected from you with third parties on an aggregate basis. The aggregated information is not linked to any individually identifiable information.

You also understand and acknowledge that Midwest may disclose your Personal Information: (1) to the government or third parties in response to legal processes (e.g., in court or regulatory proceedings, or in response to a request from a law enforcement agency), and/or (2) to a user’s employer and/or internet service provider in cases where a party is not in compliance with the Terms of Use Agreement. 

Finally, although Midwest takes appropriate measures and makes a good faith effort to safeguard against unauthorized disclosures of information, we cannot assure you that Personal Information that we collect will never be disclosed in a manner that is inconsistent with this Privacy Policy. 

 

Opting Out and Access to Personal Information

This Midwest Site provides you with the option to opt-out of receiving e-mail from Midwest.  To opt-out of receiving e-mail from Midwest, either (1) elect not to receive e-mail from Midwest when creating your Account, or (2) send an e-mail message to webmaster@mwcradio.comrequesting that we not send you any e-mail.  In addition, Midwest provides you with the opportunity to remove or modify Personal Information that you have previously provided to us. You may do so by accessing your password-protected Account information on this Midwest Site.

If you do not contact us to make an election or enter your preferences online, we may send you offers for products or services from Midwest, our affiliates, advertisers, sponsors, and promotional partners, as well as offers of third-party goods and services by mail, e-mail, and telephone.

Internet Protocol Address and Log Files, Cookies, and Banner Advertising

Midwest may use your internet protocol or IP address to administer this Midwest Site, to analyze trends, to help diagnose problems with our website, to track users’ movements, to help identify you, and to gather demographic information for aggregate use. 

This Midwest Site may also use a standard technology referred to as a “cookie” to collect information about how you use this Midwest Site.  Cookies help our website to recognize you as a previous visitor. Cookies allow us to customize delivery of information to you. For example, this Midwest Site may use cookies to: (1) save and remember Account information; (2) to deliver content specific to your interests; (3) to ensure you do not see the same ad content repeatedly; and (4) to save your Account password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit this Midwest Site.  Midwest only uses cookies to gather information as set forth in this Privacy Policy. 

From time to time, this Midwest Site may also set a “session cookie.”  A session cookie helps us to administer this Midwest Site. A session cookie expires when you close your browser; it does not retain any information about you following its expiration. 

In addition, Midwest licenses ad-server software from other parties to place banner advertising on this Midwest Site.  Midwest retains possession of all data provided and generated by such ad-server software; no such data is transferred to the licensor of the software.  This ad-server software may, on occasion, set cookies on your computer. All information collected or stored by the ad-server software or its cookies is treated identically to other information as described in this Privacy Policy.
