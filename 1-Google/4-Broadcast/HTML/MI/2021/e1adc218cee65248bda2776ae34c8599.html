Living Right: How to Spot a Deepfake

Living Right: How to Spot a Deepfake

Whitney Amann
03/23/2021 5:34 PM EDT

Manipulating videos to persuade someone is nothing new.

However, technology is making it harder and harder to tell what’s real and what’s not.

As of 2020, there are 100 million Deep Fake videos online and the economic cost of disinformation is as high as $78 billion a year.

Living Right explains how these videos get their uncanny realism and how to spot a fake.
