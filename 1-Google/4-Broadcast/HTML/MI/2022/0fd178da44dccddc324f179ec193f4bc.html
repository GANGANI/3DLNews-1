Taylor Gattoni

Anchor/Multimedia Journalist
Lansing, MI

Taylor Gattoni is the anchor for News 10 Today at 11 and First @ 4. Her bubbly personality keeps you energized through your mid-day slump!

Taylor graduated from Michigan State University in 2021, with a bachelor's degree in journalism, and minors in sports and broadcasting. After reporting for WSBT22 in South Bend, Indiana, Taylor has made her way back to the Lansing area.

Prior to moving to the anchor desk, Taylor was the morning reporter for News 10 Today Wednesday through Friday and the morning anchor for News 10 Today both Saturday and Sunday.

Taylor enjoys sharing the community's stories and connecting with others. Feel free to reach out to her over Twitter (GattoniTaylorTV), on Facebook (WILX Taylor Gattoni), or by email Taylor.Gattoni@wilx.com
