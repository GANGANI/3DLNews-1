La'Nita Brooks

Reporter

Born and raised in Chicago, La’Nita recently graduated with her master’s in journalism from DePaul University and is excited to start her career in mid-Michigan. During her time at DePaul, she reported and anchored for their show Good Day DePaul. She also was the president of DePaul's National Association of Black Journalists where she led and moderated events.

She is a proud member of Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority Inc. La’Nita loves animals, traveling, tacos, and spending time with her family. When she is not out in the field reporting, she loves snuggling up watching movies with her doggy Kingston, shopping, and exploring Michigan. She is excited to serve mid-Michigan and tell some great stories. If you see La’Nita out, feel free to say hi or find her on Twitter (@iamLanitaB)
