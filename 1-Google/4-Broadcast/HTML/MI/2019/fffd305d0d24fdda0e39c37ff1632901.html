Pedestrian dies after being hit by a semi on U.S. 131 just south of Schoolcraft

SCHOOLCRAFT, Mich. — A 23-year-old man died Tuesday after being hit by a semi truck on U.S. 131 just north of X Y Avenue in Kalamazoo County.

Kalamazoo County Sheriff’s Office investigators responded, and closed U.S. 131 for a short time. The crash site is near Schoolcraft, south of Kalamazoo.

"The person was walking with traffic northbound and at some point got in front of a semi-truck and was struck and killed at that point," said Kalamazoo County Sheriff Richard Fuller.

Fuller said investigators aren't done.

"We're going through the investigation trying to figure out what could have been the cause or what would have led to the person stepping out in front of the semi truck," he said.

He said witnesses told investigators they noticed the man walking and watching traffic before the incident.

The sheriff says the 64-year-old truck driver was cleared of any wrongdoing.

"They've done nothing wrong, no alcohol, no drugs, nothing to indicate that that person was altered or anything other than doing their job driving their truck," Fuller said.

Schoolcraft police also responded, and a Michigan State Police Motor Carrier officer.
