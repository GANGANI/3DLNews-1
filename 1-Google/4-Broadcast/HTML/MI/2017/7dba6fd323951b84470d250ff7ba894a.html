Video shows moments surrounding deadly explosion at gas station

by WWMT Staff

Thu, April 13th 2017 at 6:25 PM
speedway explosion.PNG

KALAMAZOO, Mich. (NEWSCHANNEL 3) – We are getting our first look at the moments surrounding a deadly explosion at a Kalamazoo gas station.

In video obtained by Newschannel 3 you can see a driver backing into a pump at the Speedway on Westnedge in January.

Video later shows witnesses and eventually police rushing over to help Michael Seyoum, who was pumping gas before the accident and caught fire as a result. He later died at the hospital.

Police have confirmed that the driver was a 74-year-old woman from Mattawan. There have been no charges filed as a result of that incident yet.
