Mr. ChimeTime dishes on The Noon

Rashad Mooreman, better known as Mr. Chimetime has been taking the city by storm with his sometimes-scathing reviews of the Detroit food scene. Mr. Chimetime sat down for his first ever TV interview with the cast of the noon. He shared his favorite and least favorite eateries in metro-Detroit and revealed he might make the motor-city his new home.

By Maurielle Lue
