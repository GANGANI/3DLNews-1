Michigan voter registration deadline for 2024

By WILX News 10
Published: Feb. 9, 2024 at 7:20 AM EST

LANSING, Mich. (WILX) - Michigan’s presidential primary election is just a few weeks away, and on Friday morning, local city clerks want to remind residents of upcoming voter registration deadlines.

The presidential primary is on Feb. 27. The last day to register to vote online or by mail is Monday, Feb. 12.

If you miss Monday’s deadline, you can register to vote in person by going to your local clerk’s office through Election Day. If you are using an absentee ballot to cast your vote, you can fill it out and return it to your dropbox location by 8 p.m. on Election Day.

You can also drop off your ballot in person or through the mail.
