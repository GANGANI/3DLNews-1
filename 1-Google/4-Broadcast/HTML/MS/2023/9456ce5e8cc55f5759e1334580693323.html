Anna Williams

Multimedia Journalist

Anna Williams was born and raised on a small farm right outside of Meridian, MS. She graduated from William Carey University in 2020 with a Bachelor's degree in Mass Communications concentrating in Broadcast Journalism. She then continued her education at WCU, earning a second degree in Secondary Education. While in college she served as the Editor-in-Chief of the university newspaper where she truly grew her passion for journalism. She also served the great State of Mississippi as 2018 Miss Hospitality.

After college, she worked as a recruiter for WCU and most recently taught at Hope Academy in Gulfport, MS as a librarian/media specialist and a fourth grade teacher. She is married to her college sweetheart, Chance Williams, and they are expecting their first child in 2024.

Anna is excited to be back home to pursue her lifelong dream of being a journalist for WTOK News 11. She loves connecting with her community and telling the captivating stories that it holds.
