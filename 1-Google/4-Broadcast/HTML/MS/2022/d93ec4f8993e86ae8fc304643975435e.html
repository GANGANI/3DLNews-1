Ross McLeod

Anchor/Multimedia Journalist/Producer
WTOK

Ross McLeod is a native of Brandon, Mississippi. He joined the WTOK team in May 2022. He graduated from Mississippi State University with a bachelor’s degree in broadcast journalism in May 2022. Along with being a journalist, Ross enjoys singing and spent his college years performing across the country, including a Carnegie Hall performance, with the Mississippi State Singers. Not only does Ross enjoy singing, but he loves to just be around good friends and spend time with his family. Despite not being an avid sports participant, Ross really enjoys the sport of disc golf and plays multiple times a week. Ross is excited to be in Meridian and join the WTOK team! Have a story idea? Email him at Ross.McLeod@wtok.com!
