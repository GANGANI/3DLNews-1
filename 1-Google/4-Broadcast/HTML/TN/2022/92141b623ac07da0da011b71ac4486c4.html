Kaitlin Miller

Reporter

Kaitlin Miller is so excited to work and live in Music City. Her passion for country music and sports drew her to Nashville. She spent the last two and a half years in Greensboro, NC as a news and sports MMJ/reporter. Kaitlin covered Vice President Kamala’s Harris first visit to the Triad since taking office to talk about the American Jobs Plan. Kaitlin was one of the first to report the 2020 ACC Men’s Basketball Tournament was canceled due to COVID-19, making it the first cancellation in the 67-year history of the tournament. She did an exclusive one-on-one interview with the three-time world champion, Nathan Chen, at the 2020 U.S. Figure Skating Championships, covered PGA’s Wyndham Championship, American Ninja Warrior events, and more. Kaitlin has a passion for building strong connections with her community and has told some of the most difficult stories during the worldwide pandemic to help give a voice to those who are often voiceless.

Prior to Greensboro, Kaitlin spent two years working as an MMJ and fill-in anchor for ABC FOX Montana in Missoula. She led a series about missing and murdered indigenous women and covered several of former President Donald Trump’s visits to Big Sky Country in 2018, the year which tied for the most number of visits to Montana from a sitting president.

Kaitlin is a UCLA alum and her sister graduated from Vanderbilt University so we are big Commodores and Bruins fans. During Kaitlin’s free time, she loves to play volleyball, golf, and pickleball. You can also find her out watching live music. She can’t wait to attend Titans and Predators games. She always loves hearing new story ideas and meeting new people in the area, so please introduce yourself.
