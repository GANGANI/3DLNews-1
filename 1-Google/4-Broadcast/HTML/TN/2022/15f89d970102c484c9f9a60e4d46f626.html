Goodbye Stacy Case: 32 years in TV news

by Scott Couch

Tue, June 7th 2022 at 11:01 PM
Updated Tue, June 7th 2022 at 9:22 AM
7
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Stacy Case (Photo: FOX 17 News)

NASHVILLE, Tenn. (WZTV) — They say if you do something you love, you never work a day in your life.

For the past 32 years, my co-anchor Stacy Case has been waking up and asking God "Who can I help today?"

This Thursday, Stacy has decided to retire from TV news.

When she first got into TV News, we had typewriters, not computers. White out, not delete. Pay phones, not cells. Encyclopedias, not internet. Letters, not email. Camera operators, not robotics.

My how times have changed and oh what a career it has been. Watch part one of Monday's send off for Main Anchor and Award Winning Journalist Stacy Case who’s spent the last 13 years of her career here at FOX 17 News, Nashville’s only Prime Time Newscast.
