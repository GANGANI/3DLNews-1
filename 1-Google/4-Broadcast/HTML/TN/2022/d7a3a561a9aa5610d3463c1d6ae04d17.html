Caroline Klapp

Anchor/Investigative Reporter

Caroline Klapp joined the 14 News team in 2022 as the noon and 4 p.m. anchor.

She is thrilled to be living in the Tri State, and is excited to explore the area. In addition to anchoring, she will be working on investigative stories.

Caroline comes to us from our sister station, WAFF 48, in Huntsville, Alabama where she spent two and a half years working as a reporter and fill in anchor.

During her time in Alabama, Caroline was the winner of Alabama Broadcasting Association’s Best General Assignment News story in 2021.

Caroline got her start in television news in northern Michigan at the CBS affiliate, 9&10 News.

She’s originally from North Texas and graduated from Texas Christian University in Fort Worth with a degree in Journalism.

When she’s not anchoring or reporting for 14 News, Caroline loves to bake, exercise, spend time outside and visit her friends and family.

Have a story idea or just want to say hi?

You can email her at: caroline.klapp@14news.com
