Person shot on Wilcox Boulevard in Chattanooga Thursday

by WTVC

Thu, February 8th 2024 at 4:38 PM
3
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Image: WTVC.

CHATTANOOGA, Tenn. — A person was shot on Wilcox Boulevard Thursday, Chattanooga Police says.

CPD says officers responded at 1:58 p.m. to the 1500 Block of Wilcox Boulevard.

When officers arrived, CPD says they found a victim with a gunshot wound.

The victim was taken to a local hospital and reported to be in stable condition, CPD says.

They say investigators are working to determine the circumstances of this incident.

Chattanooga Police ask anyone with information about this incident to call (423) 698-2525.
