Blount County Sheriff’s Office boosts presence in Wildwood area to continue search for suspect

Officials continue the search for suspect Kenneth DeHart
By Avery Jordan
Published: Feb. 10, 2024 at 1:09 PM EST

KNOXVILLE, Tenn. (WVLT) - The Blount County Sheriff’s Office took to Facebook to announce that the law enforcement presence in the Wildwood area would be increased to help with the search for murder suspect Kenneth DeHart.

DeHart is wanted for shooting and killing one officer and injuring another on Thursday after a traffic stop. Two people have already been arrested in connection with the event.

BCSO said there is no need to be alarmed by the increased law enforcement presence.
