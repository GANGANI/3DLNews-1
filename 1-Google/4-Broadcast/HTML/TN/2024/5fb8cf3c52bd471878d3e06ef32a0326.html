HORN LAKE, Miss. - Police are looking for an armed man who robbed a bank in Horn Lake, the Horn Lake Police Department (HLPD) said.

HLPD went to a robbery just before 5 p.m. about an armed bank robbery at Renasant Bank.

When officers got there, they found that a man entered the bank, brandished a gun and demanded money from several of the bank tellers.

HLPD said No one was injured at the bank and no shots were fired.
