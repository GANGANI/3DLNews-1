Welcome to Tire World

Since 1983, Tire World has provided trusted auto service and affordable name brand tires to customers throughout Middle Tennessee and Rutherford County! Through the years, we've expanded to include auto shops in Smyrna TN, La Vergne TN, and Murfreesboro TN. Our automotive repair services are done right the first time! Don't forget to check your brakes! Is something wrong with your vehicle but you're not sure what? Check out our Diagnostic Center where you can diagnosis an automotive issue right here online!

Our large selection of name brand tires and wheels can meet the needs of any Tennessee driver. Come into the Tire World location nearest you for a quote on your next car repair or a set of tires, or simply schedule your next appointment with us online. If you need help finding one of our locations, view our online locations list for addresses and accompanying hours of operations.

Our mission statement is simple, it’s the “Golden Rule” which to our team means honesty, trustworthiness, integrity and southern hospitality. We understand that they are just words without meaning until they are earned. Let us show you!
