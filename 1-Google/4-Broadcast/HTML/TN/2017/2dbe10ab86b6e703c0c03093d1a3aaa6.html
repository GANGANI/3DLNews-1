Welcome to Hearing Aid and Audiology Services

For more than 25 years, we have been helping improve the quality of life for many people in the Murfreesboro community and surrounding areas. The audiologists at Hearing Aid and Audiology Services have a reputation for providing high quality service and using the most innovative hearing aids on the market. We provide excellent hearing care by evaluating your hearing with diagnostic testing and advanced assessment techniques. Offering the best hearing solutions in the business, Hearing Aid and Audiology Services evaluates your hearing loss, individual lifestyle, and unique requirements before suggesting a hearing aid.

We’re Here To Help You Hear

The audiologists and staff of Hearing Aid and Audiology Services understand how hearing loss can cause problems with relationships and overall quality of life. We know just how frustrating a hearing loss can be.

In order to better serve you, our audiologists continually update their training, taking continuing education courses and attending seminars. Our office is equipped with the most advanced computerized instruments for evaluating your hearing, diagnosing your problem, and fitting your hearing aids.

Our staff will make your office visits pleasant by providing you with top-notch customer service. We focus on patient education, we take time to listen with you, and do our very best to help you achieve better hearing.

Hear What You’ve Been Missing!

Your hearing level and your individual lifestyle are both significant factors in determining what kind of hearing assistance is right for you. At Hearing Aid and Audiology Services, we will explain your results in terms you can understand. Depending on your lifestyle and hearing loss, we can make recommendations for treatment and improved hearing.

The hearing professionals at Hearing Aid and Audiology Services are committed to providing you with the best care possible. We carry a large selection of digital hearing aids from only the top manufacturers in the business and wireless Bluetooth-enabled devices to help with those difficult listening situations. We also carry a full line of assistive listening devices, including personal amplifiers, TV listening systems, and special telephones for the hearing impaired.
