Courtney Goins

Courtney Goins graduated from Dalton State College in 2022 with a bachelor's degree in Communication and a minor in Criminal Justice. She gained experience in marketing before starting at News 12. Courtney is also a licensed cosmetologist in Georgia and Tennessee. Courtney grew up around Chattanooga, being native to Chickamauga, Georgia. She likes to stay busy, enjoys the outdoors and has many hobbies.

Jacob Ownby

Jacob Ownby joined the WDEF News 12 team in October, 2023.  He was born and raised in Oak Ridge, Tennessee where he graduated from Oak Ridge High School. He attended Johnson University for three years where he was a 3-time First Team All-conference Catcher for the baseball team. Jacob then transferred to the University of Tennessee at Knoxville to complete his Bachelor's Degree in Journalism/Electronic Media.

Kay Blevins

Kay brings a decade of experience in broadcast journalism spent covering her hometown and the surrounding Tennessee Valley. Born and raised in the Tennessee Valley, she graduated from Red Bank High School and then attended UTC. There she anchored the UTC Mocs News and graduated with a degree in communications. “My love of broadcast journalism began in college. I love storytelling. This is my hometown, and I want to make a difference in this community. I hope to deliver accurate and compassionate news each day. I want each viewer to be informed and prepared with what they need to know.”

Nick Hill

Nick is glad to be serving his home community as a reporter! Born right here in Chattanooga, Nick is a graduate of Hixson High School, then went to Knoxville to attend the University of Tennessee.

Timbrel Williams

Timbrel Williams graduated from Tennessee State University in 2023 with a Bachelor's Degree in Communications. She is a Chattanooga native and is happy to be back home with friends and family.
