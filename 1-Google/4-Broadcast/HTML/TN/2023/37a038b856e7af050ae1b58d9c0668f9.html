Talking dog taking social media by storm joins FOX 17 News for paw-some interview (WZTV)

NASHVILLE, Tenn. (WZTV) — A dog named Bunny has been taking social media by storm over the past few years. “Bunny the Talking Dog” and her owner joined FOX 17 News This Morning to discuss the pup’s talents.

Bunny has been trained to communicate by tapping her paws on buttons with words. Her owner, Alexis Devine, recently released a book about the process of teaching a dog to “talk.”
