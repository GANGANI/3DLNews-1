Country stars and hot real estate: See FOX 17 News' viral stories of 2023

Updated Sun, December 31st 2023 at 4:35 PM

NASHVILLE, Tenn. (WZTV) — A number of FOX 17 News stories out of Middle Tennessee went viral this year! We’re breaking down our Top 10 web-exclusive stories (on the lighter side) that trended at some point in 2023.

Wondering which ones made the list? See them below, starting with No. 10.

10. Before Tanger Outlets opened in Antioch, the outlet mall unveiled a glimpse of what shoppers could expect from the 290,000 square-ft. space. Midstate shoppers couldn't resist getting a sneak peek at the renderings.

STORY | Tanger Outlets to unveil new Nashville location featuring local and national brands this fall

9. In early March, country music superstar Morgan Wallen announced he'd be performing a free concert at Bridgestone Arena. The news brought fans to downtown Nashville within minutes, and lines wrapped around the venue for those hoping to score free tickets.

STORY | Morgan Wallen to perform free concert at Bridgestone Arena

8. When the Tennessee Titans beat the LA Chargers in overtime Sunday, superfan James Rembert didn’t know it would be the last game he would ever attend. The news of his death prompted his granddaughters to reach out to FOX 17 News, resulting in running back Derrick Henry signing the late fan's favorite jersey.

STORY | Final victory cheer: Titans superfan celebrates overtime win before heartbreaking goodbye

7. Flight diversions always cause a stir, and the plane that was diverted to Nashville in February sparked plenty of intrigue from Tennesseans.

STORY | Plane heading from Milwaukee to Orlando diverted to Nashville

6. Popular radio host Bobby Bones put his $8.75 million Nashville home on the market in the fall. Our online gallery showing 15,000 square-ft. of resort-style living space garnered tens of thousands of views.

STORY | PHOTOS: Bobby Bones' $8.75 million Nashville home for sale

5. Real estate was a hot topic in 2023. So hot in fact, a hilarious Zillow listing of a Williamson County home trended for weeks.

STORY | Tennessee mansion that caught fire last year goes on market with hilarious Zillow listing

4. Our No. 4 trending story is a surprising one. The Nashville Zoo hosted an adult-only fundraiser earlier in the year featuring live music, drinks, and close-ups with animals. "Sips for Species" was not only a hit for the zoo, but for fox17.com as well!

STORY | Adult-only fundraiser at Nashville Zoo features live music, drinks, close ups with animals

3. In May, Gov. Bill Lee signed the state's largest tax cut in history into law. The Tennessee Works Tax Act created a three-month sales tax holiday on grocery items for Tennesseans from August 1 through October 31.

STORY | Largest tax cut in Tennessee history becomes law, residents get 3 month sales tax holiday

2. Will Middle Tennessee see snowfall this winter? That's the question we asked Chief Meteorologist Katy Morgan, whose winter outlook is still trending online.

STORY | Higher snowfall predicted across Nashville this winter

1. Finally, our top trending story involved none other than the CMA Award-winning king himself, Jelly Roll. The country music star granted a dying Madison woman's wish. It's a story that still brings tears to our eyes — and it was written by one of our summer interns.

TOP STORY | A dream come true: Nashville native Jelly Roll grants Madison woman's dying wish
