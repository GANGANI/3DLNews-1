True crime, investigations, and big stories: How to listen to WSMV4′s podcasts

The podcasts can be found on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, iHeart, Google and Amazon Music.
By Caleb Wethington
Published: Dec. 21, 2023 at 12:06 PM CST

NASHVILLE, Tenn. (WSMV) - WSMV4 launched a series of podcasts featuring true crime, reports from the WSMV4 Investigates team, daily coverage of the biggest stories across the Mid State, and intimate details from the WSMV4 journalists who covered them.

In ‘Monster Coming Out,’ WSMV4 Investigates tells the story of a long-haul truck driver suspected of being a serial killer.

‘Troubled Waters’ is another production from the WSMV4 Investigates team that reveals how Middle Tennesseans weren’t protected by multiple levels of government before and after deadly floods.

‘WSMV4 Headlines’ features the biggest stories affecting the Mid State while ‘Behind The Story’ reveals intimate details from the WSMV4 journalists who covered them.

The podcasts can be found on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, iHeart, Google and Amazon Music.

  • Monster Coming Out
    • WSMV4 Investigates digs deeper into the life of a long-haul truck driver suspected of being a serial killer. Hear from the kidnapping survivor that sparked questions surrounding Roy Nellsch’s past, and why one investigator believes he “embodies evil.”
  • Troubled Waters: Heightened Risk
    • A three-year investigation by WSMV4 Investigates uncovered systematic failures by local, state and federal government to prevent and protect residents from deadly flooding ravaging Middle Tennessee at an alarming pace.
  • Behind The Story
    • Go behind the scenes of some of Nashville’s biggest stories to hear intimate details from the WSMV4 journalists who covered them.
  • WSMV4 Headlines
    • WSMV’s coverage of the biggest stories impacting Nashville and Middle Tennessee.

Don’t forget to leave a rating on each podcast no matter where you listen!
