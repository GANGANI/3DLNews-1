Andy Santoro

Reporter

Email Andy
Andy has been involved in Chattanooga media since her days at UTC. When she is not sharing the Scenic City’s breaking news as a general assignment reporter, Andy is shuttling her two teenage boys to sports, playing piano gigs around town or taking her dogs for walks across the Walnut Street Bridge.
With a special heart for survivors of sex trafficking, pregnant women in crisis and children in foster care, Andy serves on the boards of Foundation House Ministries and Rise Up.
Andy grew up in Annapolis, Maryland but has enjoyed raising her family in Chattanooga.
She joined WDEF in June 2021. Andy can be contacted at asantoro@wdef.com
