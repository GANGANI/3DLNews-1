Apple TV

  1. On the home screen, scroll down and click on the magnifying glass.
  2. Search for "Fox13 Memphis".
  3. Click on the Fox13 app icon and then click to install.

    • Apple TV Setup Guide

Amazon Fire TV

  1. On the Home screen main menu, navigate to the magnifying glass.
  2. Enter "Fox13 Memphis" and select it in the search results.
  3. Click on the Fox13 app icon and install.
  • Extra Resources
    • How To Setup Amazon Fire
    • Download the FireTV App

Roku

  1. On the Home screen, scroll down to Search.
  2. Search for "Fox13 Memphis".
  3. Click on the Fox13 app icon and then click "Add Channel".
  • Extra Resources
    • Download the Roku App

Samsung Smart TV

  1. Click the SmartHub button on your remote.
  2. Click the magnifying glass.
  3. Search for "Fox13 Memphis".
  4. Add the channel.
  • Extra Resources
    • How To Setup Your Samsung TV

Google TV

  1. On the home screen, navigate to the app icon.
  2. In the search field, type in "Fox13 Memphis" and then click the magnifying glass.
  3. Select the station icon from the search results.
  4. Click on install to download the app.
