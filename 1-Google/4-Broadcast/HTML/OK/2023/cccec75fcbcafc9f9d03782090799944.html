Grant Palmer

Weekend Anchor/Reporter

Grant Palmer joined the FOX 25 news team as the weekend anchor and dayside reporter in April of 2023.

Before coming to FOX 25, Grant started out his career in TV news at KRCG 13 in Jefferson City, Missouri. During his time in the capital city, Grant produced and anchored the weekend newscasts and reported three days during the week, in addition to sometimes filling in at the anchor desk. In the nearly two years in the Show-Me state, Grant covered a variety of stories, ranging from closing hospitals affecting healthcare in rural communities to affordable housing challenges.

Grant graduated from Valdosta State University in the spring of 2021 with a Bachelor’s degree in Mass Media. In his time at VSU, he wrote for the school newspaper, The Spectator, and anchored and reported for the school’s news program News Valdosta. He also interned at ABC affiliate WDHN in Dothan, Alabama in the summer of 2019.

When he’s not working, Grant loves to lift weights, play golf, watch sports, or discover new places around town. Having been born and raised in metro Atlanta, he’s excited to be back in a large city and explore everything it has to offer!

For news tips, email gtpalmer@sbgtv.com. You can follow Grant on Twitter @grantpalmertv and on Instagram at grantpalmer_tv.
