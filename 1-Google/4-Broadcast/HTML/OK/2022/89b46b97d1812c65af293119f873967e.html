Chase Scheuer

Morning Anchor

Chase Scheuer anchors Good Morning Texoma.

His passion for tv started when he was young and spent most of his time watching sports and highlight shows. However, he found a passion for news because it allowed him to tell stories that matter to the community.

Chase is originally from Mason, Ohio, a suburb of Cincinnati. He earned a degree in broadcast journalism and a minor in business at the University of Kentucky before going on to cover Southeast Iowa and Northeast Missouri as a multimedia journalist.

When not in front of a camera, you can find Chase on a golf course or watching sports. He loves to root on his Cincinnati Bengals, Cincinnati Reds, or Kentucky Wildcats.
