Cheyenne Cole

Multimedia Journalist & Weekend Anchor

Cheyenne Cole was born and raised in Lawton and graduated from Eisenhower High School in 2014. A proud Cameron University alumna, she earned a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Media Production in 2018. She began her news career right here at KSWO-TV as an intern that year. In college, she was the Managing Editor of the Cameron Collegian, winning awards in photojournalism and feature story categories, and a reporter for CU News.

Cheyenne covers city government, and during her time at KSWO, she's worked on stories about police shootings and the treatment of residents in nursing homes. She serves on the Board for Crime Stoppers of Southwest Oklahoma and has also volunteered for Lawton Community Theatre and Hungry Hearts Feeding Ministry.

Her hobbies include reading, writing, watching classic movies and spending time with her dog, Apollo. If you have a story idea, contact her at cheyenne.cole@kswo.com.
