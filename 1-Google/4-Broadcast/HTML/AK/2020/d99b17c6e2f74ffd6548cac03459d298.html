The strength of our brand not only benefits our community, but also bolsters military recruitment and contributes to readiness. In a time when antidemocratic forces threaten our experiment in self-government, we believe the leadership of the veteran and military family community is an important part of the solution. But because veterans and military families hold sway in American culture, they’re also attractive targets. Even the strongest communities are not perfectly immune. We aim to engage a partner group to develop and launch strategies that build on the strengths of our community, crowd out vulnerabilities on the margins, promote pro-democratic behaviors, and safeguard our community’s valuable reputation. We will work to protect the veteran and military family community from predators of our trust and leadership.
