Opinion: AOC and comrades do not have Americans' best interest at heart on Amazon HQ issue

by Boris Epshteyn, Chief Political Analyst

Thu, February 21st 2019 at 12:14 PM
Photo: Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez Campaign

    TOPICS:

    AOCAlexandriaAlexandria Ocasio-CortezAmerican WomenBernie Sanders Presidential CampaignBoris EpshteynClimate Change PolicyCortez

Tech giant Amazon has decided to withdraw from its commitment to bring approximately 25,000 jobs to the City of New York.

This comes after radical members of the Democratic Party such as Congresswoman Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez called for Amazon to reconsider coming to New York.

It would appear that encouraging businesses to come to New York and invest in the local economy is not a priority for Ocasio-Cortez.

Amazon also had plans to invest heavily into providing technical education to New Yorkers–but nope, not a priority for AOC. She’s busy spending her time proposing disastrous policies like those outlined in her Green New Deal or the “Green New Disaster.”

Good news if you’re a constituent of hers – she’s only going to advocate for ways to improve your livelihood if they advance her dangerous socialist ideals. I am, of course, being sarcastic.

If the “progress” championed by those on the left means denying access to better education and higher paying jobs for American citizens, we should all quickly denounce it.

Here’s the bottom line: One has to wonder whether Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and her comrades have Americans’ best interests at heart.

She seems hyper focused on ensuring that people become dependent on the federal government when she should be helping the American people achieve their version of the American dream.

EDITOR'S NOTE: Boris Epshteyn formerly served as a Senior Advisor to the Trump Campaign and served in the White House as Special Assistant to The President and Assistant Communications Director for Surrogate Operations.
