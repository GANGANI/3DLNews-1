AMERICA'S MOST WANTED: AMERICA FIGHTS BACK

Thu, October 4th 2007 at 8:27 AM
7167871_BG1.jpg

This Saturday, October 6, 2007, AMERICA'S MOST WANTED: AMERICA FIGHTS BACK will be airing the following cases.

Greensboro, North Carolina...JUAN CARLOS RAMIREZ CAPTURE...Police say Juan Carlos Ramirez wanted one thing for Christmas, and when his ex-girlfriend, April Dawn Caldwell, wouldn't give it to him, he killed her. April's family tells us that's when he called them to explain why he did it. But the Caldwells can rest a little easier now: U.S. Marshals arrested Ramirez in Arizona.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=45982

Las Vegas, Nevada...CHESTER STILES...When 26-year-old Darrin Tuck handed in a videotape to Nevada authorities on September 8, 2007, it began an investigation into an appalling case of sexual abuse against a child. On the tape, authorities say, are disturbing images of an adult male, Chester Stiles, sexually assaulting an adolescent girl. AMW sat down with Tina Allen, Stiles' ex-girlfriend, and she helped to paint a fuller picture of one of the most wanted men in the country.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=49286

Goochland County, Virginia...IRVIN "CHUBBY" MARTIN...When Marie Riley, a Goochland County, Virginia mother, didn't pick her child up from the bus stop on February 5, 2007, the school called Marie's parents. A few hours later, Marie's father went to her house to check on her, and saw what no parent should have to see -- Marie was in her bed covered in blood. Cops say she'd been beaten and left for dead. But Marie survived, and police say she boldly announced her attacker's identity as Irvin Leroy "Chubby" Martin, her neighbor across the street; and cops say Marie wasn't Martin's first target.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=46828

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania...UNKNOWN FAIRMOUNT PARK RAPIST/MURDERER...After four years of silence, DNA evidence confirms that the Fairmont Park Rapist has struck again. Cops in Philadelphia have linked an August 11 attack to several others, including one that resulted in the death of a 21-year-old woman.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=28718

Daggett County, Utah...JUAN DIAZ-AREVALO AND DANIEL GALLEGOS CAPTURE...One week after two convicted murderers broke out of a medium security jail near the Utah-Wyoming border, authorities tell AMW that both escapees are back in police custody. Danny Martin Gallegos and Juan Carlos Diaz-Arevalo hardly went quietly as they led cops on an 11-mile car chase before officers used a spike strip to halt the cons' stolen SUV. Cops say the pair exited the vehicle and a shootout began. Gallegos was shot in the stomach and taken to a nearby hospital in critical condition, but Diaz-Arevalo made a break for it on foot that was ultimately unsuccessful.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=49145

Williamstown, Kentucky...ANTHONY ARTRIP...The FBI says that escaped fugitive Anthony Artrip has struck again. According to agents, Artrip -- who spent the summer robbing banks and thumbing his nose at the law -- struck a bank in Calhoun, Georgia on the morning of September 28, making his getaway in a dark-colored SUV.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=46301

Richmond, Virginia...UNKNOWN ROBERT "POPS" RUSH KILLERS...Robert Rush was known as "Pops" to folks who shopped at the Exxon gas and convenience store where he worked, but two thugs killed him while robbing his store. Now, cops need help to track these guys down.

http://www.amw.com/fugitives/brief.cfm?id=44332
