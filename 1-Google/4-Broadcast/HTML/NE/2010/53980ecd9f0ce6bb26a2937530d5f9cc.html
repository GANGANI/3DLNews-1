Officers Involved in Shooting Identified

Thu, September 30th 2010 at 9:37 AM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Kathy Belcastro

OMAHA (KPTM) The Omaha Police Department has identified four officers that were called to the Creighton University Medical Center Wednesday morning.

Officers Lee Kerniskey and Eric Picht as well as Sgt. Lori Schenkelberg and Captain Katherine Belcastro were checking on a man's well being after a relative of Jeffrey Layten's called police saying he was making suicidal comments. Layten was using a pay phone in the hospital on the main lobby floor across from the cafeteria.

The officers told Layten to put his hands up. At that time officers say Layten pulled out a handgun and pointed it at police. Police Chief Alex Hayes says officer Kerniskey then deployed a taser, that's when Layten fired his gun. Officers Picht and Schenkelberg then fired their guns at Layten who was hit in the upper torso area.

He died just after 2:00 Wednesday afternoon. Officer Kerniskey had a gunshot wound to his thigh and was treated and released. Officer Picht was shot in the foot.

Per Omaha Police Department Policy, any officer who discharges his/her firearm that results in a death or injury will be placed on paid administrative leave pending an investigation from the Homicide and Internal Affairs Units.
