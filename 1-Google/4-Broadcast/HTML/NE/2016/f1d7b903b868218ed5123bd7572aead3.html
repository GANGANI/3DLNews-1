Police: Teen gives birth; throws premature baby out window

by FOX 42

Fri, September 30th 2016 at 4:22 PM
Updated Mon, October 3rd 2016 at 10:45 AM
3
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Police have charged 16-year-old Antonia Lopez with child abuse resulting in death. She is being charged as an adult. (Photo: OPD)

Update: Police have charged 16-year-old Antonia Lopez with child abuse resulting in death. She is being charged as an adult.

OMAHA, Ne (FOX42KPTM)- A teen is facing charges after Omaha Police say she gave birth to a baby and then threw it out of her apartment window.

Police were called to Kellom-Knolls Apartments near 25th and Izard Friday morning. Police say someone called 911 saying a baby had been thrown from a window.

Police say a 16-year-old went into labor at the apartment Friday morning. Police say she gave birth to a premature baby and then threw it out of the second story window. Police say she then told her mother what happened. Police say the teen's mom tried to give the baby CPR before first responders got to the apartment. The child died at a local hospital.

Police are waiting on autopsy results which could take several days. The Douglas County Juvenile County Attorney has been notified of the investigation. Police say charges are pending until they get autopsy results.
