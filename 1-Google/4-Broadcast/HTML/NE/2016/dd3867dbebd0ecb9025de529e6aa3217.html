Bob Downey introduces First News Nebraska to Barney Stinson and Clementine

By 1011 News
Published: Feb. 15, 2016 at 7:35 AM CST

Bob Downey from the Capital Humane Society stopped by First News Nebraska Monday morning to introduce us to Barney Stinson and Clementine.

Both cats are up for adoption at the Pieloch Pet Adoption Center. If you're interested in either cat, call the Center at 441-4488.

If you adopt a new cat, there are several steps you'll want to take when introducing that cat to your current pets. Watch the video to see what Bob Downey recommends you do.
