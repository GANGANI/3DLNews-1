Fremont Police investigating threats against middle school

By 6 News staff reports
Published: Feb. 8, 2024 at 2:28 PM CST

FREMONT, Neb. (WOWT) - Fremont Police are investigating rumors of a threat against Fremont Middle School.

FMS Principal Todd Niehaus sent a message to parents and families Thursday saying there had been social media posts circulating regarding the threat of a school shooting, which was rumored to be taking place Thursday at 2 p.m.

Fremont Police then posted a notice on Facebook that they are investigating the matter. Both the police and the school asserted there is no imminent threat at this time.

Anyone with information related to the threats is urged to call the Fremont Police Department at 402-727-2677.
