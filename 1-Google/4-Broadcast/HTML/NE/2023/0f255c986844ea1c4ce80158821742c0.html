Meghan Packer

Anchor/Reporter
Atlanta News First

Meghan Packer is an anchor and reporter at Atlanta News First. She anchors the Atlanta News First weekend morning news.

She joined the Atlanta News First team in November of 2015.

A native of suburban Philadelphia, Meghan moved to Atlanta after spending two years at WFMZ in Allentown, Pennsylvania. Before returning to her home state, Meghan spent several years in North Carolina.

She reported and anchored at WFMY in Greensboro and WWAY in Wilmington.

Meghan graduated from Elon University in North Carolina with a degree in broadcast communications and a minor in Spanish. During her time at Elon, Meghan received several awards and recognitions for her work in the student news program. She studied abroad in Costa Rica and Ireland.

Meghan enjoys experiencing everything Atlanta has to offer from the theater to sporting events to great restaurants.
