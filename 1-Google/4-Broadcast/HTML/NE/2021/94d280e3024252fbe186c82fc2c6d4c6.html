FBI warning of COVID-19 scam targeting vaccinated people
OMAHA, Nebraska (Iowa's News Now) —

The FBI Omaha Field Office is warning of a new COVID-19 scam that is targeting people who have received a COVID vaccination.

ADVERTISEMENT

An increasing number of those vaccinated are being asked via email and/or text message to participate in a fake post-vaccine survey.

If they participate, they are promised a prize or cash.

However, no post-vaccine surveys are being conducted by Pfizer, Moderna or Johnson & Johnson.

Any emails or text messages that appear to be sent from those companies seeking personal financial information, are fraudulent.

In addition to the fake vaccine survey, FBI Omaha is also warning people not to post photos of your vaccine card to social media websites.
