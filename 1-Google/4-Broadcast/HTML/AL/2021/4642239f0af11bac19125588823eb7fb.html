Ashley Brown

Reporter, WTVY
Dothan, AL

Ashley Brown started as an intern in January 2019 and officially joined the team in May as a multimedia journalist.

Ashley was raised in Dothan and grew up watching WTVY.

She ran track at and graduated from Dothan High School. Ashley then attended Troy University and graduated with a broadcast journalism degree. While at Troy University she was a reporter, anchor and weatherperson for TrojanVision News and a staff writer for the Tropolitan.

In her free time, she enjoys crafting, reading classic novels, and hitting the beach. She is excited to launch her career near the people she loves and the place she calls home.
