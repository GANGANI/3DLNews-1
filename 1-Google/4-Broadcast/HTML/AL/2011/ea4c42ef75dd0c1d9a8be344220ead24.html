Ms. Helyn Owsley Cashion

     Helyn Owsley Cashion, age 92, of Hamilton, Alabama, passed away Wednesday, January 26, 2011, at her residence.

Visitation, 11:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m., Friday, January 28, 2011 at the Hamilton Church of Christ, Hamilton, Alabama.  Funeral service 1:00 p.m., following visitation, at the church.  Ted Burleson and Howard Johnson officiating.  Burial in the Hamilton City Cemetery.

Ms. Cashion was born July 4, 1918, in Marion County, Alabama, to the late Leonard and Alma Hamilton Owsley.  She was a retired Teacher.

She is survived by 3 sons, Eugene Cashion (Linda Kay), Bob Cashion, and Ben Cashion (Mary); 5 grandchildren, Sunny Cashion, Katie Cashion, Lee Cashion (Carrie-Beth), Ethan Acres, and Chris Satcher (Bridgett); 4 great-grandchildren, Curt Cashion, Canon Satcher, Cyrus Acres, and Delilah Acres.

She was preceded in death by her parents, Leonard and Alma Hamilton Owsley; and her husband, Gene Cashion.
