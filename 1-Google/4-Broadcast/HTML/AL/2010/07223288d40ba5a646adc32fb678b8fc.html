Mrs. Charity Isom Childers

     Charity Isom Childers, age 30, of Hodges, Alabama, passed away Tuesday, July 20, 2010 at her residence.

Visitation, 6:00 - 9:00 p.m., Thursday, July 22, 2010 at the Hamilton Funeral Home Chapel.  Funeral service, 1:00 p.m., Friday, July 23, 2010 at the Hamilton Funeral Home Chapel.  Burial in Mt. Olive Cemetery.

Ms. Childers was born October 23, 1979 in Amory, MS. to David and Carol Herman Isom.

She is survived by her husband, Trae Childers; 2 daughters, Madilyn and Ashlan Childers; her parents, David and Carol Herman Isom; 1 sister, Crystal Isom (Stuart) Clack; 1 brother, Josh (Janine) Isom; nephews, Joseph Clack and Fisher Isom; and 1 niece, Kristin Clack.
