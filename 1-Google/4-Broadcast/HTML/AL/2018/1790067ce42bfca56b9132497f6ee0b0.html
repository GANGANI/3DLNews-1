Small Rain Chances Return

From Meteorologist Zack Webster in the 4Warn Storm Center
Updated: 6 hours ago

SYNOPSIS – After some dry and warm afternoons through the middle of the week, our Thursday is off to a cloudy start with some scattered showers moving through the norther half of the region. That rain winds down and we’ll start to see a little more sunshine later this afternoon, then we’ll return to what we’ve seen for much of the week so far on Friday. Isolated showers and thunderstorms will increase rain chances a little more for the weekend, and we’ll still keep some smaller rain chances around into early next week.
