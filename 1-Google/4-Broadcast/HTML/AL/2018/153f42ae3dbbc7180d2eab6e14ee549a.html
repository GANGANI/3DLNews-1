Your House Inside and Out

Each Saturday from 8-10 AM on Talk 99.5, host Jim Butters of Advanced Roofing and Metals talks with you, the listeners, about everything you need to know, and a few things you don’t care to know about ‘your house, inside and out’. With over 30 years in the housing industry, Butters and his staff are experts and want to help you with all of your housing needs and questions.

Whether it’s a new roof, damp crawl spaces, or a crack in the chimney, rely on Jim Butters to give you the advice you need on Saturday mornings.
