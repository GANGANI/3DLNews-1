Joe Hubert Robison - Age 87 - Albertville, AL (Formerly of Winfield)

JOE HUBERT ROBISON, lovingly known as "Preach" age 87, of Albertville, died Monday, January 1, 2007 at his residence.

Preach was born in Heflin, AL January 10, 1919 to the late Hubert Scott Robison and Emma Raye Lightsey Robison. He attended Snead College and Emory University. He was employed by Southern Railway as a fireman and engineer for 12 years. He entered the ministry in 1948 when he and his wife Margaret "Mrs. Preach" served as counselors for the Birmingham Sub-Districts. His first appointment was in 1951 in Albertville. He served Mt. High, Corbinville, and Nixon Chapel Circuit; St. James Methodist Church in Florence, Carbon Hill Methodist, Beverly Methodist, Rock Methodist, Winfield and Pisgah United Methodist; and retired from St. Andrews Methodist. However, after retirement he served Fairview Methodist in Albertville for 15 years, and continued to serve Oak Hill and Oak Grove. Preach was a member of the Masonic Lodge for over 50 years.

Preach is survived by his wife, Margaret "Mrs. Preach" Robison of Albertville; two daughters, Jo Ann (Mark) Washington of Jacksonville, AL and Marie (Larry) Henson of Guntersville; three grandsons, Scott (Carolyn) Rains of Albertville, Joe Mark (Susan) Washington of Pine Beach, NJ, and Nick (Cindy) Henson of Albertville; and two great grandchildren, Casey and Colin Rains.

There will be a funeral service held Tuesday, January 2, 2007 at 2:00 PM from the Miles Funeral Home Chapel with visitation from 12:00 until 2:00 p.m. just prior to services. The celebration of Preach's life will be held Wednesday, January 3, 2007 at 2:00 PM from the Fairview Methodist Church in the Hustleville Community of Albertville. The family will receive friends at the church beginning at 12:00 PM Wednesday.

Preach requested in lieu of flowers, donations be made to Southern Care Hospice at 219 So. 4th St. Gadsden, AL 35901; Big Oak Ranch at P.O. Box 507 Springville, AL 35146; or Byron Bible Camp at P.O. Box 211 Huron, South Dakota, 57350.

Miles Funeral Home of Winfield, directing.
