Belk, Riley - Age - Muscle Shoals, AL (Formerly of Sulligent, AL)

(January 27, 1912 - February 9, 2008)

U.S. Veteran Mr. Riley Belk age 96 of Muscle Shoals, AL, formerly of Sulligent, passed away Saturday morning Feb. 9,2008 in Muscle Shoals.

Funeral services will be Monday, February 11, 2008 at 2:00 p.m. from the Otts Funeral Home Chapel in Sulligent Alabama with burial in the Sulligent City Cemetery. Visitation will be at the funeral home Monday from 12 until services time at 2 p.m.

Mr. Belk was born January 27, 1912 in Lamar County, AL to the late Luther Daniel and Sally Real Belk. He was member of Avalon Baptist Church in Muscle Shoals. Mr. Belk was a veteran of the U.S. Army serving in WWII and also was involved in the Normandy Invasion (D-Day). He was a 1932 graduate of Sulligent High School. Mr. Belk previously worked for Waddell Trucking Company in Rockford, IL. He enjoyed gardening.

SURVIVORS: Daughter: Becky (Larry) Hammond, Muscle Shoals, AL; Grandchildren: Tim (Gina) Mashburn, Terry (Susie) Mashburn, Chad Young & Robin (Kim) Bryant; Great Grandchildren: Chelsea Bryant, Carly Bryant, Houston Mashburn, Blake Bryant, Ross Mashburn & Colin Young.

PRECEDED IN DEATH BY: Parents: Luther Daniel and Sally Belk; Wife: Lorene Johnson Belk; Sisters: Nettie Spruiell & Ludie Spruiell; Brothers: Wiley Belk, James F. Belk & Clifton Belk.

Otts Funeral Home of Sulligent, AL. officiating.
