Multiple cars catch fire at Hillcrest High School in Tuscaloosa

by Sumner Harrell

Wed, May 25th 2022 at 1:16 PM
3
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Multiple cars catch fire at Hillcrest High School in Tuscaloosa on Wednesday, May 25, 2022 (Tuscaloosa Police Department)

TUSCALOOSA, Ala. (WBMA) — Firefighters responded to multiple car fires on the parking lot of Hillcrest High School Wednesday morning, according to the Tuscaloosa Fire Department.

The fire department said the first report of the fires came in shortly after 10:00 a.m. There were at least five cars that were damaged, but no injuries were reported.
