Welcome to the Legal Advocate

Legal Advice, Done Right. This resource is provided as a public service by Brian Caubarreaux and Associates, attorneys at law, Alexandria and Marksville. For over ten years, we have proudly served the people of Central Louisiana by providing legal guidance, representation and counsel. From an early age, Brian’s father instilled a desire within him to help others. He always told Brian, “If you’re going to do something, do it right.” Brian never forgot those words and they’ve guided him to success, and have become the foundation for his Firm. The attorneys at Brian Caubarreaux & Associates are not only successful but passionate about using their talents for the good of every victim they represent. Surrounded by like-minded attorneys, Brian and his Team are ready to make a difference, in your case and in your life. That is why this service is provided free of charge.

How to Best Use This Service

Simply click “Ask the Legal Advocate” button below. This will take you directly to a free and confidential inquiry form on the Brian Caubarreaux & Associates website, where you ask questions about your situation/case and get easy free information that will help guide you toward making the smart decision and doing it right for you and your family. For additional information, take a few moments to watch the videos of legal tips below. They contain helpful Dos and Don’ts concerning legal matters and invaluable information for protecting you and your rights. If you need immediate help, call 318.253.0900, the office of Brian Caubarreaux and Associates, Your Legal Advocate.
