Fred Hickman

Published: Jul. 31, 2014 at 2:13 PM CDT

With over 30-years of award winning broadcast experience under my belt, I still wake up each day eager to work with new people and challenge myself professionally. My talents are diverse as a speaker, spokesperson, host, voice/over artist, narrator and consultant. But that's not where they stop, that's where they begin.

My greatest achievements in media are still ahead of me. And even though I have taken great opportunities to work with some of television and cable's industry giants such as Ted Turner, Bill MacPhail and John Filipelli, things are just getting warmed up.

The knowledge I've taken away from working with the all-time greats is unparalleled to any other experience imaginable. And it's these life experiences, along with countless others, that have sculpted and prepared me to take on with confidence "any and all' future endeavors.
