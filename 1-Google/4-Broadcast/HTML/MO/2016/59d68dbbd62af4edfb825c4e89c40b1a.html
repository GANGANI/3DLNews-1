Madeline Odle

 Madeline Odle is a Journalism student at the University of Missouri. She is studying Radio/TV Journalism and is expected to graduate in December 2016. She has worked at KBIA in the past and is currently anchoring and reporting at KOMU-TV. This is Madeline's first year working at the Capitol in Jefferson City. She hopes to go into political reporting upon graduation.
