Update: Friday, May 18 at 5:43 p.m.:

Fri, 18 May 2012 16:57:20 GMT — The Highway Patrol said the woman killed in a Friday morning wreck was Twila Stokes, 52, of Columbia.

Troopers said the collision happened when Stokes turned onto the shoulder of Route B and abruptly attempted to turn left to make an illegal U-turn. She did this in front of a van that was traveling behind her.

The van T-boned Stokes, hitting her directly in the driver door.
