Everyday Hero Award: US Army Lt. Col. brings Afghan interpreter to America

by Madison Corney

Thu, November 17th 2016 at 4:06 PM
American Red Cross Everyday Hero Award: Military Hero

Two years ago, U.S. Army Lt. Col. John Strahan promised his Afghan interpreter, Shafiullah Nasratzada that he would help him and his family move to America after his tour of duty.

As Madison Corney reports, now retired U.S. Army Lt. Col. John Strahan stayed true to his word and shocked his Afghan interpreter with his generosity.
