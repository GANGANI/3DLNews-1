Jamie Kennedy shares some laughs with News 4

by KRNV News 4

Sat, March 12th 2016 at 5:56 PM
Jamie Kennedy visits News 4, March 11, 2016

RENO, Nev. (MyNews4.com & KRNV) — Comedian and actor Jamie Kennedy dropped by News 4 on Friday to talk about his engagement this weekend at the Laugh Factory at Silver Legacy in Reno.

Kennedy, who starred in "Malibu's Most Wanted," will perform Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

WATCH the interview to learn about his upcoming television series that will premier right here on NBC.

For full times and ticket information, visit laughfactory.com/clubs/reno.
