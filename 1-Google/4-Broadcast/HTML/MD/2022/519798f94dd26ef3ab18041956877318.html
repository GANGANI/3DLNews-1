February 1st, 2022 by WCBC Radio

The Cumberland Police department is seeking assistance from the public with a missing person.

The missing person is Peggy Welsh, 39 Y/O, W/F.

Welsh was reported missing on Monday, January 31st @ 7:22 p.m. from her N. Centre Street residence.

If anyone has any information on Peggy Welsh they are requested to contact the Cumberland Police department at 301-777-1600, email at cpd@cumberlandmd.gov or on Facebook at www.facebook.com/CumberlandPD.

 

September 30th, 2017 by WCBC Radio

7 PM UPDATE:  Cumberland Police say Sullivan has been located.

INITIAL REPORT:

The Cumberland Police Department is currently conducting an investigation on a Missing Person.

The person reported missing is 22 Y/O BAILEY NICOLE SULLIVAN.

Sullivan is a W/F, 5’-06 “in height and weighs approximately 165 lbs.

She was last seen in the Cumberland/LaVale, MD area.

If anyone has any information on Sullivan they are encouraged to call the Cumberland Police Dept. at 301-777-1600.  Contact can also be made through social media @ www.facebook.com/CumberlandPD.
