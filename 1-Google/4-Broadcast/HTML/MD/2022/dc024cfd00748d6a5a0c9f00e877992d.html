Police Make Disorderly Conduct Arrest

Follow Us
  • facebook
  • twitter

July 18th, 2022 by WCBC Radio

On July 17, 2022 an Officer with the Cumberland Police Department was on patrol when he observed a male walking in the center of Greene St, screaming.  He stopped to check on the individual and after making contact with him, the Officer advised him not to cause any further disturbance.  A short while later, several people outside of local busines contacted the Officer to advise that the same male was still walking through town, screaming at random people.  The subject, Paul William Umstead Jr, age 34, was located on the Downtown Mall.  Umstead was placed under arrest and taken to the Police Station.  He was issued two Criminal Citations charging him with Disorderly Conduct and Fail to Obey a Lawful Order and released.

 

Arrested:  Paul William Umstead Jr  Age: 34  Cumberland, MD

Charges:  Disorderly Conduct, Fail to Obey a Lawful Order

April 19th, 2022 by WCBC Radio

The Cumberland Police were called to a business on the 400 block of Maryland Ave for a report of a disorderly subject.  Upon arrival, the officers learned that the subject had broken a display rack in the business and caused a disturbance with employees and other customers.  He had already left the area.  Officers were able to identify James Robert Michael Langway, age 33 of Cumberland, MD on the surveillance video.  Langway was located a short time later on Park St.  He was again creating a disturbance, yelling at passing vehicles and throwing garbage in a parking lot.  He was placed under arrest and taken to Central Booking for his initial appearance with the District Court Commissioner.  Langway was charged with two counts of Disorderly Conduct, Destruction of Property, and Littering.    He was held on a temporary bond. 

 

Arrested:  James Robert Michael Langway  Age: 33 Cumberland, MD

Charges:  Disorderly Conduct (x2), Destruction of Property, and Littering
