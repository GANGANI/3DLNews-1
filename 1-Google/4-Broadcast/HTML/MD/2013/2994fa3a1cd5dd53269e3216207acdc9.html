June Arrest Warrant Initiative – “Operation Takedown “VII”

Follow Us
  • facebook
  • twitter

June 3rd, 2013 by WCBC Radio

The Cumberland Safe Streets Program is conducting its June Warrant Initiative to be accomplished in combination with citizen cooperation. The Operation Safe Streets Initiative will begin Monday, June 3, and conclude Friday, June 7, 2013.

In our efforts to reduce the backlog of current arrest warrants, the Cumberland Police Department’s Safe Streets Program is requesting citizens to assist in the apprehension of numerous individuals wanted for various criminal activities.

The Safe Streets Operation is seeking your help in locating the following sixteen (16) individuals on open arrest warrants.
