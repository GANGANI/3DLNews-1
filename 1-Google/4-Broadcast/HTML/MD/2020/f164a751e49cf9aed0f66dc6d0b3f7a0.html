Twenty Arrested on Garrett Grand Jury Indictments

Follow Us
  • facebook
  • twitter

March 13th, 2020 by WCBC Radio

On March 11, 2020, members of the Garrett County Narcotics Task Force and the Maryland State Police, Criminal Enforcement Division conducted a joint crime suppression initiative in Garrett County.

Earlier in the week, Detectives from the Task Force testified before the Grand Jury for Garrett County. The cases presented were the result of investigations conducted by the Task Force into the distribution of methamphetamine and other drugs over the last seven months.  The following indictments were handed down and have been served:

The Maryland State Police Criminal Enforcement Division, consisting of Western Region Drug Investigators, Gang Enforcement Unit, Gun Enforcement Unit and the Maryland State Apprehension Team, served the above listed warrants after locating the individuals throughout the county.

Simultaneously, four search and seizure warrants were executed by numerous SWAT teams. The search warrants, which stemmed from cases investigated by Task Force members, were conducted at the following locations:

100 block of Spring Lane Oakland, Maryland (Maryland State Police S.T.A.T.E.)

500 block of Main Street Deer Park, Maryland (Maryland State Police S.T.A.T.E.)

400 block of Edgewood Drive Deer Park, Maryland (Garrett County Sheriff’s Office S.R.T.)

200 block of Spring Lane Oakland, Maryland (Cumberland Police Department C.E.R.T)
