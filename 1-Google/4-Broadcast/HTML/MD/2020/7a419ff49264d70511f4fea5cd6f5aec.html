Allegany Declared 2nd Amendment Sanctuary

Follow Us
  • facebook
  • twitter

February 28th, 2020 by WCBC Radio

Allegany County has been declared a Second Amendment sanctuary. This comes after the Allegany County Board of Commissioners approved a resolution during a meeting Thursday. The resolution opposes any unconstitutional changes to the 'right to bear arms.' Among those speaking in favor of the resolution was Allegany County Sheriff Craig Robertson…

Commissioner Creade Brodie Jr was enthusiastic in his support of the action…
