Charity Softball Game
Posted on June 21, 2021 by Monadnock Broadcasting Group

The challenge has been issued and it’s going down on September 25! Tickets are now on sale for the 2nd Annual North Woods Throwdown. Catch the Maine Department of Inland Fisheries & Wildlife Game Wardens taking on the NH Fish and Game Conservation Officers in a charity softball game at Delta Dental Stadium. Tickets are just $10 and benefit the NH & ME Operation Game Thief chapters, Make-A-Wish Maine, and New Hampshire’s Barry Camp. Buy your ticket today, this event is bound to sell out! bit.ly/NorthWoodsThrowdown
