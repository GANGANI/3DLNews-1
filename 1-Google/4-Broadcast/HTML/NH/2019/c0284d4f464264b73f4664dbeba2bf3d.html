KPD announces Free Community Thanksgiving dinners

5 years ago in Local

Photo: clipart.com


KEENE, NH- Folks looking for somewhere to go to celebrate Thanksgiving have a few options around the region.

The Keene Police Department posted a list of Free Community Thanksgiving Dinners on Facebook Thursday morning, just a week ahead of the holiday.

The first is set for Sunday November 24th at Grace Christian Fellowship in Winchester. It is free and open to Winchester residents and their friends.

The rest of the dinners are set for Thanksgiving Day, November 28th, at several locations around the region. Details are as follows from the KPD posting;

“The Ralph Rines Memorial Dinner
Doors open at 10 am, dinner at noon
Community Church of West Swanzey
3 Holbrook Ave
Transportation is available, and meals can be delivered to people in Swanzey, center Winchester and the Cranberry Hill apartments in Troy. Volunteer drivers are needed.
For information or to volunteer, contact Joanne Barnes at 357-2831

The Fraternal Order of Eagles #1413
Noon-3 pm
115 Church St., Keene
Home deliveries available in the Keene area
Volunteers are welcome to help prepare on 11/27 any time after 12:30 pm. Volunteers can also pick up turkeys to cook and return. Volunteer drivers are needed for deliveries.
Donations of food or money are greatly appreciated and are tax exempt
352-9253

Beaver Brook Lodge #36 of the Independent Order of Odd Fellows
4-6 pm
181 Marlboro St., Keene
All are welcome. Donations welcome but not required
352-2312

Keene Assembly of God
11:30 am-4:30 pm
121 Park Ave. (Next to Wheelock Park), Keene
Carry-out available
357-3677

Bellows Falls, VT
American Legion Post 37
Noon-2 pm
42 Rockingham St., Bellows Falls
802-463-9700

Brattleboro Community Thanksgiving Dinner
Noon-5 pm
St Michael Roman Catholic School
48 Walnut St., Brattleboro
802-282-7905

Those looking to volunteer should contact the appropriate locations.
