Kelsey Marier

Anchor/Producer
Rochester, MN

Kelsey Marier became an Anchor/Producer for the morning newscasts at KTTC in July 2021.

During her time so far at KTTC, Kelsey has been tasked with anchoring KTTC News Today weekdays from 4:30-7 a.m. You may have also seen her live in the field, reporting or anchoring election coverage for broadcasts. She's created a story series titled "Making an Impact," which focuses on highlighting area nonprofits and the people they serve. She's emceed several charitable events, including the RT Autism Awareness Foundation Gala and the Brighter Tomorrows Cornhole Tournament, and has been privileged to host a handful of Eagles Cancer Telethons broadcast live on KTTC. Additionally, Kelsey helped the station bring home an Emmy Award in 2023 for winter snow coverage on KTTC News Today.

Previously, she was a general assignment reporter at KOLO 8 News Now, the ABC-affiliated station in Reno, Nevada for nearly three years. She primarily worked evenings, as well as some daytime shifts, and covered a variety of topics in northern Nevada and California including wildfires, the COVID-19 pandemic, racial unrest, and the 2020 Presidential election. Kelsey also led the design and production of the first and only local 10 p.m. newscast in northern Nevada, which later won the station's first Emmy Award.

Before KOLO 8, she worked at WCTV, the CBS-affiliated station in Tallahassee, Florida for three years. She was a producer for the morning newscasts, later getting the opportunity to be a reporter on weekend mornings. She also led the design and production of the inaugural 4 p.m. lifestyle newscast. Kelsey produced a number of events while in Florida, including four major hurricanes, the Parkland school mass shooting, the Pulse Nightclub massacre, and the 2016 Presidential election.

Prior to moving to the sunshine state, Kelsey graduated from the University of Minnesota-Duluth with a Bachelor's Degree in Writing Studies-Journalism, and Arts in Media. She scored her first job out of college at WDIO, the ABC-affiliated station in Duluth as a part-time news producer. Before heading north to college, Champlin, Minnesota is where she called home. Ever since she was little, Kelsey knew she wanted to work in broadcast news. She has a strong passion for writing and telling people's stories.

Outside of work, Kelsey loves to explore new cities and outdoor spaces. She is a foodie and is always taking restaurant suggestions. In the summer, she prefers spending as much time on or near a lake. Snowboarding is a favorite activity of hers during the winter months. The Minnesota State Fair is her favorite event of the year (just ask her and she'll share all her tips and recommendations!). Kelsey will also always ask to pet your cat or dog, as she has a strong love for animals! She enjoys being a volunteer at Paws and Claws Humane Society in Rochester.

If you have any story ideas, news tips, or just want to send a warm hello, feel free to contact her through email (kmarier@kttc.com), Twitter (@KelseyMarier), or Facebook (@KelseyMarierTV).
