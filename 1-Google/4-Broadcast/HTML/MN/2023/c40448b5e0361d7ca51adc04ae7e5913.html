Kendall Jarboe

Anchor/Reporter
Duluth, MN

Kendall Jarboe joined Northern News Now in May 2020 and has worked just about every schedule since. From nights to weekends, mornings to midday, Kendall anchors, reports, and produces. She also works on special enterprise projects, including her Emmy-nominated segment called The Local Exchange which highlights small businesses in the Northland.

Kendall is from Fort Worth, Texas, where her passion for local news began. She attended Biola University in Southern California, majored in broadcast journalism, and minored in Spanish. Kendall interned at NBC 5/KXAS-TV in Fort Worth in 2019.

Kendall has loved every minute of her time living and working in the Northland and has learned to embrace the cold! In her free time, Kendall loves going on hikes with her husband, catching up over a cup of coffee, and snuggling with her brand-new baby daughter.

If you have a story idea, e-mail Kendall at KJarboe@NorthernNewsNow.com
