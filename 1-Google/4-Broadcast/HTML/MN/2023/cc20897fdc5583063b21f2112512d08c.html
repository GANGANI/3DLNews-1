Chandler Feed Co. Sees Grain Elevator Explosion Wednesday

Fri 11-24-2023

(Chandler)- Chandler in neighboring Murray County saw a grain elevator explosion on Wednesday afternoon, with one person injured in the blast. The injured person was taken to a local hospital, with an unknown condition at this time.

Several fire departments responded to the scene and they were on the scene for a long time, between 12:40 pm Wednesday and 4:20 am Thursday, with some firefighters sticking around on the scene to make sure nothing in the rubble reignites.

It's unknown at this time what caused the explosion at the Chandler Feed Co.

Locally, the Minnesota State Patrol, Worthington Fire Department and Nobles County Emergency Management all assisted at the scene.
