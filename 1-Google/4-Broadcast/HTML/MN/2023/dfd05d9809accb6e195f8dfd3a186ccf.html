Crash In Fairmont Sees Car On Pedestrian Accident

Fri 11-24-2023

(Fairmont)- A crash Tuesday in Fairmont saw a Ceylon resident head to the hospital. The Minnesota State Patrol responded to the crash at 3:33 pm Tuesday when a 2007 Volkswagen Jetta, driven by Tina Marie Beavers, age 57 of Ceylon, struck a pedestrian while crossing highway 15.

The pedestrian was Steven Alan Thiesse, age 69 of Ceylon. He was reported to have non-life threatening injuries due to the car on pedestrian crash. The Fairmont Police, Martin County Sheriff's Office and Mayo Clinic Ambulance all assisted at the scene.
