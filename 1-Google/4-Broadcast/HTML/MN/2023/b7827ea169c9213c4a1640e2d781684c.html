About Fergus Falls, Minnesota

Fergus Falls MN, rich in history and community, is nestled in Otter Tail County, which boasts the most lakes in Minnesota. The 1,100+ lakes and strong job market make it an ideal location for families and working professionals. With an excellent school system and continual city development, Fergus Falls, MN and the surrounding area is a great community to raise a family and grow a business.

Radio Stations in Fergus Falls, MN

Our Fergus Falls radio stations can be heard in most west-central Minnesota cities, such as Park Rapids, Alexandria, Benson Wheaton, and Moorhead, as well as some eastern North Dakota towns like Fargo, Lisbon, Wahpeton and a few South Dakota cities like Britton and Milbank. These small towns are proud of their “Minnesota-nice” way of life and enjoy the perfect combination of farmland, local sports, friendly neighbors and honest businesses.
