Donated Pottery

Become a member of college and community public radio. KVSC 88.1FM. A special piece of pottery is a sweet way to say thank you too!

NEW! Jasper Bond custom pottery coffee mugs

Jasper Bond is a Sauk Rapids pottery (and SCSU faculty emeriti). His pottery is dishwasher and microwave-safe, and the glazes are lead free and oven-proof. Each mug is a unique piece!

A pledge of $60 or above qualifies you for this mug, but you must contact the on air host at 320-308-5872 (KVSC) to set one aside in your name with your pledge.

The 10-ounce hand-thrown mugs feature the KVSC Badge Logo embossed on the outside.  The mugs come in a variety of colors…from blue to terra cotta to earthy/creamy brown tones.

If you are a potter and would like to donate your pottery to KVSC, please contact Dan Seeger at daniel.seeger@stcloudstate.edu or during business hours at 320-308-3053.
