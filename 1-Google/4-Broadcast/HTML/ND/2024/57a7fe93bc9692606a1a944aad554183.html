GF alcohol check results released

By Doug Barrett Feb 9, 2024 | 1:13 PM

A recent alcohol compliance check by the Grand Forks Police Department yielded mixed results. The GFPD sent underage buyers into ten establishment on February 6th in an attempt to buy booze. The under 21 individuals were under police supervision and did not attempt to conceal their age. Authorities say six establishments passed the test…four did not. The GFPD conducts several compliance checks each year. Those who flunk are subject to further enforcement action.
