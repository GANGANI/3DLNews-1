About the Show

The Mighty 790 & 104.7 FM, KFGO, continues to be a leader in talk radio around the country. It Takes 2 is no exception. These two bright, engaged, local talents are engaging listeners on and off-the-air. It Takes 2 is committed to having familiar favorites, new features, great guests and local, relevant talk the community cares about!

Show Hosts

Amy Iler

Amy Iler has been a show producer since 2007. Her charming, recognizable voice and animated personality have become an important part of KFGO. As part of KFGO’s “new generation”, Amy Iler has already helped build the legacy through numerous awards and recognitions. Amy’s passion for talk radio stems from her love of community and conversation.

JJ Gordon

JJ Gordon, born and raised in Fargo, ND as well as spendy summers in the Minnesota lakes country. Off air, JJ is the Artistic Director of The LineBenders Comedy Troupe and has had the pleasure of touring the country entertaining audiences. Venues such as a American Legion Hall in Harvey, ND to the deck of the USS Lexington in Corpus Christi, TX allowed JJ to meet people from all walks of life and enjoy some laughs. At home in Fargo, JJ is a supporter of the arts, young entrepreneurship and spending time with his family. Being on KFGO is one of his proudest achievements, something his father would be proud of as he was a die hard KFGO listener! In the studio, JJ likes to talk about the events of the day, promoting stories that enrich the soul of our communities and movies. He confidently claims to be the biggest movie buff at KFGO. You can hear JJ every weekday from 11:00am to 2:00pm as one half of “It Takes Two with Amy & JJ.”
