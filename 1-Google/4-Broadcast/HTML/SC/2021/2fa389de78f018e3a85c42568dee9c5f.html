Online.

Live5News.com

Live5News.com is your home for news, weather sports, traffic, investigative reports and much more. Whether you're just starting your day or tracking a tropical system, Live5News.com has all the information you need.

Watch live Online

Live video from Live 5 is available on your computer, tablet and smartphone during all local newscasts. When Live 5 is not airing a live newscast, you will see replays of the most recent newscasts.

Let's Get Social

Social Media is rapidly growing as one of the largest news and information platforms and Live 5 is there. Join the conversation. Don't just watch the news, be a part of it. Like and follow Live 5 on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
On Mobile.

Live 5 News App

On the go? Take Live 5 News with you. Download the Live 5 News  App on your iOS or Android device for the latest news, traffic, alerts and 24/7 live streaming wherever you are.

Live 5 First Alert Weather App

Severe weather can strike at any time. Be prepared with the Live 5 First Alert Weather App. Download it now to get the latest alerts, watches, warnings, closures, interactive radar and live streaming.
