1 dead following Friday shooting at Goose Creek apartment complex: GCPD

by Ian Kayanja

Sat, February 10th 2024 at 8:44 PM
1 dead following Friday shooting at Goose Creek apartment complex: GCPD

GOOSE CREEK, S.C. (WCIV) — One person is dead following a shooting at a Goose Creek apartment complex Friday.

Around 4:45 p.m., the Goose Creek Police Department responded to a call referencing a person shot at Saint James at Goose Creek Apartment Complex, located on Channing Way, the Goose Creek Police Department said.

When officers arrived on the scene, they found a male victim shot in the head on the front porch, the Goose Creek Police Department said.

The suspect fled the scene in a gray Nissan Sedan, police said.

No arrest has been made.

The name of the victim has yet to be released.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Goose Creek Police Department Criminal Investigation Unit at (843) 863-5200.
