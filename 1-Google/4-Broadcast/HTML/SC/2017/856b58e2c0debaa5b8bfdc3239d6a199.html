Inmate found dead at Lieber Correctional Institution in Dorchester County

by ABC News 4

Fri, June 2nd 2017 at 6:39 PM
Updated Sat, June 3rd 2017 at 8:01 PM
Lieber Correctional Institution (File/WCIV)

RIDGEVILLE, S.C. (WCIV) — *UPDATE: The Dorchester County Coroner's Office has ruled the inmate's death a suicide.
-------------------------
State authorities are investigating the death of an inmate Friday at Lieber Correctional Institution in Dorchester County.

Sommer Sharpe, spokesperson for the S.C. Dept, of Corrections, confirmed Friday an inmate was found unresponsive in their cell at the prison Friday afternoon.

The Dept. of Corrections is not revealing the inmate's name at this time, as authorities are still working to notify the person's family, Sharpe says.

SLED and the Dorchester County Coroner's Office are investigating the incident.
