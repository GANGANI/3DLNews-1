For Schools and Districts

Group Trainings

Standard-aligned workshops and training for SC districts and schools that are FREE, customizable, and expandable. Can be conducted remotely.

WiFi Hotspots

We provide Wi-Fi hotspot devices free of charge to schools, early learning centers, and after school programs.

Education Research

We collect and report research on South Carolina’s educational systems.

Adult Education

Courses and resources for adult education learners and educators.

Datacasting

We are helping to address the digital divide by providing instructional content to students without Internet access.

eLearning

Resources for participating eLearning districts for teaching and learning outside of the traditional classroom.

Career Day Visits

Coordinating Career Day for your school? We’d be happy to come out and talk to your students!

For Agency Partners

Instructional Design

ETV serves South Carolina’s public safety community and state government agencies by providing development services for essential online training and recertification programs.

Workforce Development

SCETV is committed to developing strong pathways to jobs that need workers in South Carolina.

Production Services

Practical solutions for teleconferencing, streaming video, instructional videos, public service announcements, and more.

For the Public

Carolina Classrooms

ETV's educational series for teachers, parents and citizens. Each episode focuses on key educational topics and initiatives in our state.

College Student Volunteer

Volunteer and work with industry professionals while discovering a career path that aligns with your interests and passions. Learn skills that look great on resumes and may lead to future job opportunities.

Kids Club

Kids who are 12-and-under can get free surprise gifts and access to special SCETV PBS Kids activities and events through our Kids Club.

Storytime with SCETV

Storytime with SCETV is an online series that promotes the love of reading and literary stories connected to South Carolina.

Learning+

Programs, lesson plans, and curriculum to facilitate meaningful at-home learning.

Meet the Helpers

Meet the Helpers is a new public media initiative designed to introduce children to public safety careers and to assist them with emergency preparedness.

Bright by Text

Caregivers: get free tips, games, and resources about language, literacy, safety, and developmental milestones tailored to your child’s age - prenatal up to age eight!
