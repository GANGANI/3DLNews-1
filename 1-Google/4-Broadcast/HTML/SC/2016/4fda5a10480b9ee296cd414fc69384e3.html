ETV World

About ETV World

ETV World is ETV's news/talk service, with live global newscasts, an international perspective on U.S. news, and South Carolina news/talk, including live gavel-to-gavel coverage of the South Carolina Legislature from January through June of each year.

Live newscasts in English come to ETV World from around the world, including DW-TV (Germany's version of public TV), the BBC, and NHK from Japan.

ETV news/talk programs also appear on this channel, including State House Today, Palmetto Scene, and Carolina Business Review.

ETV World broadcasts the West Coast feed of PBS, including programs that may not have appeared on ETV at all during late night programming. This service, a three hour delay of the east coast schedule, is known as "PBS Rewind" and is also used each weekend for viewers who may have missed a favorite program. See full schedule

How to Get ETV World

There are two ways you can receive ETV World.

(1) Use an antenna and tune to the stations listed below.

(2) Explore your cable provider’s list of stations for ETVW. While select cable services offer this channel, currently satellite services do not. The legislative portion of ETV World’s schedule is also online at scetv.org.

ETV World is currently broadcast on:

  • WRLK-DT (digital channel 35-3) in Columbia
  • WNTV-DT (digital channel 29-3) in Greenville
  • WITV-DT (digital channel 7-3) in Charleston
  • WRJA-DT (digital channel 27-3) in Sumter
  • WNSC-DT (digital channel 30-3) in Rock Hill
  • WHMC-DT (digital channel 23-3) in Conway/Myrtle Beach
  • WEBA-DT (digital channel 14-3) in Allendale
  • WJPM-DT (digital channel 33-3) in Florence
  • WNEH-DT (digital channel 38-3) in Greenwood
  • WRET-DT (digital channel 49-3) in Spartanburg
  • WJWJ-DT (digital channel 16-3) in Beaufort

ETV World is part of your local ETV service as ETV.3 via antenna. Beaufort, for example, would be 16.3. It is also available on selected cable outlets, marked as ETVW.

South Carolina schools can access ETV World via webstream on Web 2.
