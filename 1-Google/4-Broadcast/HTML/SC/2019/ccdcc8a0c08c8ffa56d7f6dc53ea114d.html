Connor Ingalls

Evening Anchor

Connor Ingalls is the co-anchor for ABC 15 News at 5, 6, and 7 weekday evenings.

He took over the role in 2022, after serving as a co-anchor for Good Morning Carolinas since 2019.

Connor grew up in Mount Dora, Florida, before graduating from the University of Florida with a BS in Telecommunication in 2016. He started his journalism career in college covering Florida Gators athletics before trying out news reporting and getting hooked.

After graduating, Connor took a morning anchor job at KTVE NBC 10 in Monroe, Louisiana. While at KTVE, he also produced the morning shows and regularly reported.

Connor loves his family, friends, football, and food. Also golf, but that doesn't start with an "f."
