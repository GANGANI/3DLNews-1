Justin Dougherty

Anchor, FOX Carolina News

Justin Dougherty is an anchor for FOX Carolina News.

Dougherty is originally from Springfield, Missouri, and graduated from Evangel University. Before moving with his wife and two boys to the Upstate, Justin spent years chasing major breaking news stories in Boston and chasing tornadoes in Oklahoma.

He’s won awards and received recognition for his anchoring and reporting on severe weather, politics, and education. Justin has also covered many national news stories throughout his career and has traveled abroad to report on and study school systems in other parts of the world.

He first fell in love with news as a teenager and his passion for accurate and in-depth storytelling has only grown since.
