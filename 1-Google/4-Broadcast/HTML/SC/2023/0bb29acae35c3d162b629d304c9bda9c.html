Armstrong Williams on presidential primary, Nikki Haley, Trump, Biden and...the Super Bowl?

by Brian Mcconchie

Sat, December 23rd 2023 at 10:21 PM
7

During a recent visit to WACH FOX News, prominent Sinclair political commentator, Armstrong Williams, sat down for an in-depth interview with WACH FOX News anchor, Brian McConchie.

Williams covered a number of topics from presidential primary politics, to U.S. border policies, to predicting who will win the the White House, and why former South Carolina governor Nikki Haley likely won't be part of it, President Joe Biden and his son, Hunter, and even who will win the Super Bowl (here’s a hint, it’s his favorite team).

As always, Williams held nothing back in his trademark honest hold-nothing-back style.

You can watch the Armstrong Williams show every Sunday at 5a.m. on WACH FOX 57.
