Construction worker injured in accident at CityPlace work site

By WCAX News Team
Published: Dec. 6, 2023 at 11:50 AM EST|Updated: Dec. 6, 2023 at 11:56 AM EST

BURLINGTON, Vt. (WCAX) - A construction worker is recovering after an accident at the CityPlace site.

The Burlington Fire Department says it happened at about 8:40 a.m. Wednesday at the site at 130 Bank Street. Crews say the worker was pinned beneath a concrete shoring box that had partially slid off a boom truck.

The man was successfully pulled out and sent to the hospital -- with what CityPlace developers say are minor injuries. They say it’s the only safety incident at the site since construction started in the fall of 2022.
