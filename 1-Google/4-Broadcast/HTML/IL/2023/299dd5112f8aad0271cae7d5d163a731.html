1 driver hurt when 2 pickups collide on gravel road outside Baring

by John Garlock

Thu, December 28th 2023 at 3:54 PM
Updated Fri, December 29th 2023 at 4:57 AM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
One driver was injured when two pickup trucks collided Thursday on a gravel road south of Baring, Mo. (Photo: Echo Menges/The Edina Sentinel)

NEAR BARING, Mo. — A crash on a northeast Missouri gravel road left one of the drivers injured.

It happened at noon on Thursday on Dairy Avenue, one mile south of Baring.

State troopers said a pickup truck driven by Ivan Good, 58, of Rutledge, was northbound when it crossed the center line and collided with an oncoming pickup truck.

The southbound truck was driven by David Poston, 55, of Baring.

Poston suffered minor injuries. He was taken by private vehicle to Scotland County Hospital in Memphis.

Good was not hurt.

The patrol’s online crash report states that neither driver was wearing a seat belt.
