"It was well overdue," East side Poplar Place redevelopment project is now halfway complete
by Kaira Willis
Wednesday, Dec 27th 2023
3
View All Photos
Poplar Place (Kaira Willis/WICS){p}{/p}
SPRINGFIELD, Ill. (WICS) —

More redevelopments are occurring on Springfield's east side. Officials said these renovations will bring more value to Springfield.

ADVERTISEMENT

We have an update on an east-side neighborhood’s revamp.

Just seven months after starting renovations, the Poplar Place project is halfway finished.

Phase one is already complete and nine families are now living in their new homes.

Phase two of the remodeling process is now underway for the other half of the neighborhood.

"It was definitely needed. It was well well overdue," said Ward 3 Alderman Roy Williams Jr., "People are finally seeing it come to realization. That's so important to people, there have been people in the surrounding communities and neighborhood associations that have been waiting for years because this is going to impact their property values.”

Alderman Williams expects all 75 homes will be completed by next spring.

The new homes come with updated appliances including a garage and the neighborhood will have a new park.

ADVERTISEMENT

Alderman Williams wants to give a special thanks to former Mayor Jim Langfelder and Senator Doris Turner for getting the project off the ground, saying this redevelopment will bring more value to the east side.
