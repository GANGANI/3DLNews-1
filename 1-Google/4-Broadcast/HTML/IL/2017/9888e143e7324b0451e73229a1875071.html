Jobs at FOX 32

The following represents brief/broad position descriptions. It is not intended, nor should it be construed, to represent a comprehensive and/or exhaustive description of all duties/responsibilities and requisite skills for each position.

To review a list of and to apply online for job opportunities available at Fox Stations Sales, including the FTS Sales Training Program, click here.

Meteorologist: FOX 32 News is currently on the hunt for a passionate and experienced meteorologist to join our dynamic team.

Key Responsibilities/Specific Duties

Presentation: Deliver weather forecasts with excellent communication and presenting skills.

Scientific Analysis: Utilize a strong background in science to interpret and simplify weather data.

Storytelling: Convey the weather story using engaging graphics and video aids.

Daily Planning: Guide viewers through their day with actionable weather insights.

Adlib Capabilities: Exhibit strong adlib skills for dynamic and unscripted delivery.

Graphic Creation: Develop comprehensive weather graphics using the Baron Lynx system.

Traffic Reporting: Provide traffic updates and conditions during newscasts as assigned.

Ideal Candidate Traits: The ideal meteorologist for our team is someone with enthusiasm for all aspects of weather - from the science behind it to the everyday impact it has on our viewers. Your passion for meteorology will be key in winning weather coverage consistently.

  • Passionate about engaging and informative weather reporting.
  • Dynamic and adaptable presentation skills.
  • Expertise with Baron Lynx weather graphics system.
  • Ability to adlib effectively during live segments.
  • Skilled at working under deadline pressure and multi-tasking.

We look forward to welcoming a talented weather expert who will excel in our fast-paced and collaborative environment to provide our viewers with essential weather coverage. Apply Now!

News Photographer: WFLD Chicago Fox 32 has an immediate full-time job opening for a staff photographer for our news broadcast(s). Candidate will shoot and edit spot news, feature stories and sports. They will also perform LiveU and ENG microwave shots from in and around the Chicago area. Candidate must be a high caliber news photographer with a minimum 3-5 years’ experience. Bachelor’s degree in Communications or a related field preferred. Candidate must be able to shoot and edit a package in the field and must be familiar with Adobe Premiere.

Candidate must be able to lift over fifty (50) pounds of equipment, walk carrying a tripod, batteries and Live U backpack. Must be willing to work in extreme hot/cold weather conditions and be willing to work weekends, holidays, and overnights. Individual must also work well with Assignment Desk, Writers, and Reporters. Position necessitates travel and working overtime (reg. 40 hour work week).

Employee must show proof of a valid driver’s license and to work within NABET CBA. Apply Now!

Multi-Media Producer: Do you like to tell great stories and work with leading edge gear? FOX 32 is looking for a Producer/Photographer/Editor to join the Creative Services Team. You need to be a creative, high-integrity, driven person who takes pride in your work and thrives on collaboration. We are a tight team that works hard and loves to win. We’re looking for people with ideas beyond a typical promotional spot and who want to make short-form videos and digital franchises in addition to topicals and image spots. Must have a can-do attitude and enjoy problem-solving.

Job Summary:

The Multimedia & Commercial Producer will work with our Sales Team to create attention-grabbing commercials for clients on linear TV and streaming platforms.  Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following duties.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Conceive, pitch, write, produce, and edit engaging commercial spots;
  • Produce client-sponsored promotional content;
  • Successfully execute projects in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment;
  • Ensure that assigned projects are organized, executed, and delivered on schedule;
  • Demonstrate responsibility for all equipment issued by FOX;
  • Comply with all FOX Corporate computer requirements;
  • Comply with all FOX Corporate and station policies;
  • Perform other duties as assigned.

Required Skills and Abilities

  • Strong editing and graphic design skills;
  • Strong image and promotion writing skills;
  • Extensive knowledge and proven experience in Adobe Premiere and After Effects;
  • Working knowledge of Adobe Creative Cloud products;
  • Proficiency with TV/Film lighting and audio gear;
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to collaborate successfully with all departments;
  • Well organized, detail-oriented self-starter;
  • Ability to work a flexible schedule that may require nights, early mornings, weekends, holidays, and emergency scenarios;
  • Must provide a reel of work examples as part of the interview process;
  • Must have valid driver’s license and clean driving record.

Education and Experience

  • Minimum three (3) years of video production and marketing experience preferred;
  • Experience shooting with DSLR cameras;
  • Experience in documentary/reality production a plus but not required;
  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Graphic Design, Communications, Journalism, Radio/TV, or related discipline preferred.

To apply, please click here.
 

Sports Producer: FOX 32 Chicago is on the hunt for a dynamic Sports Producer with a passion for in-depth storytelling and a rich understanding of what makes the sports world captivating. We're not after the average sportscast producer; we need a forward-thinker who crafts compelling narratives that celebrate the human spirit and passion behind athletic endeavors. This role is about innovation—podcasts, original programming, live specials, and short-form documentaries that resonate profoundly with our audience.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead and bring to life live sports shows, including events and digital content, from conception to completion.
  • Develop and produce engaging sports content that steps beyond conventional coverage.
  • Conduct interviews, write riveting scripts, guide editing processes, and manage show budgets efficiently.
  • Oversee team operations, including working with talent, Associate Producers, Production Associates throughout each production phase.

Job Duties Snapshot:

  • Plan and create engaging Live Sports Shows for studio and on-location settings, including research, rundown generation, and graphics pre-building.
  • Produce sports segments for newscasts and longer-format programs.
  • Generate and execute new content ideas while executing existing project plans.
  • Perform pre-production, production, and post-production tasks, including both writing and editing duties.

Qualifications:

  • Minimum of 3-5 years' experience in live news and show production.
  • Background in podcasting, social media, and documentary storytelling is highly preferred.
  • Proficiency in verbal and written communication, along with effective negotiation and presentation capabilities.
  • Demonstrated aptitude for creative writing and strong narrative development.
  • Experienced in project management with the ability to juggle multiple assignments and strategically adjust to shifting priorities.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro editing software is strongly desired.
  • DSLR shooting and lighting expertise is a plus.
  • Attentive to detail with exceptional organizational skills.
  • Avid interest and broad knowledge of sports, notably Chicago sports, staying updated on developments and news.

If you are a sports enthusiast with a talent for producing captivating stories and have the ambition to lead a team in crafting extraordinary sports content, we invite you to apply for the Sports Producer role at FOX 32 Chicago and elevate the way sports stories are told. To apply, please click here.

News Producer: WFLD is seeking a staff producer to contribute to one of Fox 32’s newscasts. This role requires a producer to gather facts, find video and oversee editing of stories. The candidate must have strong writing skills and be self-sufficient for the newscast. Responsible for story content and accuracy. Must perform all other duties as assigned and deemed appropriate.  REQUIREMENTS: Candidate must have 2-3 years producing experience, experience as a line producer and computer knowledge preferred. Must be creative, concise, detail oriented, able to handle deadline pressure and take direction from managers. Reliability, good news judgment, and factual accuracy are crucial. Heaving sitting at workstation, light standing, bending, walking and stair climbing are also required. Candidate must be able to work flexible hours; early mornings, late nights, weekends and holidays may be required. To apply, please click here.

Vice President, News Director: FOX CHICAGO (WFLD 32 & WPWR 50) is seeking a visionary Vice President, News Director for a leadership opportunity in the dynamic Chicago TV market. The ideal candidate is innovative, competitive, and an excellent storyteller ready to reimagine our business and utilize social media and emerging platforms to enhance our brand. Your focus will be on finding, nurturing and developing compelling stories that are enlightening and empowering, moving away from legacy formats and engaging viewers on a daily basis.

Key roles:

  • Oversee the station's news, working in unison with the SVP/General Manager
  • Collaborate with VP/Programming & Development to produce consumer-focused content across multiple platforms.
  • Manage the news department including budgets, expenses, strategic planning, technology, etc.
  • Maintain standards for journalistic integrity.
  • Develop excellent working relationships with department heads, staff & executives as well as with key Chicago leaders.

Requirements: At least 5 years’ experience managing a news team, experience in launching new programs, good interpersonal skills, budget management, foresight of the future of local news programming, including CTV. Proven success transforming traditional news into a digital-focused operation. Demonstrated collaboration and teamwork skills. Having experience in top 30 market and a college degree will be an advantage. Ideal candidate: A hands-on leader with excellent judgment, planning, organization, team building, written/presentation skills and passion for good content.  Keen knowledge of digital content and distribution. Knowledge of libel laws, journalist rights and responsibilities is essential. To apply, please click here.

Streaming Sales & Insights Specialist: Do you want to play a key role in building a successful connected television/streaming business? Fox Television Stations is seeking to hire a newly created position within our local TV stations in Chicago, Dallas, Phoenix and Milwaukee. This Specialist role is one part marketer, one part data analyst and is fully involved from the pre-sales process all the way through to post ad campaign reporting. Our ideal candidate for this job has worked in local media sales (not necessarily as a direct seller), is familiar with the ever-changing world of streaming advertising, possesses excellent communication skills and is adept at analyzing advertising performance data.

Primary responsibilities include:

  • Working with the station sales team to create compelling advertising packages focused on delivering results for our customers
  • Monitoring ongoing campaign results within the FLX platform
  • Developing insights-focused, data-driven stories as part of our post-campaign reporting
  • Accompanying the sales team on both new business pitches and ongoing account meetings

Job requirements

  • Two + years experience working in streaming/digital media
  • Excellent communication skills – written, verbal and listening
  • Critical thinking and curious about data
  • Four-year college degree

To apply, please click here.

Sales Assistant: WFLD/WPWR seeks a highly motivated individual to join our sales assistant team.  The ideal candidate will assist members of the sales team with various projects, reports, and presentations.  Core responsibilities include: data entry of broadcast / digital orders, filing, organizing and verifying accuracy of all orders as well as complete follow through of the sales order process. Candidate must be extremely detailed oriented. The position requires daily interfacing with Sales Management, Accounting, Traffic, Research and Programming departments. REQUIREMENTS: College degree preferred. Qualifications include strong clerical, communication and problem-solving skills. Ideal candidate must be able to demonstrate the ability to provide service excellence to clients and account executives. Prior broadcast sales assistant experience preferred. Microsoft Office, Wide Orbit, One Domain and Nielsen ratings knowledge a plus. This a great career opportunity with long-term career potential. To apply, please click here.

News Anchor/Reporter: WFLD Chicago Fox 32 has an immediate full-time job opening for a News Anchor/Reporter on our news broadcast(s). The ideal candidate will display professionalism and personality, both on and off the air. Candidates must also possess strong presentation skills, strong work ethic, broad knowledge of current affairs, the ability to work effectively with a team of professionals, have an aptitude for social media, and passion for innovation. You will be expected to contribute content as an Anchor/Reporter on-air, and to other platforms including our website and social media. This is a fantastic opportunity to shine as a marquee anchor in a Top-3 market. Requirements for this position include at least 3 years of experience at the anchor desk and reporting in the field. You must show the dynamic ability to work with the news team and collaborate on newscasts. You will also be writing packages and stories on a nightly basis. Must have college degree in Journalism or related field. To apply, please click here.

Account Executive: You will be responsible for all aspects of accounts, generating new business, selling OTT and CTV platforms, and the creation and sale of unique multi-screen promotional sponsorships. Work independently and with sales management to implement strategies to maintain strong client and agency relationships, consistently grow revenue, and exceed revenue goals. Present and negotiate all aspects of the stations and digital offerings. Deliver unique and valuable packages to new and existing clients. Meet and communicate with clients regularly to help expedite the closing process. Prepare and deliver sales presentations to clients. Process transactional business. Work with clients and station personnel to develop advertising schedules. Timely management of accounts and administrative tasks. Maintain a high level of communication with station and corporate management, sales staff and all station personnel. Participate in sales trainings and meetings.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 3+ years professional experience in Sales, Account Management, and/or Business Development, preferably in a TV sales environment.
  • College degree preferred.
  • Experience developing new business, with a record of digital success.
  • Skilled negotiator and motivator.
  • Excellent organizational, time management, written & verbal communication skills.
  • Digitally savvy. Proficiency with computers and cloud-based communication applications. Experience with WideOrbit preferred.
  • Ability to accurately forecast revenue based on client pending and pipelines. Apply Now!

Technician: The production technician will operate auxiliary equipment as part of FOX 32’s live news and sports productions. These include but are not limited to robotic cameras, graphics and audio. The ability to excel under pressure in the fast-paced environment of live local news alongside a talented team of producers and directors is required. Responsibilities include: serve as technical co-pilot for all news and sports programming, experience with studio LED lighting, ETC lighting consoles, Ross studio camera robotics, SSL audio consoles, and Chyron graphics system the ability to mix multiple audio sources during any news or sports program, experience in an automated production control room environment, interaction with talent and guests using excellent interpersonal communication, verbal skills and a professional demeanor is critical. Other duties may include recording news and programming feeds and tuning in Satellite and Microwave signals, preparing content for broadcast by ingesting and timing media as well as judging technical quality. Previous experience with or ability to learn Grass Valley iTX master control automation system is also helpful. Knowledge of windows server/client platforms, high performance storage systems and video encoding/delivery systems is a plus. REQUIREMENTS: Five or more years of television production experience is preferred. Must have strong attention to detail and the ability to proactively isolate and resolve technical problems within network and broadcast systems. You must be a team player that is able to work independently as required. You must have in-depth troubleshooting skills and the ability to perform successfully under pressure are essential. You must be able to multi-task. Previous experience and knowledge in an IT architecture infrastructure is a plus. Must be flexible with schedule including working on weekends and holidays. The ability to sit and/or stand for extended periods of time, climb ladders, and lift up to 50 lbs. is needed.  Position requires membership in NABET Local 41 union. To apply, please click here.

Maintenance Technician: WFLD Fox Chicago is seeking a skilled Broadcast Maintenance Engineer to join our team. The selected candidate will assist in performing preventative maintenance, troubleshooting, and problem-solving of all equipment associated with a 24/7/365 television operation in a major market. The selected candidate will be responsible for maintenance and repair of Automated Master Control Systems, Automated Production Control Rooms Systems, Studio Production equipment, Camera Control Systems, Robotic Pedestals, Remote Production ENG equipment, Newsroom Video Playback, File Based Archive solutions, non-linear edit systems and satellite distribution systems.  The selected candidate will also assist in system design, construction and commissioning of new technologies at the Studio, Transmitter, and Remote Facilities.  Familiarity with Evertz Magnum, Grass Valley ITX, Sony switchers and ELC, Bit Central Playout, Chyron Graphics systems, and Telestream Vantage is a plus. Qualifications: Candidates must have at least 5 years’ experience in TV Engineering which must include a strong IT background. The ideal candidate must be able to communicate effectively in verbal and written form in addition to being able to work closely with all station staff in a team environment. Must possess a valid driver’s license and have the ability to lift 50 pounds. Ability to work nights and weekends is a must. To apply, please click here.

Assignment Editor/Digital Content Creator: We're hiring a hybrid position of Assignment Editor/ Digital Content Creator to be part of our next-level team at WFLD FOX 32 in Chicago. Reporting to the News Director, this position will be laser-focused on telling stories that matter most to digital and social users on weekdays. It will also handle Assignment Editor duties on the weekends. Does this sound like you? You're smart, fast on your feet, team-oriented and full of energy. You're an expert in digital and social media platforms. You know a big story when you hear it. You're obsessed with details. Nothing gets past you. You have a zero-tolerance policy for grammatical errors. You could copy edit in your sleep (but you won't— we promise). You love a fast-paced, challenging environment and crave the chance to learn new things. You've got goals, which you strive to meet and exceed (all of them). You thrive in a competitive, fearless culture of creativity and expertise. What you do matters, and you're out to make a difference. Most of all, you want to win. Every. Single. Day. The person we're looking for lives and breathes news and information, and is driven by an intense passion for storytelling and working to mine stories at the Assignment Desk. If that's you, your dream team awaits. RESPONSIBILITIES: Create compelling, UX-focused content related to news, events and topics relevant to local users and beyond, including text stories, photos/graphics, livestreaming content, breaking news, and social media elements. Engage and build loyalty among communities. Serve as liaison to newsroom, ensuring digital and social dominance. Reach and engage users on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, Snapchat and beyond. Work with Group Content Team and other FTS markets to distribute high-interest stories at scale. Follow group standards. Monitor real-time analytics. Other duties as deemed necessary and assigned. REQUIREMENTS: 3+ years experience developing and publishing content, including news, on digital and social platforms preferred. BA/BS degree, preferably in Journalism, Communications or related field with emphasis on digital publishing. Excellent writer and storyteller. Superior news judgment. Impeccable writing and copy editing skills. Overwhelming attention to detail. Thrive under deadline pressure. Stellar communication skills. Social media expert. Proficient in content management systems, video publishing, livestreaming, and digital analytics tools. Video and photo editing experience (Adobe products a plus). Apply Now!
