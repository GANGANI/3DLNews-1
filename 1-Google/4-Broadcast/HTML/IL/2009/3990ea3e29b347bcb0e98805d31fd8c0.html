High School Students Named Illinois State Scholars

Published: Dec. 3, 2009 at 11:39 AM CST

The Illinois Student Assistance Commission recently recognized more than 19,000 high school students with the honor.

State scholar winners rank in the top 10 percent of high school seniors. Selection is based on achievement test scores and/or class rank.

The recognition doesn't include a monetary award, but recipients are encouraged to cite it on applications for college admission and scholarships.

Meanwhile, many of these scholars are high school students in our area. Below is a list of local high school students who are top achievers of their class.
