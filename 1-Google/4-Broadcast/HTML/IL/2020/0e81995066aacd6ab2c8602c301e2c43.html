Springfield Police respond to 6th Street Walmart

by WICS/WRSP Staff

Thu, December 31st 2020 at 6:53 PM
Updated Thu, December 31st 2020 at 10:06 AM
A large police presence can be seen at Walmart on Sixth Street in Springfield on Wednesday evening. (Matthew Kauerauf - WICS)

SPRINGFIELD, Ill. (WICS/WRSP) — UPDATE (Thursday, 9:06 a.m.):

Springfield Police say nothing suspicious was found.


ORIGINAL:

A police presence can be seen at Walmart on Sixth Street in Springfield on Wednesday evening.

In a statement to Newschannel 20 and FOX Illinois regarding the situation, Springfield Police Deputy Chief Josh Stuenkel said:

“An employee overheard a comment and Walmart elected to evacuate the building. An SPD K9 will be utilized to assist with a search to clear the building."

As of 8:35 p.m. on Wednesday, Springfield Police are still working to clear the building.

This is a developing story. We'll provide details as they become available.
