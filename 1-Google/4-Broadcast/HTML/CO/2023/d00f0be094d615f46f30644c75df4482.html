submit a Commentary

Commentaries run after the newscast and are pre-recorded. Your commentary can be about any subject, and should be 3 minutes or less. The easiest way to submit a commentary is to record it on your phone’s memo or voice recorder app, and then email it as an attachment to the KOTO News Team at julia@koto.org and news@koto.org

contact the koto news team!

Alternately, stop by the station and head upstairs to Ben’s office to record your commentary on site. Commentaries must be received by 4 p.m. to air on the same day’s news program.
