Nora Hart

News Anchor

Born and raised in Oregon, Nora Hart is thrilled to be able to work in her hometown near her family and friends. Nora joined FOX 12 in 2014, and has worn a few different hats since then. In the past, you may have seen her at the green wall delivering the weather forecast, or perhaps reporting in the field. Now, you can watch her anchoring weeknights on the 4, 6 and 10 O'Clock News on FOX 12.

Nora graduated from the School of Journalism and Communication at the University of Oregon, where she earned her bachelor's degree. She went on to earn her master's degree in Business Management and Organizational Leadership from Warner Pacific College.

After college, Nora spent a few years in Abilene, Texas working at KRBC-TV as an anchor, reporter, and producer.

Nora is married to the love of her life, Derich. They recently welcomed their first child-- a joyful little boy that keeps them on their toes! Nora is also a proud dog-mom to a chiweenie named Celine (yes, named after Nora's favorite singer!). In her free time, Nora enjoys thrift shopping, DIY home renovation projects, Hallmark Channel movies, and traveling.
