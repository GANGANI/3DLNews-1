Working structure fire near Florence, multiple fire departments respond

by News Staff

Mon, November 6th 2023 at 6:44 AM
Florence fire.jpg

    TOPICS:

    FireFlorenceDepartmentsSceneNearStructureRespondHighway

LANE COUNTY, Ore. — Florence Fire and Western Lane Fire are on scene of a working structure fire near Florence.

The fire is in the 5000 block of Mitchell Loop Rd, near Highway 101, south of Florence.

The fire was fully involved, with water having to be brought in by tanker, as there are no hydrants nearby.

Reedsport, Mapleton and Yachats Fire all have been called in for mutual aid.

No word yet if there was anyone inside, fire crews are staying on site to mop up.

Fire investigators are on scene, and this is a developing story, we will bring you more details as we receive them.
