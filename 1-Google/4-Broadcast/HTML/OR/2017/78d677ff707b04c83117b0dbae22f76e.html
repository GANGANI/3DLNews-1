Tip leads to the arrest of Craigslist robbery suspect

by KTVL Staff

Mon, April 24th 2017 at 3:48 PM
Updated Tue, April 25th 2017 at 5:11 PM
3
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
0424_soaresmug_Copy__1_ (1).png

MEDFORD, Ore. - The Medford Police Dept. arrested 19-year-old Brandon Soares, this afternoon at a residence near 4th and Ivy.

Police say they received an anonymous tip that Soares was inside the residence, which was confirmed when police arrived. A search warrant was issued and police found Soares hiding in the attic. Soares is now in police custody.

The situation led to a 3 hour standoff where law enforcement camped outside of the home with guns drawn.

Police say Soares is the man suspected a robbery that took place last week in the YMCA parking lot, during a Craigslist transaction.
