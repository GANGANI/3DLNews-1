BEDSLIDE returns to Medford with dozens of jobs

by Ariana Rakhshani

Wed, December 9th 2015 at 5:41 PM
BEDSLIDE is still renovating at the site before beginning operation, in Medford, Oregon, Tuesday, December 8th, 2015. (KTVL/Ariana Rakhshani)

    TOPICS:

    medford

MEDFORD-- BEDSLIDE initially started in Medford, but was sold several years ago and relocated to Indiana.

Now, the original owner bought it back, and said it is coming home.

BEDSLIDE is a truck accessory manufacturer; it turns your truck bed into a sliding drawer.

Southern Oregon Regional Economic Development Incorporated (SOREDI) said BEDSLIDE will be opening its doors this month, and is looking to hire about 35 employees.

"It's an exciting achievement for us and it's kind of a good time to celebrate a gift to the holidays for the area for sure," SOREDI's Executive Director Ron Fox said.

Fox said this business has been successful over the years, and will continue its success in Southern Oregon.

This is not the only business making its way into the Rogue Valley. Fox said more businesses should announce their moves soon.
