Join us for Kid's Day on Saturday, December 2nd from 8am-11am.

For 66 years Grants Pass Broadcasting has been hosting a radio-thon to raise money for less fortunate kids here in Josephine County. This events gives them a Christmas worth remembering!

This year we have partnered with the Grants Pass Active Club.

All monies raised during our "Kids Day" radio-thon and on the biddable items (listed below) will go to purchase gifts for the kids. In addition to gifts, the children (and their families) will receive food, a handmade blanket, and even a Christmas tree.

Please join us for this heartfelt tradition! We have found it to be a great way to get into the holiday spirit and bless the kids of Josephine County.

Tune-in Saturday, December 2nd from 8am-11am with KAJO (99.7 fm), KLDR (98.3 fm), and KRRM (94.7 fm).
