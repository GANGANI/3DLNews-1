Karen Daborowski

Producer
Washington, D.C.

Karen Daborowski joined the Gray Television Washington News Bureau in January 2019 as a producer.

A graduate of the University of Maryland, Karen began her career in 1992, as a Capitol Hill producer for Tribune Broadcasting. After spending three years covering political stories and scandals on the Hill, she took a job as a producer for the Fox hit television program “America’s Most Wanted.”

Karen spent the next 17-years working alongside John Walsh, the USM and the FBI apprehending some of the countries most wanted fugitives and recovering missing children. When “America’s Most Wanted” came to an end in 2015, Karen teamed up with Warner Brothers as producer for the program “ Crime Watch Daily.”

She is very excited to be back on Capitol Hill and part of the Gray family. When she is not running around Capitol Hill or tracking bad guys, Karen enjoys running and boating.
