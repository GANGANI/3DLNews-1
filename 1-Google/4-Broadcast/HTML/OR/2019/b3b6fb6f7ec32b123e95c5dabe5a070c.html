VIDEO: 'Get off my dog' owner yells as Yorkie puppy is nearly swept away by large bird

by Kyndell Kim

Sun, February 17th 2019 at 6:17 PM
Updated Sun, February 17th 2019 at 9:32 PM
A young Yorkie puppy, Lulu (left), was nearly swept away by a large bird, possibly a hawk (right). (KSNV)

    TOPICS:

    BarkBritish MusiciansCelisCelis WhiteCreative WorksLuluMusicMusical Compositions

LAS VEGAS (KSNV) — What started like any typical Friday evening quickly escalated to something much more traumatic for Cecilia Celis and her puppy Lulu.

“We were inside making tacos, and we hear crying. We just hear them barking and crying, so we are like ‘oh they’re just play fighting’ and a second later, I just look outside. We see a huge bird fly up, and I yell at the bird, ‘get off my dog! Get off my dog!” Celis explains.

Surveillance video from Celis’home shows her rushing outside to her two-pound Yorkie who was in the talons of a large bird, possibly a hawk.

Another angle of the footage shows Celis running out with a pillow, fighting to free her puppy.

The 15-year-old swings and hits the bird three times before the bird finally lets Lulu go.

The close-up of the bird shows torn straps around its legs, which leads Celis to believe it is also someone’s pet.

Celis says she and her family rushed Lulu to a veterinarian, who told them that Lulu is going to be okay.

Now, they want others in Southern Nevada to be on high alert.

“We just hope that the bird gets captured,” said Celis. “Bird comes out of nowhere. It could happen to any of us.”
