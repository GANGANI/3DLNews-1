Army attack helicopters visit Oregon Coast

by KCBY News

Fri, January 25th 2013 at 4:00 PM
9
VIEW ALL PHOTOS

NORTH BEND, Ore. - Five U.S. Army AH-64 Apache helicopters spotted at Southwest Oregon Regional Airport on Thursday are in town for military exercises, KCBY News reports.

The aircraft are used in combat zones as attack helicopters.

Unlike the orange Coast Guard helicopters often spotted along the Oregon Coast, the Apaches are black.
