Contest Rules

Here are the rules governing all contests aired on KAJO-AM1270, KAJO-FM 99.7, KLDR-FM 98.3 and KRRM-FM 94.7, and on the station websites. In the following rules, wherever the words "KAJO" or "KLDR" or "KRRM" appear, they include the business entity to which the station is licensed, and any member companies, parent corporations, and affiliates.

No purchase is necessary to enter any KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM contest. For free contest admission information, call (541) 476-6608.

To be eligible to win any prize on KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM, you must not have won anything on KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM in the past 30 days or any prize valued at $600 or more in the past year. Some KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM contests will have specific rules that may amend these eligibility values and these rules will be aired on KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM during said contests. Only one winner per household is permitted within 30 days after a member of that household has won a contest. A "household" is defined as any individuals residing at the same address.

Any prize awarded to an ineligible listener will be deemed null and void, and an alternate eligible winner may be named.

Anytime you call the KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM studio, you assume that your call will be broadcast or recorded and you grant us permission to broadcast the call or record it for later broadcast.

As a participant of a KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM contest, you assume that your voice, name, and/or picture may be used for promotional, broadcast, or Internet purposes.

Prizes awarded are not redeemable for cash. No prize is transferable. No substitution for a prize shall be made at the request of a winner. KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM reserves the right to substitute a prize of similar value.

All winners of all contests are responsible for all taxes on prize awarded. All winners of a prize valued at $600 or more will be required to complete the appropriate tax forms at KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM prior to claiming prize.

In the event a winner is subject to or the subject of any order or legal process issued by any government agency having jurisdiction over the affairs of the winner (i.e. garnishment, child support order, judgment, lien, and the like), KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM's delivery of the prize to the official representative of the government entity claiming a right to the prize shall be thus deemed as KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM awarding the prize to the winner. KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM shall be entitled to rely in good faith, upon any documents presented by the representative seeking to collect the prize in lieu of the winner. KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM shall not be liable for any claim by any winner for damages incidental thereto.

All KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM contests are open to all eligible U.S. residents 18 and over (unless otherwise specified). Contestants must possess a valid form of identification bearing the picture of the contestant acceptable to KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM that will be presented to KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM and accepted before the prize is awarded.

Winners will be expected to claim prize at KAJO/KLDR/KRRM, 888 Rogue River Highway Grants Pass, OR 97527 between the hours of 8:00am-5:00pm, Monday through Friday (excluding holidays) within 30 days of being notified that they are winners, unless there is an expiration date on prize which will be specifically stated.

Employees of KAJO, KLDR or KRRM, their advertising agencies, affiliates, contest sponsors, employees, and immediate families of each, and employees of all media of mass communication within a one hundred mile radius of the KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM main studio are not eligible to win any contest. Immediate family includes the spouse, great-grandparents, grandparents, parents, brothers, sisters, children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren of the employee and his/her spouse. This also includes individuals for whom the employee is current legal guardian.

All federal, state, and local laws and regulations apply. Contest void where prohibited.

KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM reserves the right to disqualify any winner if any contest rules are violated in any way.

KAJO, KLDR, KRRM and its parent corporation, Grants Pass Broadcasting Corporation, assumes no liability for situations, repairs, or incidents arising from any aspect of any prize awarded in any contest.

As a licensee of the FCC and a trustee of the public airwaves, KAJO, KLDR, and KRRM reserves the right to interrupt and/or discontinue any contest or promotion (and not award the offered prize) at the discretion of KAJO/KLDR/KRRM management if world events, the national mood, or public safety so warrant.

KAJO, KLDR, KRRM and its parent corporation, Grants Pass Broadcasting Corporation, their advertising agencies, affiliates, contest sponsors, employees, and immediate families of each absolve themselves and shall be held harmless from any action or liability arising from any contest or prize awarded in any contest.

Although KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM will make reasonable efforts to conduct its contests substantially as announced or advertised, KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM reserves the right in extraordinary situations to change or modify the rules of its contests as exigent circumstances may require. In such cases, KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM will amend the rules for that contest and broadcast the amended rules as promptly as possible. In such case, the contest will be conducted in accordance with the amended rules.

KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM reserves the right to amend the rules to any contest at any time. KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM will provide all contest judges. The decision of the judges is final.

For any contest requiring phone entry, you must use only the following numbers unless otherwise specified: 541-472-1270 for KAJO, 541-479-9898 for KLDR, and 541-916-5947 for KRRM. The person operating the telephone at KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM has the final decision over who is the winning caller.

For any contest requiring written entry forms, they may be faxed to KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM at (541) 476-4018, e-mailed to a contest specific address on www.kajo.com, www.kldr.com, or krrm.com, or mailed/delivered to KAJO/KLDR/KRRM, 888 Rogue River Highway Grants Pass, OR 97527. All entries become property of KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM and will not be returned. KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM reserves the right to retain any or all contest materials for any reason that KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM deems necessary to the completion of the contest, or reasons relating to publicity and/or advertising. KAJO, KLDR, or KRRM shall have no responsibility for a contestant's failure to win or claim any prize based on difficulties with telephone, facsimile, e-mail, or internet difficulties, or any circumstances in any contest.
