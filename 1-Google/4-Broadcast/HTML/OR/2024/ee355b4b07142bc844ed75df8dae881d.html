Human skull and skeletal remains discovered near Brownsville

by News Staff

Fri, February 9th 2024 at 9:56 AM
Updated Sat, February 10th 2024 at 6:08 PM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Human skull and skeletal remains discovered near Brownsville. Photo courtesy of MarQee Maunakea/SBG.

    TOPICS:

    Skeletal remainsHuman skullLinn County Sheriff's OfficeBrownsvilleInvestigationPropertyDetective Scott TennantHighway 228

LINN COUNTY, Ore. — According to the Linn County Sheriff's Office (LCSO) a caller reported finding skeletal remains on their property.

On February 8, 2024, Linn County Sheriff Michelle Duncan reports that deputies responded to a property located on rural acreage off of Highway 228, outside of Brownsville.

LCSO deputies located a human skull in an area where brush was being cleared by the resident.

Detectives will continue the investigation as to cause of death, how long the skull has been there, and who it belongs to.

Anyone with information on this case should call Detective Scott Tennant at 541-967-3950.
