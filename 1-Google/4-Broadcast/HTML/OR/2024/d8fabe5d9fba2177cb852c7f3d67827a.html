Cyndi Lauper’s son arrested for gun and drug possession, police say

By CNN Newsource staff
Published: Feb. 9, 2024 at 10:06 PM PST

(CNN) - The son of 1980′s music icon Cyndi Lauper appeared in a New York courtroom Friday after he was arrested for gun possession earlier this week.

The New York Police Department said they were responding to a shooting in Harlem Wednesday night and were in the process of conducting a canvas for evidence when they located 26-year-old Declyn “Dex” Lauper nearby, and he was in possession of a loaded gun.

Lauper, a rapper, was charged with criminal possession of a weapon and criminal possession of a controlled substance. He was not charged in the shooting. A judge set his bail at $20,000.

The shooting victim is hospitalized in stable condition and was also charged with criminal possession of a weapon.

The investigation is ongoing.
