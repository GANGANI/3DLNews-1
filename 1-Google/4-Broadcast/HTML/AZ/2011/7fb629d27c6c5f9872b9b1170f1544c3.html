2023 Power of the Purse!

Sunday, October 29 from 2-5 pm

 

For more than 50 years, Beatitudes Campus has been serving older adults and their families in north central Phoenix. The success and continued growth of Beatitudes Campus is possible because of our strong commitment to our vision to be a retirement community where residents can live, learn and grow through all the stages of their lives. We serve not only the people who call the campus home — we open our doors and serve people throughout our community and nation.

Our enduring mission to serve older people and their families is at the core of Beatitudes Campus and is what has made Beatitudes Campus a strong and respected leader in retirement living for more than five decades.

To maintain this vibrant community of older adults and to expand the world of possibilities for the next generation require the investment and support of leaders like you to sponsor our signature fund-raising event, Power of the Purse. Your sponsorship will help support initiatives and programs for the campus, such as our commitment to assist residents who need financial help because they have outlived their own personal assets. Your support will also advance our programs that focus on wellness and ensure that our residents have the greatest chance for living life at the highest potential. Another program support by Power of the Purse includes our Comfort Matters™ program, an award-winning dementia care program dedicated to improving the quality of care and quality of life for people living with dementia, including Alzheimer’s disease.
