Female serial bank robber strikes again in Georgia

by FOX 31 News Team

Tue, November 23rd 2010 at 11:07 AM
Updated Wed, December 30th 2015 at 4:33 PM
Photograph courtesy, FBI

Tue, 23 Nov 2010 16:07:12 GMT — The FBI is looking for a female bank robber who has hit Georgia banks three times since last Thursday, reported the Atlanta Journal-Constitution.

Law enforcement officials say the latest robbery took place about 10:20 a.m. Monday at the Wells Fargo Bank on Scenic Highway in Snellville. Authorites say they believe the same woman is responsible for robberies at a BB&T in Lilburn and a Wells Fargo in Redan Village.

The FBI describes the woman as being black, 25- to 35-years old, medium build, and wears a white bandage on the right side of her face.

Read more about the bank robberies at the Atlanta Journal-Constitution.
