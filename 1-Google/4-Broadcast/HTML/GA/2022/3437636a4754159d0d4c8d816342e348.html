Macon-Bibb County Fire Department remembers fallen Firefighter Randy Parker

by Haley Garrett

Fri, February 11th 2022 at 4:26 PM
Source: Macon-Bibb County Fire Department

MACON, Ga. -- Today, Macon-Bibb County Fire Department honored and remembered the life of one of their own.

Randy Parker was killed while fighting a fire on Fairview Drive in February of 2015.

While battling the fire for over an hour, crews that were inside the house fell into the basement when the floor collapsed.

Tragically, Parker was trapped for a significant amount of time and was later pronounced dead.

He had served in the Macon-Bibb community for more than 20 years.

"Today we pay tribute and remembrance to Lt. Randy Parker who was taken away from the world too soon on February 11th, 2015, in the line of duty. The Department honors our Fallen Firefighter and pray for his family, friends, and all affected by the loss," stated Macon-Bibb County Fire Department.
