One Murder Suspect in Custody, Second Remains at Large

Published: Aug. 19, 2004 at 10:21 PM EDT

Police have captured one suspect in a deadly shooting, but the second is still on the loose. Torrance McRae was arrested yesterday afternoon after police spotted him along 40th Street. Police say he was involved in the August 8 shooting of Antonio Harris.

Harris died from his wounds Tuesday at Memorial Health. McRae is being held in the Chatham County Detention Center.

Police are still looking for the second suspect, 20-year-old Abdulla Rasheen Thompson. If you have any information on his whereabouts please call CrimeStoppers at 234-2020. A reward is being offered.

Reported by: Katie Pike, kpike@wtoc.com
