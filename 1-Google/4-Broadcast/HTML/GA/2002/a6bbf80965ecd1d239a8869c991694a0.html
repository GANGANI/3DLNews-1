Mikelya Fournier

Multi-Platform Producer
Albany, Ga.

Mikelya Fournier joined the WALB News 10 team in August 2022 as a multi-platform news producer. She’s a native of Albany, where she attended Albany High School and Albany State University.

Mikelya has previous experience as a freelance journalist/contributor and content marketing specialist for major media sites where she developed stories on fashion, beauty and home living. Her specialties include trend reports, live video content production and making an impact through storytelling

As a wife and mother of four, Mikelya leads a quiet yet robust lifestyle in Southwest Georgia. In her free time, she enjoys traveling, cooking, gardening, reading, writing and hiking.
