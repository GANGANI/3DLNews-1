7 hospitalized after incident at Japanese steak house in Florida (WEAR)

SANTA ROSA COUNTY, Fla. (WEAR) — Seven people were taken to the hospital Saturday following an incident at a Japanese steak house in Florida.

"All are currently receiving treatment at local hospitals," the Santa Rosa County Sheriff's Office said in a release. "This is an active and ongoing investigation."

While information has been circulating online that the situation at the Nikko Japanese Steak House and Sushi Bar in Pace may involve narcotics, authorities have not yet confirmed those details with WEAR.

The sheriff's office added that the Division of Alcoholic Beverages and Tobacco is also conducting its own independent investigation.
