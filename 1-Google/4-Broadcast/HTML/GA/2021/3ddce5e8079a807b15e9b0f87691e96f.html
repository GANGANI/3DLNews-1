Middle TN woman claims 'maybe an ounce' of marijuana on farm, officers find 40 plants (15th Judicial Drug Task Force)

JACKSON COUNTY, Tenn. (WZTV) — A Middle Tennessee woman told investigators there was "maybe an ounce" of marijuana on her farm before officers found dozens of pot plants and 20 pounds of the drug.

The 15th Judicial Drug Task Force identified the woman as Peggy Brewington. Authorities say Brewington was arrested on a trespassing charge Monday, closing out a month-long investigation.

Investigators then searched the Brewington farm and questioned Brewington about the amount of drugs on her property.

Officers ask Ms. Brewington how much marijuana was in the residence? She responded 'maybe about an ounce.'

Officers report they ended up recovering 20 pounds of marijuana from the home and 40 marijuana plants on the property. A search of a nearby residence yielded more growing and processed marijuana, the drug task force said.

The case will be presented to the Jackson County Grand Jury.

The Tennessee Bureau of Investigation and Tennessee Highway Patrol assisted in the investigation.
