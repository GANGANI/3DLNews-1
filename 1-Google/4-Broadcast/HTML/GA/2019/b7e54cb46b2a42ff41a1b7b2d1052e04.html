TELEMUNDO ATLANTA CELEBRATES A DECADE OF UNSTOPPABLE GROWTH AND IMPACT

Por Redacción de Noticias Telemundo Atlanta
Actualizado: 25 oct 2019, 22:48 GMT-4

ATLANTA, Georgia (Telemundo Atlanta) - Telemundo Atlanta marked their 10 year Anniversary by holding a private cocktail reception followed by a night at the opera with long-time clients, community partners, and team members.  The milestone celebration took place on Wednesday, October 9, 2019 at the Sandy Springs Performing Arts Center where the Atlanta Opera’s highly acclaimed production of Frida was showcased to a full house.

Telemundo Atlanta officially launched as a local station in the Atlanta DMA in 2009 and relaunched as the New Telemundo Atlanta in 2013 with a local newscast, more expansive full power coverage area, and the hire of a general manager.  It was at the relaunch event on May 2, 2013 that predicted Telemundo Atlanta would not only be the New Telemundo Atlanta, but that it would be number one.

“What a great achievement for Telemundo to be the number one Spanish Network in primetime for three consecutive years. It takes great effort from a national and local level to pull off such unstoppable growth,” said general manager, Ivan Shammas.

“Telemundo Atlanta is elated to celebrate the exponential growth over the last ten years in primetime ratings, digital capabilities, news offerings and overall impact in the community through signature events and powerful community partnerships. All of this success was possible because we had the unwavering support of our clients, community partners, staff and Telemundo Network,” said managing partner, Susan Sim Oh.

Telemundo Atlanta is a main player in the Spanish media landscape in the Atlanta DMA and has accomplished many milestones over the last decade.

• Telemundo has been the number one Spanish network in primetime among adults 18–34 and 18-49 resulting from fresh, new story lines in cinematic quality, news that resonates with the growing Hispanic audience, and prominent entertainment and sports properties.

• Since 2012, Telemundo Atlanta has garnered 41 Emmy® Awards by the Southeast Chapter of the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences (NATAS), with the highly prominent distinction of Best Newscast and News Excellence Emmy Awards for the last three consecutive years.

• Telemundo Atlanta was the first local Spanish-language television station to consecutively garner the coveted ‘Overall Station Excellence’ Emmy® Award by the National Academy of Arts and Sciences in the Southeast region in 2013, 2014, 2016 and 2017.

• Telemundo Atlanta is the only Spanish-language media outlet in Atlanta with a locally dedicated digital marketing team handling online presence, paid media campaigns, strategy, analytics and social media campaigns for new and existing clients.

• Telemundo Atlanta has the most local Spanish news than any other media outlet in the Atlanta DMA. The station airs 3 newscasts a day, Monday through Friday at 5:30pm, 6pm, and 11pm.

• Telemundo Atlanta hosted 165 families during NaviDando, a signature event where families in need are provided a night of merriment for the entire family. Presents for the entire family, food and entertainment are given in an effort to lift spirits and share the magic of Christmas among families who may be experiencing hardship.

• 36 Local heroes were honored at Todos Somos Heroes (We Are All Heroes), an annual Telemundo Atlanta event held during Hispanic Heritage Month, where local unsung heroes are recognized for their contributions to the community.
