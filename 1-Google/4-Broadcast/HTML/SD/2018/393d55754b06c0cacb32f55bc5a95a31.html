KOTA Territory News “Live Newscasts” and “Newscasts On Demand”

To watch live newscasts from KOTA Territory, click the associated tab where you can watch local news live, presented by journalists who live and work in the community.

Live Newscasts provide LIVE coverage from LOCAL journalists at any of Gray Media’s more than 110 television stations, including KOTA.

LIVE Newscasts Stream Schedule:

Monday - Friday from 5:30 a.m. to 7:00 a.m.

Monday – Friday 12:00 p.m. to 12:30 p.m.

Monday – Sun 5:30 p.m to 6:00 p.m.

Monday - Sunday 10:00 p.m. to 10:30 p.m.

In between our Live Newscasts, you can watch Newscasts On Demand content any time that is convenient for you.

To watch Newscasts On Demand simply click the associated tab and browse through KOTA Territory’s recent newscasts.
