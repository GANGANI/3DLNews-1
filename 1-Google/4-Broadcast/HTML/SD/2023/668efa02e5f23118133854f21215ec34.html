Ezra Garcia

Digital Content Producer

Ezra is the Digital Content Manager/Producer at KOTA Territory News and KEVN Black Hills Fox.

A native to Los Angeles, they grew up with an interest in crime. They went to California State University Northridge and graduated with honors in the spring of 2022. Ezra has a degree in Criminology and a minor in English. They hope to someday work for the Federal Bureau of Investigation and serve the people.

In moving to beautiful Rapid City, South Dakota, they are excited to be a Gray Television employee. They hope to bring KOTA and KEVN up to speed with their social media output. Catch them on the KOTA Territory TikTok!
