Lynchburg Police arrest man for fatal shooting

By Kaitlyn Dillon and Sophia Borrelli
Published: Feb. 9, 2024 at 6:23 PM EST

LYNCHBURG, Va. (WDBJ) - A man has been arrested for a fatal shooting Friday.

Lynchburg Police have arrested Dabney Thomas, Jr., and charged him with second-degree murder, reckless handling of a firearm, use of a firearm in the commission of a felony and discharge a firearm in the city limits.

Officers arrived in the 2600 block of Morson Street just after 3:30 p.m. Friday, and found Carroll Mack, 62, with a gunshot wound. He died after being taken to a hospital.

Anyone with information about this incident is asked to contact Det. Bond at (434) 455-6161 or Crime Stoppers at (888) 798-5900. Enter a tip online at p3tips.com or use the P3 app on a mobile device.
