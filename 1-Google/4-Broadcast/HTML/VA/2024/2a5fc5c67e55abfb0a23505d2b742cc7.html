Baby zebra dies after running into fence

By 13 News Staff and Debra Worley
Published: Feb. 8, 2024 at 2:56 PM EST

TUCSON, Ariz. (KOLD/Gray News) – A baby Zebra born at Reid Park Zoo in Arizona on Christmas Day died Tuesday.

Zoo officials said a gust of wind appeared to startle the foal’s mother.

She moved abruptly into a different portion of the habitat and stopped at the wood-paneled fence, but her foal, Tikiti Maji, did not stop and crashed into it.

Veterinary staff responded immediately, but Tikiti Maji died instantly from a neck injury.

Zebras can become startled easily, and young zebras have a high mortality rate.

Reid Park Zoo lost a male foal in July 2020 in a similar event.
