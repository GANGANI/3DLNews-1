Donald J. Trump chosen as Washington and Lee Mock Convention winner

by Rachel Tillapaugh

Sun, February 11th 2024 at 11:14 AM
Donald J. Trump predicted to be 2024 Republican Nominee. (Credit: Rachel Tillapaugh/WSET)

LEXINGTON, Va. (WSET) — The Washington and Lee Mock Convention chooses a winner for each presidential nomination cycle.

For this year's convention, the Mock Convention political department predicted that Donald J. Trump will be the 2024 Republican Nominee for President.

They said Trump will win 2,353 out of 2,429 total delegates.

Representative Elise Stefanik was predicted to be the Vice Presidential Nominee.

RELATED: Gov. Glenn Youngkin, Donald Trump Jr. speak at Washington & Lee Mock Convention

According to the convention's Press Secretary Addie-Grace Cook, the predictions are made by using a variety of tools.

"Mock Convention’s prediction is made using a robust, data-driven model that combines state and national poll crosstab analysis and aggregates with modeled and actual GOP likely voter demographic breakdowns for each state and relevant congressional district. The political team backed the data up by extensive political outreach, talking to GOP leaders from every state and members from every campaign team on the trail," Addie-Grace Cook said.

Mock Convention also boasts an accuracy of 100% for Republican cycles.
