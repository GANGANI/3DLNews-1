Sports Reporter

Brooke Leonard is the newest addition to the 10 Sports team, joining in June 2019.

A graduate of Lord Botetourt High School, she earned her college degree from Virginia Tech.

After graduating, Brooke moved to Bozeman, Montana to work for the CBS affiliate as a sports reporter but is excited to return home to the valley.

In her free time, Brooke likes to teach cycling, hike, ski and spend time on any body of water.
