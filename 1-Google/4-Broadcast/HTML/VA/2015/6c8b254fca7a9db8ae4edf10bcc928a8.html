Body pulled from truck that drove off dock into James River

By Kelly Avellino
Published: Jul. 9, 2015 at 9:09 AM EDT|Updated: Jul. 9, 2015 at 4:31 PM EDT

RICHMOND, VA (WWBT) - A death investigation is underway after a pickup truck drives off a dock at the James River by Rocketts Landing.

Richmond Police confirm a male body was found inside the pickup truck. They have pulled the body from the truck and are now working to remove the truck from the river.

The truck was in a parking lot and plowed through the gate and off the platform into the river around 2:30 a.m. Thursday.

Police are working to identify the body and notify the family.
