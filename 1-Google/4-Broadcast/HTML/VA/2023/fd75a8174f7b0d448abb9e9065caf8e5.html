Noah Harrison

Daybreak Anchor/Producer

Noah is from Montgomery, Alabama. She graduated from Huntingdon College in 2020 with degrees in English and Communication Studies. In May 2022, she graduated with an MS in Media with a journalism concentration from Indiana University-Bloomington.

Noah got her start in sports media, working with IUSTV-Sports, as an anchor and beat reporter for IU football and men’s basketball. She sideline reported for Big Ten Network Student U, covering women’s volleyball, softball, and baseball.

Additionally, Noah worked in the Arnolt Center for Investigative Journalism partnering with Gray TV’s national investigative team.

Noah loves being around friends and family, the Boston Celtics, trying new restaurants, learning to play new sports, and attending every sporting event possible.
