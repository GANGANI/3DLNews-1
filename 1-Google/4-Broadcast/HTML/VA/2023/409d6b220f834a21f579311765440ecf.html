VIDEO: Suspect wanted in breaking and entering incidents across the New River Valley

by Caitlyn Frolo

Tue, August 29th 2023 at 5:05 PM
6
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
The Pulaski County Sheriff's Office has shared a video of the suspect wanted in several breaking and entering incidents in the area. (Credit: Pulaski County Sheriff's Office)

PULASKI COUNTY, Va. (WSET) — The Pulaski County Sheriff's Office has shared a video of the suspect wanted in several breaking and entering incidents in the area.

The video shared on Tuesday is from an area business.

"The employees were just trying to be decent human beings and help out someone in need that they had no idea had done the things he had done," the Sheriff's Office said.

RELATED: Carroll County deputies looking for wanted individual

In the video, you can hear the suspect asking for water.

The Sheriff's Office said he is currently known to be traveling between, Pulaski, Wythe, and Carroll Counties along the New River Trail. They said there will be an increased law enforcement presence in these areas to include Game Wardens and State Parks Police.

RELATED: Pulaski Co. deputies seeks public's help in identifying breaking and entering suspect

This individual is last known to be wearing camouflage clothing and has stolen firearms from a cabin in Wythe County, the Sheriff's Office said.

If you see anything suspicious, please call 911 immediately and report it.
