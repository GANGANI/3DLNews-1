Christopher Jones

Account Executive

Christopher B. Jones is the most senior advertising account executive here at WHSV. In addition, he represents the people of the Friendly City as a member of the Harrisonburg City Council.

He became a resident of Harrisonburg during his academic career at James Madison University in 1996. In May 2000, he graduated from JMU with a bachelor’s in sociology and a minor in business.

His civic involvement, in addition to City Council, includes serving on the Harrisonburg-Rockingham Chamber of Commerce Board and the Harrisonburg Downtown Renaissance Board. He loves helping local and regional businesses thrive!

Mr. Jones has received numerous awards during his career. In 2020, he received the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Community Service Award presented by Alpha Phi Alpha, Inc. for his contributions to the betterment of the City of Harrisonburg and Rockingham County, Virginia; and the Harrisonburg Rockingham Chamber of Commerce Diversity in Business leadership award for leadership in the business community.

Above all Mr. Jones is a husband, father of four, and a proud member of Divine Unity Community Church and the Omega Psi Phi, Fraternity Incorporated.
