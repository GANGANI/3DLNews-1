More than 4,000 without power in Lynchburg area

by Brenna McIntosh

Sat, October 16th 2021 at 3:04 PM
Updated Sat, October 16th 2021 at 3:06 PM
ABC 13 Breaking News

LYNCHBURG, Va. (WSET) — More than 4,000 people in the Lynchburg area are without power, according to Appalachian Power.

The main areas affected are Sandusky and Richland Hills.

The cause of the outages is unknown at the time.

According to Appalachian Power, power should be restored by 9:30 p.m.
