Special honors for News 5 WCYB

by WCYB

Sat, November 6th 2021 at 4:17 PM
6
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
From left to right: Caleb Perhne, Kristen Quon and Heather Williams (WCYB photo)

The News 5 WCYB team is pleased to announce we won five awards for outstanding content, coverage and presentation from the Virginias Associated Press Broadcasters.

The yearly awards are presented to news organizations in Virginia and West Virginia.

The News 5 team won an award for Best Continuing Coverage for the Evelyn Boswell case.

Our 30-minute documentary on Evelyn Boswell was awarded Best Documentary and In-Depth reporting by Kristen Quon and the News 5 team.

Heather Williams was awarded Best TV Sports Anchor.

News 5’s Caleb Perhne received second place honors for Best Investigative Reporting and Best Serious News Feature for his reporting related to the COVID-19 pandemic.

“I am extremely proud of our News 5 Team for their commitment to journalistic excellence in the Tri-Cities region,” said WCYB News Director, Keith Weiss. “Receiving these special honors of distinction from our broadcast journalism peers is truly rewarding and inspiring, as we make it our mission at News 5 WCYB to showcase unique and impactful, point-of difference content on all platforms for our viewers. The year 2020 proved to be particularly challenging as a news organization, especially with the COVID-19 pandemic.”

The awards were presented at a special ceremony this weekend at the Greebrier Resort in West Virginia.
