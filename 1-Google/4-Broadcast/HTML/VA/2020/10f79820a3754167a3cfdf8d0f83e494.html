Dozens in Lebanon, Virginia participate in 'Justice for George Floyd' rally

by WCYB Staff

Sat, May 30th 2020 at 6:38 PM
Updated Sun, May 31st 2020 at 11:05 AM
12
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
(Photo Courtesy of Dustin Keith)

LEBANON, Va. (WCYB) — Approximately 50 peaceful protestors lined the streets of Lebanon Saturday afternoon. They gathered while wearing masks and holding signs saying "Justice for George Floyd" and "Black Lives Matter."

The heartbreak and devastation felt across the country follows Floyd's police-involved death in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
