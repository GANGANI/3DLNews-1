Bryant Reed

Anchor, reporter
Rocky Hill, CT

Bryant Reed joined WFSB in October of 2022 as the Weekend Evening Anchor and reports in the field during weekdays.

He comes to Connecticut from Pittsburgh where he was a morning reporter and earned an Emmy for Breaking News coverage.

A native of North Carolina, Bryant continues to move further up the East Coast to tell impactful news stories and ingratiate himself to New England.

Community concerns? Questions? Just want to chat? Feel free to email her at bryant.reed@wfsb.com.

In 2016, Bryant had the opportunity of a lifetime to intern with NBC at the Olympics in Brazil.

Bryant has a few family members already in the media so journalism runs in his blood!

You’ll always be able to find him out with friends and taking in all Connecticut has to offer. If you have any story ideas, don’t hesitate to reach out! Email Bryant at bryant.reed@wfsb.com.
