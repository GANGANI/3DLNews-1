Good Morning. We are off to a bit of a chilly start with most of yesterday's heat having completely been lost to lack of cloud cover. Today, our cold air mass overhead is rapidly replaced with a warm one as high pressure builds. As soon as the sun rises, the sunshine will be plentiful and unending. With the shift in air masses, the afternoon will be breezy with 15-20 MPH winds popping up widespread and even faster gusts over the Coast. Temps will be above average today with a high of 65 along the Coast, and mid to low 70's inland. The warm and dry conditions are set to continue tomorrow with temps peaking before cooler and wet weather returns for the weekend and early next week. 

-Meteorologist Cameron Landfield
