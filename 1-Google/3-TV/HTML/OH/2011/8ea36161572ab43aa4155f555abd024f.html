We have had several people ask, “What exactly is the Central Ohio Association of Christian Broadcasters (COACB)?”  Well, we are a non-profit Christian organization that owns and operates 4 Christian television stations.  These stations are WXCB TV-25 Delaware, WGCT TV-8 Columbus, WQIZ TV-33 Ashland, and WOCB TV-22 in Marion, Ohio.
