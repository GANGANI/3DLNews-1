Name released in deadly McLean Co. crash

By Aaron Chatman
Published: Jan. 30, 2024 at 6:28 AM CST

MCLEAN CO., Ky. (WFIE) - Officials say a deadly accident early Tuesday morning completely shut down the Livermore Bridge in McLean County.

Kentucky State Police say 54-year old Angela Gay Johnson, of Island, died in a head-on collision involving two vehicles on the bridge.

Troopers say a 19-year-old driver lost control of his car and slid into Johnson’s.

14 News spoke with dispatch and tells us that the crash was called in shortly after 5:30 a.m.

Kentucky State Police tells us troopers and an accident reconstructionist were called to that area.

Officials say ice covered the bridge Tuesday morning.

“We’ve had accidents on this bridge before,” said McLean County Sheriff Ken Frizzell. “It’s an unfortunate situation, but when people are out on the roadways and vehicles are traveling, and you add in just a bit of inclement weather, there was some significant ice on the bridge this morning. Unfortunately these things happen.”

Around 12 p.m., we learned that the bridge reopened to the public.

We will keep you updated on-air and online with the latest information.
