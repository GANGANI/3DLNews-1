Man killed in quadruple shooting in Russell neighborhood was 22-years-old

By Julia Huffman
Published: Feb. 1, 2024 at 5:22 PM EST

LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WAVE) - The Jefferson County coroner has released the name of the man killed in a shooting that left three others in critical condition.

The shooting happened around 12:30 p.m. at South 12th St & W Muhammad Ali Blvd, not far from nearby schools. Police said four men were shot, one of them killed.

He was identified as 22-year-old Corey Dillard.

UofL says the other three men remain in critical condition.
