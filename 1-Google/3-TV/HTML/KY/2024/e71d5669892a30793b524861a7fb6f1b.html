Better Call Charles: Bob Odenkirk discovers he’s related to the King of England

By CNN Newsource staff
Published: Jan. 31, 2024 at 4:36 PM EST

(CNN) – Bob Odenkirk has a reason to be “royally” amused.

The star of “Better Call Saul” found out he is an eleventh cousin of King Charles.

The discovery was made during Odenkirk’s appearance on PBS’ “Finding Your Roots” program.

Odenkirk said the news makes him feel like he’s a part of history.

Despite the actor-writer’s link to Buckingham Palace, Odenkirk joked he prefers democracy over royalty.
