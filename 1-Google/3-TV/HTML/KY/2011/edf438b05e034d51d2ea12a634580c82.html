Police: Deer in road caused deadly school bus crash

Published: Jun. 10, 2011 at 12:28 AM EDT|Updated: Jun. 17, 2011 at 12:52 AM EDT

Posted by Megan Kean - email

BARDWELL, KY (WAVE) – A police report matches up with the story of a Carlisle County school bus driver who told Kentucky State Police she was trying to avoid a deer when her bus flipped.

The bus driver said she tried to steer toward the deer in an attempt to miss it, thinking that it would cross the road. Instead, the deer took off and the bus tumbled off the right side of the road into a ditch.

The May 16 crash killed six-year-old Logan Simpson, and injured 15 other students.
