CONCERNS ABOUT PUBLIC SAFETY

Since 2016, over 120 carriage related incidents occurred placing both the public and carriage animal safety at risk.

Public SafetyCharleston.Carriage.Horse.Advocates2022-10-25T09:59:16-04:00

We Want to Hear from You!

In August 2021 alone, Charleston Carriage Horse Advocates reached 206,882 folks through social media. Recently, when a horse fell to the pavement on Queen Street (see photo) many people jumped onto the Next Door Neighbors app to voice concerns about the plight of these horses and mules in Charleston. CCHA was there with clarifying, truthful comments to counteract misinformation spread by carriage industry supporters. Local citizens are becoming increasingly aware of the working and living conditions of these voiceless animals and they are speaking out.

We want to hear your opinions on the Carriage Horse issue. Help us by responding to this survey. Thanks!
