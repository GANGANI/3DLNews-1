Smell ya later! Dashcam video shows good Samaritan helping skunk trapped in a cup

By Lindsey Grewe and Gray News staff
Published: Feb. 2, 2024 at 1:36 AM EST|Updated: Feb. 2, 2024 at 1:37 AM EST

COLORADO SPRINGS, Colo. (KKTV/Gray News) - This story doesn’t stink: a lucky skunk was rescued from a busy road by a good Samaritan in Colorado Springs.

A driver and their friend shared a dashcam video with KKTV of the rescue.

The pair says they were traveling on South Union Boulevard near Highway 24 on Monday when they saw a skunk struggling with a cup stuck over its head, moving dangerously close to speeding traffic.

The driver stopped the vehicle slightly away from the skunk, got out of the vehicle and pulled the cup off the skunk’s nose.

The driver then quickly moved away from the skunk, possibly to avoid getting sprayed, while the skunk ran off the side of the road to safety.

Thankfully, both the driver and the skunk seem to be OK after the encounter.
