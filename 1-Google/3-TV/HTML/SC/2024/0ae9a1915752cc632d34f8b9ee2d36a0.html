Lexington County Coroner identifies body in death investigation along Interstate 20

By Jalen Tart
Published: Feb. 2, 2024 at 12:01 PM EST

LEXINGTON COUNTY, S.C. (WIS) - The Lexington County Sheriff’s Department (LCSD) is investigating after a body was found Friday morning on the side of Interstate-20.

Shortly after 7 p.m. Friday evening, the Lexington County Coroner Margaret Fisher identified the body as Stephanie Chanel Quintana, 22.

Investigators were on scene most of Friday morning near mile-marker 62 on the westbound side just before Sunset Boulevard, where the body was found.

The investigation led to lengthy traffic delays with vehicles backed up to Bush River Rd.

WIS News 10 first learned of the investigation when viewers reached out with news tips around 9 a.m. Friday. The body was found on a grassy area near Exit 61.

A large police presence was visible at around 10 a.m. Friday. The South Carolina Highway Patrol (SCHP), LCSD and the Coroner’s Office were all at the scene.

An autopsy is scheduled for Monday, Feb. 5, at MUSC in Charleston. The cause and manner of death remain under investigation by SCHP and the Coroner’s Office.
