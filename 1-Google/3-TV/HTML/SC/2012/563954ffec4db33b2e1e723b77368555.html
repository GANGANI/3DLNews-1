Half million dollars found after traffic stop

Published: Jun. 6, 2012 at 8:01 PM EDT

According to the Sumter Sheriff's Office, around 9:34 a.m., on May 10, deputies stopped a Dodge Charger with a Florida license plate near mile marker 140 of I-95.
