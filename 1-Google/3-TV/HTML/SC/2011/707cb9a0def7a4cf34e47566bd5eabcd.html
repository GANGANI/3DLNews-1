Tracking more sunshine and lower humidity into the weekend!

Updated: 7 hours ago

CHARLESTON, S.C. (WCSC) - High pressure will remain in control as we head through the rest of the week and into the weekend! We will see plenty of sunshine for the rest of the day with temperatures in the mid to upper 80s inland and lower 80s along the coast. We’ll stay dry through the end of the week as deep moisture remains off to our west. Rain chances should remain low this weekend, with just a slight chance of a shower or storm along the sea breeze beginning Sunday. Even this chance is low though, which will be a nice change from last week/weekend. Afternoon highs will remain in the 80s! Our current area of high pressure will slide offshore on Sunday, which will increase our humidity and the rain chance later into early next week.
