Little Mr. and Little Miss crowned at annual Cherry Blossom Pageant

by Ereina Plunkett

Sun, February 13th 2022 at 7:21 PM
Updated Sun, February 13th 2022 at 8:57 AM
Photo Source/Ereina Plunkett

MACON, Ga. (WGXA) --With the Cherry Blossom parade around the corner, parents filled Porter Auditorium at Wesleyan College for the Little Mr. and Little Miss Pageant.

Five through seven-year-olds across Middle Georgia competed for the coveted title of Mr. and Miss Cherry Blossom.

In the 40th anniversary of selecting and celebrating Cherry Blossom royalty, children competed at 10 a.m.

Contestants answered a series of questions to show their personalities and their outfits.

At the end of the pageant, winners, Olivia Grigley of Lakeview Primary School and Andy Parkhurst of St. Andrews Montessori School were crowned.
