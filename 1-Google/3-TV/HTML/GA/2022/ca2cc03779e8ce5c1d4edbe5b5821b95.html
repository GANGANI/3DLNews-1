Kim McCullough

Digital Content Executive Producer, "Binge It!" Creator/Writer

Kim is the digital content executive producer at WALB. She joined the team in December 2017 after a semester-long internship at the station. Kim has worked in different departments such as production, digital, and producing. She also works part-time as an Adjunct Professor in the Social Sciences department at Albany State University.

In 2010, Kim graduated from Monroe High School with a Georgia Work Ready Certificate in business. In December 2017, she graduated from Albany State University with a Bachelor of Arts in Mass Communications.

While at ASU, she anchored and reported for "Ram News Network", the school's student-run newscast. She is also a member of Zeta Phi Beta Sorority and Alpha Phi Omega National Service Fraternity. In December 2019, she graduated from Full Sail University with a Master's of Arts degree in New Media Journalism.

When not working, Kim likes to participate in events and services with her sorority's local chapter and spend free time with her son, Jason.
