Students evacuated after bomb threat called into Southwest High School, BCSD says

by Claire Helm

Tue, December 14th 2021 at 12:19 PM
Updated Tue, December 14th 2021 at 2:37 PM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Source: WGXA News

MACON, Ga. -- Students at one Bibb County high school were evacuated due to a bomb threat Tuesday morning.

A spokesperson for the school district said the alleged threat was called into Southwest High School by phone.

First responders made it to the scene and determined it was safe for students to go back inside.

The Bibb County Sheriff's Office said Bibb deputies, BOE campus police, Macon-Bibb firefighters, and the sheriff's office bomb unit all responded.

Officials said all students and staff were safely evacuated and that parents were notified through Remind.

After law enforcement determined that the scene was safe, students, staff, and faculty were permitted back inside the school.
