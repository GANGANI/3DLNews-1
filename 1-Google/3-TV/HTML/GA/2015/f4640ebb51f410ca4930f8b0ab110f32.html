UPDATE | Third suspect indicted in connection to 2015 murder outside of Teleperformance building

By Lindsay Tuman, Staff
Published: Dec. 10, 2015 at 6:21 PM EST

Thursday, Sept. 7, 2017

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW/WAGT) -- Suspects arrested in the 2015 murder outside of the Teleperformance building in Augusta have been indicted.

Jesse Daniel Jackson, Rashaun Terez Gordon and Terrell Marquise Yarbary are all indicted with murder, armed robbery and possession of a firearm during the commission of a crime.

The suspects are accused in the robbery and murder of Randy Ramos, 29, in the parking lot of Teleperformance in December. 2015.

Sunday, June 25, 2017

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW/WAGT) -- Richmond County deputies have arrested a third person in connection to a murder from 2015.

Deputies say information and leads helped them arrest Jesse Daniel Jackson, 29, in connection to the murder of Randy Justin Ramos.

Jackson's last known address is the 2500 Block of Meadowbrook Drive in Augusta, Ga.

Georgia State Patrol in Morgan County arrested Jackson on June 24, 2017.

He's charged with murder, armed robbery, and weapons charges.

In 2015 deputies arrested Marquise Terrell Yarbary, 26, and Tarez R. Gordon, 20, in connection to this crime. Yarbary and Gordon are both facing two counts of felony murder, armed robbery, and the possession of a weapon during a crime.

The suspects are accused in the robbery and murder of Randy Ramos, 29, in the parking lot of Teleperformance in Dec. 2015.

Wednesday, March 2, 2016

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW/WAGT) -- Suspects arrested in the 2015 murder outside of the Teleperformance building in Augusta have been indicted on murder, armed robbery and weapons charges.

Marquise Terrell Yarbary and Tarez Rashaun Gordon are both facing two counts of felony murder, armed robbery, and the possession of a weapon during a crime.

The suspects are accused in the robbery and murder of Randy Ramos, 29, in the parking lot of Teleperformance in Dec. 2015.

Friday, Jan. 29, 2016

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- A judge denies bond for an 18-year-old facing charges in the deadly 2015 shooting outside of the Teleperformance building in Augusta.

Tarez Gordon was denied bond in court. Gordon was arrested Dec. 19 in connection to the shooting death of Randy Ramos. He is charged with murder, armed robbery and the possession of a firearm during the commission of a crime.

Meanwhile, The Richmond County Sheriff’s Office is hoping a stolen ring from a murder victim could help them find new leads into the investigation. Authorities say a gold nugget men's ring may have been stolen from Ramos after he was killed.

A cash reward is being offered to anyone who has information that leads to an arrest or recovery of the ring. Anyone with information is asked to contact the Criminal Investigations Division, Violent Crimes Unit at 706-821-1020. Callers may remain anonymous.

UPDATE | Wednesday, Jan. 27, 2015

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- The Richmond County Sheriff’s Office is hoping a stolen ring from a murder victim could help them find new leads into the investigation.

Investigators say a gold nugget men's ring may have been stolen from Randy Ramos after he was killed in the shooting outside of the Teleperformance behind the Augusta mall in December.

A cash reward is being offered to anyone who has information that leads to an arrest or recovery of the ring. Anyone with information is asked to contact the Criminal Investigations Division, Violent Crimes Unit at 706-821-1020. Callers may remain anonymous.

UPDATE | Saturday, Dec. 19, 2015

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- The Richmond County Sheriffs Office made a second arrest in the murder of Randy Ramos.

According to the Richmond County Sheriff’s Office, Investigators and members of the Crime Suppression Team arrested Tarez Gordon at his home on the 2600 Block of Shalimar Drive in Augusta around 10:00 Friday night.

Gordon has been charged with the murder of Randy Ramos who was shot and killed on Dec. 10th in the Teleperformance parking lot on Wylds Rd. Gordon is also being charged with Armed Robbery, and Possession of a Firearm during the Commission of a Crime.

Sheriff’s Office investigators say they are continuing the investigation and working to identify additional suspects involved in the incident.

UPDATE | Tuesday, Dec. 15, 2015

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- A murder suspect will appear in court Wednesday.

The Richmond County magistrate court said Marquise Terrell Yarbary's first appearance is scheduled for 2 p.m.

Yarbary is accused in the murder of Randy Ramos, who was shot and killed Dec. 10 in the parking lot of Teleperformance, located just behind the Augusta Mall.

Investigators say they are still collecting evidence to identify additional suspects involved in the shooting.

Monday, Dec. 14, 2015

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- Richmond County deputies have arrested a man accused in a deadly shooting in a call center parking lot.

According to a news release, Marquise Terrell Yarbary has been charged with the murder of Randy Ramos.

Investigators said they are collecting more evidence and are working to identify additional suspects involved in the shooting.

Ramos was shot and killed on December 10, 2015 in the parking lot of Teleperformance on Wylds Road. The call center is located behind the Augusta Mall in the old Waccamaw building.

If you have any information about this deadly shooting, call the Sheriff's Office at (706) 821-1020 or (706) 821-1080. You can be anonymous.

News 12 @ 11 o'clock / Thursday, Dec. 10, 2015

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- The Richmond County Sheriff's Office says they found the 1999 Crown Victoria three suspects drove away in after shooting an Augusta man.

Workers at Teleperformance tell News 12, they ended their workday to the sound of gunfire. Deputies say two suspects shot and killed 29-year-old Randy Ramos in a parking lot right behind the Augusta Mall.

Kaalynn Tate walked out of her job at Teleperformance, to find a crime scene. "I was actually by chance outside when it happened and that's when I heard the gunshots," Tate said.

Investigators say two suspects fired the shots around 5:30 Thursday night, just as workers were heading to their cars. "I didn't know it was our parking lot. I parked closer and I saw the police reviving him and that's when I realized that he died," she said.

One of those gunshots hit and killed, 29-year-old Randy Ramos, one of Kaalynn's coworkers. "I didn't know him personally but I did see him every single day. He seemed to be a nice person," she tells News 12.

Deputies say Ramos was walking to his car when two men confronted him, there was an altercation, and Ramos was shot. The suspects took off in this car, a silver Crown Victoria with a black bumper and no hubcaps. A third man was driving.

The Richmond County Sheriff's Office says at this point, this does not appear to be a random crime and it looks like the suspects were targeting Ramos. Nonetheless, it has some employees on edge. "Some people are talking about not coming in tomorrow and I don't really blame them. Some people just don't feel safe," she said.

Kaalynn says this is a reminder that you never know what can happen, even right where you work. "I just saw him today, and even though I didn't know him that well, I didn't think that would be my last time seeing him," Tate said.

The last time for her, his friends, and his family. "Just thinking about his family. Instead of celebrating the holidays they will be mourning his loss," she said.

Deputies say the suspects behind this murder are armed, and obviously dangerous. Right now the sheriff's office does not have a good description of any of the three suspects. If you have any information about this deadly shooting, call the Richmond County Sheriff's Office.

Thursday, Dec. 10, 2015

UDPATE: 8:55 p.m.

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- The Richmond County Coroner's Office has identified the man killed in a shooting in the Teleperformance parking lot.

According to Kenneth Boose, Richmond County Chief Deputy Coroner, the victim is Randy Ramos, 29, from Augusta. Boose said Ramos was shot one time and died at 6:13 p.m. at an Augusta hospital.

Boose said an autopsy on Ramos' body will be done at the GBI Crime Lab in Atlanta.

UPDATE: 8:15 p.m.

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- Richmond County deputies say person has died after a shooting in the parking lot of a call center near the Augusta Mall.

According to a news release, investigators are "aggressively" searching for a 1999 Ford Crown Victoria car they say is connected to the homicide Thursday afternoon.

Deputies provided us with a picture of the vehicle. It is silver with a black front grille and a black front bumper cover. It has no hubcaps and appears to have tinted tail lights.

Deputies responded to the parking lot of the Teleperformance call center around 5:30 p.m.

Investigators have not made an arrest, but told News 12 the scene is secure.

The person who died has not been identified.

News 12 is working to get more information and will pass new details along as they are released.

UPDATE: 7:05 p.m.

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- The Richmond County Sheriff's Office is on the scene of a shooting in the parking lot of a call center near the Augusta Mall.

According to deputies, one person was shot around 5:30 p.m. Thursday near the entrance to Teleperformance on Wylds Road.

Deputies have not made an arrest, but told News 12 the scene is secure.

Investigators said the victim has life-threatening injuries. The victim has not been identified.

News 12 has a crew on the scene. We will continue to keep you updated on this developing story.

AUGUSTA, Ga. (WRDW) -- Richmond County Sheriff's Office is on the scene of a shooting at a call center near the Augusta Mall.

Dispatch said one person was injured after reports of shots fired near Teleperformance on Wylds Road. The call came in at 5:35 p.m.

News 12 has a crew on the way to the scene. We will continue to keep you updated on this developing story.
