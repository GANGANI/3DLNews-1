New year, new you: Chiropractors share health tips going into 2024

New year, new you: Chiropractors share health tips going into 2024 Chiropractors Foseph Fowler Jr. and Brandon Williams with Aligned 4 Life Wellness came to Atlanta News First to talk about how to live a healthier life.

By Atlanta News First staff
January 10, 2024 at 11:20 am EST

ATLANTA, Ga. (Atlanta News First) - According to a Forbes poll, nearly half of Americans say improving their fitness is a top priority in 2024 — but that’s easier said than done.

Chiropractors Joseph Fowler Jr. and Brandon Williams with Aligned 4 Life Wellness came to Atlanta News First to talk about how to live a healthier life. They also shared details about what chiropractic practices can do for you.
