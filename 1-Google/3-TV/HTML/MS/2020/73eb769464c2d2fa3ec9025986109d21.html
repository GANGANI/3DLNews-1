Jeffrey Morgan

Multimedia Journalist
Moselle, MS

Jeffrey Morgan joined the WDAM News team in June of 2019 as a Multimedia Journalist. Jeffrey graduated from Penn State University in May of 2019 with a degree in Broadcast Journalism.

He is a proud native of Pennsylvania and enjoys cheering on his Philadelphia sports teams when he can find them on TV here in Mississippi.

In his free time, Jeff enjoys playing golf and going to the gym. Jeffrey has spent time working in Minor League Baseball and for Penn State Athletics.

This is his first time in Mississippi, so if you see him out in the area, feel free to say hello!

If you have a story you think Jeffrey should know about, feel free to email him at jmorgan@wdam.com
