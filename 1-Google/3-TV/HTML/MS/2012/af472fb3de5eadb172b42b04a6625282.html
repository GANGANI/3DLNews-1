Fourth Greene County teen dies from injuries received in Wednesday accident

Published: Jan. 19, 2012 at 9:23 PM CST|Updated: Jan. 19, 2012 at 10:00 PM CST

A fourth teen has died from injuries suffered in a one vehicle accident Wednesday morning in Greene County.

Seventeen year old Joseph Lister died Thursday afternoon at USA Medical Center in Mobile after suffering severe injuries to the head in the crash. After consulting with doctors, the family made the decision to remove Lister from life support.

The crash also took the life of three other Greene County teens, Kodi Henderson, 15, Dillion Reed, 16 and Steven Nicholson, 15. A fifth, Russell Green, 17, remains in stable condition at USA Medical Center.

All four of the deceased as well as Green were students at Greene County High School.
