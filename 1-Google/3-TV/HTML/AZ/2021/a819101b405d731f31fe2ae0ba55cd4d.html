Celebrate Hispanic Heritage
Hispanic Heritage Month is celebrated annually from September 15 - October 15 and recognizes the contributions and influence of Hispanic Americans to the history, culture, and achievements of the United States.
Tile artist Carlos Valenzuela in front of his Tonali Mural in South Tucson. Watch the full Arizona Illustrated story here.
