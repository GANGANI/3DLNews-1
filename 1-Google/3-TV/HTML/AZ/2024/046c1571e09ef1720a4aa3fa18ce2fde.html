One dead and three others injured in midtown crash

TUCSON, Ariz. (KVOA) — Officers from the Tucson Police Department responded to a fatal crash in central Tucson.

TPD says officers and personnel from the Tucson Fire Department responded to a crash near Grant and Campbell just before 4:00 a.m.

Grant and Campbell is closed in all direction at this time.

According to TPD, the crash involved two vehicles and one person was declared dead at the scene.

Three others were sent to the hospital with life-threatening injuries.

The investigation is ongoing.
