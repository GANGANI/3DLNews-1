FIRST ALERT FORECAST: Staying hot and dry to wrap up May

Updated: 4 hours ago

TUCSON, Ariz. (13 News) - Tucson is looking to wrap up May without a drop of rainfall at the airport for the month. High temperatures will continue to run a few degrees above normal but stay below the daily records to finish May and kick off June. We’ll remain dry in Tucson through the 7-day forecast under mostly sunny skies with typical afternoon breeziness. Overnight lows will continue to cool into the 60s in Tucson.
