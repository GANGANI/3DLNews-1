Carole Sullivan

Carole Sullivan Hosts “Today In Nashville”, a live, local, lifestyle show that airs weekdays on WSMV4.

An adventurous home cook and experimental mixologist, Carole loves all things food and highlighting Nashville’s diverse restaurant scene. Nationally, she hosts the brand, allrecipes! ‘Eating In!’ and ‘Make it Merry’ shows.

Carole’s passion for TV is rooted in news. She anchored the morning news at KGTV-ABC in San Diego and WKYC-NBC in Cleveland. Her early years were spent as a lead reporter with WPXI in Pittsburgh, WKBW in Buffalo and WAKC in Akron, Ohio.

Carole took a brief break from the full-time news business raising her three young kids, but during that time was a reoccurring guest on NBC’s TODAY Show.

Carole is married to Mike Sullivan, an assistant coach for the Tennessee Titans. She is a board member for the local charity Waves of Grace www.waves-of-grace.org. She is a graduate of John Carroll University. Her family includes a rescue dog, Gracie, from Nashville Humane and a stray cat named Cody.
