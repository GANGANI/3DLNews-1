Man allegedly ran away with his daughter after killing her mother

By Will Whaley and Amanda Alvarado
Published: Feb. 1, 2024 at 8:01 AM CST

PRINCETON, Ky. (WBKO/Gray News) - A Kentucky man was arrested after he allegedly ran away with his daughter and killed her mother.

Byron Black was charged with the murder of Kelly Black on Monday after Kentucky State Police arrested him and an Amber Alert was issued for his daughter, 5-year-old Lela Black.

Authorities believe Kelly Black was shot in the head.

Lela was found safe Monday morning, according to authorities.

Byron Black’s cash bond is set at $1 million.
