Advertising

Our goal is to help clients develop their businesses through creative marketing via the most powerful advertising medium ever created, broadcast television. Many small advertisers assume that television and Web advertising is prohibitively expensive. KIEM-TV and KVIQ has a wide variety of advertising and marketing programs that can easily be afforded by local businesses.

Our sales team will design a custom plan for your business that will reach your spot advertising goals and fulfill your complete marketing objectives. KIEM and KVIQ extends the NBC and CBS brands to the Internet with interactive content that goes beyond TV to allow the viewer to get their news on any device they are using.  Our Production Manager Ross Rowley and his assistant are the best in the area at conceptualizing and producing local ads that work.

KIEM-TV airs 5pm, 6pm, and 11pm news, and 6pm and 11pm on the weekends. We are the only station that has local sports, a local Meteorologist, and the only newscast at 5 pm. CBS and NBC are number one and two in primetime shows.  Our stations have a wide array of high profile national sports including NFL, College Basketball, NASCAR, Golf, Hockey, Horse Racing and more.
