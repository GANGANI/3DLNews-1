Chloe Vincente

Anchor and Investigative Reporter
Roanoke, VA

Chloe Vincente joined WDBJ 7 in February 2023 as an Anchor and Investigative Reporter. You can watch her weekdays, co-anchoring the 10 p.m. news on WZBJ and the 11 p.m. news on WDBJ.

Chloe has a passion for investigative journalism. Before heading to Virginia, Chloe was an Investigative Reporter and Weekend Anchor at CBS 42 in Birmingham, Alabama. She helped her station launch its award-winning investigative franchise, ‘Your Voice Your Station.’ Her series on Birmingham’s Bluestone Coke plant won an Alabama Broadcaster’s Association award, after she uncovered the plant was operating with air quality violations.

Prior to that, Chloe spent four years in the Southern Tier of New York, where she was a Main Evening Anchor at the CBS affiliate in Binghamton. She created an award-winning series called ‘Contaminated,’ where she investigated health hazards in the community. Her reports on dirty water in Endicott, NY led officials to invest in infrastructure upgrades, improving water quality for residents.

Chloe was born and raised on the Connecticut shoreline. She graduated from the University of Connecticut with degrees in Journalism and Communications.

In her free time, Chloe is an avid tennis player. She enjoys hiking, playing with her dog Lulu, and traveling. Chloe is also a major foodie and is excited to explore Roanoke’s restaurant scene!

If you have a story idea, email Chloe at chloe.vincente@wdbj7.com. You can also connect with her on Instagram (bit.ly/3lFZ3QW), Facebook and Twitter.
