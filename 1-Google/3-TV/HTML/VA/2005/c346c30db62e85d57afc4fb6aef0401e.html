Nicole Del Rosario

Digital Content Producer

Nicole Del Rosario joined the WSLS 10 team as a digital content producer in August 2020.

Born and raised in Virginia Beach, Nicole is no stranger to what the Commonwealth has to offer. Over the course of six years, she has slowly journeyed further west.

She graduated from Longwood University in 2019, where she focused her studies in broadcast journalism and video production.

With a passion for content creation, she worked at Kings Dominion as a digital marketing coordinator and video lead where she honed her craft for social media.

Even though she traded in the beach for the mountains, Nicole loves getting to work with the rest of the web team and talented newsroom staff on a daily basis.
