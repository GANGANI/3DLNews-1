Meteorologist Payton Major

Meteorologist
Roanoke, VA

Payton has had her ‘head in the clouds’ since she was 12 years old. After visiting a local news station in her hometown of Portland, Oregon, she knew she wanted to pursue meteorology.

She got a taste of the blistering Southwest heat when she attended Arizona State University (ASU) to study meteorology/climatology and broadcast journalism. While in Phoenix, Payton worked for Cronkite News as a meteorologist and digital producer. She also worked for WeatherRate and the Arizona State Climate Office.

In the summer of 2022, she interned for CNN Weather in Atlanta, Georgia. There, she wrote digital weather/climate stories and forecasted major storms around the world. She graduated from ASU in May 2023 with a dual degree in Geography and Journalism, as well as two certificates.

Payton joined the WDBJ7 weather team in July 2023. She is excited to kick-start her career in the Star City and cover all four seasons. She wants to help bridge the communication gap between science and the public to make weather accessible to all communities.

Outside of being a weather nerd, Payton enjoys spending time with her kitty Dakota, going on hikes, and exploring the local food scene.
