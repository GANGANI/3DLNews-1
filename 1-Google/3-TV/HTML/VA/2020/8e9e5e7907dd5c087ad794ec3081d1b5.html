Caught on Camera: Seal-looking animal spotted swimming in James River

By Hannah Eason
Updated: Aug. 17, 2020 at 12:34 PM EDT

RICHMOND, Va. (WWBT) - A group of friends was walking alongside the James River on Monday when they saw an animal unusual for the Richmond area.

Ryan Sheets and friends watched a large mammal swim for about 30 minutes as it came up for air every minute or so.

The group thought it was a seal, based on its horse-like nostrils and length of about 6 feet.

In the video, the animal can be seen coming up for air and gliding through the water.
