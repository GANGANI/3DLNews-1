Crash near IL 49 kills man from Royal

  • Jun 11, 2016
  • Jun 11, 2016
  • 0

  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS

  • Save

OGDEN – A 32-year-old Royal man is dead after Illinois State Police say they found his pickup truck overturned early Saturday morning.

Christopher Gossett, of Royal, was pronounced dead by Champaign County Coroner Duane Northrup at 4:20 AM on June 11. Northrup says he died as a result of injuries he sustained in a single vehicle crash.

According to our partners at the News-Gazette, Gossett’s overturned pickup truck was seen north of Royal at around 3 AM by a train conductor passing by the area. His truck was located at 2733 County Road and 2400 North in Ogden.

Champaign County Sheriff’s Sergeant Jeff Vercler told the News-Gazette Gossett was heading east, went through a road closed sign and became airborne, then crashed on the other side of the railroad tracks. He was alone at the time, and it is unclear was caused the accident.

An autopsy is scheduled for later in the day, and an inquest may be held at a later date.

This death is being investigated by the Champaign County Coroner’s Office and the Champaign County’s Sheriff’s Office. WAND News will provide updates if more information becomes available.
