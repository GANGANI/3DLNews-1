Journalism History

Welcome to Journalism History, a podcast that rips out the pages of your history books to reexamine the stories you thought you knew, and the ones you were never told. Our hosts Teri Finneman, Nick Hirshon, and Ken Ward guide you through our own drafts of history with other media historians and academics in the field: https://journalism-history.org/podcast/.
