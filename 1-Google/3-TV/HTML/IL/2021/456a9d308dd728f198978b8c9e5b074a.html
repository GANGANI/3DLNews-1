SPRINGFIELD, Ill. (WICS/WRSP) — A Springfield home is a total loss after a fire broke out.

At approximately 7:43 p.m. on Sunday, March 21, Springfield firefighters responded to the 1400 block of East Capitol Street for a house fire.

Upon arrival at the scene, crews saw heavy fire on the front of the single-story home, as well as on the front porch.

The Springfield Fire Department told Newschannel 20 and FOX Illinois that five adults lived in the residence, but no one was home when the fire broke out.

The house's residents are displaced due to the significant damage to the home.

The fire remains under investigation by the Illinois Office of the State Fire Marshal.
