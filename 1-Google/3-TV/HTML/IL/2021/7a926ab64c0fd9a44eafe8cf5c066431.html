Begins Sept 17!

At Hit-Mix 88.9 WEIU-FM, we continue to harness positive energy for a brighter future in moving forward during these uncertain times. We are in our 19th year - still going strong and doing what we love to do! We are still the only station in Charleston! We are still training EIU students who have a passion for broadcasting! We continue to be on the air daily supporting our community by providing a musical and news outlet to entertain and inform the Coles County area.

Our annual Adopt-A-Watt 2023 pledge drive begins Sunday, Sept. 17. Everyone who gives $100 or more toward our goal of $16,000 will receive a Hit-Mix 88.9 T-shirt as a special thank you gift (be sure to let us know your T-shirt size) along with a record on the wall of the Hit-Mix 88.9 studio with their name on it.

In addition, a WEIU Passport membership comes with your contribution to Adopt-a-Watt. Click here to learn more about Passport does for you!



You Don't Have to Wait ... Give Today!

Giving is easy! Click here (or the "Adopt Your Watt Now!" button) and select Adopt-A-Watt or call WEIU at 217-581-9348. To our members who give monthly, please let her know if you wish to continue your monthly gift.

We realize these times continue to be tough and challenging for everyone, so we thank you in advance for your generous donation and for your continued support of Hit-Mix 88.9 WEIU-FM.



WEIU operates at 88.9 FM with 4000 watts of power. By adopting a watt -- or multiple watts! -- you can be assured that "your" radio station will be able to deliver the programming, news and sports you have come to rely on. You will “own” your watt for one year and become a member of Hit-Mix 88.9 WEIU.

What makes Hit-Mix different from those other stations? What makes us stand out?

  1. Live announcers from 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. weekdays.
  2. We take and play your requests.
  3. Extensive and growing musical playlist.
  4. Live Eastern Illinois University sports.
  5. Jazz music from 10 a.m. to noon each Sunday.
  6. Gospel Sing Time Sunday mornings 7-10 a.m.
  7. The area’s only long form live public affairs show – Issues and Attitudes — every Monday at noon.
  8. Folk music from 6-7 a.m. each Sunday morning.
  9. On TV during the morning show and during Issues and Attitudes.
  10. Online 24/7 at weiu.net.
  11. We are a small non-commercial radio station that does more for the community than larger area stations!
  12. Operated by a dedicated group of Eastern Illinois students who love working at Hit-Mix.

The money you give to support our station during Adopt-a-Watt funds all the above and more! We sincerely thank you.
