Why Give to WEIU?

We encourage you to consider your contribution a gift to everyone in the community. As a member of WEIU, you are a part of every service WEIU provides -- quality programming, Hit-Mix Radio, educational services, and a website providing 24/7 access to our program schedule, highlights and on-demand video.
