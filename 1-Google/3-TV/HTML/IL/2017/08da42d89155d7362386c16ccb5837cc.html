Arrest made after woman held against her will calls police from restaurant

Published: May. 8, 2017 at 6:36 PM CDT

ROCKFORD, Ill. (WIFR) – A Rockford man has been charged with aggravated criminal sexual assault after investigators say he was holding his victim against her will.

Rockford Police say officers responded to a local restaurant Sunday morning for a domestic related home invasion. The victim, who officers say was being held against her will, reported that the incident happened at her home in the 700 block of North Highland Avenue.

During the home invasion, the victim reported she was sexually assaulted by 45-year-old Lanier Carter, whom she had previously been in a relationship with. After the assault, Carter took the victim to the restaurant where she was able to call police.

Police arrested Carter at the restaurant. He has been charged with four counts of Aggravated Criminal Sexual Assault.
