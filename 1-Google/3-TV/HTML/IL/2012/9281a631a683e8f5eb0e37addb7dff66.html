Illinoise

Illinoise is Hit-Mix's live local music program, airing Sunday nights from 9 p.m. - Midnight. Join your host Ian Acree as he entertains you with three hours of nothing but the best local Illinois artists. Tune in for periodic live on-air performances from local bands and local music event promotions.

We Want to Hear From You!

If you are in a band or know of a local band looking for airtime, contact us at hitmix@weiu.net or by phone at 217-581-6116 during the show. Also, music submissions can be dropped off at Buzzard Hall on Ninth Street — located on the campus of Eastern Illinois University in Charleston — courtesy of Production Supervisor Rob Calhoun.
