Burlington man gets five years for fatal crash

Tue, January 20th 2009 at 10:12 AM

Tue, 20 Jan 2009 16:12:05 GMT — WEST BURLINGTON, Iowa - A Burlington man found guilty of supplying alcohol to two teenagers who died in a car crash faces up to five years in prison.

Twenty-one-year-old Michael Bernhart is to be sentenced on Tuesday for supplying alcohol to a minor. He was found guilty by a judge in December.

Bernhart was injured in the one-car crash in March 2008 in West Burlington. The driver, 18-year-old Ross Taylor, and another passenger, 17-year-old Kara Patejak, both of West Burlington, were killed.

Investigators determined Bernhart had provided alcohol to Taylor on previous occasions, including the night before the accident.
