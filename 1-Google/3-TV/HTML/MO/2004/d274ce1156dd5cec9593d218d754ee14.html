My Mom Club: cooking pizza with kids

Updated: Jan. 29, 2024 at 11:04 AM CST
Homemade Pizza Combine 1 1/2 cups self-rising flour (or 1 1/2 cups flour, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, 3/4 tsp salt) and 1 cup Greek yogurt. Mix and knead with your hands for 8-10 minutes. Allow the dough to rest for 5 minutes and then shape the dough into a circle. Place the crust on a baking sheet. Add sauce and toppings as desired. Bake for 10-12 minutes at 450 degrees. Enjoy!
