Contact Us

By KFVS Staff
Published: Jun. 14, 2019 at 10:32 AM CDT|Updated: Mar. 5, 2024 at 9:33 AM CST

Both in person and online, there are many different ways to contact us at KFVS12/Heartland News:

Email:

  • Press releases/news announcements/story ideas: send your email to news@kfvs12.com
  • Contacting on-air staff: On-air staff email addresses
  • Viewer comments: email manager@kfvs12.com
  • CLICK HERE to upload pictures and video
  • Direct email to employees: Most employee email addresses at KFVS12 follow a similar format: first name, period and then last name (without a space) “@kfvs12.com.” To email an employee not listed below, follow this format. For example, John Smith’s e-mail would be john.smith@kfvs12.com.

On social media:

  • Twitter: @kfvsnews, @kfvssports, @kfvsweather, @kfvssales
  • Facebook: http://www.facebook.com/kfvs12

By mail:

  • KFVS12 Studio: 310 Broadway, Cape Girardeau, MO 63701

By phone:

  • Main switchboard: (573) 335-1212

If you have a specific question or comment and are not sure who to contact, you may contact one of the people below:

  • Overall station operations: General Manager Chris Conroy
  • News content: News Director, Roger Seay.
  • Community event sponsorship or station marketing: Director of Marketing, Paul Keener.
  • Advertising on KFVS12 & The Heartland’s CW: Director of Sales, Greg Carter
  • Advertising on KFVS12.com: Director of Sales, Greg Carter
  • Content on KFVS12.com: Digital Content Director, Amber Ruch.
  • Programming on KFVS12 & KFVS-TOO: Program Director, Kathy Cowan.
  • Engineering questions: Director of Technology, Tracy Lee
  • Internship information: Human Resources, Kathy Cowan.
