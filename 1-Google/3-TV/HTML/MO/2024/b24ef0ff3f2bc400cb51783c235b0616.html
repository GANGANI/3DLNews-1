Can Taylor Swift make it to the Super Bowl from Tokyo in time? Here’s how it’s possible

By Emily Van de Riet
Published: Jan. 29, 2024 at 5:08 PM CST

(Gray News) – Now that the Kansas City Chiefs have secured their spot in the Super Bowl, another question remains – will Taylor Swift be at the big game?

Swift has already attended 12 NFL games since September to cheer on her boyfriend Travis Kelce.

But attending the Super Bowl – though not impossible – will be quite challenging for the superstar.

She would have to make it from her show in Tokyo back to Las Vegas within 24 hours.

The Super Bowl is on Feb. 11 and her final Eras Tour show in Tokyo is on Feb. 10.

A flight from Asia to North America is at least 12 hours, but Swift has time zones on her side, as Tokyo is 17 hours ahead of Las Vegas.

Her concert in Tokyo is scheduled to end roughly around 11 p.m. local time, which is 6 a.m. Saturday in Las Vegas.

With a quick turnaround, a 12-hour flight could land her in Las Vegas by Saturday evening, just in time to make the game Sunday.

The Eras Tour resumes on Feb. 7 for a six-month international run before returning to the U.S. and Canada for the final 15 shows in the fall and winter.
