Chris Burns

Sports Director

Chris Burns comes to KTVO after growing up in the small town of Livonia, NY. He attended the Dan Patrick School of Sportscasting at Full Sail University in Central Florida. After graduating in March of 2020, he began his career at Lakeland PBS as a news multi-media journalist, before coming to KTVO to accept the roll as KTVO's Sports Director.

During college, Chris gained lots of on-camera experience through the school's opportunities. He was the play-by-play broadcaster for a division II college, Rollins University, while also completing an internship with the WWE and working NFL games on FOX.

Growing up in a smaller town, Chris knows how passionate local towns are about their high school sports. He comes to KTVO ready to share in that passion by providing local high school sports stories and highlights.

Chris grew up with five sisters and always credits them with his success saying, "I wouldn't be the person I am today without my five sisters and mom pushing me to achieve my goals... also I'm older than my twin by seven minutes."
