TRAFFIC ALERT: MoDOT reopens I-44 after crash involving semi

By KY3 Staff
Published: Jun. 5, 2022 at 10:10 AM CDT|Updated: Jun. 5, 2022 at 12:16 PM CDT

SPRINGFIELD, Mo. (KY3) - MoDOT reopened lanes of I-44 in Springfield after a tractor-trailer rolled over Sunday morning.

The crash happened around 9:30 a.m. in the eastbound lanes near mile marker 77. The crash closed both eastbound lanes for a few hours. Investigators reported it as an injury crash.

It took nearly three hours to reopen the interstate for traffic.
