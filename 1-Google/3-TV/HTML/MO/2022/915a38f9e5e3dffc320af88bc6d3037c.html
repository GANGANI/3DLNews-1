Nicole Sanders

Digital Content Producer
KMOV

Nicole Sanders is a media maven who tackles breaking news, entertainment, and lifestyle features just one lede at a time. She considers St. Louis her second home next to her stomping grounds in New Orleans.

In 2017, Nicole secured a Digital Content Producer position at KMOV where she aggressively covers local stories. One of her passions is to provide a voice to the voiceless by shining a light on diverse communities in the area.

Nicole graduated from Lindenwood University where she earned her degree in Journalism. When she’s not in the Digital Content Center, she is exploring hidden gems across the Lou or at a local plant nursery looking for a new addition to take home.

Keep up with Nicole on Twitter and Instagram.
