Former Mid-Michigan NOW anchor goes viral for video on anti-Asian violence

by Dave Bondy

Sat, March 20th 2021 at 11:14 PM
Former Mid-Michigan NOW anchor goes viral for video on anti-Asian violence. (Mid-Michigan NOW)

FLINT, Mich - Former Mid-Michigan NOW anchor Jane Park has over three million followers on her TikTok page.

A video she recently posted got the attention of millions of views.

Click here to get the Mid-Michigan NOW Newsletter sent to your inbox every day to get your headlines and breaking news.

In the video Park talks to her children about what exactly is racism.

Park said the conversations started last summer during protests after the killing of George Floyd.

Park has got feedback from across the country and the world from other parents letting her know the viral video has helped them open up with their children about having a frank discussion about racism in America.
