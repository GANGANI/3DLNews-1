Detroit PBS's Door-to-Door Membership Drive

Beginning in October 2021, Detroit PBS launched a Door-to-Door Membership Drive by sending a few professional canvassers into neighborhoods across the metro Detroit area. Today, our community canvass program allows us to personally convey the importance of public television and quality programming to thousands of people every week. By committing to contribute through one of our canvassers you help us further our mission to enrich the lives of Detroiters through engaging and essential programs, services, and community partnerships that inform, enlighten, and entertain.

Do your part and donate to Detroit PBS. Thank you.

If you or someone you know is interested in joining our canvass team, call (312) 625-2115 to set up an interview!
