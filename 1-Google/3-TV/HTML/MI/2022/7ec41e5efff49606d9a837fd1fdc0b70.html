A Look Back at 23 Years of Corey Adkins' Storytelling

Corey Adkins, our top storyteller, is looking back on 23 years in northern Michigan on his last day with our station.

It’s a bittersweet day, as we hate to lose Corey’s brilliant talent. We know he’s touched so many of you with his work, but we are proud to see him moving on to follow his passion for telling stories.

A colleague of Corey’s for nearly all of his 23 years at 9&10 News, Kevin Essebaggers, with help from photojournalist Stephanie Adkins who met and married Corey while working at 9&10 News, takes a look back at some of the best moments of Corey’s northern Michigan adventure.
