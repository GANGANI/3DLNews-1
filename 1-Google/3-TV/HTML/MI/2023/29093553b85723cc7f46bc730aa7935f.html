Trusted PBS Resources Designed for Michigan Students

The Michigan Learning Channel provides educators and families with free PBS videos and printable resources designed to ensure all kids succeed.

Teach, Practice, Reinforce. All for Free. Always.

Teachers and educators use our videos and free printables as direct instruction in the classroom, as take-home assignments, or for students who need extra practice in specific topics. Because the Michigan Learning Channel is provided by Michigan’s PBS stations, everything is available at no cost.
