WCMU Passport is the member benefit that provides you with extended access to an on-demand library of quality public television programming. Featuring both PBS and local WCMU programs, WCMU Passport allows you to watch even more episodes of your favorite shows, binge watch select new programs before they air, and see exclusive Passport only programs, including full seasons of many current and past series. WCMU Passport is available for an annual membership of at least $60 or $5 a month Sustainer donation.
