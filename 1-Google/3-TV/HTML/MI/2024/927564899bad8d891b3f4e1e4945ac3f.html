Erin Bowling

Multimedia Journalist
Lansing, MI

A Mid-Michigander through and through, Erin found her passion for news in a TV Broadcasting class at Williamston High School. She graduated a semester early from MSU, with a degree in Broadcast Journalism in December of 2022. Her love for stories began at an even younger age, as her mom teaches English locally. She hopes to use storytelling to uplift the stories of Mid-Michiganders.

Outside of work, Erin can usually be found eating something with ranch dressing or planning her next travel adventure. She also loves spending time with family and friends, including the three family dogs (all golden retrievers). If you’re not being interviewed for News 10, she’ll want to know your zodiac sign (she’s an Aquarius).

If there’s anything you think people should know about, she wants to know about it too. You can email her your insights, experiences, and interests at erin.bowling@wilx.com
