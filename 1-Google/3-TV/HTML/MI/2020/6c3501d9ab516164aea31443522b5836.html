Production Services

Detroit PBS is a leading PBS station with expert coverage of arts and culture events, conferences, public forums and concerts at a local and national level.

We understand that video continues to be one of the most powerful methods of sharing your message in a multi-media world. Whether you need a commercial spot, online promotional video, branding message, livestream production or any other type of video, we are here to help. We offer a full range of services from production to post-production, and specialize in a wide range of work, from recording your message in our studio, to capturing your live event on-site.

Detroit PBS's in-house production staff can handle many types of projects right at our Metro Detroit studio located at Lawrence Technological University in Southfield, Michigan. The state-of-the-art facility features studio space with industry-leading equipment, expert on-site video production services and affordable pricing options for small businesses, nonprofits, large corporations and other organizations.

In addition, our 35-foot Production Truck or Custom Flight Packs are perfect for any project beyond our studio – no matter how small or large.

Some examples of our work include:

  • Ally Financial
  • Ford Motor Company Fund
  • First Independence Bank
  • Tim Bogert, Timothy Bogert, CLU, ChFC, LIC, AIF®
  • Concert of Colors
  • Detroit Regional Chamber’s Mackinac Policy Conference
  • Knight Media Forum
  • PBS Books
  • MPC20 Conversations

You can also be assured that your safety is of the utmost importance to us. Detroit PBS has implemented the following protocols to maintain a safe environment for our staff and our partners:

  • Only a small list of essential employees are allowed in the building
  • All visitors and staff members are required to have their temperature checked before entering the facility
  • All visitors and staff members are required to answer questions about potential exposure and symptoms
  • Masks are required for all staff and guests except during recording
  • Masks and hand sanitizer are available on site
  • All studio equipment and common areas are cleaned and disinfected after each use
  • We maintain 6ft social distancing at all times

No matter if you’re looking to film a video for broadcast or online use at our studio, or if you need our team to travel to you to capture an on-site event, Detriot PBS's highly skilled team is standing by to help you create an impactful and engaging production.

Packages

Standard package

  • 30-minute time slot for filming
  • Camera operator and producer to oversee production
  • Choose from one of our standard studio backdrops or film in front of a green screen to add in your own background image later
  • Receive your raw footage on a pen drive before you leave our studio so that you can edit it as you wish

Deluxe package

  • One-hour time slot for filming
  • Camera operator and a producer to oversee production and coach you throughout the process
  • Choose from one of our standard studio backdrops or film in front of our green screen then provide your own branded image, or choose from our extensive catalog of background images to be added into your video
  • One hour of editing after filming is included plus up to one round of post-production modification requiring no more than one additional hour of edit time
  • Receive two versions of your video via Dropbox with 5 business days after filming: Raw footage and Final edited version
