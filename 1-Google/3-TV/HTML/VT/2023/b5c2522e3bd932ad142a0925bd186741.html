John McMahon

Anchor, Channel 3 This Morning
Burlington, Vt.

John McMahon is a morning news anchor for Channel 3 This Morning. He joined WCAX in November 2023, but is no stranger to the morning shift. He started his career in broadcasting as a morning anchor and reporter with KTVL in Medford, Oregon, and most recently finished a stint in the Midwest as a morning anchor in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.

Throughout his time in the field, John has covered everything from wildfires, COVID restrictions and contested state measures, and has interviewed everyone from governors, state officials, to "Wiper Blade Man" and tiger trainers.

John graduated from Quinnipiac University in 2020 (via Zoom!) with a degree in Journalism & Communications. Though he's worked on the West Coast and Midwest, he's happy to be back in his native New England.

In his free time, John likes to run, hit the slopes and try to improve his pitiful golf game.

If you have any VT recommendations or story ideas, email John at John.McMahon@gray.tv.
