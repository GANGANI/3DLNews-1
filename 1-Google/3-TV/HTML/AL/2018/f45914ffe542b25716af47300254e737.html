Tickled Pink | September 29, 2018 | 9 a.m. – 3 p.m. | Multiplex at Cramton Bowl

Join Alabama News Network as we kick off Breast Cancer Awareness Month on Saturday, Sept. 29 with the 4th Annual Tickled Pink Women’s Expo. All ticket sales benefit the Joy to Life Foundation .

This year will be even BIGGER and BETTER than last year with more beauty, more fitness, more food, and even more fun!

To be a Vendor, contact Austin Saunders or Paul Neace at 334-271-8888.

Tickets

Tickets will go on sale Sept. 1, 2018.

$10 on the day of the event
$8 in advance (tickets will go on sale Sept. 1)
$8 military discount with ID
$5 for groups of 10 or more in advance
Children age 10 or under get in free

Official Rules and Regulations
1. Contest open to Tickled Pink 2018 attendees, who attend Tickled Pink on Saturday, September 29, 2018 only.

2. All legal residents of the United States of America; 19 years old or older are eligible to enter.

3. Patrons will come inside the Cramton Bowl Multiplex and exchange their Tickled Pink ticket for a drawing ticket beginning at 900 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Maximum of one entry per person.

4. Drawing will be held on the Jackson Hospital Stage in the multiplex. One winner will be chosen (at each drawing) at random from all entries on Saturday, September 29, 2018. Drawing for $100 each will be at 9:30 a.m., 10:30 a.m. 11:30 a.m.; Drawing for $200 each will begin at 12:30 p.m.; 1:30 p.m.; and the final drawing for $500 at 2:30 p.m. You must be present to win. If prize money is not claimed/awarded at a drawing then the monies will roll into the next drawing. Ticket collection will end 15 minutes prior to drawing.
