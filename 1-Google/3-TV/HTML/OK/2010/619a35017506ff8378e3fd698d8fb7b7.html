How Can I Watch ABC Texoma?

On-Air:

Do you own an antenna and a Digital Ready TV or DTV Converter Box?

If so, we're now on Digital Channel 10.3

Our transmitter is in-between Bromide, OK and Wapanucka, OK. Aim your UHF or combination UHF/VHF antenna in this direction to receive all the quality programming of KTEN which includes NBC (10.1), Texoma's CW (10.2), and ABC Texoma (10.3)

Satellite - Dish Network:

On May 26th, 2010 if you are a Dish Network subscriber in our TV Market, you should be able to receive ABC Texoma on Channel 9.

We're still in negotiations with DirecTV, but if you'd like to request ABC Texoma for your DirecTV system, here is their contact number: 888-777-2454
