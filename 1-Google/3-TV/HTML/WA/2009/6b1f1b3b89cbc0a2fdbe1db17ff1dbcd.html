Divers Find Man's Body in Cold Spring Reservoir in Hermiston

  • Apr 21, 2009
  • Apr 21, 2009

Divers from Columbia Basin Dive Rescue have found the body of a man who jumped in the Cold Springs Reservoir in Hermiston, to try to save his 20 month old nephew on Sunday.

They say 35-year-old Ignacio Lucas Amezcua was found about 80 feet from the shore and 20 feet under water.

The Umatilla Sheriff's Office is calling this an accident. Dive rescue staff say it appears the man drowned.
