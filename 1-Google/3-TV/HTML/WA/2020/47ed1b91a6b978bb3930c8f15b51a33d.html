Inside the Newsroom

News and Public Affairs Series / 1 Season

Sit down with Crosscut journalists as they give the inside scoop on the stories making headlines in the Northwest. From the tumult in city hall to the impacts of the coronavirus pandemic, Crosscut reporters on the ground share how these stories come together and offer the backstory on the most important issues affecting our community.
