Getting here by car

Driving I-5 Southbound (From North Seattle)

  1. Take I-5 South
  2. Take exit 165A toward James St
  3. Merge onto 6th Ave
  4. Turn left onto Yesler Way
  5. Turn left onto Broadway
  6. Cascade PBS is on the right at E Terrace St

Driving directions to Cascade PBS

Driving I-5 Northbound (From South Seattle)

  1. Take I-5 North
  2. Take exit 164A for Dearborn St toward James St
  3. Turn right onto James St
  4. Turn right onto Boren Ave
  5. Turn left at the 1st cross street onto Jefferson St
  6. Turn right onto Broadway
  7. Cascade PBS is on the left at E Terrace St

Parking around Cascade PBS

Street parking in the immediate area is by permit only..

Bus Routes

Buses 9 and 13 and the First Hill Streetcar serve Cascade PBS at Broadway & Terrace St. Buses 3 and 4 serve Cascade PBS at E Jefferson St & Broadway. Visit King County Metro Transit for schedule information.

Parking Garages

There are some nearby parking options within a 3-11-minute walk (all rates and availability subject to change).

Seattle University has multiple parking options:

  • Murphy Garage (P2 level 1) 11th & E Cherry Street, Seattle, 98122; 3-min walk.
  • Redhawk Center Lot (P7) 14th & E. Jefferson Street, Seattle, 98122; 8-min walk.
  • Visitor Lot - The Lee Center (P1) 12th & E. Marion Street, Seattle, 98122; 11-min walk.

Harborview First Hill Medical Bldg Parking Garage:

515 Minor Avenue, Seattle, WA 98104; 5-min walk. Disabled spots available, electric car charging stations, and offers a height restriction of 32' 9".

Broadway Parking Garage:

600 Broadway, Seattle, WA 98122; 10-min walk. Disabled spots available, electric car charging stations, and offers a height restriction of 11' 0".

Online Parking Resources
