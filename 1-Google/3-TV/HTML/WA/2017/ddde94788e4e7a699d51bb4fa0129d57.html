Ways to Watch

Cascade PBS (formerly KCTS 9) has come a long way since its founding in 1954.  Back then, an aerial antenna and TV set were the only way to watch.  Nowadays, we are a multi-platform television station, which is available on a wide variety of platforms. 

Cascade PBS app

The Cascade PBS app  combines your favorite PBS programming, local originals and an ever-growing selection of quality television shows and films from around the world. Available on Roku, Apple TV, Amazon FireTV, iOS and Android, our app offers thousands of free-to-stream on-demand episodes.

In the U.S., you can also watch livestreams of our broadcast channels: Cascade PBS, WORLD, Create and PBS KIDS.

Cascade PBS Passport Members get even more:

  • Access to our full programming catalog
  • Ability to track favorites
  • Continue where you left off watching
  • Smart recommendations

Use another OTT platform?  We’ve got you covered with the PBS Video App. 
 

CascadePBS.org (formerly KCTS9.org)

There’s plenty to watch right here at CascadePBS.org, including many of your favorite PBS programs and local originals. Cascade PBS Passport members receive access to an expanded selection of shows, including member exclusives.

Learn more about Passport

Livestreams of KCTS 9, WORLD, PBS KIDS and Create are available on this website, with availability restrictions due to licensing:

  • Seattle-area users can stream for free with or without a membership
  • Cascade PBS Passport members can log in and stream from anywhere in the United States
  • Unfortunately, streams are not currently available in Canada
