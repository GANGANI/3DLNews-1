Alaska Dept. of Revenue announces winners of 2024 PFD raffle

By Adrian Peterson
Published: Jan. 31, 2024 at 8:47 AM AKST

FAIRBANKS, Alaska (KTVF) - The winners of the fifth annual Permanent Fund Dividend Education Raffle have been announced with a total of $130,73.85 being split between multiple people.

From nearly 10,000 entries, four winners were selected at random to win this years Permanent Fund Dividend Education Raffle also known as the PFD raffle. They include Deborah Main, winning $69,727.92, Vassily Zavoico receiving $34,863.96, Melanie Wahl receiving $17,431.98 and Gary Lehnhart awarded $8,715.99.

The prize money making up just 25% of all the money acquired, the remaining 75% all goes to education in one way or anther. That makes for a total of $749,000. “$550,000 was raised for education,” said Adam Crum the commissioner of the Dept. of Revenue. Crum continued with the break down and said, “$499.700 will go directly to school districts, $249,800 will go to the education endowment fund for funding education in the future.”

Of the raffle fund, first prize is 8% of the raffle fund; second prize is 4%; third prize is 2%; fourth prize is 1% and the remaining 85% of the raffle fund will grow the fund for future year prize money.

In total, five years of the PFD raffle has resulted in “$3.4 million” of education funding.
