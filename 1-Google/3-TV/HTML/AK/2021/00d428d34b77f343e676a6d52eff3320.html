School Lunch Menu for the 2023-24 school year

Be prepared with a jump start on what the school lunch will be each day at your school. The School Lunch Menu brought to you by Great Harvest Bread Company

The first day of school for the 2023-24 school year in the Fairbanks North Star Borough is Thursday, August 17.

KTVF Channel 11 has your back and will help you plan your day by featuring the daily school lunch menu during the Fairbanks Morning News and again on Newscenter Final at 11 p.m. with the next day’s school lunch menu.

The school menus for elementary, middle, high school, and charter schools are displayed below. They are color-coded and use a 3-week cycle.

Logging into your Fairbanks North Star Borough School District SchoolCafé online portal will give you quick access to images and descriptions of food items, as well as nutrition and allergy information!
