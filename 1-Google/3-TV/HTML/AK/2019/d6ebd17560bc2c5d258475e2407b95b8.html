KJNP AM is a 50,000 watt station and is on the air approximately 19 hours a day in the summer and 24 hours a day in the winter.

The format is Country/Gospel music with News, Bible, Family and Children's programs.

KJNP AM reaches North Pole, Fairbanks, and remote villages in interior Alaska. We have also received listener reports from around the arctic.

Trapline Chatter can be heard each evening around 9:20 pm. You can check the schedule for the exact time.

KJNP FM is a 25,000 watt station that broadcasts 24 hours a day.

It reaches the greater Fairbanks area and the Tanana Valley.

The format is light Contemporary with a mix of Classical and Sacred music.

We are affiliated with Moody Broadcasting Network.

Trapline Chatter can be heard each evening around 9:20 pm. You can check the schedule for the exact time.

KJHA FM is located in Houston, Alaska. (by Wasilla)

It broadcasts to the Matanuska Valley and North Anchorage area in South Central Alaska.

The programming comes from our studios in North Pole.

Trapline Chatter can be heard each evening around 9:20 pm. You can check the schedule for the exact time.

 

KJNP Translators

Translators rebroadcast the programming of a larger station, we use them to provide better coverage in some remote Alaska villages.
