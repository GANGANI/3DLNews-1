Madison Pitsch

Evening Anchor
Lincoln, NE

Madison Pitsch has been a multimedia journalist with 10/11 since January of 2019. After anchoring the weekend newscasts and 5 p.m. newscasts, she was promoted to 5, 6 and 10 p.m. anchor.

During her time at 10/11, she's covered severe weather, global pandemics, under-represented populations, economic development and everything in between.

She is originally from Concordia, Missouri, and graduated from Concordia University Nebraska in 2019 with degrees in Journalism, Spanish, Communication and Intercultural Studies. She likes to cook, travel, go for walks, golf, play basketball, do yoga, read, watch movies, call her family on the phone and be with friends.

For Madison, it's hard to pick a favorite story she's covered, because she's met so many amazing people with amazing stories during her time in Lincoln. She wants to thank you for making Lincoln, and Nebraska feel like home.

If you have tips or stories for Madison reach her at her email address, madison.pitsch@1011now.com or call her at (402) 467-9270. She's looking forward to hearing from you.
