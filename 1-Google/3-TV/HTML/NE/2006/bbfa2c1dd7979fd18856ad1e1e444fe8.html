Where to watch METV

Published: Jun. 28, 2006 at 1:21 PM CDT

You can receive METV over the air on channel 4-2, 10-3, or 11-3. Just like 10/11 and NBC Nebraska, METV is broadcast free, over-the-air for viewers to receive using an antenna. In order to receive digital television, a digital tuner is needed to decode the signal.

METV is home to classic television like

I Love Lucy

,

Perry Mason

, and

Gunsmoke

. It’s also your source for quality local programming like

MyNews at 9

,

10/11 PrepZone Weekly

,

MomsEveryday: The Series

, and

Pure Nebraska

. Plus, MyNetworkTV's primetime lineup includes more recent classics from the past decade.

Contact your local provider for more information on how you can receive METV in your area.
