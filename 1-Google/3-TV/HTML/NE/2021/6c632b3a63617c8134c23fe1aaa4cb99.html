Emily Dwire

Anchor / MMJ / Reporter
Omaha

Emily Dwire joined the WOWT News team in late 2019. She currently anchors the weekend evening newscasts and reports on general assignments during the week.

Emily previously worked at WPTA in Fort Wayne, Indiana for 7 years where she was most recently the morning and noon anchor, as well as a producer.

Emily has covered an array of breaking news scenes, presidential visits, the George Floyd protests, and the impacts of the Coronavirus on families and communities.

Emily hails from Fort Wayne and graduated from Ball State University in 2012.

When she's not working you'll find her spending time with her family and dog, enjoying a good movie or book, and typically longing for the beach.

You can follow Emily on Facebook and Twitter, and email her with story ideas: emily.dwire@wowt.com
