More t-storm chances through the weekend; warmer air awaits

Updated: 2 hours ago

We will continue our active weather pattern as we finish the workweek and go through the weekend. After a cooler day on Friday, we will warm up once again with highs in the mid-80s being common. A typical early summer pattern where isolated storms develop right after peak daytime heating is setting up again on Friday. Under partly cloudy skies, it will be cooler with highs in the mid-70s. Saturday we will have partly cloudy skies, and will be in Storm Watch mode, with a good chance of showers/t-storms; highs will be in the mid-80s. We make it to the upper 80s for Sunday as we keep partly cloudy skies with a chance of PM showers/t-storms.
