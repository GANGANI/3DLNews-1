Actor Anthony Anderson hospitalized after movie stunt gone wrong

By Gray News staff
Published: Feb. 2, 2024 at 10:25 AM PST

(Gray News) - Actor Anthony Anderson was hospitalized after a botched stunt on the set of his upcoming film “G-20″ in South Africa.

Anderson talked about the mishap on Instagram on Thursday and included a photo of himself on a stretcher.

He called the incident a “movie set fight gone wrong.”

“Me against two goons and a chair! Guess who didn’t win! Who needs a stuntman? Me that’s who!” Anderson said.

He wrote he spent the night in the emergency room.

Anderson added he didn’t have any broken bones, just a deep contusion.

“That chair will never be the same though!” he added.

Other celebrities responded to the post with a flurry of well-wishes and good-natured teasing.
