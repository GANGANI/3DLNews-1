Fort Wingate

On a document dated June 30, 2020, the Pentagon added Fort Wingate to its list of more than 700 military installations nationwide that might have PFAS contamination.

Holloman Air Force Base

PFAS levels in the waters below Holloman AFB just outside Alamogordo are up to 1,294,000 parts per trillion—more than 27,000 times the lifetime advisory level.

Cannon Air Force Base

PFAS levels below Cannon AFB were found at levels more than 370 times what the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency considers safe for an entire lifetime of exposure.
