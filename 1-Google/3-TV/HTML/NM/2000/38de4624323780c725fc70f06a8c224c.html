Common questions

  • Do you offer discounts?

    Yes. When you book directly on kasa.com, you get a 15% discount compared to other booking channels like Airbnb or Expedia.

  • Do you have weekly or monthly rates?

    Yes. Discounts are automatically applied and reflected during checkout for stays longer than seven and thirty days.

  • Do you offer parking?

    Reserved parking is available at most Kasa properties. Please review the listing description carefully to understand parking availability.

  • What time is check-in and checkout?

    Our standard check-in time is 4pm and our standard checkout time is 11am.
