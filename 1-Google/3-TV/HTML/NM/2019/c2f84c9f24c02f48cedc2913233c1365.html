The Hubbard Broadcasting Commitment.

Career Growth

At Hubbard Broadcasting, we offer vast possibilities for professional development. Whether you're an on-air anchor, production engineer, or a back office administrator, we create opportunities for employees in every position to succeed. We believe we make the greatest impact when we retain and develop talent from within our organization.

Collaborative Environment

With strong leadership and an engaged workforce, our employees tackle challenges as a team. At Hubbard, you'll find open doors and office environments that encourage collective brainstorming. From informal stand up meetings to offsite team building activities, we believe that our best work is done together.

Exciting Work

Every day our employees get to do the most exciting work in the business. From interviewing music and television's biggest stars to investigative journalism to learning about the latest digital marketing trends, employees from every facet of our business are always engaged in thrilling work.

Opportunities to Succeed

At Hubbard, we equip our employees with the tools they need to achieve their dreams. Hubbard's employees and companies have garnered numerous accolades and awards over the years, from the local to the national level. We aren't just dreamers, we're doers.

The Benefits of Working For Hubbard Broadcasting

We invest in your whole self and are dedicated to the well-being of both you and your family.  We offer a competitive total compensation plan which includes full benefits, opportunities for growth and a creative and enjoyable work environment. This includes a holistic approach that provides resources to assist you with all your wellness needs including physical, mental, and financial.

 

Physical Wellness

All the Resources You Need To Achieve And Maintain Your Optimal Physical Health

  • Medical Plan Options with HSA and Co-pay Plans
  • Free Preventative Care
  • Telehealth Option
  • Programs to Support Weight Management, Diabetes, and High Blood Pressure
  • Dental Preventative Visits Covered 2x/year

Financial Wellness

Available Resources To Set You On The Path To Prepare For Your Financial Future – Wherever Life Takes You

  • 401(k) with match (fully vested on day 1)
  • Licensed Financial Planner through the HBI 401(k)
  • Financial Counselors and Legal Consultation Services Available Through the Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • Health Saving Account (HSA)
  • Flexible Spending (FSA) Options
  • Travel Assistance
  • ID Theft Protection Service

Family and Personal Time Off

Growing Family?  Aging Parents?  Sick Loved One?  Flu bug got you?  We will work with you to find a solution to take care of your life events with time off. 

  • Vacation
  • 9 Holidays
  • Sick Time
  • Medical Leave
  • Maternity
  • Bonding leave for new parents
  • Family Leave

Mental Health and wellness

Taking Care of Your Mental Health Is Every Bit As Important As Your Physical Health So We Provide The Following:

  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • 4 Free Counseling Sessions through the (EAP)
  • Wellness Gift Cards
  • Wellness Challenges
  • Company Paid Life and Disability with Supplemental Coverage Available

Learning and Development

We Are Committed To Your Growth And Development And Provide The Following Learning Opportunities:

  • New Manager and Leadership Training
  • Learning and Development Resources
  • Mentoring
  • NextGen Program
  • Life Coaching and Career Development via EAP Website
