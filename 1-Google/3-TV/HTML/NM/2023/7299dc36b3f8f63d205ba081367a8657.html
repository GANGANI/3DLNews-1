Top 10 Trending Stories of 2023: Number 9

by Jamie Burch, ABC 7 News

Fri, December 22nd 2023 at 7:58 AM
19
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
One person was killed in a three vehicle crash on Hwy 385 near Boys Ranch on June 19, 2023 (Ash Albright/KVII)

OLDHAM COUNTY, Texas (KVII) — As we wrap up 2023, we take a look back at the top 10 trending stories of the year.

Number 9 is a crash that killed one person and injured six others, including two children.

The crash happened June 19 on Hwy 385 just north of the Canadian River Bridge.

According to the Texas Department of Public Safety, Bernard Sneller, 83, of Amarillo, slammed into the back of another tractor trailer which was stopped at the red light in the construction zone.

The second big rig was then pushed into a pickup which was also stopped at the red light.

Sneller was pronounced dead at the scene.

All six people in the pickup were taken to Northwest Texas Hospital in Amarillo with minor injuries.

According to his obituary, Sneller was a "a modern-day MacGyver. There wasn’t anything he couldn’t figure out or fix."

Sneller is survived by his wife, nine children, 33 grandchildren, 44 great-grandchildren, and four great-great grandchildren.
