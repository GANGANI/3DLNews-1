Gangs in JC

  • May 10, 2019
  • May 10, 2019

A Jefferson City police spokesman says there are gangs in the Capitol City.

But Lieutenant Dave Williams says they're not the kind of gangs responsible for hundreds of murders in big cities.

"Most situations you talk about; a gang is a group of people who have a common mindset and a common purpose.  In this case, we have several groups of individuals in Jefferson City who are trying to associate themselves with like-minded people."

Williams says Jefferson City's gangs are more likely into drugs and guns or stolen property, rather than turf.  

He says the police department is using every resource that's available to them, including federal agencies such as the ATF to solve crimes and keep criminals on the run.

"Our common thread is weapons and that's one of the reasons why we're working with our federal partners to try and address the extra amount of guns in our community right now."

Williams says gun owners need to take better care of their firearms.

Williams says stolen firearms are falling into the hands of gangs of area criminals.
