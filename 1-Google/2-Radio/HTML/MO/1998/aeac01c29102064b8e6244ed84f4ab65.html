About KESM Radio 105.5 FM

Our Story

"Welcome to KESM 105.5 FM - Your Ultimate Music Destination!

At KESM, we're all about the music! From 5:30 until Midnight we're your go-to source for the finest in Country music. Tune in for a blend of your favorite Top 40 hits, the latest releases, and some cherished country classics you may not have heard in a while. Fridays are a special treat, featuring Oldies rock and roll chart-toppers from the 50s, 60s, 70s, and a dash of the 80s.


Don't miss our soul-stirring Bluegrass Music show every Sunday from 11 AM to 12 PM, where tradition and innovation harmonize in a musical journey you won't want to miss.

Here at KESM, expect the unexpected! With our eclectic mix, you never know what you're going to hear next.


Explore our website for more - from the Birthday Bash to our dedicated Personalities, Programs and Shows, and local news updates at 6:30 AM, 7:30 AM, noon, and 5:00 PM.


Serving the Tri-County area, we're proud to cover Cedar, St. Clair, Vernon, and portions of Barton, Bates, Benton, Dade, Henry, Hickory, and Polk counties.


Experience the rhythm of your community with KESM on 105.5 FM!"

Our Team

 "Meet the KESM Team

Our KESM FM team is a group of dedicated and skilled individuals who work tirelessly behind the scenes to bring you the best in radio entertainment. With a shared passion for music and a commitment to our community, our team members play integral roles in making KESM FM a beloved radio station. While their specific tasks may vary, they all share a common goal - to provide you with quality programming and a memorable listening experience. Together, we make the magic happen at KESM FM!"

Our Music

 "At KESM FM, our music selection is a harmonious blend of timeless classics, current chart-toppers, and hidden gems from a variety of genres. Whether you're a fan of Top 40 country hits, beloved country classics, or the soul-stirring sounds of Bluegrass, we've got your musical tastes covered. And remember each Friday we give a taste of past with our Oldies Rock and Roll. Our carefully curated playlists ensure that every moment you spend with us is filled with the best music, no matter your preference. Discover the perfect soundtrack for your day at KESM FM."
