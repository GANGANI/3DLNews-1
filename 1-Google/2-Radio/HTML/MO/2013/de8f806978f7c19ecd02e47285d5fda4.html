    KOPN Membership

    Supporters who donate at least $60/year are considered KOPN members. KOPN members receive voting rights for the annual Board of Directors election.

    KOPN is powered by your contributions. You make it possible for us to keep broadcasting  news, music, and entertainment. KOPN is a broadcast service of the New Wave Corporation, a tax-exempt 501(c)3 non-profit. Your donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
