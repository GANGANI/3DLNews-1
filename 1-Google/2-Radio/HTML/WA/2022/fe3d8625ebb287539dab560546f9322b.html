KEXP Live from Argentina

Live on KEXP from Argentina Sessions

In September, KEXP took our first trip to South America for a week of live performances and a remote broadcast from Buenos Aires, Argentina, with support from Argentina’s Ministry of Culture and the Kirchner Cultural Center. KEXP hosts Cheryl Waters, Albina Cabrera and DJ Chilly broadcasted live audio and video from the Kirchner Cultural Center, with live radio broadcasts all week and three Live on KEXP sessions per day on Tuesday September 20th through Friday September 23rd. Aqui y Ahora

Join KEXP for Aqui y Ahora, September 12 through October 14, a month dedicated to amplifying the contributions, resilience, and music of Latinx, Hispanic and Latino-identified communities worldwide. Tune in Monday, September 12th, to kick off Aqui y Ahora with special on-air programming all day long, with a focus on the local community in the PNW. Exclusive performances, stories, podcasts, and tons of great music throughout the month.
