Saturday, 1 June 2024

Jun 1st

5 p.m.

KEXP Rally: Ballard FC vs West Seattle Junction FC

KEXP Music Heals Stage (outside the Gathering Space) // 472 1st Ave N, Seattle, WA 98109

KEXP presents a pre-game rally before the Ballard FC vs West Seattle Junction FC match at Memorial Stadium on Saturday, June 1st from 5:00 to 6:30 PM. 

DJ Atticus will be spinning from the Music Heals stage in the courtyard outside the KEXP Gathering Space, so fans can hang out before the match, just a few blocks away in Seattle Center. 

For more information, visit goballardfc.com.
