What is Southern Miss Radio?

Southern Miss Radio is dedicated to playing the best new alternative/modern music available. Alternative pop, rock, hip hop, and other music in the cracks is well-represented here. Southern Miss Radio provides listeners an alternative to traditional commercial radio by providing access to local, regional, national and international artists marching to a different beat.
