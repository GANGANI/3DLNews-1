Marathon 2024 a Success!

"Listener supported" is a term you hear often on the air on 91.3. That's because over 90% of our funding comes directly from our listeners. The University of Hartford provides a tremendous amount of "in kind" support which greatly reduces the amount of money we need to raise over the air allowing us to keep our on-air appeals to just two times a year.
 
Our Spring drive ran from April 14-21. Pledges and online donations brought us past our goal of $35,000. Thanks to all who participated! If you didn't get a chance during Marathon week, it's never too late to donate online. In addition to other premiums, we are offering a newly designed WWUH T-shirt!
 
John Ramsey, General Manager (ramsey[at]hartford.edu)
