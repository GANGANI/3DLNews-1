College of Arts and Sciences

Critical Skills for 21st Century Success

From our robust core curriculum, to the most technologically advanced programs we offer, we believe that the best education is one driven by both passion and purpose. We seek real answers to tough questions, real solutions to difficult problems, and real insights to evolve important conversations. Each collaboration will bring you a step closer to turning what engages you most into a rewarding career.

Productive Careers and Meaningful Lives

The College of Arts and Sciences at the University of New Haven provides students with the foundation to shape their future so that they can lead both productive careers and meaningful lives. The arts and sciences have a tremendous impact on the world, including how we live, behave toward each other and nature, and work toward achieving the goals of a just society; how we think critically, create art, solve problems, and design innovative solutions; and how we communicate and connect with others and help our communities flourish.

The College of Arts and Sciences offers a diverse range of majors including actuarial science, art, communication, environmental science, graphic and digital design, interior design, political science, psychology, marine and environmental sciences, music, and music industry, among others, that are all designed to foster broad academic and interdisciplinary discovery.

As a student in the University of New Haven’s College of Arts and Sciences, you’ll balance theory with practice, gaining hands-on experience that prepares you for the future through opportunities for internships at major organizations, design and research projects in our cutting-edge facilities, and study abroad and community-engagement activities.

The Humanities

Classical disciplines for the modern minds, the Humanities form the core of a liberal arts education.

 See More

They are the fields through which we seek to understand, enhance, and document the human experience. The more deeply we dive into humanistic knowledge, the more we strengthen our capacity for self-expression, creativity, reasoning, critical thinking, and analysis - skills that make us better learners, better collaborators, and better people.

The Arts

The University of New Haven offers unparalleled offerings in the arts.

 See More

For both the serious artist and the creatively curious student, programs in the arts offer both B.F.A. and B.A. degree options. You can pursue programs that cover broad swath of the art world: performance on the stage, art galleries and museums, curating, teaching the arts, the science of recording, and the business of promoting performers, concerts, managing talent, contracts, music publishing, etc., as well as designing in two dimensions (graphic and digital design) and three dimensions (interior design).

Nationally Recognized Center for Career Development

All University of New Haven students have access to the many resources available through the University’s Career Development Center, which has been named one of the best in the nation by The Princeton Review.

From career assessments, networking, and job shadowing to on-campus interviews and salary negotiation, the Career Development Center provides the skills and connections to identify a meaningful career and an opportunity to pursue your passion.
