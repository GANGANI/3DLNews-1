Located in Lebanon, TN, WANT FM 98.9 and WCOR AM 1490 feature a Real Country format, blending a traditional sound of today's hottest new artist, with the greatest country classics.

Start each weekday morning off with MJ and the FM 98.9 Real Country Crew's contests such as Name That Tune, Opry Trivia, Who Is It and more.

Then it's Preston Martinez on the drive home with Drive Time Trivia, Buy it or burn it, The Real Country Flashback, and a whole lot more.

Each weekday morning is also highlighted by Coleman & Company. This hour long show features long-time local radio veteran Coleman Walker and a wide variety of guests discussing topics that affect the country, the state, and the local community.

Plus, catch live stock market reports, traffic reports, news, weather, sports and the best Real Country music weekday mornings and afternoons.

WANT/WCOR also carries a variety of specialty and original programs during the week and each weekend:

  • On Saturday morning Tim Hitt has the Classics Hour and the Top Ten at Ten.
  • Saturday nights veteran DJ Fantastic Fred heats up the airwaves with a mix of Motown and Blues.
  • God's Country airs on Sunday mornings featuring gospel music.
  • And on Sunday nights The Sutton Ole Time Music Hour features some of the best Bluegrass and Ole Time Music.

WANT FM 98.9 and WCOR AM 1490 carry a wide variety of sports programming including Lebanon High School Football and the High School Football Scoreboard Show, Cumberland University Sports, and Tennessee Titans NFL Football.
