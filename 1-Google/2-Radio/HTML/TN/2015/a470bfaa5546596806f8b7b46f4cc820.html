REAL SPORTS TALK

96.1 & 98.5FM / Sports 56 has been a Memphis institution for more than 50 years. From Dewey Phillips to George Lapides, WHBQ has always been the home of Memphis legends. Elvis Presley was first heard on WHBQ, and it has been innovation after innovation since then.

Now, Sports 56 is the leader in local sports, and has been for more than 20 years. No one has more local coverage, more local programming, and more informed and respected hosts than Real Sports Talk, Sports 56, 96.1 and 98.5FM.

Thank you, Mid-South, for making us one of the most recognized and respected stations in all of America!
