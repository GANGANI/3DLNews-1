Other Ways to Support WEVL

SHOP ONLINE, HELP WEVL

Did you know that you can help support WEVL just by using the Goodshop.com shopping portal to enter your favorite retail sites and buy goods and services online? We have partnered with Goodshop.com to raise funds for our station while giving you a great and easy way to save money shopping online. All you need to do is sign up and choose WEVL as your cause. By using the Goodshop shopping portal, you'll help us provide diverse music programming to the Mid-South!
SIGN UP

SHOP AT KROGER? YOU CAN SUPPORT WEVL TOO!

Help WEVL when you use your Kroger Plus card or alternate ID in store and online. Enroll in Community Rewards, and choose WEVL as your organization. Or use the Kroger app: menu - rewards - community rewards and add WEVL. It's free for you and helps us!
enroll today

VEHICLE DONATION PROGRAM

WEVL's Vehicle Donation Program is a great way to turn your vehicle that you no longer need into cash for the station. Call 877-938-5227 or fill out this form. You get a free tow anywhere in the U.S., a WEVL membership, and a possible tax deduction. We take cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, RVs and more!
