UFC President Dana White Tackles Sturgis Rally in Latest Video

Jerry PalleschiPublished: April 17, 2017
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Dana White won't ever forget his second trip to South Dakota as he was chased out of Mount Rushmore, rode in a hailstorm, and was in the middle of "The Wall."

In his YouTube series "Dana White Lookin' For a Fight" the UFC President has experienced some unique adventures. The crew visited Sioux Falls one year ago before the fights at the Sanford Pentagon and filmed an episode. That trip included a camping adventure that saw White a little out of his element.

Sioux Falls native Devin Clark was discovered by White on his trip and has three UFC fights under his belt in the last year.

Season 2 of the series has just started and the second episode that was released on Monday (April 17) features White in an element he is a little more accustomed to. Joined by former UFC fighters Matt Sera and Din Thomas, White and the gang took a trip to the annual Sturgis Motorcycle Rally.

Everything from sidecars on motorcycles to tattoos are discussed and shown during the 20-minute episode. The video is NSFW as the crew likes to swear and there is some partial nudity. Fair warning. Otherwise, it's pure entertainment.
