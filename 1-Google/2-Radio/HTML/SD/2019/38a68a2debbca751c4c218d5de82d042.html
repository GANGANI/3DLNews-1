The Bobby Bones Show: Bobby, Amy and Lunchbox Taped Their Episode Of Celebrity Family Feud

Bobby BonesPublished: February 18, 2019
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Last week, Lunchbox finally found out his big surprise from Bobby Bones. After months of anticipation, Bones revealed that Lunchbox would be taping Celebrity Family Feud along with Bones, Amy, Jon Pardi, and Lauren Alaina. Their Bobby Bones Team would be going up against Brooklyn Decker, Andy Roddick and their family.

After finding out the news, they were all flying out that weekend to go tape the episode that would air this summer on ABC Network. Thankfully for the rest of us waiting to see any snippets of the weekend, Bones, Lunchbox and Amy were all posting from the set fun photos and boomerangs with their matching outfits and pushing buttons.

The show has been taped, but we won't get to watch what went down on the show until sometime this summer. Once the official date has been released, we will be sure to post and share the news on all social media of The Bobby Bones Show.
