Drake, Chance the Rapper + More React to the News of Juice WRLD’s Death

Jacklyn KrolPublished: December 8, 2019
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Juice WRLD, Jarad Anthony Higgins, passed away after suffering from a seizure at a Chicago airport on Sunday (December 8).

The rapper just celebrated his 21st birthday on Monday (December 2). Celebrities and fans alike shared their reactions to the news of his death and paid homage to the late "Lucid Dreams" singer.

"I would like to see all the younger talent live longer and I hate waking up hearing another story filled with blessings was cut short," Drake posted on Instagram along with a picture of the rapper.

"He knows our hearts," Chance The Rapper wrote in a tribute to Juice WRLD. "I really wish we had more talks like that one night in LA. Dude. This is ridiculous. Millions of people, not just in Chicago, but around the world are hurting because of this and don’t know what to make of it. I'm sorry. Love you and God bless your soul."

Kehlani couldn't believe the news. "This sad as f--k," she tweeted. "Rest in love Juice WRLD."
