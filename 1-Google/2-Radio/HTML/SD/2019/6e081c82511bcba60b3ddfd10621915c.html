Monday, May 13th 2019 - 12:00 am

From short trips to long DREAM vacations, go with KMIT Trips to an ever increasing number of destinations!

Browse our trips page for for the perfect getaway!

Why should you travel with KMIT Trips?
We can save you time and MONEY!
Our group status gives us access to great rates and many times we skip lines at sites we visit.
Travel with an experienced host will provide a seamless and safe trip for the group. We will handle the details, you just bring your vacation mojo!
Make it your trip!
As a group, we see all the major features/sites, then we give you free time to see more specialized places.  We provide flexible schedules so you can venture out on your own, with our guidance.
Create new friendships with people who will share memories of an awe inspiring vacation with you.

FAQ

How do I sign up for a tour?
When you are ready to sign up, call KMIT at 605-996-9667.
If you have questions, call the guy who organizes all the trips.  His name is Steve!  Reach him at 605 857 0753 or email at crawford@wnax.com

When do I pay?
For flyaway trips, we generally like full payment.  However a 50 percent deposit is accepted, with full payment due 90-days prior to the trip.  Payment plans can be set up as requested.

Are there items not allowed on bus trips?
Alcohol and smoking are not allowed on the bus.

Do you provide refunds?
We offer trip assurance and traditional Travel Insurance in the event there is a medical emergency or death and you are unable to join us on a paid trip.
We understand sometimes life can get in the way of the best laid plans. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel a trip, we will provide the best possible outcome for everyone involved.

What hotels do you use?
We know how important it is to get a good nights sleep and be comfortable in your hotel room. We generally stay in Best Westerns and Holiday Inns and other similar hotels. We take into consideration location, guest reviews and onsite inspections.

Are meals included?
Many hotels we stay at provide breakfast. All other meals, unless noted, are not part of the trip — unless otherwise noted in the trip itinerary. We recognize there are many different tastes and budgets, we want your meals to be your choice.

Can I travel with KMIT if I need a walker or wheelchair?
KMIT can accommodate travelers of various levels of health and fitness. Our bus trips are equipped with transport chairs. Please let us know if you need special accommodations when you sign up.

Where do your trips depart from?
For fly away trips, we can arrange departure from the airport of your choice. Pricing is based on flights out of Omaha, Minneapolis, Kansas City or Des Moines.  In some cases, we can book a flight from an airport of your choice for no extra charge. Additional fees may be required and would be communicated before final booking.

For Bus trips, we can pick up at locations along the path of our trip.  It will be your responsibility to secure parking at locations other than Yankton.  However, we will provide “some” locations we’ve previously picked folks up from.

Can we fly first class?
All flights are booked economy class ticketing unless you have requested at booking that you want to upgrade.  Once the flight has been booked, you will no longer that that option.  Generally, choosing seats or special seating will cost extra.

Do you accept credit cards?
Yes!  Fees can be a big expense for us, so if you choose to pay with a credit card, there is a 3 percent fee. If you choose to pay by check or money order, there is no additional fee.

Overload Museum: Chronicles the period of the Allied landing until the liberation of Paris. The collection was collected by someone who was both a witness to the conflict and involved in the reconstruction of Normandy.
