How to Listen

While you can listen to Fox Sports at 1230AM, 98.1 FM and right here at kwsn.com, there are a number of additional ways to listen to us anywhere you are!

Listen on our FREE Mobile App

Download our app through the Apple App Store or Google Play store.

Listen on Your Smart Speaker

Alexa Enabled Devices

All you have to do is say the phrase: “Alexa play KWSN”

Google Nest Speakers

All you have to do is say the phrase: “Hey Google, play KWSN”

Apple Homepod Speakers

From an Apple device, open Apple Music then search KWSN. Choose KWSN from either TuneIn or iHeart and then AirPlay that to your Apple Homepod device.

If you’re having issues getting KWSN to play on any of your devices – drop us a note and we’ll give you a hand.
