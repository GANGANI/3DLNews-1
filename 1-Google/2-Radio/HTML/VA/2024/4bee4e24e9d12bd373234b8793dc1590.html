Orgy Spring 2024

written by Abi Starr on January 24, 2024, 12:51

The Spring 2024 WUVT organizational meeting is happening on February 1st, 7:00-9:00pm in Brush Mountain A in Squires Student Center!

This meeting is where our staff heads will share information on current projects, and you can become involved. We have multiple opportunities, including creating art for our merch designs, helping to run the station in our IT or engineering departments, and training to become an on-air DJ!

In addition, we are holding a station open house before the orgy! If you want to see what it's like inside a radio station, stop by anytime between 3:00 and 6:30 on February 1st!

This is the meeting to come to if you want to join WUVT, and are interested in anything that we do!
