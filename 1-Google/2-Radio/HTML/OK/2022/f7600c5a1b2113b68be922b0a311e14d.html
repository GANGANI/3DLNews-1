Foo Fighters Drummer Taylor Hawkins Dead At 50

Posted on March 26, 2022

Foo Fighters drummer Taylor Hawkins has died at the age of 50 years old. No cause of death or further details were announced by the band or Taylor’s family. Local reports state that he was found in his hotel room before the band was set to perform at a festival in Bogota, Columbia Friday night.
