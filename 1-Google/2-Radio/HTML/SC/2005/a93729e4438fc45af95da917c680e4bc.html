About 4HIM

Connect with 4HIM

Artist Facebook FacebookArtist Youtube YouTubeArtist Website Artist Website

4HIM has been about sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ through the powerful, incomparable medium of music since their beginnings singing with the well-known ensemble group, Truth. Since their formation in 1989, their harmonic convergence blended the best of tradition from past decades in Christian music with modern pop sensibilities and no-holds-barred, real-life lyrics. Christian radio 'got it' right of the box with the group's first No. 1 single, "Where There Is Faith," and audiences began to sit up and take notice of the fresh-faced foursome from down south.

4HIM managed to transcend any of those potential pratfalls, continuing to make music that not only met the needs of listeners but pushed the creative envelope as the mainstream pop music scene evolved into something a little less slick, less produced.
