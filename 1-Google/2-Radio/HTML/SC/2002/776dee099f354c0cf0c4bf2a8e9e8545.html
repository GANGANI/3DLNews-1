Find us on Alexa

Easy Radio is now on Alexa!

  • Open the Alexa app on your iPhone or Android and tap the menu icon in the top left.
  • Tap Skills & Games in the menu.
  • Tap the magnifying glass icon to search for Easy Radio Myrtle Beach.
  • Select the skill, then tap Enable to Use
  • Once enabled, just say "Alexa, open Easy Radio" to listen to Easy Radio!
