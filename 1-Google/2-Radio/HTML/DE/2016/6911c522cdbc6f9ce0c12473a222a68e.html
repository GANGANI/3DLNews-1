Tower Cam

Mounted 417 feet above the 93.7 WSTW broadcast studios in north Wilmington, the Citizen Corps Tower Cam delivers amazing views of the surrounding countryside. This is the highest vantage point in the State of Delaware, and on a clear day you can also see New Jersey and Pennsylvania from this location. Check back often to see approaching weather systems, sunrises and sunsets, city skylines and more.

The Citizen Corps Tower Cam is brought to you by Delaware Citizen Corps, Delaware Public Health and PrepareDE.org. A disaster can occur at any time… are you prepared? Make a plan, make a kit, stay informed. Visit www.PrepareDE.org for more information. You can also like them on Facebook and follow them on Twitter for the latest updates and alerts.
