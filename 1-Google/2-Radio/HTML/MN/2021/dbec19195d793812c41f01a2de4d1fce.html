New John Mellencamp Album Coming In January

Posted: December 13, 2021
Photo Credit: Tommy Sommers

John Mellencamp will be kicking off the new year with a new album.

Strictly a One-Eyed Jack will be out January 21st.  The latest single, “Chasing Rainbows”, is out now on digital and streaming platforms.

The record includes “Wasted Days”, a collaboration with Bruce Springsteen released back in September, and two more Mellencamp/Springsteen collabs, “Did You Say Such A Thing” and “A Life Full of Rain”.
