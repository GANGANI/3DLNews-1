Sports

KMGK is your home for area High School sports, as well as the Twins, Wild and NDSU Bison!

IT PAYS TO LISTEN!

Fall, Winter and Spring, tune in to KMGK for area high school sports. During each pre-game broadcast, the Kadejan Score, or Play of the Game, will be announced. When it occurs during the game, be the third caller and win a $10 certificate from the Kadejan Markets.

Also in each high school broadcast, a clue is given for the Famous Name Game. Collect all the clues from that week's games and be the first caller on Saturday morning during Value Plus Shopping to win a $25 certificate from The Walk In Closet in Glenwood. Two more great reasons to listen to KMGK high school broadcasts!
