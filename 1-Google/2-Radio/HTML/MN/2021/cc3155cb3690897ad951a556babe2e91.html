Life can be a BEACH!

By Kat May 21, 2021 | 7:20 AM

A lot of people have a negative feeling about last summer. The pandemic. Nothing was open. But, for us, we had an amazing summer. I learned a lot about what makes a beach visit easy and enjoyable.

It’s my goal this summer to be at the beach or pool by noon every day. I found myself taking 3 trips to and from the car to grab all of our beach must haves. I would pan around and wish I had what the other moms had.

I’ve done some digging for you and will get you to the beach in style and comfort for less than $140. It might sound like more than you were willing to spend BUT, you’ll never have to buy them again and you’re set for all the summers ahead.

First up and what I consider the most important:

To avoid the multiple trips to and from the car, this gem is a lifesaver. It also has cup holders and sand tires! The price, $70.
