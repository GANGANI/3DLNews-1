Brooke Knoll

Associate Digital Producer

bknoll@mpr.org

As a lifelong classically trained harpist, associate digital producer Brooke Knoll has always had a passion for classical music and sharing the stories behind the songs. Before joining YourClassical MPR in 2019, she was the on-air host of Radio K’s all-classical show, Rock Me, Amadeus. An advocate for arts accessibility, she has worked with the St. Paul Chamber Orchestra, Cuban American Youth Orchestra and Minnesota Orchestra on their initiatives to engage younger audiences and cultivate the next generation of classical music lovers. When not writing for the web or serving as board operator for Minnesota Orchestra live broadcasts, you can find her hiking in state parks or searching for vinyl to add to her growing collection.
