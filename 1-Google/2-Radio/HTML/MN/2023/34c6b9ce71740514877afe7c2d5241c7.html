MERRY KEITH-MAS On Bop Street

December 15, 2023 - Posted in General Announcements by Miriam X.

MERRY KEITH-MAS – a rip snorting, raucous, riff-filled celebration of Keith Richards’ 80th Birthday.

Join Pete Lee on Bop Street at 4:00 on Monday December 18th for an over-the-top two hours long delve into All Things Keith – The Human Riff’s contribution to classic Stones tracks, selections from his solo albums, collaborations with other artists, a stroll through his record collection, and who knows what else……  More fun than falling out of a palm tree!
