JOY Stories - Heather

Officer Heather Mundell was involved in a near fatal car accident. They were able to save her life but because of her injuries, she had to walk away from her lifelong dream of being a cop and take a desk job. She lost vision in one of her eyes but gained perspective in her life. Heather shares how she realized that she is much more than her gifts or assignment. She is a child of God.
