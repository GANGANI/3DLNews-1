Oconee Fall Line Technical College
    ___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Oconee Fall Line Technical College will host TECH on Tuesday information sessions on the third Tuesday of every month for those interested in enrolling in one of the college’s programs.

These events are designed to provide community members with opportunities to learn more about the college, tour a program or meet with an instructor, speak with an admissions counselor, or apply for financial aid and get help with the Federal Application for Student Aid (FAFSA).

The $25 application fee will be waived for those who apply during one of these sessions.

“These TECH on Tuesday sessions are designed to help all interested students with an easy transition into the college environment,” said Dr. Saketha Adams, OFTC’s Dean of Enrollment Management. “The thought of college can be overwhelming at any level and we want to make the transition as fluid as possible for those in our communities.”

These information sessions will be held in the Stewart Building on the South Campus in Dublin and in the Atrium on the North Campus in Sandersville. Sessions will be at 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM ont the third Tuesday of every month.
