WDUN, North Georgia’s News/Talk, brings you the best lineup of local and national talk shows, North Georgia’s largest and most trusted news team, up-to-the-minute weather and traffic reports and the best high school football, college sports and NASCAR.

Our local shows welcome your calls. Call the WDUN Studio Line at 770-535-2911 or toll-free at 800-552-WDUN (9386).

Click the title below to learn more about our shows. Click here for the full lineup on shows on WDUN AM 550, FM 102.9 and 94.5 FM The Lake.

Local programming (Weekdays)

  • Mornings on Maine Street (airs 5:30-9 a.m.)
  • The Martha Zoller Show (airs 9-11 a.m.)
  • The Drive at 5 (airs 5-6 p.m.)

National and Syndicated programming (Weekdays)

  • The Mike Gallagher Show (Airs 11 a.m.-noon and 8-10 p.m.)
  • The Todd Starnes Show (Airs noon-3 p.m.)
  • The Ramsey Show (Airs 3-4 p.m.)
  • The Ken Coleman Show (Airs 4-5 p.m.)
  • The Ben Shapiro Show (Airs 5-6 p.m.)
  • Rich Valdes' America at Night (Airs 10 p.m.-1 p.m.)
  • Coast to Coast AM (Airs 1-5 a.m.)
  • American in the Morning (Airs 5-5:30 a.m.)

Fridays during football season

  • The Friday Game Night Tailgate Show
  • The Friday Game Night Game of the Week
  • The Hardy Chevrolet Scoreboard Show
