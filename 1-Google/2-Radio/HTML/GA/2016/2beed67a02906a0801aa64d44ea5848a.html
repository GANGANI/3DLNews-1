Some of the nation's most popular talkers are talking right here on WDUN AM 550 and FM 102.9. Click the links below to learn more about these national shows in our lineup.

 

The Mike Gallagher Show (Airs 11 a.m.-noon and 8-10 p.m.)

Mike Gallagher is an American radio host and conservative political commentator. He is the host of The Mike Gallagher Show, a nationally syndicated radio program that airs throughout the United States on Salem Radio Network and is also a FOX News Channel Contributor and guest host. According to Talkers magazine, Gallagher is the sixth most-listened-to radio talk show host in the United States.

 

The Todd Starnes Show (airs noon-3 p.m.)

 A conservative columnist and commentator, Todd Starnes joined Fox News Radio in 2005, and now syndicated his show through his own network. His daily radio presence has reached millions of listeners on hundreds of stations, and his syndicated column is also read by millions. 

 

The Ramsey Show (airs 3-4 p.m.)

Dave Ramsey is an American financial author, radio host, television personality, and motivational speaker. His show and writings strongly focus on encouraging people to get out of debt. His radio show is heard on more than 500 radio stations throughout the United States and Canada. He has been on The Oprah Winfrey Show and 60 Minutes.

 

The Ken Coleman Show (airs 4-5 p.m.)

Through the years, Ken has interviewed leading experts in business, sports, entertainment and politics. Now, he’s here to help answer your questions about career, passion and talent so you can maximize your potential.

 

The Ben Shapiro Show (airs 6-7 p.m.)

The Ben Shapiro Show connects listeners with the news they need to know in the fastest-moving daily program in America. A voice for conservative millennials, Shapiro covers America’s most powerful political personalities.

 

The Charlie Kirk Show (airs 7-8 p.m.)

Charlie Kirk is the next big thing in conservative talk radio and he's now hosting a daily radio show called "The Charlie Kirk Show." Charlie Kirk is the Founder and President of Turning Point USA, a national student movement dedicated to identifying, organizing, and empowering young people to promote the principles of free markets and limited government. 

 

Rich Valdes' America at Night (Airs 10 p.m.-1 a.m.)

It’s a new talk for a new generation, introducing Rich Valdés America at Night! Rich brings late-night radio alive with the perfect blend of news, entertainment, interesting interviews, pop culture, and insight.

 

Coast to Coast AM (Airs 1-5 a.m.)

Coast to Coast AM airs on more than 600 stations in the U.S., as well as Canada, Mexico and Guam, and is heard by nearly three million weekly listeners. A media phenomenon hosted by George Noory, Coast to Coast AM deals with UFOs, strange occurrences, life after death, and other unexplained (and often inexplicable) phenomena.

 

America in the Morning (Airs 5-5:30 a.m.)

America in the Morning. hosted by John Trout, offers up-to-the-minute news reporting from around the country and across the globe, with the latest overnight developments in breaking news, politics, entertainment, business and weather, delivered first thing in the morning.
