The Morning Cruise Replay - War, Weekend, Weather, and Water

Monday, October 09, 2023


The Morning Cruise Replay - War, Weekend, Weather, and Water

Our hearts are heavy as we've been following the coverage of the news coming out of Israel with Hamas. We spent most of the morning discussing the horrible tragedy.

In more personal news, the weekend was all about the grandkids for Bill and Dave. As Bill's granddaughter spent the weekend with him and Dave's grandson had a "W"-themed birthday party. 

Bill shared an article looking at revival within Gen Z. 

Cooler weather prevailed this weekend much to our delight. 

Dave told us about the benefits from fruit-infused water. 

Carmen gave away more copies of Being Real > Being Perfect by Justin Davis. 

She also shared a parenting idea to help kids stay safe with a text code for their parents. 

Bill is looking forward to the opportunity to possibly see some fall foliage. 

For Dave, watching football this weekend had him thinking back to growing up and the first fire of the fall.
