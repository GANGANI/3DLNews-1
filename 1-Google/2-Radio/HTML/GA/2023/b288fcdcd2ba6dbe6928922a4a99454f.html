The Morning Cruise Replay - Heartbreaking to Heartwarming

Wednesday, October 11, 2023


The Morning Cruise Replay - Heartbreaking to Heartwarming

Today's show was a little heavy to begin with as we shared the news that former Olympic gold-medalist Mary Lou Retton is in the ICU battling a rare form of pneumonia. 

Staying in the world of gymnastics, Bill shared the more heartwarming news that Simone Biles recently returned to the podium with a win. 

We continue to see and hear the heartbreaking news from Israel and Gaza and our thoughts turned to wrestling with evil and a warning for parents that more images and stories would be coming. But a listener shared John 14 with us and a reminder of "Do Not Be Troubled". 

As we had discussed the Olympics earlier in the show, Dave was reminded of some new sports coming to the Olympic games. But are all these "events" actually sports? 

Carmen took a PPP (Personal Point of Privilege) to wish her husband a Happy Birthday! 

Bill told us the winner of Fat Bear Week and how Amazon is offering a new feature to 'subscribe and save'. 

It's fall and with cooler temperatures, we are in the mood for some heartwarming soup recipes.

02:56 Simone Biles wins again

10:11 Wrestling with evil

18:46 John 14 - Do Not Be Troubled 

20:49 New Sports in Olympics 

27:35 Breakin' 

28:28 Happy Birthday Pete! 

31:51 Fat Bear Week Winner 

34:52 Amazon Subscribe and Save 

40:05 Soup Recipes 

43:34 Bill's BIG Word
