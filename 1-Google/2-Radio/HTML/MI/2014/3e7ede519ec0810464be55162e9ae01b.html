Guy Loses It and Dances Naked Standing In His Tesla Sunroof [Video]

ClayPublished: April 24, 2014
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Have you ever had one of those days where you just want to strip naked and dance on the top of your car?  Neither have I, but that's what separates us from this guy.

People stopped to record the man stripping off his clothes in broad daylight on the Pacific highway in California.

He got completely naked and started to dance while standing in the sunroof of his Tesla.

Yes, he was driving a Tesla.  I guess it could have been worse, he could have been riding a Unicycle like this guy.

There was some confusion about whether his movements were karate or dancing but I guess that's really not the important thing here is it.

Firefighters said the man either suffered a mental breakdown or he was under the influence of a controlled substance.
