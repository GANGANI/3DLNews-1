Reporter Blows a Snot Bubble During the News, and That’s Not Even the Worst Part [VIDEO]

George McIntyrePublished: February 27, 2014
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

KTVU reporter Heather Holmes tweeted, "Note to self: Next time bring #Kleenex," after blowing out a string of snot during a live broadcast. As the snot (OK fine, 'Mucus') dripped down from her nose, it landed ... wait for it ...

.... wait for it ...

... in her mouth!

Coincidentally, the gaffe occurred while Holmes was covering a Miley Cyrus concert, and she noted that the crowd was just starting to "trickle out."
