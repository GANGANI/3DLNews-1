RULES:

One of the reasons Q107-WMQT is "Radio Put Together By You" is how we let you win all kinds of stuff. There are, though, a few rules that you need to follow. What follows are those rules for each of our daily contests (like “Movie Trivia” and “What's Up U.P.”). Other, bigger contests may have additional rules, and those will be posted along with details on that contest!


1. Unless otherwise stated, you must be 18 or older to win
2. There's no purchase necessary for any contest
3. Only one family member and one household member win each daily contest only every 60 days, except for the Donckers Double Scoop, which you can only win once a year.
4. Employees of Taconite Broadcasting, participating sponsors, and other Upper Michigan radio stations are not eligible to win.
5. Prizes can not be redeemed for cash, and are non transferable.
6. Any taxes are responsibility of the winner
7. Odds for each contest are determined by the number of entries
8. All decisions of WMQT judges are final.

Good luck, and get ready to win with radio put together by you...Q107-WMQT!
