‘Stairway to Heaven’ Played Backwards Reveals Led Zeppelin Are Satan’s Minions

Tree RiddlePublished: March 17, 2014
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Ok, not really. This Bible-thumping mustachioed gentleman would have you believe it though. Listen to Led Zeppelin's 'Stairway to Heaven' backwards here and see if they are really "Satan's puppets" for yourself.

By the way, I am aware that this video is super old and I may be the only one who just saw this for the first time. Instead of telling me how many years ago you watched this on Facebook, just know that you're cooler than me and go on with your day. Thanks.

Let me just say up front that I'm not really a religious dude and I think Zeppelin is awesome... even if they did somehow figure out how to put reverse satanic messages in the greatest rock epic ever composed. This is probably just a bunch of coincidental s---... after all, there's like 15 minutes of this song and Reverend Mullet only found a few sort-of reverse messages.

I've set the video to start when he starts playing the record, otherwise you'd lose interest in him babbling about the "whispering winds." Here are the alleged reverse lyrics revealed in the video (assuming this all wasn't a hoax of some sort):

Cause I Live with Satan
The Lord Turned Me Off
There's No Escaping It
Here's to My Sweet Satan
I Wanna Live It Backwards Like the Zep
Whose Power is Satan
He Will Give You 666

Eff that religious boner. Zeppelin rules!
