Cardi B and Fox News’ Tomi Lahren Exchange Jabs on Twitter

C. Vernon Coleman IIPublished: January 20, 2019
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Cardi B has been very vocal about politics in the last week or so, offering her thoughts on the whole government shutdown. On Sunday (Jan. 20), Bardi and right-wing political pundit Tomi Lahren got into a brief exchange on Twitter after Lahren tried to clown the Bronx rapper for her political views.

On Sunday morning, following an anti-Trump tweet from Cardi, Lahren chimed in. "Looks like @iamcardib is the latest genius political mind to endorse the Democrats. HA! Keep it up, guys! #MAGA2020," she tweeted.

Cardi didn't back down and offered the warning, "Leave me alone I will dog walk you."

Lahren agreed, but also tried to throw in a jab by replying, "I’m sure you would. Still doesn’t make your political rambling any less moronic. #BuildthatWall."

The "I Like It" rapper got the last word. "You’re so blinded with racism that you don’t even realize the decisions the president you root for is destroying the country you claim to love so much," Cardi added. "You are a perfect example on no matter how educated or smart you think you are you still a SHEEP!"

Last week, Cardi went off on President Trump over the government shutdown that has left many federal workers without pay.

"Now, I know a lot of y'all don't care 'cause y'all don't work for the government or y'all don't have a job," she said on Instagram. "But this shit is really fuckin' serious bro. This shit is crazy. Our country is in a hell hole right now. We really need to take this shit serious."

Her speech even caught the attention of some senators who contemplated retweeting the message.
