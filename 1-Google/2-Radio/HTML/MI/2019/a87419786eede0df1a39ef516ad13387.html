Young Thug Posts Photo in Hospital Bed

Tony M. CentenoPublished: September 5, 2019
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Young Thug has apparently faced some medical trouble over the last few days.

On Tuesday night (Sept. 3), the Georgia rapper posted an image of himself in a hospital bed to his Instagram Story. In the post, Thugger shows his followers the IV that was put into his left arm. He has several snacks by his side so it looks like he's doing okay.

"I think it's cause I'm #1 still," Thugger jokingly wrote in the overlaid text of his IG story, alluding to his recent Billboard accomplishment in the prospect.

It's unclear why Thugger was sitting in a hospital bed, but he's definitely been a busy man over the last few weeks as he's been hitting up the press circuit to promote his debut album, So Much Fun. The LP debuted at the No. 1 spot on the Billboard 200 chart last week. The 19-track album racked up at least 131,000 equivalent album units in the album's first-week sales. According to Nielsen Music, 5,000 of the overall sum consists of traditional album sales while the rest came from pure streaming activity.

XXL has reached out to Young Thug's team for comment on his current condition, but based on the fact that he's posted several IG stories since then, he's probably doing just fine. He's set to tour with Machine Gun Kelly and YBN Nahmir this month.
