Grand Rapids Will Be on ‘House Hunters’ Tonight!

ChristinePublished: July 17, 2019
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Beer City USA is quickly becoming one of the most sought-after cities to buy a home in, and that will be showcased in tonight's episode of House Hunters.

I stumbled across the news as I was scrolling through Instagram and saw that Martha's Vineyard, a neighborhood corner/grocery store, posted that they will be featured on the show.

After seeing that I went and found the episode guide on HGTV, titled "Room for Three in Grand Rapids," it reads:

New parents in Grand Rapids, Michigan, have discovered that as their family expands, their space has stayed the same. They're hoping to trade up from their small apartment into a house with plenty of room to grow but they can't decide between a turnkey home and one that needs a little work.

Personally, I'd go with the turnkey but we won't be able to play the guessing game until tonight.

House Hunters airs at 10 p.m. and the episode will be replayed at 1 a.m.
