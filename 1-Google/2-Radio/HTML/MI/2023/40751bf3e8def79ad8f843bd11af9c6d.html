Patron Giving

The Kresge Society

Demonstrate Excellence in Public Media

Kresge Society members are passionate about classical and jazz music. As a member, your gift to the community through 90.9 WRCJ will help promote our region’s artists, cultural organizations and educators, while introducing people to the world’s best classical and jazz music.

 Your contribution is tax-deductible within the limits prescribed by law.
