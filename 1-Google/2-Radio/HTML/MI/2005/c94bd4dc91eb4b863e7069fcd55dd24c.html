    About

    WCSG exists to encourage, empower, and build community in Christ.

    We are dedicated to encouragement, building community, and empowering you in your walk with Christ through Christian music and media. For 50 years and counting, it's been our mission to see more people come to know Christ, grow, and be encouraged as part of the WCSG community.

    Whether you tune in to one of our various radio stations throughout West Michigan, listen online, or on the WCSG app, we consider it a privilege that over 195,000 listeners want to spend time with us every single week.

    WCSG is commercial free, funded through listener support and our Business Partners, so you get more time listening to the music and content you love. Thank you for being part of our listening community!
