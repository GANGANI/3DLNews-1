  • Lions A 23-0 Winner In Buffalo

    The Lions earned a 23-0 win against the Bills on Thursday night in Buffalo. The Lions rested most of their starters and finished the preseason with a 3-and-1 mark. Kellen Moore, who played the final three quarters in relief of Dan Orlovsky, was 17 of 28 passing for 172 yards and two touchdowns. Rookie kicker Nate Freese made all three of his field goal attempts. Detroit finished with 10 penalties for 68 yards. The Lions must trim their 75-man roster to 53 by 4 p.m. on Saturday.
