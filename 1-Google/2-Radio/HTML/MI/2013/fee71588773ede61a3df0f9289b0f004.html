Radio wouldn’t be the same without 90.9 WRCJ — and 90.9 WRCJ wouldn’t be the same without you!

We rely on voluntary listener support to keep 90.9 WRCJ classical and jazz music alive on the radio. Individual donations from listeners like you represent the single largest source of support to our station.

When you donate, you become a member! Join or renew your membership now to support your favorite programs. We invite you to choose from a number of exciting donation gifts as a thank you for your support.

Matching Gifts

What if you could double your donation in thirty seconds? One in ten donors is eligible to have their donations matched.

Many employers participate in our Matching Gift program that will automatically double your gift to 90.9 WRCJ – whether you are a current employee or retired.

It’s as easy as 1-2-3 to check if you’re eligible!

  1. Search to find you whether your employer offers a matching gift
  2. Request a match using the form provided.

Patron Giving Options

The Kresge Society

Are you interested in providing a donation that assists 90.9 WRCJ’s mission to build music literacy and ensure access to the widest audience possible? A gift of $1,000 or more per year helps 90.9 WRCJ reach our listening community in varied and ever-expanding ways. Join like-minded donors in the Kresge Society for special learning opportunities and community building with your gift today.

Learn More

Chris Felcyn Legacy Society

The family of the late Chris Felcyn established the Chris Felcyn Legacy Society to ensure that Chris’s passion for accessible classical and jazz music reaches future generations. Please consider including 90.9 WRCJ in your estate plans. Download a Will and Intent form or contact Jenna Moritz, Director, Philanthropic Giving.

Additional Giving Options

Become a Sustainer

90.9 WRCJ Sustainers are members who agree to give a set amount to the station monthly. By joining this select group of people, you are helping the station continue to serve southeast Michigan for years to come.

Donate Now

Become a Day Sponsor

Is there a special person, organization or event that you’d like to honor? When you become a Day Sponsor, your personal 15-second message will be broadcast 6 times on the day of your choice.

Donate a Vehicle

Become a Corporate Sponsor

Give the Gift of Stock

Securities are an excellent way to make a one-time or annual gift. In most cases, you’ll be entitled to an income tax charitable deduction for the full fair market value of the securities on the date of the gift and will not have to report capital gain income.
