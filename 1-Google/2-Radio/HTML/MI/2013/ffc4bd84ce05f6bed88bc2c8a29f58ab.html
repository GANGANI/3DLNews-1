‘American Psycho’ With Huey Lewis and Weird Al [VIDEO]

If you're familiar with the 2000 film "American Psycho," you will love what the gang at FunnyOrDie.com has come up with. In honor of the 30 year anniversary of Huey Lewis & The News' album "Sports," Weird Al Yankovic and Huey Lewis himself have recreated a scene from the thriller and it's truly hilarious!

If you haven't seen "American Psycho," you should probably watch the original scene that is spoofed. It's a bit graphic and there is some profanity, definitely NSFW, but you can see it here.

Now sit back and enjoy one of the funniest spoofs I have ever seen!
