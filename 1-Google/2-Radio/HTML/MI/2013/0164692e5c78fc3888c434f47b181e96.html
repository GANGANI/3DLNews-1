BREAKING NEWS: Several People Stabbed At Lone Star College Near Houston, TX

Wendy ReedPublished: April 9, 2013
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

It's being reported that at least 14 people were injured (most of them stabbed) at a college near Houston, Texas. Investigators told Click2Houston that they are searching for two suspects.

Volunteer firefighters from the area were called out to a stabbing at Lone Star College,  around 11:20 a.m. College officials have said the incident happened at the health sciences building.

Paramedics told Click2Houston that at least four of the victims were transported to a nearby hospital via helicopter. Eight others were transported by ambulance, and two others refused medical care.

Officials from the school haven't said if the victims are staff, students, or a combination of the two.

This is the second time this year that Lone Star College has been in the news. Back in January, a shooting occurred on the campus as well.
