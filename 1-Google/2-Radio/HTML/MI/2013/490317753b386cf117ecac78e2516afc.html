Will Ferrell Hilariously Ejects Shaquille O’Neal From Lakers Game

LVPublished: February 13, 2013
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

I wasn't able to catch the funny moment of Will Ferrell hilariously ejecting Shaquille O'Neal from the Lakers game last night due to watching MSU beat University of Michigan in college basketball.  Anytime Will Ferrell is in front of a camera you know something funny is about to happen. 

The clip is only 30 second long but full a comedy.  The first thing you will find funny is Ferrell dressed a security guard for the Lakers games.  In costume and character, Ferrell doesn't crack a smile.  Gesturing to Shaq, Ferrell ejects the legendary NBA player from the stands.  While escorting him out, Ferrell even tries to handcuff the Shaq Diesel.

Check out the video clip below and remember, Will Ferrell is truly a king of comedy.
