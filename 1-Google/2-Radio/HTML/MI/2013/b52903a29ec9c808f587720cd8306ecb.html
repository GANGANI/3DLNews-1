Did Rihanna Cause a Brawl Between Two Australian Football Players?

Jessica SagerPublished: November 30, 2013
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

First Rihanna reportedly inspired the infamous bottle brawl between Chris Brown and Drake, both of whom romanced the 'Diamonds' singer. Now, the Bajan beauty may have sparked another bar fight -- between two guys she's never even met!

Two Australian football players got into a brutal battle at a Los Angeles night club, and rumor has it that the altercation was over meeting RiRi.

The Herald-Sun reports that two members of the Gold Coast Suns, an Australian Football League team, got into a bar brawl sparked by Rihanna's entry into the club they were patronizing.

Campbell Brown and Steven May, who play for the same team on the field, were bitter opponents at Bootsy Bellows this week. The pair went to the club with other teammates when they were all asked to leave because RiRi and her entourage were entering.

May reportedly tried to get back into the club in hopes of scoring an autograph from the singer. When he wasn't admitted back in, May allegedly grew belligerent and insulted fellow player Josh Gibson, who tried steering him away from the bar. Brown allegedly stepped in to stop May and Gibson from arguing ... and then decked May in the face, breaking his jaw.

Brown has been suspended and May was hospitalized. No word yet as to whether he ever got his autograph from Rih, but we're betting next time he'll just ask a little more nicely!
