Taylor Swift Wins Favorite Country Album + Favorite Pop-Rock Female Artist at 2013 American Music Awards

Jessica SagerPublished: November 24, 2013
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Congrats to Taylor Swift! The starlet, wearing a short, glittery gold dress, looked like a trophy -- and she took home two of her own!

Swift earned the Favorite Country Album award for 'Red,' as well as Favorite Pop-Rock Female Artist. In the latter speech, she acknowledged her crossover success.

"Amazing!" she gushed. "I want to thank country radio and also pop radio and also hot AC and AC radio, because the fact that all those genres played songs from my album lets me not worry about genre on my album. And to the fans ... to the covers on YouTube, when you make it your own. I love you, don't ever change!"
