Funeral Services Set For Man Killed In Suspected Electrical Accident

August 30, 2017

Authorities have identified a man believed to have died from an electrical accident while working at a local church this past weekend.

24-year-old Eric Joseph French of Holly passed away Sunday following the incident at Spirit Life Christian Church on M-59 between Hughes and Argentine Roads. Crews with the Howell Area Fire Department responded and found French in the attic rafters.
