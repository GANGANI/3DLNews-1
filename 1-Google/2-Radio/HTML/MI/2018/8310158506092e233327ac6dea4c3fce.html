Large

Just call me Large. Born in Mississippi and I’ve lived everywhere from Michigan to Texas to Alaska. Been doing radio for 32+ years. I can make my way around a kitchen and a grill AND I’M ONE OF THOSE AIR FRYER PEOPLE. I love dogs and I root for the underdog (Detroit Lions fan for life). I love my moms and my pops, going new places, breakup songs, Star Wars, a good cheeseburger, superhero movies, and bad guy wrestlers. I love to gossip about pop culture, but I am not a binge watcher (go figure). I thank God for Jesus and I know that I’m blessed. Still a recovering heathen but I’m a work in progress. Thanks for listening.
