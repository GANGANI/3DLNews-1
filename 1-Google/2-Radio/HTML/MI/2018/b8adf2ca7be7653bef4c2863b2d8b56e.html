TV 5 Reporter Engaged After ‘Surprise Breaking News’ Proposal [VIDEO]

George McIntyrePublished: July 11, 2018
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Meg McLeod thought she was going out to cover breaking news, but when she got there what she found was an engagement ring!

The whole crew at WNEM was in on the ruse as former TV-5 reporter Andrew Keller organized the scene at the firefighters' training tower in Saginaw. Meg's family and best friend were also there as Keller popped the question.

McLeod and Keller have been dating for four years. What took you so long, dude?

Keller left the station several months ago to pursue a career in real estate sales.

Not date for the couple's nuptials has been set. Congratulations to both!
