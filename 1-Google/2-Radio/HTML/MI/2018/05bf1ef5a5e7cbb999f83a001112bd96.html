50 Cent Questions Why Vivica A. Fox Brings Up Their Sex Life 14 Years Later

Peter A. BerryPublished: March 20, 2018
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Fourteen years or so after their fairly short-lived run as a pop culture power couple, details of 50 Cent and Vivica A. Fox's romance are still making their way into the news cycle. As has usually been the case, it was brought there by Fox herself, and Fif is none too pleased.

In a new NY Daily News story promoting her forthcoming self-help book, Everyday I'm Hustling, Fox describes elements of her old sex life with 50.

“Most of the time, I would be the one initiating sex, because I really enjoyed making love with him,” Fox wrote in her new book., before going on to describe the sex as being "PG-13" and “cherished and special.”

Responding in an Instagram post, 50 screenshotted the article and wrote about his dismay in the caption. "I’m waking up to this shit, that was14 years ago. smh who does this? What the fuck!" Fif wrote.

This isn't the first time 50 and Vivica have butted heads over something like this. Far from it. For years after their romance came to an end, the two traded shots, usually after Fox made a revelation about their romance together.

As of early 2017, Fox and Fif appeared to be on good terms again. Now? Not so much.
