About Sam Sessa

Sam Sessa is the Director of Events & Community Engagement for WTMD and WYPR. From 2006-2022, he was the host of Baltimore Hit Parade (formerly Baltimore Unsigned). He has been producing events and live broadcasts for WTMD since 2013. From 2005 to 2013, Sam worked as a reporter and editor in The Baltimore Sun's Features Department.
