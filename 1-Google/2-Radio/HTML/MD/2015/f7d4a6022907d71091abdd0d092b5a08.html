COMMUNITY RADIO

Your Connection to Careers and the Community.

WHFC is about much more than just great music— we're about supporting Harford County and showcasing the talents of our students and volunteers.

WHFC is designed for—and run by—the Harford County community. Focused on teaching and learning, WHFC is centered around a state-of-the-art Student Learning Lab. It’s open to anyone interested in radio, podcasting, the broadcasting business and more. It provides a fun, safe college radio experience with a constant focus on entertaining our listeners. 

Support College Radio!

Supporting 91.1 WHFC means you help our students in the on-campus Learning Lab, you support local college radio, and you support music of all genres for all listeners! It’s a win-win for you and for the community.
