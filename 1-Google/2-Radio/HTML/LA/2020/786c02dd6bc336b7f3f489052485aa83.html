KMRC Radio has served St. Mary Parish longer than any other radio station. The very popular Swamp Pop music format has proven itself to be one of the most popular for all ages. It is our music and local. No one else offers this 24/7. 

Because Swamp Pop isn't available on Satellite Radio or full time on any other stations, KMRC has been able to capture over 25,000 local & loyal listeners each week. Don Rich, Waylon Thibodeaux & many other artists have recorded liners & songs for KMRC.
