My Son Ties the Knot with His Best Friend in Gorgeous Hawaiian Wedding [VIDEO]

Erin McCartyPublished: May 23, 2016
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Lots of people pay big money for destination weddings in Hawaii. But some get lucky and have the gorgeous Hawaii beaches right in their own back yard. That's the case for my son and his now wife who are both in the Marines and stationed in Hawaii. They are planning a wedding for next year in her home state of Pennsylvania, but over this past weekend, they tied the knot in a spectacular wedding on my favorite Hawaiian beach of Lanikai. Here's the video of the incredible service. Take note of the ring bearer. So cute.

The sand at Lanikai is hard to describe. It is bright white and as soft as cotton. The water is so clear and blue. When you go out in the water, you can see your feet at the bottom. I can't imagine a more perfect setting for such a spectacular ceremony. I'm so proud of my son and wish them a lifetime of happiness.
