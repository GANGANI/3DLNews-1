Bernadette Lee

She has been in radio for more than 30 years, all here in the Lafayette market. She started working in radio shortly after being hired for a news internship in 1994. In April of 2023, she began hosting Mornings with Chris and Bernie. She grew up in Lafayette and loves Acadiana. She loves radio, music, news, politics, and talking! When she's not cracking jokes, poking fun at herself, or giving the public their daily dose of news, she's at home spending time with the world’s greatest dogs, Gracie and Tony. She is passionate about family, friends, the community, news, music and movies.
