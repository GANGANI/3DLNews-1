The Radio Shopping Show: Save Big on Everyday Purchases

How It Works:
The Radio Shopping Show lets you save up to 60% or more on daily products and services. Tune into WRMN-AM and call 847-931-1410 to enjoy discounts on dining, entertainment, travel, auto services, and more.

Why Such Great Deals?
Our unique barter system allows businesses to trade their products and services for advertising. This results in significant discounts, new customers for local businesses, and great savings for you.
