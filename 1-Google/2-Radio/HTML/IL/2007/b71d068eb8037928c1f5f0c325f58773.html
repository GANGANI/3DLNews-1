About WGRN

WGRN is a non-commercial radio station, operated by Greenville University students. Our music spans the spectrum of sounds, from faith-based music in the morning; a variety of contemporary, indie, and alternative music during the day; a special throwback program every day from 4 to 6 PM; rock music in the evening; and hip hop at night. We also air a wide variety of programs, including many student produced shows, Panther sports, GU chapel, Focus on the Family, and the weekly Greenville Free Methodist Church service.

We’d love to hear from you! Email us at 895wgrn@gmail.com or find us on the socials!
