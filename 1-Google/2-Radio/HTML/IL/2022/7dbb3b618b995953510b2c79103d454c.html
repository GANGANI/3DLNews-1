About Moody Radio Chicago

Moody Radio Chicago is the flagship station of Moody's 35 owned and operated radio outlets and hundreds of affiliates that broadcast Moody Radio programming. A ministry of Moody Bible Institute, WMBI debuted in 1926, making it one of the first radio stations to exclusively air Christian programming. It has been a pioneer in ministering to listeners in Chicago and around the world.
