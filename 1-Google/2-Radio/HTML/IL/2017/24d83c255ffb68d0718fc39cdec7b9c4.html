Lineage

Host: Adam Ramdin

Lineage is a multi-faceted educational tool designed to help you understand your spiritual heritage and explore the links between the past, present and future. History shapes identity; identity defines mission and a clear mission determines the trajectory of your entire life. So sit back and enjoy the journey; revolution is a mouse click away.

About Adam Ramdin

Adam currently serves as Youth Director for the North England Conference of Seventh-day Adventists. He has a passion for young people and seeks to equip them to make bold, intelligent decisions for Christ. He sees Lineage as another avenue through which to do just that. When not serving the youth of the NEC Adam may be found careering down the slopes on his snowboard.
