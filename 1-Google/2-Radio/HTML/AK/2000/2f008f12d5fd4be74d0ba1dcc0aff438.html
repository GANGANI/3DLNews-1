KSKA 91.9 FM in Girdwood

By
Kristin Spack
-
February 23, 2011

KSKA engineers will be traveling to Girdwood today to resolve the issue with signal outages on 91.9 FM . If you can’t hear KSKA on your radio in Girdwood right now, you can listen online. KSKA will be back on-air in Girdwood soon.

Thanks for your patience and understanding.

UPDATE: As of Thursday 2/24 12:00 pm, KSKA is back on the air in Girdwood.
