The Morning Cruise Replay - Deserving of a Medal

Friday, July 23, 2021


The Morning Cruise Replay - Deserving of a Medal

As the Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games get underway, we highlighted a few people deserving of a medal today on The Morning Cruise. 

Dave knew that today would be special. One daughter-in-law was at the hospital getting ready to give birth and another daughter-in-law is being induced today. And just as soon as he mentioned it, he had news to share! 

With one grandson here, we're all on babywatch with Dave for grandson #2. 

Rest can be a difficult thing to achieve. But according to an online quiz, Bill is doing pretty good in at least one of seven categories. 

Carmen shared the sad news that the legendary FSU football coach, Bobby Bowden, has been diagnosed with terminal pancreatic cancer. 

Come February 2022, we won't be getting any medals for making the podium, but we will be coming together as Team Freedom to celebrate a milestone as we continue to raise money and awareness in the fight against human trafficking. 

Carmen decided to see how she would place on Bill's rest quiz as she shared her results. 

To help with your rest, at least of the spiritual variety, Bill shared from Matthew 11. 

Dave got the first pictures of his grandson, Loren, and once he gets the go ahead, we will share them with you. 

And just before heading for the door on their vacation, Bill gave us his BIG Word of the Day.
