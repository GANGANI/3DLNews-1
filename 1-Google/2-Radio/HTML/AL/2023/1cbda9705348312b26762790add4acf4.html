Operation Christmas Child 2023 National Collection Week Begins - Various

Monday, November 13, 2023, 12:00 AM

Group: Homepage Events

The 2023 National Collection Week for Operation Christmas Child, an outreach of Samaritan's Purse in which shoeboxes full of toys, school supplies, and other items are sent to children around the world, will begin on Monday, November 13 and continue through Monday, November 20.  There are a number of collection sites around the Faith Radio broadcast area, and for more information, you can visit the Samaritan's Purse website.
