Gene Murrell

Scheduled on

On Air 2:00 pm 6:00 pm
  • 2

Catch Gene on the air Monday thru Saturdays from 2pm- 6pm on 92ZEW. He Brings you the best of the local music scene in Mobile with  Area 251.  Gene kicks off the ride home with fun facts, the latest traffic and weather, Kelly Finley joins Gene at 4:35 pm to Tell Us Something Good then it's The 5 o'Clock Traffic Jam!
Gene came to 92ZEW in 2006 from Hilton Head Island, SC where not only worked in radio there but was also a professional musician. Gene grew up in Winter Haven, Fl and moved to Los Angeles when he was 21. Soon thereafter he began making his living as a musician and an actor.  He first started playing summers on Hilton Head in 1988 and moved there full time in 1998. Since moving to Mobile, Gene has been very active in the local music scene playing at many venues around the area including Dahlia’s Dueling Piano Bar for many years. Gene is also involved with the local theatre scene, performing in many productions over the years including “Fiddler On The Roof” (Teyve), “Cat On A Hot Tin Roof” (Big Daddy) as well as directing many award winning productions including “Assassins” at MTG, “Young Frankenstein” and the Alabama Community theatre premiere of “Escape To Margaritaville” at the Joe Jefferson Playhouse. Gene is also a history geek and avid drone pilot.
