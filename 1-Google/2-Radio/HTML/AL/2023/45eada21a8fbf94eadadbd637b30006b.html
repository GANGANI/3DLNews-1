Morning Show: Easter Eggs in the Dorm

Posted by Noah Haynes | Apr 5, 2023 | Entertainment x News, Feature Display, Morning Show | 0 |

Ahead of Easter this Sunday, the Wednesday Morning Show team imagined where they would hide eggs for an Easter egg hunt in their own dorm rooms. Here are the results.

RICHARD

Richard would hide his egg behind this light in his room. Being able to easily life and lower the plastic shield makes it a perfect spot.

CHRISTIAN

Christian would hide his egg beneath his jackest and cotes, but atop this blue tote. Only a pro egg hunter would think to look here! 

NOAH

Finally, Noah would hide his egg in his bahtroom cabinet. Often the source of an extra roll of toilet paper, this would be an EPIC hiding spot for an unsuspecting egg.
