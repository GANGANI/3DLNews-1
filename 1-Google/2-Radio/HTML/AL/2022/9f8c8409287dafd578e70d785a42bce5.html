Hey everyone, it’s me, Paula C.!

I have been in radio since 1996 and with Gradick Communications for a lot of those years, but most recently, I have been back on WKNG in the afternoons from 2-5:30 for the past eight years.

I love spending time with family and friends, going to Auburn football games, and working in my garden.  My favorite country artists are Garth Brooks, Reba McEntire, Chris Stapleton, George Strait, and Trisha Yearwood.
