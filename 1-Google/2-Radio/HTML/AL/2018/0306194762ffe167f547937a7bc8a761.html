The JOY FM Sports

Listen to The JOY FM Sports

The JOY FM Sports covers high school athletic events in Alabama. In addition, we air National Christian Sports Talk on our very own 24/7 streaming channel on The JOY FM Alabama App.

The JOY FM Sports Radio Team:

Play-by-Play - Matt Johnson
Football Analysis - Mike Lawson
Sideline Reporter - TJ Haywood
Rules Expert - Chuck Locke
Producer - Cory Bradley
Social Media Director - Broxton Gannon
Production/Technical Coordinator - Russell Brooks


The heart of this ministry is to show how God works in and around sporting events. We believe “It’s More Than A Game!” Our broadcasts are different than what you normally hear from a sports broadcast. Here, we strive to glorify and honor God. We pray throughout the broadcast for the school and families that support them. We honor those who truly show the love of Christ in their school in an effort to bring our listeners JOY.

 Check out what one listener wrote us:
“I am from Flomaton, AL where the playoff football game was broadcast from, on Friday. I couldn’t pick up the broadcast from the Flomaton radio station, so I listened to your broadcast. I would like to recognize the two commentators that called the game. That was a great call on the football game but most of all to take the time to pray for the players when they were hurt on the field made this broadcast the best I have ever listened to. And at the end of the game when they prayed for the Padgett family was nothing short of an amazing job. Thank you for all the prayers for the players as well as the Padgett family. You have a faraway listener from here on out.”

 Connect with us!! We are on Facebook and Instagram @thejoyfmsports. In addition, you can hear past broadcast we have done on our SoundCloud page.
