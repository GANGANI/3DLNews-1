WATCH: Carrie Underwood on NBC’s “Today Show”

By Ian Sep 16, 2023 | 3:16 PM

Carrie Underwood stopped by NBC’s Today Show earlier this week as part of their concert series. Along with performing some of her hits including Before He Cheats, she sat down with Hoda Kotb and Jenna Bush to talk about her new album, life as a mom and how she limits her kid’s technology time, and helping emergency workers.
