Together We Can Make Miracles Happen!

The Cares for Kids Radiothon raises thousands of dollars each January for Sanford Children’s Hospital but the need continues throughout the year. That’s why we’re partnering throughout the Fargo-Moorhead-West-Fargo community on Miracle Mondays. This miracle of a promotion encourages you to patronize local businesses with a portion of their sales going directly to benefiting the CMN Sanford Children’s Hospital right here in Fargo!

All you have to do to get involved is to support the local businesses listed below on Mondays and you have done your part! Plus, each business will provide you with amazing customer service, a great product and they will be donating a portion of your purchase! It’s that easy to save a life!

What is Children's Miracle Network?

Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals helps children by raising funds and awareness for children’s hospitals across North America. More than 170 Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals care for millions of children each year.

Sanford Children’s Hospital in Fargo, ND, is the region’s only designated children’s hospital. 100% of every gift supports the good that is felt and seen from magical spaces to lifesaving equipment, family and patient services, research and more. It all adds up to help retain the region’s most comprehensive pediatric and neonatal intensive care units, maintain the state’s only Level II pediatric trauma unit, and deliver word-class care to thousands of children and families each year.
