How to Listen

While you can listen to Y94 at 93.7 FM and right here at y94.com, there are a number of additional ways to listen to us anywhere you are!

Listen on the Y94 App

Download our app through the Apple App Store or Google Play store.

Listen on Your Smart Speaker

Alexa Enabled Devices

All you have to do is say the phrase: “Alexa, play Y94 from TuneIn”

Google Nest Speakers

All you have to do is say the phrase: “Hey Google, play Y94 from TuneIn”

Apple Homepod Speakers

From an Apple device, open Apple Music then search Y94. Choose Y94 from either TuneIn or iHeart and then AirPlay that to your Apple Homepod device.

If you’re having issues getting Y94 to play on any of your devices – drop us a note and we’ll give you a hand.
