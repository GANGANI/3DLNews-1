The Scariest Kitchen Contest

Bob 95 FM and Showplace Cabinetry Design Center Are Looking For The SCARIEST Kitchen!

We want to give your scary kitchen a huge makeover….valued at $15,000!

Horrible counter tops? Out of date and broken cabinets? The scarier the better!

Turn your scary kitchen into a Showplace…with Showplace Cabinetry Design Center and Bob 95 FM.

Submit your photo before Oct 18th. We’ll open the voting on October 19th and announce our 5 finalists on October 25th.

The final winner will be announced on October 31st at 7:20am.

THE GRAND PRIZE: The grand prize winner, subject to verification, will receive a kitchen cabinet and countertop makeover voucher that is only eligible towards $10,000 in Showplace Cabinetry brand kitchen cabinets and $5,000 towards countertops that are available through Spaulding Stone (“Grand Prize”). The winner is eligible to deduct $10,000 from the retail price of Showplace Cabinetry brand kitchen cabinets. The winner is eligible to deduct $5,000 from the retail price of stone or quartz countertop available through Spaulding Stone. The winner is responsible for any applicable state or federal taxes that may apply. Winners may exceed the cabinet or countertop allowance but will be responsible for any overage.
