In conversation with Herbie Hancock

By Matt Fleeger (OPB) and David Stuckey (OPB)
Herbie Hancock in California studio Sept. 13, 2023 6:39 p.m.

A seminal discussion with a music legend who refuses to close the door on creativity.

Herbie Hancock is so much more than the world’s greatest living jazz musician. His storied career includes several platinum albums, crossover hits on the billboard charts, a war-chest full of Grammy awards and even an Oscar.

At 83, he’s as sharp as ever – whether it’s working with Flying Lotus, Thundercat, Terrace Martin or DOMI and JD Branch – and he’s still at the forefront of modern music. We caught up with Herbie at his studio in LA to talk about his upcoming tour, including his performance in Oregon at McMenamin’s Grand Lodge on Sept. 19.

Listen to the conversation by clicking play, above. This is the first in a series of talks between KMHD and Herbie Hancock, with more to follow in 2024.
