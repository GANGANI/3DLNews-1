All Classical Radio & ICAN in the news

Posted on January 27, 2022

All Classical Radio thanks regional and national news and media outlets for recognizing our outstanding programming, innovative initiatives, and wide-reaching community service.

Read on for press coverage on the Recording Inclusivity Initiative, International Children’s Arts Network, and more of All Classical Radio’s radio, community, and arts programs.
