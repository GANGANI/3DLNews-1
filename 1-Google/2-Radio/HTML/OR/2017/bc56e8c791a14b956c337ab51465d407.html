Contribute

 

Support KWAX Radio

As a listener-supported service of the University of Oregon, KWAX relies heavily on gifts from listeners like you to maintain our classical music format. Donating to KWAX takes less than five minutes online or you can send a check. And now, KWAX is able to accept your donation of a car, boat, motorcycle, or RV.

Become a KWAX Underwriter-Join dozens of local arts groups, professionals, and businesses in the KWAX Underwriting Program. Effective and affordable, KWAX offers businesses and arts groups access to its outstanding local and syndicated programming throughout the day and week. To learn more about the KWAX Underwriting Program, call during business hours at (541) 345-0800 or email kwax@uoregon.edu.
