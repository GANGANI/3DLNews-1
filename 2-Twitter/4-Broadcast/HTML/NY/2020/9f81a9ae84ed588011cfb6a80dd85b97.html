Medical Group: More Than 500 Patients' Data Exposed in Cyber Attack

By Spectrum News Staff Albany
PUBLISHED 8:34 AM ET Feb. 20, 2020 PUBLISHED 8:34 AM EST Feb. 20, 2020
SHARE

Community Care Physicians medical group released more information about a cyber-attack in December that involved patient information.

The attack targeted BST, an accounting firm that works with CCP.

That firm said no financial information or medical records were included in the affected files.

BST said that more than 500 patients’ data was exposed but, at most, their names, dates of birth, and insurance information were compromised.

The company sent out letters Friday to anyone whose data was affected and is offering one free year of "identity monitoring" for these individuals.

For more information, call BST's assistance line at 866-977-0784.
