Selena’s favorite products from the Our Place Food Lover’s Sale

Beautiful, thoughtfully designed and multipurpose, Our Place’s Always Pan was an instant social-media favorite upon its release in 2019. Our Place makes its cookware from enameled aluminum and cast iron with the intent of providing high performance in an all-in-one piece. Its curated cookware multitaskers save shoppers money and also save space in their cupboards — assuming the beautiful pans don’t stay out on display.

Part of Our Place’s appeal comes from its safety. The brand’s ceramic nonstick coating is free from the potentially toxic materials sometimes found in nonstick cookware, including per-and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS), lead and cadmium.

Ultimately, the aesthetic of Our Place cookware is responsible for its skyrocketing popularity. The trendy shapes and vivid colorways look beautiful both in the kitchen and on your Instagram feed, making cooking more fun and inviting.

Selena Gomez x Our Place

Selena Gomez partnered with Our Place to celebrate the joy of cooking and dining with loved ones. Her ideas for her collaboration included smaller sizes of the popular Always Pan and Perfect Pot, allowing home cooks to cook more efficiently and with greater versatility. She also added a touch of glam to the previously monochromatic cookware by adding gold-brushed knobs.

Selena’s collaborations also added four limited-edition colors to products such as the Always Pan, Perfect Pot and Knife Trio:

  • Azul, an electric blue
  • Rosa, a bold raspberry that celebrates her popular cosmetics line, Rare Beauty
  • Cielo, a soft sky blue paired with the new gold-tone knobs
  • Tierra, an earthy brown, also paired with gold hardware

Best of all, shopping Selena’s collaboration pays it forward. Ten percent of profits from the sale of these colors goes to her Rare Impact Fund, a non-profit helping young people access mental health services.
