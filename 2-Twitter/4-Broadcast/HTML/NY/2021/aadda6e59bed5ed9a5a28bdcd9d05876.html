PREVIEW: No. 23 Gannon Continues Road Swing at Roberts Wesleyan Tuesday Night

 Game Details  Tuesday, January 26, 2021 | 7:00 p.m. | Voller Athletic Center
 Records  No. 23 Gannon (3-0) | Roberts Wesleyan (2-1)
 Game Coverage  Live Video – ECC Network | Redhawks Sports Network

ERIE, Pa. -- No. 23 Gannon continues its two-game road swing with a trip to Roberts Wesleyan on Tuesday night. Tip-off is set for 7:00 p.m. No spectators will be allowed into the Voller Athletic Center. 

FASTBREAK POINTS
  • Gannon improved to 3-0 after topping Mercyhurst, 68-44. It marks the first time the Golden Knights have started a season 3-0 since the 2014-15. 
  • The contest will mark just the second all-time meeting between these two programs and first since the 2000-01 season.
  • Gannon entered the D2SIDA Top 25 Poll last week at No. 23 after receiving votes in the previous week. The Golden Knights are also slotted No. 3 in the D2SIDA Atlantic Region poll. 
  • Sam Pirosko (Eastlake, Ohio/Eastlake North) came off the bench led all scorers in the win over Mercyhurst last week with a career-best 17 points on 6-of-9 shooting from the field. She also added in a season-high six rebounds and two assists while logging a career-high 25 minutes. 
  • Emily Poling (Defiance, Ohio/Defiance) notched her 14th double digit scoring performance of her career after netting a season-best 15 points to go along with seven rebounds and team-high three assists. 
LAST TIME OUT
  • Sam Pirosko (Eastlake, Ohio/Eastlake North) led all scorers with a career-high 17 points and Emily Poling (Defiance, Ohio/Defiance) chipped in a season-best 15 points to guide No. 23 Gannon past rival Mercyhurst, 68-44. Sydney Mitchell (Baltimore, Md./Seton Keough) was the only other Knight to reach double digits in scoring with 11 points to go along with four rebounds, one assist and one steal.
  • The contest marked the 94th all-time meeting in a rivalry that dates back to 1974-75. Gannon has won the last four meetings and leads the all-time series, 62-30.
SCOUTING THE REDHAWKS
  • Roberts Wesleyan enters the week with a 2-1 overall record and most recently cruised past Clarion, 70-52, last week. The Redhawks are members of the East Coast Conference and finished 2019-20 with 15-13 (8-8 ECC) overall record. 
  • Roberts Wesleyan is led by head coach Kevin Clifford. He has compiled a 32-31 overall record over three seasons at the helm.
  • Dimitra Gkizani leads the Redhawks on both ends of the floor with a team-best 12.3 points and team-high 9.3 rebounds per contest. She's reached double digits in scoring in all three games this season, including a double-double against Bryant & Stratton with 15 points and 12 rebounds on Jan. 12. 
SERIES HISTORY
  • This will mark just the second all-time meeting between these two programs and first since the 2000-01 season. In the only meeting in 2000-01, Gannon topped Roberts Wesleyan, 101-60, at Hammermill Center. Jacki Wesleyan led the Knights setting a program record with nine made three pointers in the contest. 
  • This season will be the first time Gannon makes the trip to Roberts Wesleyan.
MILESTONE WATCH
  • Head coach Cleve Wright is four wins away from reaching the 300 mark. He has totaled 296 wins over 15 seasons. 
  • Tori Obenrader (Fryburg, Pa./North Clarion) is two points away from reaching 800 career points with 798. She is also 15 rebounds away from joining the 500 rebounds club with 485. 
  • Sydney Mitchell (Baltimore, Md./Seton Keough) is closing on her 100th career game as a Golden Knight. She has appeared in 93 career games (66 starts) over four seasons. 
LOOKING AHEAD
  • Gannon returns to Hammermill Center on Friday against Daemen. Tip-off is set for 6:00 p.m. The Knights will then wrap up the two-game home-and-home series traveling to Daemen on Sunday. Tip-off is set for noon.
