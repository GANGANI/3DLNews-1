Discover the Food Scene in Rochester, New York

The culinary culture in Rochester, New York is rich with deep roots. We know, because the team here has been writing about it for years. This site is a collaborative effort of local food writers, podcasters, reviewers, and photographers.

If you’re looking to know more about food in Rochester, this site should prove a worthy guide. Join us at the table!
