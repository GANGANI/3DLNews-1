Bordas & Bordas WTOV9 High School Football Awards Show airs Thursday

by NEWS9 Sports

Thu, December 16th 2021 at 11:40 PM
Updated Thu, December 16th 2021 at 10:18 AM
The 2021 Bordas & Bordas WTOV9 High School Football Awards Show airs 7 p.m. Thursday on FOX9 and WTOV9.com


    TOPICS:

    FlorenceFlorence,Province of Florence,ItalyBELMONT SAVINGS BANKBurtonHammClairsvilleRyan McVickerJohn MarshallLucasLaneJohn MarshallSpenserOstovich

Thursday night at 7 p.m. on FOX9, the 16th annual Bordas & Bordas WTOV9 High School Football Awards Show will hit the air. For the second consecutive year, the program will have to originate inside the WTOV9 studios.

Award winners in eight different categories will be announced in the 30-minute program.

The players who are up for the awards were first nominated by their head coaches, and those nominations were then narrowed down to the final lists by a coaches and media panel voting process.

Winners in each category involving area players on Thursday night will be a result of ballots sent to all area coaches, along with area media, and returned by a Monday, December 13th deadline.

Below are the players who are up for awards on the 16th annual Bordas and Bordas WTOV9 High School Football Awards Show, which again airs at 7 p.m. Thursday on FOX9, wtov9.com and the WTOV NEWS9 Facebook page.
