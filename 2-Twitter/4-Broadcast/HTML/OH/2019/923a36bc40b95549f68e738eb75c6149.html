Local golfers take advantage of warm weather

by WSYX/WTTE

Fri, December 27th 2019 at 10:00 PM
Updated Fri, December 27th 2019 at 10:13 PM

COLUMBUS, Ohio (WSYX/WTTE) — Thursday’s unseasonably warm temperatures gave golfers a rare chance to play a round in December.

Local golf courses like Raymond Memorial Golf Course in West Columbus were packed.

The team there said 147 rounds of golf were played at Raymond Memorial Golf Course Thursday. Typically, they see one to two rounds a day this time of year.

Right down the road, the nine-hole Wilson Road Golf Course had 86 rounds played.

With more warm weather expected, the staff is expecting another big day on Friday.
