Suspect arrested for allegedly shooting man near Bexley

by Chris White

Thu, December 26th 2019 at 9:33 AM
Carlos Garrett was booked into Franklin County Jail after allegedly shooting a man near Bexley on Christmas Eve, Dec. 24, 2019. (Franklin County Jail)

    TOPICS:

    Mass Shootings In The United States

COLUMBUS, Ohio (WSYX/WTTE) — A 26-year-old man is facing assault charges after he allegedly shot a man near Bexley on Christmas Eve.

The shooting happened in the 2600 block of Avalon Place around 1:30 p.m. Tuesday.

Police arrested Carlos Garrett after they say he shot 27-year-old Christopher Jackson.

Garrett was taken to the Franklin County jail before he appears before a judge. No word on Jackson's condition.
