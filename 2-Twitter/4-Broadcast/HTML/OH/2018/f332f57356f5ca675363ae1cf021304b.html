Steubenville Christmas Parade set for Saturday

by Kate Siefert

Fri, December 7th 2018 at 2:47 PM

Starting at noon on Saturday, the Eastern Gateway Sights and Sounds of Christmas Nutcracker Magic Parade will make its way down 4th and Market Streets.

    TOPICS:

    Illuminated ProcessionOhioParadeSanta Claus ParadeSteubenville, OhioStreet CultureThanksgiving ParadesUrban Geography

STEUBENVILLE, Ohio — Steubenville will hold its Christmas parade this weekend.

Starting at noon on Saturday, the Eastern Gateway Sights and Sounds of Christmas Nutcracker Magic Parade will make its way down 4th and Market Streets.

If you can't be there you can watch the whole thing live on WTOV9, or on WTOV9.com

"It will be great to see on TV, but it is even better in person because you can really hear the music and see the costumes and all of the detail that the entrants have put into their floats,” said Judy Bratten, executive director, Fort Steuben. “Plus, right after, you can just walk a block and go to the Nutcracker Village."

Parades continue all weekend across the valley. On Friday night, the Children's Lantern Parade is in Steubenville at 7, beginning at Drosselmeyer's.

Then Saturday, New Cumberland's parade begins at 5 o'clock, and the combined YTR parade at 6.

And if you missed our broadcasts of the Perkins Restaurant and Bakery Fantasy in Lights Parade, or the Weirton Christmas Memories Parade, both are now available to watch here.
