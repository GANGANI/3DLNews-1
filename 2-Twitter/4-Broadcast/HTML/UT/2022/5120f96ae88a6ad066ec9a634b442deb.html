Utah Film Trivia Night with Utah Public Radio
at The Cache Bar

DATE: Tuesday, May 7, 2024
TIME: Doors open at 6:00, trivia starts at 7:00 p.m.
VENUE: The Cache Bar - 119 S Main St, Logan, UT 84321
REGISTRATION: $25/person

Join Utah Public Radio for an evening of pub trivia, themed around movies filmed in Utah! Joining in to celebrate 100 Years of Utah Film and Television this year, questions will span the decades of prominent and more obscure film that were filmed in our beautiful state – from Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid, to Sandlot, High School Musical, The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City, and so many more. We'll also have a costume contest, prizes, and dinner is included with your ticket purchase. Dinner menu options will be burgers (veggie and gluten-free options available), mini corndogs, green salad, and tater tots. ("Napoleon, give me some of your tots!") Drinks will be available for purchase. *Must be 21 or older to attend.

Registrations have now closed. Contact Katie Swain at katie.swain@usu.edu to inquire about last minute ticket availability.
