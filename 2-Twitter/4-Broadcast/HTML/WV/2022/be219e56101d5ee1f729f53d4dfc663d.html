Live coverage planned Friday, Oct. 28, when Capital visits George Washington

by STAFF REPORTS

Mon, October 24th 2022 at 4:31 AM
Updated Sat, October 29th 2022 at 4:24 AM
Live coverage is planned Friday, Oct. 28, when Capital visits George Washington. (WCHS)

    TOPICS:

    ComcastKanawha County,West Virginia,United StatesCOMCAST CORPORATIONTime-WarnerFacebookPaul BlazersportsMETA PLATFORMS, INC.

CHARLESTON, W.Va. (WCHS) — Two Kanawha County high school football teams will face off this weekend in the Par Mar Friday Night Rivals Game of the Week, presented by Optimum.

Capital will visit George Washington for a gridiron battle on Friday, Oct. 28. All the action begins at 7:30 p.m. and will be shared in this story once it starts and also can be viewed on the Eyewitness News' Facebook page.

The game also will be streamed live on Stirr and can be viewed over the air on 11.2. Optimum (Charleston) customers can view the game on channel 125; Comcast (Huntington) customers on channel 133; Time-Warner (Ashland-Ironton) customers on channel 305; and Armstrong Cable customers on channel 94.

Capital struggled through a 1-9 campaign last season and is trying to find the right formula this season, with a 2-6 record so far. The Cougars’ two wins have come over St. Albans, 35-6, and Riverside, 26-6. Capital’s losses this season have been against Parkersburg (44-14); Spring Valley (64-0); Huntington (56-7); Cabell Midland (49-0); Hurricane (48-0); and Parkersburg South (59-0).

Meanwhile, George Washington, 8-4 last year, has put together a 6-2 mark this season and is coming off a big victory, a 39-27 win over perennial powerhouse Spring Valley. The Patriots also have victories over Cabell Midland, 28-21; Paul Blazer, 21-14; South Charleston, 48-7; Parkersburg, 42-10; and St. Albans, 64-26. George Washington’s two losses this season have come at the hands of Hurricane, 56-7, and Huntington, 58-13.
