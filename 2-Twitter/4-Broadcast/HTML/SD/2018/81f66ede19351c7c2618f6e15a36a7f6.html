New Student Registration

Parents and guardians looking to enroll their child in the Pierre School District must complete our Student Online Registration.

Use a tablet, laptop, or desktop computer to complete the online registration. Each school in the district has computers available to complete online student registration if needed.

Select "Start New Registration" to provide information for all children. All students can be added to the same application.

Please reach out to Jeff Hegge our Infinite Campus Specialist, or your school's Student Registrar, with any questions regarding the student registration process.

Please also visit our Back to School Website for additional school information.

Free & Reduced Meals

The Pierre School District would like to remind families that the USDA is no longer providing free meals to all students. We encourage eligible families to fill out and submit the free & refuced meal application, which you will find on the Pierre School District Food Service Webpage.

Thank you for visiting the Pierre School District!

The Pierre School District prides itself on the quality of education it provides to children of the Pierre area. The teachers and support staff of the Pierre School District are of the highest caliber. Our students have great opportunities to learn and grow as a result of dedicated educators, directors, coaches, and quality programs and courses. As a district we take pride in our school and community. If you have any questions regarding the Pierre School District, please contact our Administrative Offices. We would like to hear from you!
