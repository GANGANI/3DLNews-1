Overnight thunderstorm leaves behind flooding and road closures in northern Michigan

by Trevor Drew

Fri, October 23rd 2020 at 9:37 AM
Updated Fri, October 23rd 2020 at 10:52 AM
5
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Thunderstorms and heavy rain overnight are taking their toll on northern Michigan, with flash flooding leaving inches of water behind in a short time. (WPBN)

GRAND TRAVERSE COUNTY, Mich., (WPBN/WGTU) -- Thunderstorms and heavy rain overnight are taking their toll on northern Michigan, with flash flooding leaving inches of water behind in a short time.

Friday morning, Grand Traverse 911 warned motorists that Peninsula Drive, north of Gray Road to Island View Road on Old Mission Peninsula, is closed after portion of the road was washed into the bay.

Grand Traverse 911 also reported that the North Three Mile Road, South Airport Road intersection will likely be underwater for “a while.”

Drivers are urged to avoid the area if possible.
