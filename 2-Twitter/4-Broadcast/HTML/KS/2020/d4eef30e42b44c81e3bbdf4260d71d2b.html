10 hours ago 0

After, U.S. 24 will reopen, and K-9 will close for a week. Motorists should follow detours and be cautious in work zones. For updates, visit http://www.kandrive.org or call 5-1-1.

10 hours ago 0
The 2024 Kansas Football season is set to kick off, with game times and TV designations announced. Kansas' first game against Lindenwood will be aired on ESPN+, followed by a match against Illinois on FS1. The season's highlight will be a Friday night ESPN Primetime game against UNLV at Children’s Mercy Park. All home games will be played in the Kansas City metro area due to construction.
