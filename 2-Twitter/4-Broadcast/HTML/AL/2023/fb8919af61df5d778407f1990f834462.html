Mobile PD: 1 shot on Dauphin Island Parkway

By Mike Brantley
Published: Nov. 24, 2023 at 1:15 PM CST

MOBILE, Ala. (WALA) - The Mobile Police Department confirmed a male victim was shot late Friday morning on Dauphin Island Parkway.

Officers were dispatched to the 700 block of Dauphin Island Parkway shortly before noon after receiving a report of someone being shot.

Authorities said they discovered a victim, identified as Joe Henderson, 43, had sustained a gunshot wound.

Henderson was taken to the hospital and later succumbed to his injuries.

Investigators said they were able to identify Jamal Hicks, 22, as the suspect and were able to apprehend him.

Hicks is charged with murder, according to the jail log.
