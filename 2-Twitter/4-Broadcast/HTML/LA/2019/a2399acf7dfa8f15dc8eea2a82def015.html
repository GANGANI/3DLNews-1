2003 Chevy truck, over 12 firearms stolen in Union Parish

Published: Dec. 24, 2019 at 12:28 PM CST

Authorities are investigating the theft of a truck and over 12 firearms in Union Parish.

According to the Union Parish Sheriff's Office, it happened Monday morning between midnight and 5 a.m. at a home on James Roberts Road in Downsville.

Authorities say over a dozen firearms, jewelry, and other items were stolen.

They say a red and gray 2003 Chevy S-10 was also stolen.

If you know anything about this incident, you are asked to call Crime Stoppers of Union Parish at (318) 368-9679.

You can also submit tips online.

They say a cash reward will be offered.
