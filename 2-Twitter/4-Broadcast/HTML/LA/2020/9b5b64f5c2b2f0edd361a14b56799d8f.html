The latest details on Central Louisiana weather
Updated: 2 hours ago

ALEXANDRIA, La. (KALB) - SYNOPSIS: Afternoon temperatures will remain pleasant this Thursday afternoon, continuing into the weekend. We will once again see isolated to scattered showers and storms this afternoon with the potential for severe activity. The severe outlook today is just a marginal (level 1/5) risk, with the main threats being damaging winds (58 mph or stronger) and large hail (1″ in diameter or larger). Friday afternoon will bring another round of storms, but the SPC has the western Cenla parishes under a slight (level 2/5) risk, with the same threats as today, strong winds and large hail. The pattern of afternoon storms will continue into the weekend, for another marginal risk of severe winds and hail on Saturday. Low-end rain chances continue into the workweek, and temperatures will rise back into the low 90s with heat index values near or above 100.
