Havelock Fire and Rescue breast cancer fundraiser tops $5,000

by Jordan Honeycutt

Tue, December 13th 2022 at 11:43 AM
The City of Havelock Fire and Rescue Department raised more than $5,000 with its "Brave Enough to Wear Pink" fundraiser. (Photo: Havelock Fire and Rescue)

    TOPICS:

    Disaster AccidentHealth Medical PharmaHuman InterestJared BrinkleyBreast cancerRTT

HAVELOCK, Craven County — The City of Havelock Fire and Rescue Department has raised $5,406.86 so far in its "Brave Enough to Wear Pink" fundraiser.

Since they've done the fundraiser, the total amount donated to the CarolinaEast Foundation is $151,646.71.

Executive Director of the CarolinaEast Foundation Jared Brinkley received the donation on the foundation's behalf.
