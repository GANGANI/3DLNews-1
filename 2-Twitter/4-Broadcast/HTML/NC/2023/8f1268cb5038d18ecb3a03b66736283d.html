Wherever there is a need in Mecklenburg and Union Counties

you'll find The Salvation Army of Greater Charlotte. Find out how we can help you.

  • Homelessness Services

    We're compelled to do everything we can to help restore security and hope to families, through the Center of Hope and the Booth Commons Family Center.

    Homelessness Services

  • Boys & Girls Clubs

    Our Boys & Girls Clubs’ mission is to enable all young people to reach their full potential as productive, caring, responsible citizens.

    Boys & Girls Clubs

  • Christmas Assistance

    The Salvation Army is here for families in need during Christmas. Thanks to the community's generosity, many children and seniors receive gifts.

    Christmas Assistance

  • Combat Substance Abuse

    When you donate or shop at our Family Stores, you are helping to change the lives of men, battling substance abuse, by giving them a new beginning.

    Combat Substance Abuse

  • Emergency Disaster Services

    Salvation Army officers, staff and volunteers provide food, shelter, clothing and spiritual comfort to first responders and survivors of disasters.

    Emergency Disaster Services

  • Fight Human Trafficking

    The average age of entry into the American sex trade is 13 years old.

    Fight Human Trafficking

  • Resources to Build Your Faith

    Our two Worship Centers are where people from all walks of life can find healing and a sense of family through weekly worship, bible study and more.

    Resources to Build Your Faith

  • Camp Walter Johnson

    Each summer, youth from many Salvation Army units all over the Carolinas come to Camp Walter Johnson on High Rock Lake for a life-changing week.

    Camp Walter Johnson

  • Volunteer

    Help those in need and make an impact in your community.

    Volunteer

  • Stories of Impact

    Everyday, we are changing the lives of people in our programs. Check out he latest news, as well as heartwarming stories of impact.

    Stories of Impact

  • Thrift Stores

    When you shop or donate at our thrift stores, you can make a difference in the lives of countless people in our community.

    Thrift Stores

  • Better The Future Campaign

    A VISION to make our community all it aspires to be.

    Better The Future Campaign

The Salvation Army Mission Statement

The Salvation Army, an international movement, is an evangelical part of the universal Christian Church. Its message is based on the Bible. Its ministry is motivated by the love of God. Its mission is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to meet human needs in His name without discrimination.
