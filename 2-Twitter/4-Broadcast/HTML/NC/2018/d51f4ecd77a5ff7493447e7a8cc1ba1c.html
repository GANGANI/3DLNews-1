Morehead City Kmart set to close

by Jaime McCutcheon

Sun, December 30th 2018 at 3:00 PM
Kmart store in Morehead City set to close in March 2019.

    TOPICS:

    Chicago Metropolitan AreaCook County, IllinoisHoffman Estates, IllinoisKmartPhillipsburg MallRetail ApocalypseSearsSears Holdings

MOREHEAD CITY, Carteret County — Sears Holdings announced another round of store closings, including the Kmart on Arendell Street in Morehead City.

The company told associates at the stores on December 27 of the impending closures. This closure lists includes 80 stores, both Sears and Kmart locations. They are scheduled to close in March of 2019. Liquidation sales at these stores are expected to begin in two weeks.

This is in addition to the previously announced closure of 40 unprofitable stores expected to be completed in February 2019. The closings are part of Sears Holdings' processes to accelerate its strategic transformation and facilitate its financial restructuring.
