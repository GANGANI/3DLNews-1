KRWC Sports Broadcast Schedule, November 1 – November 6

By KRWC Staff
October 30, 2021
Sports

Week two of fall playoffs is maybe even more busy than week one was – we’re looking forward to bringing you great action from your hometown teams!

Thursday, November 4th, #1 Watertown-Mayer vs #4 Maple Lake, 6p and #2 Annandale vs #Maranantha, 7:45p Section 5AA Volleyball @ STMA

Friday, November 5th, Class 6AAAAAA State 2nd Round, Centennial@STMA, 7PM

Friday, November 5th, #1 Annandale vs #2 Spectrum, 5AAA Finals, 7:45p @St. Cloud,

Saturday, November 6th, Should Annandale and/or Maple Lake advance to the Section Championship, KRWC will be covering it live. 6p @STMA

Saturday, November 6th, #1 STMA vs. #2 Rogers, Section 8AAAA Final@Monticello, 7 PM
