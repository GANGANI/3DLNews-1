Monday Night Live returns on for its 27th season in 2018-19. Every Monday at 9:00 p.m., it's an hour of live music from the KVSC performance studio featuring the best in Minnesota music. You can revel in the energy of a live band from the comfort of your home! Monday Night Live broadcasts from 9-10 p.m. directly following KVSC's "The Area" program. MNL is simulcast on UTVS-Channel 180 in the St. Cloud area.
