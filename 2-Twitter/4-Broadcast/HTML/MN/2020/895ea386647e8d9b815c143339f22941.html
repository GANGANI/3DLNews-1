Hi!
I’m Erin.

I'm an illustrator 🎨 who loves to bring concepts to life both digitally and on paper. From personalized portraits to capturing a special moment, from branding elements to childhood homes, I enjoy drawing pieces that help people capture a moment, connect with a memory, or support a cause.


In addition to offering custom work, I also have an online shop featuring ready-made art that includes prints, cards, and calendars.


Whether you're seeking a one-of-a-kind custom illustration or browsing for a new set of thank you cards, I’ve got you covered.
