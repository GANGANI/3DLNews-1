    News Radio 920

    News Radio 920 KXLY and 100.7 FM. Home of 920 News Now with Dave Spencer. Spokane's only live and local morning news. Traffic and weather every 10 minutes on the 9's. Local news, sports & business news. Followed by The Dan Bongino Show, Dave Ramsey, Ben Shapiro, Lars Larson, Mark Levin and the Markley, van Camp & Robbins show. Home of the Seattle Mariners and Washington State Cougar Football and Basketball.
