11 Friday Aug 2023

Posted by tomhillradio in WATR

Tags

Tom Chute, WATR

I would like to thank Tom Chute general manager, and morning drive host from watr.com and owner/partner, Dave Webster, for allowing me to be the “interim host”  of talk of the town for the last six months. Also, the callers & active listeners  in the audience were very gracious in their support of the program .

My lead sponsors, the Savings Bank of Danbury: with three branches in greater Waterbury and the Blasius Automotive Group: with 14 dealerships in CT & MA gave us the financial horsepower to deliver daily a credible LIVE program. 

I hope to be back with some sort of interesting programming in mid September. Also, would like to congratulate Tom Chute on his retirement at the end of September.  I’ve had the pleasure working with Tom for the last 35 years and he’s a Top Shelf Pro & everything he does.

Most Sincerely 

Tom Hill III, CCIM/SIOR
