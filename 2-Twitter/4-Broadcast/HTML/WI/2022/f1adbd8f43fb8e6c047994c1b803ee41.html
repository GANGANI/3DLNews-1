      We have recently upgraded our Listen Live player and strongly recommend using Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox or Safari as your browser to listen on desktop. (Keep using the WTMJ app on mobile.) If you are having any issues, please refresh the page. If that doesn’t work, try clearing your browser’s cache and history, and then refresh and try again. If the player still does not work, feel free to e-mail us here for assistance.

      GOOD NEWS! If you’re located within a 75-mile radius of Downtown Milwaukee, you can stream Milwaukee Bucks games with WTMJ. Click here to visit our Milwaukee Bucks streaming page. The stream will be available approx. 30 minutes before tip-off, generally following Bucks Shootaround on 620 WTMJ.

      NOTE:  Per MLB, Listen Live now features Brewers Radio Network broadcasts if the listener is located in Milwaukee, Ozaukee, Racine, Washington or Waukesha counties. WTMJ Mobile podcasts will air on the Listen Live page during Bucks games as well as during programming that does not originate from WTMJ.
