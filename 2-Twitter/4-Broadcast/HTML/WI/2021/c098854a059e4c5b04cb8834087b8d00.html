Northeast Wisconsin designers decorate trees in the 10th annual Tiny Tree Throwdown

by Rachel Manek, FOX 11 News

Tue, December 14th 2021 at 6:32 AM
Updated Tue, December 14th 2021 at 10:26 AM

Three local designers decorate Christmas trees to be donated to military families, December 14, 2021. (WLUK/Rachel Manek)

GREEN BAY (WLUK) -- Every December, three small-ish trees have come to life in the FOX 11 studios. Last year, due to COVID, the trees were decorated virtually.

This year, to allow for distancing, the designers met at Gather on Broadway to take part in the Tiny Tree Throwdown.

Interior designer Jennie Gafvert, floral designer Nichole Campbell of Petal Pusher and artist Kim Geiser of Hello Happiness Creativity Center, came with brand new ideas for the 10th year!

How did you decorate your Christmas tree? Share photos and videos with us here:

Although we call it a throwdown, everyone's a winner. The trees were donated by Whispering Pines Tree Farm and will be delivered to three deserving families of military veterans.


Green Bay Vet Center Director Scott Katzka joined Rachel Manek at Gather to talk about the services they provide to veterans.
