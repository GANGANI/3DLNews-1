Vikings and Eagles in Opener

Posted on November 30, 2020 by Chad Michaels

Denmark will play at Southern Door in a non-conference boys basketball game on Tuesday. The Vikings return their top three scorers from last year in Donovan Short (18.6) Hayden Konkol (14.2) and Brennen Miller (10.1) The Eagles return four starters from last year, including Alex Daoust (14.6) Denmark beat Southern Door, 65-62 last season. Our coverage begins with the Jorns Chevrolet of Kewaunee pregame show on 93.9FM / wdor.com
