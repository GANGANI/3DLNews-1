Stick with me to celebrate National Stamp Collecting Month

by McKenzy Parsons (KPTM)

Sun, October 9th 2022 at 3:55 PM
National Stamp Collecting Month
Why couldn’t the stamp go to the party?
He had to stand post

OMAHA, Neb. (KPTM) — October is National Stamp Collecting Month, according to nationaldaycalendar.com.

Stamp collecting is considered to be the most popular hobby in the world.

The first stamp was invented to help people pay for mail in 1837.

Die-hard collectors are known as philatelists.

Roughly 5 million Americans collect stamps.


Did you hear the joke about the letter with no stamp?
You wouldn’t get it

How to observe the sticky holiday:

  • Start collecting stamps
  • Read up on the history of stamps
  • Talk to the post office to see if they can help with your collection
  • Use #StampCollectingMonth on social media to share how you celebrated

National Stamp Collecting Month was started by the U.S. Postal Service and the Council of Philatelic Organization in 1981, said the website.
