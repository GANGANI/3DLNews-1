Concordia too much for Hastings College men, Broncos fall 82-68

By Spencer Schubert
Published: Jan. 26, 2022 at 11:24 PM CST

SEWARD, Neb. (KSNB) - It was all Bulldogs in the men’s game Wednesday night.

Concordia started off on fire against Hastings College. AJ Watson knocked down the game’s first points with a 3-pointer from the corner. He finished with 13 on the night.

Hastings College tried to keep things close, as Phil Cisrow found Zach Rust running the baseline for two of his 17 points on the night. Dashawn Walker also played well, backing down a defender for a nice bucket en route to his team-high 20 points on the night.

However, Concordia never let up on the offensive end, as Gage Smith led all scorers with 28 points on the night, and the Bulldogs rolled to an 82-68 win.
