Other Ways To Watch

YouTube TV / Hulu + Live TV / DirecTV Stream
Live simulcast of our PBS channel. Enjoy our award-winning, locally produced public television programming and children’s favorites.

Livestream on PBS Video
Live simulcast of our PBS channel. Enjoy all of your favorite programs online and on the PBS Video App.

Nebraska Public Media App
Developed for mobile devices to provide Nebraskans free and fast access to live and on-demand television, radio and digital content.

Livestreaming
Watch Nebraska Public Media, Nebraska government, and live PBS and PBS KIDS broadcasts.

PBS Roku App
Watch live PBS broadcasts along with thousands of full-length episodes, award-winning documentaries, and series from PBS and member stations on demand.

Watch FREE Over-the-Air
You may be able to receive our signal over the air with an antenna. Click here to input your address and find a recommended antenna type along with directional advice. You may also want to view our detailed television coverage map above.

Looking For Help?

Are you experiencing a technical issue with TV, radio or web reception? Having trouble locating our channels? Call our help desk weekdays from 7:30 a.m. – 5 p.m. CT. at (800) 698-3426. If it is after hours, leave a voice mail message and they will return your call the next business day.

For television programming questions call (800) 868-1868 or submit your question here.
