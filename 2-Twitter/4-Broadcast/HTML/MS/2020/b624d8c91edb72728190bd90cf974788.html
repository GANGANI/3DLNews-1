Navigator Credit Union offers personalized financial services to Members all along the Mississippi and Alabama Gulf Coasts. Founded in 1939 as a not-for-profit financial institution, our mission is to build a legacy of excellence for our Members through service, commitment and substantial value. We promise to work in the best interests of our Members to help them achieve financial success. Free interest-bearing checking accounts; unique Save'N Up debit card savings program which can return up to $300 in free money each year and pays 5.5% interest; auto loans with the same low interest rates for both new and used vehicles; mortgage loans including traditional mortgages and HELOCs serviced locally; low interest rate VISA(r) credit cards with uChoose(r) Rewards and no annual fee, no cash advance fees and no balance transfer fees; all accounts federally insured up to $250,000 by the NCUA. (Subject to credit approval. Terms and Conditions apply.)
