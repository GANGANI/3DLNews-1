Hope and healing happen here

A unique summer camp experience

Pediatric cancer changes a family forever. Everyone in the family faces the many struggles and challenges that come with a diagnosis. Between the appointments, treatments, and constant change, it’s tough for kids to simply feel like kids.

But there's a special place that makes a difference. At Camp Wapiyapi, both patients and siblings find laughter, friendship, and lasting memories through an equal and dynamic summer camp experience — at no cost to families.

Join us for a summer to remember

Our campers call it “the best week of the year” and they’re not wrong! Discover an unforgettable summer camp experience in the beautiful Colorado mountains. Our campers certainly love traditions like the talent show and dance party as well as exciting activities such as archery, mountain biking, and the ropes course.
