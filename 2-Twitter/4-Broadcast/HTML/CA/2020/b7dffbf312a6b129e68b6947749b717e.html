DIVAS Simply Singing! Raising Health Awareness Thanks Our Sponsors:

The DIVA Foundation’s signature fundraising event, DIVAS Simply Singing! Raising Health Awareness is a highly anticipated and prestigious annual benefit concert that raises awareness and funds for programs that promote the prevention of HIV/AIDS and other life-threatening health conditions. DIVAS Simply Singing! has a rich 33-year history, making it the longest-running consecutive running HIV/AIDS and Health Awareness benefit concert in the United States. The event is filmed annually in Los Angeles, CA.

DIVAS! is executive produced and hosted by Emmy® Award Winning Actress & Activist Sheryl Lee Ralph. Over 300 iconic, award-winning, and highly decorated performers have shared their health awareness journeys and donated their precious time and talent. Some of our renowned performers included: EGOT Winning artists Whoopi Goldberg and Jennifer Hudson; Oscar® Nominated & Emmy Award® Winning Actresses Sharon Stone and Alfre Woodard; Emmy® Award Winning Eartha Kitt and Loretta Devine; Grammy® Award Winning Patti LaBelle, Brandy, Faith Evans, Michelle Williams, Lalah Hathaway, Chaka Khan, Jody Watley, Jennifer Holliday, Yolanda Adams, Stephanie Mills, The Clark Sisters, Roberta Flack, Deniece Williams, Natalie Cole, Mary Wilson. Iconic artists like Deborah Cox, Oleta Adams, Raven Symoné, Angie Stone, Netta, Bresha Web, Keke Wyatt, Lil Mo, and Syleena Johnson have graciously blessed our stage.

DIVAS Simply Singing! is filmed annually in Los Angeles, CA, and is set to air on ABC Owned Stations during the 2023 holiday season. In 2022, ABC Networks celebrated Sheryl Lee Ralph and DIVAS Simply Singing! Raising Health Awareness for over 30 Years of Service with an “On the Red Carpet” special. Take a look!

DIVAS Simply Singing! is a health awareness program of The DIVA Foundation.
