The 2024 Vacaville Taco Trail Challenge Launches May 1st!

Vacaville Taco Trail was created by Visit Vacaville to highlight the Mexican Food scene in Vacaville. The Vacaville Taco Trail gives local foodies and families the opportunity to enjoy specialty tacos from participating restaurants throughout town. A taco trophy prize is awarded to the person who can be the first to complete the 2024 Vacaville Taco Trail in its entirety.

Get ready to eat some delicious Vacaville tacos from May 1st - May 31st and a chance to win some amazing prizes.
