Gamecocks tee off at Top Golf ahead of Gator Bowl

by Matthew Vereen

Thu, December 29th 2022 at 8:59 PM
(WACH)

Jacksonville, FL (WACH) — Bowl Games not all about the football, but also the fun.

Ahead of Friday's Gator Bowl matchup with Notre Dame, South Carolina football taking over Top Golf for a chance to crown the top golfing Gamecock.

"We don't get a lot of time to play," junior quarterback Spencer Rattler said. "The guys are getting into it. It's fun getting out of football and doing these activities. We went to the naval base yesterday which was really cool. And now obviously Top Golf today. It's cool."

Some of the Gamecocks showing that athletic prowess can translate from the gridiron to the tee box.

Others, like wide receiver Antwane "Juice" Wells Jr. talked a big game beforehand.

"I'm gonna have the best swing," Wells said after the morning practice. "I've got my form and all that. I'm good."

But showed there's still probably plenty of room to keep learning.

Though Head Coach Shane Beamer, a talented golfer himself, happy to provide tips and encouragement.

No one perhaps shining brighter though than special teams as both punter Kai Kroeger and kicker Mitch Jeter showed their loft control extends across sports.

But today also another chance for these guys to get to know fans and locals alike, and keep representing the Gamecock brand.

"Oh yeah, definitely just getting out in the community and seeing your fans," Rattler said. "I know we have a lot of South Carolina fans travelling up to the game so it's always good to see them."
