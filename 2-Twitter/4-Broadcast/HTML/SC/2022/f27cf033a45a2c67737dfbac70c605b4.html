Four arrests lead to seizure of guns, drugs

by Brittany Breeding

Sun, December 18th 2022 at 2:43 PM
11
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Four arrests lead to seizure of guns, drugs (OCSO)

ORANGEBURG COUNTY, SC (WACH) — Four Orangeburg County men are behind bars and officers have seized multiple weapons and narcotics.

“At least two of these firearms were stolen, and some of the marijuana was altered to an extremely high concentration of the THC content,” the sheriff said. “These are serious firearms and dangerous narcotics.”

The first two arrests came from incidents on November 28. 18-year-old Nasir Washington was charged with murder in connection with a November 28 fatal shooting. Officers say he was originally charged with possession of a stolen vehicle and then just days later charged with breach of peace in connection to a shootout at a fast food restaurant on December 1. The other arrest was 18-year-old Alexander Bloodworth. After a shooting between two vehicles on November 28 - Bloodworth was charged with possession with intent to distribute. Officials say the drug was an altered and highly concentrated form of marijuana.

The third arrest was on December 8 after a traffic safety checkpoint. 18-year-old Rodrizuez Mack was charged with possession of a controlled substance.

The final arrest was on December 9. Officers say they were following up on a vehicle pursuit at the suspect’s home. Officers say the suspect, 19-year-old Ahmari Millhouse tried to run and had a handgun. That gun turned out to be stolen out of Beaufort County. Millhouse faces charges of possession of a stolen pistol.

In these investigations officers seized a total of 8 firearms, a drum magazine for a handgun, ammunition, as well as multiple containers containing a highly concentrated marijuana derivative, marijuana, digital scales, grinders, and marijuana-laced brownies.

Officials say more charges across multiple jurisdictions are expected as the investigations continue.
