Where do I find my Priority Date?

If you are a prospective immigrant, you can find your priority date on Form I-797, "Notice of Action", for the petition filed on your behalf.
The waiting time before receiving an immigrant visa or adjusting status depends on the:

  • Demand for and supply of immigrant visas.
  • Per-country visa limitations; and
  • Number of visas allocated for your preference category.
