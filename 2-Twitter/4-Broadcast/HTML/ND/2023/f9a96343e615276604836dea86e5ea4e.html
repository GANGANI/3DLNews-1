Burgum’s State of the State address and North Dakota Legislature Special Session coverage

By KFYR Staff
Published: Oct. 23, 2023 at 9:08 AM CDT

BISMARCK, N.D. (KFYR) - Governor Burgum gave a State of the State address Monday morning to address the 2023-2025 appropriations bill for the state Office of Management and Budget (OMB) after the North Dakota Supreme Court issued an opinion on Sept. 28 voiding the bill, ruling it violated the state Constitution’s single-subject rule.

Continuing Coverage of the Special Session:

  • Burgum completes signing of special session bills, but partially vetoes one
  • The 17th special legislative session wraps up after three days of work
  • Senate fails to pass income tax relief bill, Governor Burgum responds
  • What some legislators do while waiting to vote at the special session
  • House passes Governor’s bill on income tax relief, will now head to the Senate
  • Lawmakers at the State Capitol for the 17th special session
  • Bill drafts submitted to Legislative Management that won’t be in the special session
  • Governor Doug Burgum gives State of the State address at Special Legislative Session
