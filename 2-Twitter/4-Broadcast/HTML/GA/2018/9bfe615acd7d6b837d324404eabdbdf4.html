Opioids: 'A National Crisis:' Hosted by Eric Bolling

by Sinclair Broadcast Group

Sun, December 16th 2018 at 10:51 AM
Updated Thu, April 11th 2019 at 10:35 AM

(Sinclair Broadcast Group) -- Join host Eric Bolling for a roundtable discussion on the crisis that has affected his life so personally and that has the nation on edge.

On Tuesday, December 18, 2018, (7pm ET/4 pm PT) Bolling and his wife Adrienne, who lost their son to an accidental overdose, will lead the panel of influencers. We will be streaming the event, live, right here at the top of our website.

The Bollings are committed to fighting the opioid epidemic in America, ever since their son passed away. Sinclair Broadcast Group is committed to fighting the crisis, right alongside the Bollings.

The Town Hall will be streamed on all of Sinclair's websites and it will be re-broadcasted on multiple Sinclair stations.

The panel will feature President Donald Trump's advisor, Kellyanne Conway; Secretary of Veterans Affairs, Robert Wilkie; former Major of the Maryland State Police, Jim Pyles; veteran, Norman Hooten; former Prosecutor Kendel Ehrlich; and Kimberly Lohman Clapp.
