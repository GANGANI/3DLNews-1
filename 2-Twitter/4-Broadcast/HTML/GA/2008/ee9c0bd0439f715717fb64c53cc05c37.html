4.27.2009

THE MUNDO MIX - Saturday April 25, 2009

The Mundo Mix. The Mundo Mix is simply a mix of global music I put together for my first DJ set. I wanted to share what the party goers ALL loved. This mix has Japanese Dub, Bossa Nova, Afrobeat,Romanian Gypsy Punk and other interesting sounds of the Globe.



Being a DJ is definitely fun and interesting. This set embodies the group of cultures I had the pleasure of entertaining. I met people from Brazil, Africa and Costa Rica. The music set tone for a home away from for them in the wide eyed city of Atlanta.

Enjoy,
Ms. Unique

Let There Be HOUSE!!!! Mundo playlist for March 21sst.


Whats up! On the night of March 21st I decided to take it to the house! House music that is. I started from the top with Frankie Knuckles, walked it out to Larry Levan, jumped the pond to Britains own T-Coy, landed in Botswanna with DJ Boogie Sid and brought it on home again with Monique Bingham.

Here's your playlist:

Frankie Knuckles - A remix of Jamie Principles Your Love

Larry Levan - Changes

Interview with the Mother of House Music: Mother Diva

T-Coy - Carino

DJ Boogie Sid feat. Tsotiology - Tell Me Why

Monique Bingham - Pure
