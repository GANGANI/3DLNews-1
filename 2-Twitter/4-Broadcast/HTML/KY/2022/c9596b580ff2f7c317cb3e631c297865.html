New Drone Video of Parkville, Missouri Makes It Look Like a Dream

Doc HollidayPublished: November 7, 2022
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

I've never been to Parkville, Missouri that I can remember. Maybe I should plan a trip as a new drone video makes it look like a place that you would see in a dream. A very good dream.

Get our free mobile app

I'm not sure of what the backstory is behind this new drone video that was just shared featuring Parkville, Missouri...but it seriously looks otherworldly. See if you agree. The video is appropriately titled "Dreamers".

Dreamers | Missouri 4K from ImageStream Press on Vimeo.

I know that Parkville, Missouri is near Kansas City (5 miles from downtown as a matter of fact) and is very much a historic city. The fact that it hugs the Missouri River doesn't hurt the scenic sights at all.

The official website for the city of Parkville, Missouri isn't shy about bragging about how much history has happened there. Who can blame them? One of the best parts of Missouri it could be argued. Wikipedia says it was platted way back in 1844. Those were steamboat days back then.

I have to wonder if this new drone video is gonna be part of a tourism project or something of that sort. Whoever the drone pilot and videographer are should get a big ole pat on the back for a job well done. They seriously made Parkville, Missouri look like something out of a Disney movie.
