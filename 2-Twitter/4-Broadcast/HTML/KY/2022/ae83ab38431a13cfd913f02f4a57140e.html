Whitesville Lions Club Raising Money For Breast Cancer Awareness & You Can Help

Angel WelshPublished: November 9, 2022
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Attention Deer Hunters:  Come hungry and get your butts out of the stands to enjoy a yummy breakfast prepared just for you this weekend.

WHITESVILLE LIONS CLUB DOING SOMETHING SPECIAL FOR OTHERS

The Whitesville Lions Club has a long history of helping others.  This year is no different and they are planning a huge and delicious breakfast that will fill hungry tummies and support Breast Cancer Awareness.

The Annual Deer Hunter's Breakfast is happening this weekend.

There is no set ticket price for breakfast. The Whitesville Lions Club is simply serving breakfast for donations.  As Cathy Roby, a proud Lions Club member says, they're cooking up a mess of something. Actually, they're cooking up just about everything you could want in a big, hearty breakfast. They're serving "bacon, sausage, eggs, biscuits, gravy, and donuts."

The Deer Hunter's Breakfast is from 4 am to 10 am, Saturday, November 12th at the Whitesville Senior Center in Whitesville Park on Hwy 54.  Everyone is welcome.

Get our free mobile app

IT'S HUNTING SEASON

The deer are moving and the temperatures are dropping.  Deer Hunters have been scouting their bucks and making plans to harvest a big one.  My husband, Joe, starts preparing for hunting season months before it is even time.  He puts in so much work you would think it is a full-time job.   When he gets home he is absolutely starving from walking all over and checking his cameras and feeding the deer.

We appreciate the hearts of the Whitesville Lions Club members and all their hard work to make this happen for our community.  Get out and support them this Saturday.
