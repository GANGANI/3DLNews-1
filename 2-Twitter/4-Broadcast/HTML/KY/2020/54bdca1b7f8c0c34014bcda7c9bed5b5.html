Precious Blood Drive-Thru BBQ Picnic This Saturday

Erin GrantPublished: August 27, 2020
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

I'll admit, I miss BBQ church picnics. The fellowship, the atmosphere, and the FOOD. As we head into the fall season, yeah, fall will be here before we know it, check out what Precious Blood Catholic Church is doing to satisfy our BBQ cravings.

Precious Blood will be hosting a drive-thru BBQ picnic this Saturday, August 29th starting at 3:00 p.m. So, what's on the pit?

Burgoo $20.00

Mutton $14.00

Pork $12.00

Chicken $8.00

You can pick up your BBQ and pay safely at their social distanced drive-thru. Precious Blood Catholic Church is located at 3306 Fenmore Street in Owensboro.
