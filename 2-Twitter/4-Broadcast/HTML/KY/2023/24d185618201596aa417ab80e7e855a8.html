RADIO AFFILIATES

The Clemson Athletic Network broadcasts LIVE football, men’s basketball, women’s basketball and baseball games across the state of South Carolina and the nation. Before and after each football game, be sure to tune in to the Tiger Tailgate Show and the Post-Game Show. In addition, each Monday night during football and basketball seasons, don’t miss Tiger Calls with Coach Dabo Swinney and Coach Brad Brownell. Listen to LIVE Clemson sports on the Clemson Athletic Network.

The Roar – WCCP-FM and WAHT(AM) – are proud to be the official Flagship Stations for Clemson Athletics.
