Oregon implementing mask mandates outdoors regardless of vaccination status

Summary by Ground News
Governor Kate Brown is reinstating Oregon’s outdoor mask mandate starting Friday. Brown said everyone will need to wear a mask outside in public unless they can keep physical distance. An indoor mask mandate was reinstated earlier this month. Rule does not apply to outdoor gatherings at private residences.
