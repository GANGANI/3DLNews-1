Canyonlands Mysteries

Many of the country's national parks are filled with ghostly tales and mysterious incidents. One of the most fascinating is Canyonlands National Park located in Utah.

Tales of Vortexs, skinwalkers, devilish semi trucks, and so much more come out of this region. It's an area I've had the pleasure of investigating on many occasions and recently I joined Historium Unearthia to discuss some of the fascinating and strange occurrences from this mysterious park. 

Check out the program at the link below:
