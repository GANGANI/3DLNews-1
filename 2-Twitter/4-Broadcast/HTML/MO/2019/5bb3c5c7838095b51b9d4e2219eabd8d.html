Mom to Mom: Helpful ideas when holiday shopping for your little one

by Maria Miller

Wed, December 11th 2019 at 1:06 PM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS

JEFFERSON CITY — Wednesday marks two weeks until Christmas and, for some, it’s just the beginning of the shopping hustle and bustle. For new moms, and those of toddlers, shopping for little ones can be extra challenging.

The thought of finding room for even more stuff for baby can be overwhelming. That's why Whaley's Mommy and Me is ready to help.

“As a parent, I like getting gifts that are multi-functional,” said Ilana Holland, the director of marketing for Whaley’s. “So, things that they are going to enjoy but something that is also going to be useful and help their development, too.”

From nursery to potty training to fashion and functionality, there's something unique and safe for all ages.

“We have all-natural finger nail polish,” said Holland. “It’s safe for teething, chipping and all of that fun stuff. You can put it in your mouth, and it doesn’t have the harsh chemicals most [nail polishes] do.”

Speaking of the little ones who pick up just about anything they get their hands on, to see how it tastes, Holland said the store’s Chew Cube is a great choice.

“[It’s] a really great toy because it’s something they love to play with,” she said. “It has really great texture and noise for some sensory develop and the educational aspect, but it also is something fun for them to play with and provide relief.”

Ah, yes. Relief. There's something to help parents too.

‘Ewan the Sleep Sheep’ is ready to help with those never-ending non-silent nights.

“[Ewan] is a sound machine as well as a nightlight all in one,” Holland said. “So, it's a little bit of a comfort for baby but also a product that parents will enjoy and hopefully let them sleep a little better.”
