KMOV.com Contest 2022

KMOV.com Contest 2022 is organized by KMOV, an affiliate of Gray Media Group at kmov.com/contests where you need to enter the correct keyword of the day for your chance to win a $100 Mobil on the Run gift card, $100 Save a Lot gift card, $400 Visa gift card or receive one night trip for your family up to 4 people allowed.

How To Enter? Watch KMOV News 4 between 6:00 a.m. and 7:00 a.m. (CST) each weekday, Tuesday through Friday when KMOV will announce the Key Word of the Day and then visit this link https://www.kmov.com/contests and then follow the online instructions to register by entering all information requested on the registration form including entering correct keyword / code word of the day and once you filled out the entry form now click on the submit button to complete the registration form. Click Here for rules.!!

KMOV Free 4 You Contest Keyword Of The Day

Entry Date:Code Word of The Day:
November 24, 2022–NA–

KMOV Contest 2022 Details:

Contest Period: This Contest entry begins at 6:00 a.m. Central Time (CT) on Monday, October 31, 2022, and all entries must be received by 4:15 p.m. CT on Friday, November 25, 2022.

Eligibility: This Contest is open only to legal residents of MO or IL, in the KMOV viewing area residing in Pike, Lincoln, Warren, St. Charles, St. Louis, St. Louis – Ind Co., Jefferson, Franklin, Gasconade, Phelps, Crawford, Washington, Reynolds, Iron, St. Francois, and Ste. Genevieve counties in Missouri and Calhoun, Greene, Jersey, Macoupin, Montgomery, Madison, Bond, Fayette, St. Clair, Clinton, Marion, Clay, Monroe, Washington and Randolph counties in Illinois and who have reached the age of 18 years old at the time of entry.

Who’s Sponsor: The KMOV.com Contest is Sponsored by KMOV, an affiliate of Gray Media Group, Inc., 1 Memorial Drive, St. Louis, MO 63102.

Entry Limit: One (1) entry per person with a valid email address once a day.

Prize Details:

Prizes are $100 Mobil on the Run gift card, $100 Save a Lot gift card, $400 Visa gift card or receive one night trip for your family up to 4 people allowed, one (1) winner will receive a Night of Fun with four (4) tickets to see Amy Grant with Michael W. Smith Christmas, on stage at The Fox Theatre, RUDOLPH, on stage at The Fox Theatre, Night of Fun with four (4) tickets to see DISNEY PRINCESS THE CONCERT, on stage at The Fox Theatre.
