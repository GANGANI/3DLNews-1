Spring activities available within the city of Reno

by Mackenzie Walters

Thu, April 1st 2021 at 9:10 AM
Updated Thu, April 1st 2021 at 1:43 PM
Idlewild Pool will reopen in June, according to City of Reno

    TOPICS:

    RenoEvelyn Mount PoolTanerHospitality RecreationGeography Of NevadaIdlewild ParkNevadaReno, Nevada

RENO, Nev. (News 4 & Fox 11) — The City of Reno Parks and Recreation Department announced Spring recreation activities currently available, and what the community can look forward to for Summer.

Aquatics

Northwest Pool will begin offering evening lap swim time for the general public starting in April. Lane times will begin at 5:15 p.m. Lane times can be reserved by calling Northwest Pool at 775-334-2203.

Evelyn Mount Pool remains open Monday through Friday, from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m., with lap and open lane time, as well as water fitness classes.

The pool is also open Saturday, from 9:30 a.m. to 3 p.m., which includes lap lane and lesson programming. Lane times can be reserved by calling Evelyn Mount Northeast Community Center at 775-334-2262.

Idlewild and Traner Pools will open for the summer season for public lap and open swim starting in June.

Idlewild pool schedule available here.

Taner pool schedule available here.

Athletics

Youth Sports Association (RYSA) leagues are in full operation at the City of Reno fields.

Softball and kickball seasons are currently taking place at the Reno Sports Complex and Idlewild Park.

Volleyball leagues are scheduled to resume at Plumas Gym in the summer.

Seasons for all adult sports leagues will offer online and in-person registration starting in May.


Health and Wellness

Mount Northeast Community Center and Neil road Recreation Center are open.

The following programs are available:

  • Chair Thi Chi
  • Chair Yoga
  • Gentle Yoga
  • Hatha Yoga
  • Bean Bag Baseball
  • Pickleball
  • Badminton
  • Table Tennis
  • Sit and Fit
  • Zumba
  • Tai Chi
  • Bunco, Karate
  • Shuffleboard

Click here for the Neil Road Recreation Center activity schedule.

Click here for the Mount Northeast Community Center activity schedule.

Pickleball is by reservation only. Call the center you would like to attend to make a reservation:

  • Mount Northeast Community Center - 775-334-2262
  • Neil Road Recreation Center - 775-689-8484

Sierra Kids Before and After School Program

Kids before and after school programs are in session until June 9, 2021.

Vacation Station

Station Summer Camp locations are planned for Idlewild Park’s California Building and Evelyn Mount Northeast Community Center. Registration will open soon.

Adaptive Recreation

Fit but not Forgotten Veteran fitness classes are being offered each month, including refreshers for Veterans with expired memberships.

Call 775-333-7765 for a schedule of classes.

Adaptive equipment rentals are available, and a current inventory and the request form is available here.

The city of Reno has launched a new sled hockey program at Reno Ice. Call 775-333-7765 to join the team!


Parks and Recreation services are being provided to the public in compliance with current COVID-19 mitigation measures.
