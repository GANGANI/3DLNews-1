Food Bank nutrition classes go virtual for the month of June

by News 4 and FOX 11 Digital Team

Fri, June 5th 2020 at 8:49 AM
The Food Bank of Northern Nevada said it is holding free virtual nutrition classes for low-income families and seniors every Friday during June. (Photo per the FBNN Website)

    TOPICS:

    Health Medical Pharmasocial issuesArticlesFood ScienceHealthNutritionSelf-care

RENO, Nev. (News 4 & Fox 11) — The Food Bank of Northern Nevada said it is holding free virtual nutrition classes for low-income families and seniors every Friday during June.

Smart Shopper is a series of classes that discuss how to live a healthy lifestyle while on a budget.

Seniors Eating Well is a series for seniors that aims to improve their health, nutrition knowledge and behavior.

The virtual classes will be held each Friday in June from 1 p.m. to 2 p.m.
