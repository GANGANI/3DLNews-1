Another year of 'Crafts for Christmas' at the Nugget Casino Resort

by Issmar Ventura

Sun, November 25th 2018 at 7:05 PM
4
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Another year of 'Crafts for Christmas' at the Nugget Casino Resort

    TOPICS:

    Nugget

SPARKS, Nev. — The Nugget Casino Resort held their 9th annual Crafts for Christmas from Friday, November 23 to Sunday, November 25.

The free, three-day shopping event was presented by the Nugget Casino Resort and Williams LTD.

It featured offers on unique gifts and family-friendly activities. Attendees were able to shop at a huge selection of arts and crafts vendors, enjoy live cooking demonstrations, take selfies with Santa, purchase tickets to win fabulous raffle prizes and get Christmas shopping done – and wrapped – all in one place.

The schedule of holiday performances and demonstrations included:

Friday, November 23rd

11:00 am: Cookie Decorating Demonstration presented by the Nugget

12:00 – 4:00 pm (with breaks): Guitarist Milton Merlos

4:00 pm: Holiday Cocktail Demonstration presented by the Nugget

Saturday, November 24th

10:00 am – 12:00 pm (with breaks): Reed High School Band Performance Jazz Combo

11:00 am: Cookie Decorating Demonstration presented by the Nugget

12:00 – 4:00 pm (with breaks): Lolli Jones & Squeek Steele

4:00 pm: Holiday Cocktail Demonstration presented by the Nugget

Sunday, November 25th

11:00 am: Cookie Decorating Demonstration presented by the Nugget

12:00 – 2:00 pm (with breaks): Guitarist Milton Merlos

For the Kids

11:00 am – 3:00 pm daily: Santa Claus will be making a very special stop to visit with children each day of the craft fair. Come and take selfies with Santa.

In addition to the fair, the Sparks Fire Department and Sparks Police Department made the holidays extra special for children in need with their 2nd annual Holiday with a Hero program. People could give back by donating to the cause during Crafts for Christmas at the Nugget.

All donations went directly to children in the community through Washoe County Human Services. They will identify at-risk children who will be escorted by a “local hero” for a memorable holiday shopping experience at Target located at Legends at the Sparks Marina.
