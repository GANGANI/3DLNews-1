Sparks Police seeks suspect of grand larceny in Nugget Casino

    TOPICS:

    NuggetRenoSuspect

SPARKS, Nev. — The Sparks Police Department is asking for assistance in identifying a suspect in a grand larceny that occurred at the Nugget Casino on Saturday, September 15.

The incident happened at around 3:30 p.m. The suspect entered the ball room area where a wedding was being held.

He took a gift box containing monetary gifts for the bride and groom and left the area. He was captured on video surveillance removing the money from the box and concealing the gifts on his person and leaving the casino.

At this time, the suspect is unidentified.

If you have any information on this case, contact the Sparks Police Department at (775) 353-2231 or Secret Witness at (775) 322-4900.
