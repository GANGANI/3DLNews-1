Assistant Football Coach Arrested for Sexual Contact with Student

wsiltv.com: Police say they have arrested an in-school detention proctor and assistant football coach at Lone Oak High School for having a sexual relationship with a minor.

On November 21, 2011 Lone Oak High School Resource Officer, Larry Zacharetti, contacted the McCracken County Sheriff’s office regarding allegations of a relationship between a student and an in-school detention proctor and assistant football coach.
