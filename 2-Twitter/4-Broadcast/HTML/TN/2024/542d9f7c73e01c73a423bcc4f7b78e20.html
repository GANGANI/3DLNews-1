Live Streams

3rd and Lindsley is partnered with Volume for all of your streaming and recording needs. Through our partnership, we are able to give artists and fans a whole new way to connect. Live streaming is quickly becoming a go-to method for artists to reach new fans and promote their music. With the addition of live streaming at 3rd and Lindsley via Volume, artists are provided with yet another avenue for promotion, discovery, and interaction. This addition also allows for artists to take home a high-definition recording of their performance for their own content usage, as the ownership of all content always remains with the artist.
