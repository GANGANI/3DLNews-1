Football Friday Night (11/22/19)

By Chris Hudgison
Updated: Nov. 22, 2019 at 11:46 PM CST

Friday marked another playoff edition of Football Friday Night. Hoxie pulled off the biggest upset in Arkansas. The Mustangs knocked off reigning state champion Booneville in the 3A 2nd Round. Across the border, Kennett and Thayer are the only local teams left standing. Both will play Saturday in the Missouri state quarterfinals

You can follow Chris Hudgison or Matthew Schwartz on twitter for more updates. You can also check out updates on the Football Friday Night facebook page.
