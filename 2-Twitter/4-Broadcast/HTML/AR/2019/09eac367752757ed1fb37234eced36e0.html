Football Friday Night (11/1/19)

By Chris Hudgison
Published: Nov. 2, 2019 at 12:20 AM CDT|Updated: Nov. 2, 2019 at 12:28 AM CDT

Week 10 of Football Friday Night was headlined by a 2A-3 showdown. Salem beat East Poinsett County 12-6 in overtime in our Game of the Week. The Greyhounds clinched back to back conference titles and a first round bye in the state playoffs.
