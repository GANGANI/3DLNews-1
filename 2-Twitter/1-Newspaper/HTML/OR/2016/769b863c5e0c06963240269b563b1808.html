About Us

Established in 1942, we are Lewis & Clark College's award-winning student-run newspaper. By employing the highest standards of ethics, integrity and transparency, we produce stories that inform and challenge the Palatine Hill community.

How To Contribute

The Mossy Log holds weekly staff meetings every Monday at 5 p.m. in J. R. Howard Rm. 135. Staff meetings are open to all members of the Lewis & Clark community. Come and hear pitches for articles and visuals and connect with The Mossy Log’s editorial board.
