5 Illinois Valley players named to All-District 6 baseball's 1st team

Illinois Valley senior Dylan Beals earned first-team All-District 6 honors at two different positions, pitcher and utility player. As a pitcher, Beals was 9-1 with 101 strikeouts and a 2.15 ERA over the course of 52 innings. He allowed just 27 hits during the season.
Daily Courier

Senior Dylan Beals earned two first-team All-District 6 baseball awards in honors recently announced by the league.

Beals got his nods as a pitcher and a utility player.

On the mound, he went 9-1 with a 2.15 ERA. Beals struck out 101 hitters in 52 innings pitched, allowing just 27 hits.

"He was our ace, as he has been the last two years," said Illinois Valley coach Jesse Dugas. "He's a four-pitch kid, kind of a Greg Maddux type. We're going to miss him." Read More »

Summer Reading Program set (Between The Pages)

This year's Summer Reading Program was put together by Josephine Community Library staff, including the artwork for the poster, which was created by staff graphic designer Sarah Meyer. The reading program and all related events are free for participants. No library card is needed to join the fun.

Every nonprofit has its signature event. For the Boys and Girls Club, it's the Taste of the Rogue; for Our Foster Kids, it's the Masquerade Ball. For our Josephine Community Library, the main event is the Summer Reading Program.
