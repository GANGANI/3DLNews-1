Cyberbullying Report: Anti-Bullying & Internet Safety Services

Welcome to Cyberbullying Report Anti-Bullying and Internet Safety Services where you can fight cyberbullying and make the internet a safer place on a global level safely and anonymously. Cyberbullying Report offers four main types of services that are empowering, promotional, protective, and informational. The Cyber Bullies Exposed! Anti-Cyberbullying Harassment Reporting System empowers victims of internet harassment to fight back online by publishing the deeds of their tormentors for all of the world to see, the Internet Safety Resource Directory and Shopping Center lets you post a ads for your products and services free of charge, the Cyber Security Newsreel delivers the latest bullying news combined with internet security alerts from leading anti-virus software companies, and the Cyberbullying Facts and Information section contains the best educational information on internet bullying. Empowerment, promotion, protection, and information combine to make Cyberbullying Report the most advanced anti-bullying and internet safety service provider in the world.

Cyber Bullies Exposed! Harassment Reporting System

The Cyber Bullies Exposed! Anti-Cyberbullying Harassment Reporting System is the crown jewel of Cyberbulling Report that offers victims of cyberbullying, cyberstalking, and cyberterrorism a way to fight back online by taking the fight to the enemy in a way never before possible. The system lets you publish the names, locations, and photographs of people bullying, stalking, and terrorizing you on the internet. Whether you, your business, or someone you know have been victimized by a few mean spirited text messages, negative Facebook comments, all out smear campaigns, stalkers following their every move, or suffering a blow to their infrastructure due to cyber terrorists this system is here for you to stop letting them get away with it by letting your voice be heard.

In addition to exposing cyber bullies the system lets anyone post a comment about the exposed safely and anonymously. This feature like all other services is provided free of charge and the exposed themselves are welcome to use it to dispute anything about the report they feel is not accurate.

Internet Safety Resource Directory

At the Internet Safety Resource Directory and Shopping Center is a place where you could post an ad for goods or services in the interest of internet safety, but it has since been discontinued due to lack of use and the depreciation of this design. Items in the directory have since been moved to a newer site with a rating system, but for old times sake you can still find the last list of resources on this site as well.

Cyberbullying Facts and Information

The Cyberbullying Facts and Information on Internet Safety section features a combination or research on cyberbullying and internet safety issues as well as the latest announcements about this website. The purpose of this section is to compile interesting facts about cyberbullying, what it is, who does it, and how to conduct yourself in a safer fashion online so that you either don’t fall victim to it or manage it better if it happens to you. It will also have tips posted about how to protect your private information as well as your computer network.

Cyber Security Newsreel

The Cyber Security Newsreel is a combination of syndicated feeds from leading news agencies as well as internet security alerts from leading companies. The news agency part just takes RSS feeds from leading news agencies and filters them for keywords relevant to cyberbullying, online harassment, and issues related to network security. The other part of the feed is comprised primarily of antivirus alerts from leading internet security companies including Symantec and Trend Micro.

Other Features

Cyberbullying Report has several other features typical of most classified advertising services for managing and distributing information. These include syndicated feeds, social networking integration, and members only features.

RSS Feeds and Social Networking Integration

Cyberbullying Report features a diverse collection of RSS feeds that you can use to add content from this site to your website, blog, Twitter, or Facebook account. We also offer any category or tag archive in RSS format that you can get simply by visiting the archive in its respected section and clicking on the RSS icon to the right of the URL in your browser. The main feeds combined with the Cyber Security Newsreel feed power our Twitter and Facebook pages automatically. You can interact with our social networking accounts using the toolbar at the bottom of your screen.

Members Only Features

Anybody can be bullied just like anyone can browse our list of cyber bullies, but only members can expose cyber bullies themselves or post comments about documented incidents of harassment. The same goes for posting information to other sections as well as for contacting member. In order to contact a member you must first be a member at which point you can use the members only contact form to send messages.
