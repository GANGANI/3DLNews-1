    A family’s blood will thin when the devil rises with murder! Shelby Davis’ perfect life seemed to end the day her one true love Chris, the man she had loved since she was eight, husband and father to their only child, was killed in a tragic car accident or so she thought leaving the young and beautiful Shelby now one of Baltimore’s richest widows left alone to deal with his death and unravel the truth around it? Will uncovering the truth about his death, her own family’s betrayal and involvement with L.A.’s largest drug syndicate cost her life and that of her only son?
