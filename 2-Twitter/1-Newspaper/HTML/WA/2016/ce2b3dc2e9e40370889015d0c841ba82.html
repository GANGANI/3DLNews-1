Letter: Rep. Troy works hard

  • Oct 31, 2016
  • Oct 31, 2016
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Print
  • Save

Vote for Caroline Nilsson Troy on Nov. 8. Representative Troy works hard for us in Boise. She brought funding to Benewah and Latah counties for our roads. She supported accountability in our K-12 education system through legislation requiring teacher evaluations be completed prior to issuance of annual salary contracts. Mrs. Troy supports Idaho’s flagship institution. She worked with the student leaders at the University of Idaho to write and pass the medical amnesty bill — a bill that will save lives on our campuses across the state.

Representative Troy was very successful during her first term. She is being recognized as one of the most successful first-term legislators ever. She isn’t afraid to work hard, to ruffle a few feathers to get the job done and dig through tough issues. She will keep us safe, help build an economy that supports good paying jobs, keep our rural communities strong, and protect our rights to protect ourselves.

Caroline Nilsson Troy is an Idaho native. She knows us — she knows Idaho agriculture in all forms, she knows our communities — she cares about us. Representative Troy is a rare individual who has dedicated her time to serve us in Boise. She works tirelessly for all of us in Latah and Benewah counties. She makes decisions that relate to us and represent her constituents. Vote for Rep. Caroline Nilsson Troy on Nov. 8. Send her back to the Idaho Legislature so she can continue to work for us.

Kay Maurin

Moscow
