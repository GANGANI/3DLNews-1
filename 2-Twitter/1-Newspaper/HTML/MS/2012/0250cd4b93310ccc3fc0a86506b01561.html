Bain Sisters Found Alive, Adam Mayes Taken by Ambulance
         
Print Story Published: 7:37 pm Share Updated: 7:52 pm

UNION COUNTY, MS (abc24.com) - Adam Mayes has been found by law enforcement officers in Union County, Mississippi, and the two young sisters he's accused of kidnapping have been found alive.

Mayes was on the FBI's Top 10 Most Wanted list for the murders of 31-year-old Jo Ann Bain and her 14-year-old daughter Adrienne, and the kidnapping of 12-year-old Alexandria and 8-year-old Kyliyah Bain on April 27 in Whiteville, TN.

Mayes was found behind a church not far from where the mother and daughter's bodies were found in Guntown, MS. The FBI will not confirm reports that Adam Mayes was killed, but ABC 24's Eli Ross stated that an ambulance carrying Adam Mayes has just left the scene. Alexandria and Kyliyah were found alive but their conditions are unknown.
