Have You Had “The Talk” With Your Aging Loved One?

March 09, 2021 at 9:04 am CST

When you are older, “the talk” may be different, but just as important as when you were younger.

This conversation deals with longevity and the financial costs and burdens that come from aging and health issues.

Whether you are having this discussion with your mom or dad, spouse or children, an open conversation about your concerns and plans for long-term care is key to retirement planning.

“It is always best to be proactive instead of reactive,” said Matt McCann, one of the nation’s leading experts on long-term care and Long-Term Care Insurance. “Crisis management leaves you with few options. Advance planning give you choice, affordability and peace-of-mind. With no advance plan the consequences can be devastating on you and your family,”

You have limited options. You pay for care yourself and drain your assets, but the cost of care is expensive and impacts income and lifestyle. You could have your spouse or family members be caregivers, but this is stressful and creates tremendous burdens on them. Finally, there is affordable Long-Term Care Insurance.

“There really is only one logical and affordable solution: Long-Term Care Insurance,” said McCann. “The fact is these insurance plans are very affordable and give you many choices and options. This gives you the resources for quality care in the setting you prefer, either at home or in a facility. This way you can safeguard your assets and reduce the pressure and burdens otherwise placed on those you love.”

Illinois now is the 45th state to offer special Long-Term Care Partnership policies that provide additional dollar for dollar asset protection. Most states offer reciprocity, so there are no worries. There are also asset-based plans with death benefits and short duration plans that are available.

Speak with a qualified experienced Long-Term Care specialist who works with all the major companies.

McCann, licensed nationwide, is endorsed by the American Association for Long-Term Care Insurance and works with all the major insurance companies. He assists people nationwide with his unique process where they speak with him on the phone while seeing his computer screen on their computer.

“I make it easy to learn, shop and decide without pressure,” McCann notes.
