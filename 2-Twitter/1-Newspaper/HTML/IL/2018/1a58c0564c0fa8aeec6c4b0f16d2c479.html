Atlanta is quickly becoming the Land of the Giants in Logan County. Over the weekend the American Giants Museum celebrated its official grand opening. A unique ribbon cut was hosted by the Atlanta Betterment Fund and featured Bill Thomas on a ladder giving a quick introductory speech. Next, Joel Baker with American Giants ascended the ladder and snipped a ribbon that was tied to Snerds leg and the post of the Texaco sign on the property.

A new arrival was also celebrated as the giant Texaco man was a bit late to the party but managed to be standing tall before the weekend was out.

See more in todays edition of Lincoln Daily News, including a video of the ribbon cutting.
