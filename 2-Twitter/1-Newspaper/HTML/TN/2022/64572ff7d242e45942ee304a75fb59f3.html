    Richard Daigle

    I’m a late bloomer artists, released my first CD in 2020.
    I’m a retired writer/editor and communications specialist turned singer/songwriter the last several years. Things happen in life and we all find ways of processing. Songwriting is often that for me. I was so blessed and fortunate to be married for 34 years to Jan White Daigle, a fine woman, beautiful inside and out. She loved God and loved people, always cheerful and faithful. In 2014, cancer took away what should’ve been a fuller life but she made the most of the years she had and she touched so many people with me at the front of that list. Since then, I’ve met some wonderful women and had relationship and life experiences that have inspired songs.
    When it comes to songwriting influence, John Prine is at the top of the list.

    New Grass Express

    Band

    New Grass Express is a popular band based out of Chattanooga, Tennessee, who began their extraordinary journey playing bluegrass music together in 2011. This energetic group of very experienced musicians/singers play hard-driving contemporary and traditional Bluegrass/Gospel music capturing the very essence of the bluegrass genre’ and beyond.
    Additionally, their sincere gospel message along with their “honor tribute” to all Veterans and Active-Duty Service members alike, sets them apart as a musical group during each and every performance. Their distinctive harmonies, coupled with their “fast pickin” undoubtedly makes even the staunchest of listeners tap their toes and smile. Their intention is to touch hearts, lift spirits and hopefully take the audience away from the world’s stresses that encompass everyday life.
