New Warden hired at Wilcox prison
By valdostadailytimes.com
Published: 06/22/2018

FORSYTH — Georgia Department of Corrections Commissioner Gregory C. Dozier announced the reassignment of Artis Singleton as Warden at Wilcox State Prison, effective July 1.

As Warden, Singleton will be responsible for overseeing 269 staff and 1,827 medium security offenders, according to release.

“Singleton has shown great leadership skills in each position he has held with the department,” said Commissioner Gregory C. Dozier.

“We look forward to his continued leadership and guidance at Wilcox State Prison.”
