We are two Columbus Foster Dads. Have been licensed for over five years and have and adopted son. We are passionate about education, sports, politics and the fostering process. So many kids that need a safe, structured home in which to thrive. I have a masters degree in education and doctorate in pedagogy. My other half has a political science degree (from a rival university to my own no less).
