Oklahoma – Sons and Daughters of Liberty – Trevor Loudon

 September 27, 2013  Leave a comment

EnidNews.com:

On Monday, Sept. 30th, Sons and Daughters of Liberty groups from Garfield, Major and Dewey counties will host New Zealand author and lecturer Trevor Loudon.

That meeting will be 6 p.m. at YWCA Enid.

Loudon recently released “The Enemies Within: Communists, Socialists and Progressives in the U.S. Congress,” which includes profiles of 14 senators and more than 50 representatives and their ties to what Loudon says are Communist Party USA, Democratic Socialists of America, Workers World Party, Institute for Policy Studies, Council for a Livable World and other radical anti-American organizations.

Monday’s event is free and open to the public; however, seating is limited.
