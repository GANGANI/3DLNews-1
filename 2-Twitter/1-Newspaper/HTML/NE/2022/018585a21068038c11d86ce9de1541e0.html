As thousands of people continued to cross from Ukraine into Poland on Sunday, a special guest was on hand to welcome youngsters into the country. At the Medyka border crossing, children were greeted by Santa Claus, who gave out gifts to young people who had been forced to leave their homes due to the fighting. The man behind the idea, Chris Gural, said he wanted to give the youngsters something to smile about and to distract them from the thoughts of the war.
