EXHIBITIONS

The LUX Center for the Arts provides free gallery exhibitions, 18 or more annually, from local, regional and national artists. Each 'First Friday,' you're invited to see new exhibits in our galleries, a must-see stop for those seeking thought-provoking, relevant and critically-acclaimed art experiences. Our gallery store features an extraordinary selection of fine art and fine craft from local, national and international artists. Our galleries and our gallery store are open from 10AM - 5PM, Tuesday - Friday and 10AM - 5PM on Saturday. First Friday openings are from 5 - 8 PM each first Friday of the month.
