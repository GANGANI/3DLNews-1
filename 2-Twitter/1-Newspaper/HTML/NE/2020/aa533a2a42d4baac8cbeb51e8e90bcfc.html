Anderson O’Brien Fine Art is dedicated to the promotion of regional and national visual artists. We offer project design, delivery, installation, and consultation for either corporate or residential needs. 

 

Our gallery offers an expansive collection of modern and contemporary art, sculpture, fine art, and photography. Visit our online gallery or visit us today to learn more about our artists, upcoming exhibitions, and events.

Our showroom is now located in Midtown Crossing on the Northeast corner of 33rd and Farnam St. 

 

The gallery is available for viewing by appointment Monday and Tuesday and is open 10 am-5:30 pm Wednesday-Saturday. 

 

Our associates are available by phone, email, or live video feed for virtual art consultations at your convenience.
