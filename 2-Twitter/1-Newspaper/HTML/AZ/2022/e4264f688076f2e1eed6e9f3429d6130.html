    Here Weed Go! Ep. 22 It's Election Season 2022

    It’s that time of year again. And we're not talking about harvest season. It's election season!

    To get a better understanding of the candidates and initiatives that will shape the future of cannabis policy at the federal and state levels, host and cannabis reporter Eddie Celaya is joined by Justin Strekal, former national director of politics for NORML (the National Organization to Reform Marijuana Laws) and Jon Udell, the current co-interim director of Arizona NORML.

    In just about two weeks, five states (Arkansas, Maryland, Missouri, North Dakota and South Dakota) around the country will have the opportunity to join the Ganja Green club. Here in Arizona, local elections could have potential consequences for how both the medical and recreational industries run and operate.

    Celaya, Strekal and Udell discus if party really matters when it comes to getting cannabis legislation passed at both the federal and state levels, what chance legalization has on the ballots its on this season and what the Biden Administration’s announcement to pardon past charges and consider cannabis’s current scheduling status could mean.

    Our conversations kicks off with Strekal talking about his current work with BOWL Pac, a marijuana-centric policy political action committee he helped found.
