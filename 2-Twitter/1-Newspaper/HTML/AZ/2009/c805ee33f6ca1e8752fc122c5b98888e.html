Stock market today: Asian shares shrug off latest Wall St rout as Chinese factory activity weakens.

  • Updated 41 mins ago
  • Comments
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email

Asian shares are higher as investors shrug off yet another decline on Wall Street. An official survey showed a weakening in Chinese factory activity, but benchmarks in Hong Kong and Shanghai were higher. On Thursday, the S&P 500 fell 0.6%, even though the majority of stocks within it and across Wall Street rose. The Dow Jones Industrial Average dropped 0.9%, and the Nasdaq composite lost 1.1%. Nvidia finally ran out of momentum after leaping higher following its blowout profit report last week. Treasury yields eased in the bond market following weaker-than-expected reports on the job market and overall economy.
