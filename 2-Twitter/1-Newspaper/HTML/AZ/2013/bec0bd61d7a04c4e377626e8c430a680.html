GOP needs to embrace grass-roots Liberty Wing | WMICentral.com

17 Wednesday Apr 2013

Posted by sunrizec in Uncatagorized

≈ Comments Off on GOP needs to embrace grass-roots Liberty Wing | WMICentral.com

“Robert Graham has been on the job for just under three months, and the state GOP chairman is already being tested by party members.
Dara Vanesian, the Navajo County GOP chair, has sent him a letter asking him to distance himself from the Aug. 28, 2012, party rules formulated at the national convention in Tampa.
Known as the Tampa Rules, they were put forth by the Romney campaign specifically to silence or otherwise suppress the grass-roots wing of the party.
They have been called Tea Partiers; Vanesian refers to them as the Liberty Wing.”
