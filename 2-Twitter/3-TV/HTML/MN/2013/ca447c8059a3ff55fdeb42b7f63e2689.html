At the Minnesota Technology Network (MTN), our mission is to foster innovation, education, and collaboration in the fields of technology, media, and the arts. We aim to empower individuals and organizations to explore, learn, and grow in an ever-changing technological landscape.


Our Core Values:


  1. Lifelong Learning: We believe that continuous learning is essential for personal and professional growth. We strive to provide accessible and engaging educational opportunities that inspire curiosity, creativity, and innovation.
  2. Collaboration: We recognize the power of collaboration and teamwork. We aim to create a supportive and inclusive environment where individuals can come together, share ideas, and work towards common goals.
  3. Community: We are committed to building a strong and vibrant community that promotes diversity, equity, and inclusion. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels to join our network and contribute their unique talents and perspectives.
  4. Empowerment: We are dedicated to empowering our members to reach their full potential through mentorship, networking, and access to resources. We celebrate and uplift the achievements of our community and encourage members to pursue their passions and dreams.
  5. Impact: We believe in making a positive impact on our local and global communities through technology and media initiatives. We strive to support projects and programs that address pressing social, economic, and environmental challenges.


Together, we are shaping the future of technology, media, and the arts in Minnesota and beyond. 


Join us on our mission to create a more connected, innovative, and inclusive world.
