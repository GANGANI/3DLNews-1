Missing 20-year-old Welsh woman located

By Michael Cooper
Published: Dec. 24, 2013 at 6:06 PM CST|Updated: Dec. 26, 2013 at 2:38 PM CST

WELSH, LA (KPLC) - A 20-year-old Welsh woman who was reported missing on Monday has been located.

Authorities issued a news release early Tuesday, asking for the public's help in locating Chelsey Bonin.

Around 1 p.m. Tuesday, Bonin's grandmother told KPLC that she has been located and that she was "out with friends."

The Welsh Police Department later confirmed that Bonin had been found.
