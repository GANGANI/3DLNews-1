#ORUMBB Featured in Half Hour Special

3/25/2021 3:03:00 PM

Story Links

TULSA, Okla. – The Oral Roberts athletic department announced that KOTV-Channel 6 in Tulsa will air a half hour feature about ORU men's basketball Thursday, March 25 at 8:30 p.m.

"This is a special time for our men's basketball program, athletic department and university," ORU Deputy Athletic Director Tim Johnson said. "We are honored and humbled to be in this position and ultimately thankful to God for trusting us with this platform. Thank you KOTV-News on 6 for hosting this special about ORU basketball and our historic run in the NCAA Tournament."

 Thursday's special, "Road to the Sweet 16", will feature memorable moments in ORU basketball history with many stories and interviews. The special will begin at 8:30 p.m. CST on KOTV-News on 6 as well as newson6.com.
 
For the latest information on ORU Athletics, follow @ORUAthletics on Twitter or go to www.ORUAthletics.com. Fans can also receive updates on the team by downloading the new ORU Athletics App in the Google Play or App stores.
