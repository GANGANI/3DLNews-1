Papa John's Football Picks & the 4-for-4 Pizza Meal Deal



Play PAPA JOHN'S Football Picks on KFOR.com

Papa John's and NewsChannel 4 invites you to register and play at http://www.kfor.com/footballpicks.

As a special incentive for playing, Papa John's is offering you 4 pizzas for the price of $4 each.

That's up to 4 Pizzas, with 1 topping each, for the price of $4 each!
Here's how you can get the 4-For-4 Pizza Meal Deal:
  • Go to http://www.kfor.com/footballpicks and register to play Papa John's Football Picks on KFOR.com
  • The Papa John's Football Picks Exclusive promo code for 4-For-4 Pizza Meal Deal is KFOR
  • To order the 4-For-4 Pizza Meal Deal, go to https://www.papajohnsonline.com/customer_signup.jsp
  • If you already have a Papa John's account, enter your log in information at the top of the page
  • If you do not have a Papa John's account, please fill out the registration form
  • This special offer is available for delivery or carryout order
  • Once you get to the menu page, enter the KFOR promo code at the top of the page
  • Select your pizzas and then check out to receive your 4-For-4 Pizza Meal Deal
This offer is available for use once per Papa John's Online registered user.  All applicable taxes and delivery charges apply. 

Thank you for signing up to play Papa John's Football Picks.

As you're playing the contest, continue to watch your Papa John's Football Picks e-mail reminders for more exciting offers from Papa John's.

Have fun playing and thank you from your Oklahoma Papa John's Stores in Norman, Oklahoma City, Stillwater, and Enid.
