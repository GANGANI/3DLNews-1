The Fiesta Bowl Parade is a cherished tradition, bringing together communities from across Arizona and beyond to celebrate the spirit of unity, creativity, and cultural diversity. As a marching band, you have the incredible opportunity to be an integral part of this exciting event, showcasing your musical talents and adding to the vibrant tapestry of the parade.

We welcome marching bands of all sizes and styles to apply and share their passion for music with thousands of spectators lining the streets of central Phoenix. Whether you specialize in traditional marches, contemporary arrangements, or innovative performances, we welcome your unique contributions to this iconic parade.

Participating in the Fiesta Bowl Parade is not just about marching; it's about creating unforgettable memories, forging lasting friendships, and inspiring audiences with your dedication and artistry. We encourage you to bring your creativity, energy, and spirit of camaraderie as you march through the heart of Phoenix, spreading joy and excitement along the parade route and on television.

The Fiesta Bowl Parade travels a two-mile route through historic downtown Phoenix. In addition to marching bands, the lineup features a wide variety of brightly-colored floats, helium balloons, equestrian units, specialty groups and local celebrities.

View the parade broadcast live throughout Arizona on Arizona's Family channels 3 and 5 or online at www.azfamily.com.
