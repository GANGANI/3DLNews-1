2012-2013 Fiesta Bowl Parade Lineup


SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. (Dec. 21, 2012) — High school marching bands from eight states, giant balloons and colorful floats are among the highlights of the 42nd Annual Fort McDowell Fiesta Bowl Parade presented by Fort McDowell Yavapai Nation. The parade travels through central Phoenix begining at 11:00 AM (Mountain) on Saturday, December 29.

Olympic Gold Medal-winning swimmer Missy Franklin will serve as the grand marshal. Marching bands representing the teams playing in that afternoon's Buffalo Wild Wings Bowl football game — Michigan State and Texas Christian University — will also participate.

Curbside seating along the parade route is free to the public. Reserved seating in several locations along the parade route is available for $10-$25 through the Fiesta Bowl Ticket Office at 480-350-0911 or by visiting www.FiestaBowl.org.

The Fiesta Bowl Parade will be broadcast live in the Phoenix area on ABC 15, and can also be viewed live online at www.abc15.com.
