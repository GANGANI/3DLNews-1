YOUR VOTE 2018

Congressional District 2 Debate

Season 1, Episode 2 of 3

Democrat Ann Kirkpatrick and Republican Lea Marquez Peterson will meet Oct. 9 in Tucson for a live, televised debate in their bids to represent Southern Arizona's 2nd Congressional District in the U.S. House of Representatives. The debate will be moderated by AZPM reporter Christopher Conover and feature a panel of journalists, including Lorraine Rivera (AZPM), Steve Goldstein (KJZZ) and Joe Ferguson (Arizona Daily Star). (Tue, Oct 9th)
