About KIROTV.com Mobile. Seattle-area news, weather, traffic & sports

With the KIRO 7 News app, you can stay informed on breaking and developing news across Seattle, and all of Western Washington. Get alerts targeted to your location so you know when news happens where you are. Stream live KIRO 7 newscasts directly from your phone or tablet. Plan and prepare for your day with KIRO 7 PinPoint Weather forecasts and alerts. Stay up to date with KIRO 7 RealTime Traffic alerts that help you plan your commute.

Features

• News alerts on breaking, developing and major news of the day

• KIRO 7 PinPoint Weather updates

• Stream live KIRO 7 Newscasts

• KIRO 7 RealTime Traffic alerts

• Customizable KIRO 7 News notifications

• Access to exclusive KIRO 7 content

The KIRO 7 News app alerts you to major local news in all areas of Western Washington, including Tacoma, Renton, Everett, Snohomish, Kirkland, Bellevue, Mercer Island, Shoreline, Redmond, Woodinville, Bremerton, Silverdale, Issaquah, Kent, Auburn, Lynnwood, Bothell, Edmonds, Lake Stevens, Marysville, Lakewood, Olympia and Puyallup.
