Joint Cities Development’s mission, is to share economic and intellectual resources, accelerate business growth, and normalize positive social impact strategies, by providing unique, high-quality collaborations, to build underserved communities, primarily in Metropolitan Detroit.

About

At Joint Cities, we are dedicated to supporting small business owners and entrepreneurs to succeed in today’s ever-changing marketplace. Our focus is on creating opportunities for growth and expansion, connecting business owners with supportive resources, and providing valuable training and education. Our team is passionate about making a positive impact on our communities and we believe that small businesses play a critical role in building strong and vibrant communities.
