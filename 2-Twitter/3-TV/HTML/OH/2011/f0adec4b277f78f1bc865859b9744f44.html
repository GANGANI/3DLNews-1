Many Who Heard the Message Believed (ACTS 4:4)

Since 1982, WTLW TV-44 has been broadcasting Christian and family-safe programming to West Ohio. In the late 1970’s, founders Ron and Becky Mighell envisioned Christian TV for Greater Limaland; and it was through their faith-filled efforts that TV-44 became a reality on June 13,1982 with the broadcast of our first signal. The station is owned and operated by ACTS Ministries, a 501c3 organization overseen by a board of directors consisting of local community leaders. TV-44 is viewer-supported both in prayer and financial giving. We welcome your partnership as we continue to spread the Gospel throughout West Ohio!
