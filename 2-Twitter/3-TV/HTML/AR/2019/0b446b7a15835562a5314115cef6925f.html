Fast Break Friday Night - 1/11

By Chris Hudgison
Published: Jan. 11, 2019 at 11:33 PM CST|Updated: Jan. 11, 2019 at 11:36 PM CST

Week 2 of Fast Break Friday Night is in the books.

Jonesboro moved to 12-4 with a 62-50 win over Greene County Tech in our Game of the Night. Avery Felts dropped 43 pts as Westside moved to 14-2.

Matthew Schwartz profiled the Hoffman family. Spencer coaches his daughters Lotti and Carolina, Riverside girls are 22-3 this season.
