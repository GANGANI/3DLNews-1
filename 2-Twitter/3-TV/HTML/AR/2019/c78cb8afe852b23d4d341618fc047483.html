Fast Break Friday Night (2/8) - Watch highlights

By Chris Hudgison
Published: Feb. 8, 2019 at 11:36 PM CST|Updated: Feb. 8, 2019 at 11:39 PM CST

Week 6 of Fast Break Friday Night featured conference games in the 5A East and non-conference tilts for 4A, 3A, 2A, & 1A teams.

Valley View beat Bay 71-57 in our Game of the Night. The Blazers take a 7 game winning streak to next week’s 4A-3 Tournament.

We’ll update this page later tonight with highlights.

You can follow Chris & Matthew on Twitter along with following the Fast Break Friday Night Facebook page.
