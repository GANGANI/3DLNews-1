Save the Date: 2024 Radiothon is December 5

The 12th annual Radiothon raised $2,064,014.00!

Thank you to everyone who donated, bid on an auction item, tuned in to watch or listen, and helped spread the word. To everyone who planned, volunteered, hauled and cleaned, thank you! And to everyone who graced us with their presence and entertained the audience through fifteen hours — live! — we had a wonderful time and we hope you did, too!

Thank you to all of you who support SAY Detroit year after year, all year long. You make it possible to provide pathways to success for Detroiters in need, and we are so thankful for you!
