United States Navy Sailor from Carrollton dies in accident while serving in Japan

by Dave Bondy

Fri, October 8th 2021 at 9:38 PM
Updated Fri, October 8th 2021 at 12:17 AM
5
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
23-year-old Logan Leaym passed away following an accident while he was serving with the United States Navy in Japan. (Leaym family)

CARROLLTON, Mich - 23-year-old Logan Leaym from Carrollton passed away on September 18 while serving for the United States Navy in Japan.

Logan's family says he died from an accident.

Logan's aunt Windi Brown tells us he loved playing the guitar, studying martial arts, and taking pictures.

Leaym joined the United States Navy in April of 2020.

Click here to read Logan's obituary.

Brown tells us Logan was also very passionate about wrestling and working out. Logan had a great sense of humor;

In Lieu of flowers, those planning a memorial contribution are asked to consider the “Wishes of the Leaym Family” in care of Brenda Leaym.
