Texas Instagram model found dead in apartment

RICHMOND, Texas - A model murder mystery.

An Instagram influencer, found dead in her Richmond Texas apartment -- just outside of Houston.

The body of the man accused of killing her -- also inside.

Shelley Childers has the story.

On Instagram, she was known as Miss Mercedes Morr, her adult content followed by nearly three million people... Including super stars Snoop Dogg and Megan Thee Stallion.

Her real name was Jenae Gagnier.

The 33-year-old was found dead inside her upscale apartment.

Police say they were called to this quiet complex for a welfare check Sunday.

Inside the apartment they found Mercedes Morr and another man-- believed to be the suspect who killed her, Kevin Alexander Accorto.

He was also dead, police calling it a murder-suicide.

Investigators say there is no relationship between Mercedes and the suspect, telling us his last known address is from Florida.

Neighbors we spoke with call the complex quiet and secure.

"It would be kind of surprising to know that this was an intruder, someone that broke into the house because I wouldn't think that would happen here," said Field Ledford, neighbor.

Police have not said how the two died, telling us the cause of death will come from the medical examiner.

The Fort Bend County Medical Examiner reporting the cause of death was strangulation and that she also suffered a traumatic concussion.

The suspect -- Kevin Accorto's death ruled a suicide.

The medical examiner says his cause of death was "multiple sharp force trauma".
