Motorcyclist killed in crash on Montwood and Saul Kleinfeld ID'd

by Jessica Gonzalez | Javier Gutierrez

Fri, November 3rd 2017 at 7:36 PM
Updated Fri, November 3rd 2017 at 4:40 PM
3
VIEW ALL PHOTOS
Police say one person was seriously injured in a motorcycle crash on Saul Kleinfeld near Montwood. (KFOX14/CBS4)

EL PASO, Texas (KFOX14) — El Paso police know the identity of a motorcyclist killed Thursday evening on Montwood and Saul Kleinfeld.

Benjamin Sanchez, 19, was killed after his motorcycle struck a curb along the 2000 block of Saul Kleinfeld near Montwood Drive in east El Paso.

The crash happened just before 7 p.m. Thursday.

Police investigators determined Sanchez was speeding before he crashed.

During the investigation, Saul Kleinfeld from Montwood to Robert Wynn Street were closed.

Sanchez died at the Del Sol Hospital, said police.
