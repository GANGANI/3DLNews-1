'Joe Gatto's Night of Comedy' tour stopping in Amarillo in June

by Matthew Watkins

Tue, December 13th 2022 at 4:43 PM

Joe Gatto, best known for his time on the television series Impractical Jokers, is bringing his 'Night of Comedy' tour to the Amarillo Civic Center Complex in June 2023. (Amarillo Civic Center Complex)

    TOPICS:

    Entertainment CultureUnited SupermarketsJoe GattoAmarillo Civic CenterAmarillo, TexasAmerican Television SeriesCities In The United StatesImpractical Jokers

AMARILLO, Texas (KVII) — Joe Gatto, best known for his time on the television series Impractical Jokers, is bringing his 'Night of Comedy' tour to the Amarillo Civic Center Complex in June 2023.

Gatto announced additional dates for the ongoing 'Joe Gatto's Night of Comedy' tour, including a stop in Amarillo on June 11, 2023.

Tickets go on sale Friday and will be available through panhandletickets.com, the Amarillo Civic Center box office, calling (806) 378-3096 and at area United Supermarkets.
