Apostle Blair

Dr. Apostle

Winston Blair

A native son from the beautiful island of Jamaica and affectionately known as "Brother Blair," Dr. Apostle Winston Blair is the host of the growing popular Christian program on his online radio show. As a radio host for over 30 plus years, "Brother Blair" has reached and touched many lives with his gospel music, charismatic personality, and compassionate heart.

Well-loved by many, he's a community activist who works with many organizations in the Atlanta areas and worldwide to help those in need, whether they are physically, mentally, emotionally, or spiritually exhausted. Dr. Apostle Blair is in great demand as a host, teacher, and preacher for numerous events in the United States and abroad with his charismatic personality.

Hope Redemption Worship Center

Our mission is to do what the word of God has commanded them to do.

Please Join Us Online or In Person.

We are helping people become more passionate and devoted followers of Jesus Christ.

In Person or Online

Our mission at HRWC is to do what the word of God has commanded us to do. Our vision is to witness God’s manifestation of ministries that will build up this community. Our goal is to educate, equip and empower humanity to do the will of Jesus Christ. To provide support, encouragement, spiritual education, and guidance to the youth and adults, in the community.
The purpose of these teachings is for us to become united as one.

Hope Redemption Worship Center was placed on Pastor Blair’s heart in December 2011. HRWC is a place that may help or save someone. HRWC is also defined as grounds for believing that something good may happen. Redemption is second chances: it is the act of being saved from sin, error, or evil.
