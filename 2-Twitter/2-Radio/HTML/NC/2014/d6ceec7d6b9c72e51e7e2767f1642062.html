Listen to a Community Postcard

From the majestic mountains of the Appalachians to the serene shores of the Outer Banks, each county has a unique tale to tell. Discover hidden gems, historical landmarks, mouthwatering cuisine, and vibrant cultures, all packed into bite-sized narratives that will leave you wanting more. Watch for new Postcards each month, and listen now.

Spring Book Club Read

In honor of Asian American, Native Hawaiian and Pacific Islander Heritage Month we'll be reading Slow Noodles: A Cambodian Memoir of Love, Loss, and Family Recipes. In Slow Noodles, author Chantha Nguon recounts her harrowing life as a Cambodian refugee who lost everything but was able to retain the memories of her mother's kitchen. Please join us at Scuppernong Books in Greensboro on Saturday, June 8th at 2:00 to join the discussion!
