Our Story

Since 1996 Waverly Beach has been known for its fun atmosphere, the Valley’s best comfort food, live entertainment, its Wisconsin fish fry and of course the spectacular view! Located on the shores of Lake Winnebago it’s where fun begins! 

This unique destination offers a variety of spaces to enjoy including indoor and outdoor bars, patio seating along with indoor seating and a ballroom perfect for any special occasion or corporate event.
