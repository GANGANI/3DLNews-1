Classical 90.5

One of the nation's last remaining, fully-curated classical music stations, with 24/7 radio broadcast, online streaming, schedules, and more.
Classical 90.5 is supported by listeners like you.

Listen with your smart speaker

Alexa
Tell Alexa to Enable the Arizona Public Media Skill. Then you can "Ask Arizona Public Media to play Classical."
Google Home
Ask your device to "Play Classical 90.5 from Tucson, Arizona."

Classical Extempore interviews with Andy Bade

Helios Ensemble Extempore

Ben Hansen, Artistic Director of the Helios Ensemble, talks with AZPM's Andy Bade about their upcoming Community Concert performance.

Khris Dodge Extempore

Tucson Pops Orchestra music director Khris Dodge speaks with AZPM's Andy Bade about their upcoming spring concert series.

Choral Conductor James Higgs Extempore

AZPM's Andy Bade spoke with James Higgs about the modern premiere of an 18th century mass.

"From the Top" host Peter Dugan Extempore

Peter Dugan, pianist and host of NPR's "From the Top", talks with AZPM's Andy Bade about the young performers he works with on his show.

Morris Robinson Extempore

AZPM's Andy Bade speaks with opera singer and former football star Morris Robinson about the relationship between sports and the arts, the state of diversity in classical music, and singing Verdi’s Requiem.
