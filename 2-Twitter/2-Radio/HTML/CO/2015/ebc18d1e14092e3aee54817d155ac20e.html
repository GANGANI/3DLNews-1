          about

          What can I say? I know it's cheesy and gushy and cliched. Mea culpa! And really, I shouldn't apologize, right?
          I was (and still am) madly in love. The heavens opened, the earth moved, and fireballs and other portents literally filled the sky. Literally. No, really. Total strangers came up to us on the streets and thanked us for being in love. No, really. At one point, we were seriously scanning the horizon for unicorns. This song came out and I just went with it. In the tradition of "Dirty Dancing," and all the other love duets from the past 30+ years! Would love to hear Blake and Carrie do it. Or Ed Sheeran and Joss. Or Luther and Celine. Maybe even Alison and Robert?
          For those truly in love, or at least feeling all those chemicals. They'll get it.
