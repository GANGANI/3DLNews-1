YOUR LOCAL VOICE

KSUN Community Radio, Inc. is a Colorado non-profit corporation that owns and operates KDBN FM “KSUN” is a listener supported, volunteer run, commercial FM radio station that is dedicated to serving the Grand Valley situated in Garfield County and Mesa County, Colorado on Colorado’s Western Slope. KDBN strives to reflect the Grand Valley’s culture, artistic, educational, civic, and business interest by offering stimulating and diverse programming. You can enjoy our quality programming 24-hours a day, 7 days a week by tuning your radio to 101.1 FM

Why Donate to Community Radio?

When it comes to sharing your story or promoting a creative project, community radio plays a critical role. It provides a platform where diverse voices can be heard, stories can be shared, and local talents can be showcased. By donating to a community radio, you are directly contributing to the amplification of unheard narratives and the empowerment of local initiatives.

CORPORATE SPONSORS

  • Williams Corporation

    The Williams Corporation is an American energy company based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Its core business is natural gas processing and transportation with additional petroleum and electricity generation assets. Williams strives for strong involvement in the communities where we operate. Safeguarding our neighbors and people is engrained in our culture and fundamental to everything we do. We are invested in long-term partnerships with our local communities – the areas where our people live, work and play. Thank you Williams Corporation for being one of KSUN’s Corporate Sponsors.

  • Clarks Market

    Clark’s Market is a KSUN Community Radio Corporate Sponsor and KSUN is so proud to have Clark’s Market as part of our local family. The philanthropic projects and dedication to their home communities which Clark’s Market servs, is deeply appreciated by all. The Clark’s group is always there to lend a hand or service to the organizations of their communities. The Clark’s family has been in the grocery business for over 45 years and they continuously strive to provide their clientele with the products and service that are so appreciated. Thank you Clark’s Market for always being there for KSUN Community Radio and our local citizens and businesses.

  • Town of Parachute

    KSUN Community Radio is pleased to have the Town of Parachute as one of their corporate members. The Town of Parachute contributes to the welfare and enrichment of the Parachute Community with parks, business districts, walking/biking trails, information/rest area center with a dog park and the newly developed Top Adventure Park which includes rental of recreational equipment and guided tours. KSUN Community Radio is pleased to have the Town of Parachute as a member of our corporate family.

  • FCI Constructors Inc.

    FCI Constructors began building in Colorado in 1978 dedicating their company to truth, hard work, honesty, professionalism and support of their local communities and contributing to the communities where they build. KSUN Community Radio is honored to have FCI Constructors as one of our KSUN Corporate Sponsors program. FCI’s goal is to reinvest in the communities they serve and create a better place to work and live. Thank you FCI Constructors from KSUN Radio for your support.

  • Grand Valley Fire Department

    The brave firefighters and 911 responders from the Grand Valley Fire Protection District do a great job of keeping our communities safe, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. KSUN Community Radio is fortunate to have the Grand Valley Fire Protection District as one of our Corporate Sponsors for KSUN Community Radio. The Grand Valley Fire Protection District is recognized by the Grand Valley communities for their quick response times and care and concern for our citizens. Welcome to the KSUN Corporate Family Grand Valley Fire District members.

  • Parachute/Battlement Mesa Parks and Recreation District

    Parachute Battlement Mesa Parks and Recreation District is dedicated to providing facilities and activities from the 18 Hold Battlement Mesa Golf Course, the Community Park, 53,000 square foot Recreation Center and ball fields in neighboring Parachute. The Parachute Battlement Mesa Parks and Recreation District is an integral part of the community’s lifestyle. KSUN Community Radio is very pleased to have these two entities as members of the KSUN Community Radio.

  • Grand Valley Resident's Team

    Improving the quality of life in the local communities of Battlement Mesa and Parachute is the mission of the Grand Valley Resident’s Team. The Team’s motto of diversity, equity, unity and growth is exemplified in the many community projects and activities the Grand Valley Residents Team hosts and promotes – The Grand Valley Days Rodeo with entertainment, food and games, local holiday events and celebrations and an active group that meets monthly for education, socializing and organizing their next event or community project. Thank you so much Grand Valley Resident’s Team for being part of our KSUN Corporate Family.

  • Grand River Health

    Grand River Health District is here in Garfield County to serve our 27,000 plus individuals within our 1500 square mile service territory. This District accomplishes this through the dedication of over 550 employees and a 100 plus affiliated physicians. Grand River Health offers twenty four hours, 7 days a week ER services, diagnostic imaging, lab services, a surgery center, in-patient medical and surgical unit, physical therapy department, infusion center and acute care unit. Grand River Hospital District served over 27,000 individuals within a 15 square mile territory, utilizing over 550 employees and 100 plus affiliated physicians. The district is governed by an elected seven member Board of Directors and a Chief Executive Officer. KSUN Community Radio is so appreciative of Grand River Health’s continued and constant support of KSUN Community Radio.

  • Garfield County Board of Commissioners

    The Garfield County Board of Commissioners is the governing body for Garfield County and has been one of the “original friends’ of KSUN Community Radio with their very generous yearly donation of funds to allow KSUN to continually upgrade their broadcasting equipment. The Garfield County Commissioners are involved in our community from Carbondale to Battlement Mesa/Parachute and have enabled KSUN to expand and diversify our programs and increase our listening audience. You were the first KSUN Corporate Sponsor and we truly appreciate your loyalty and support.

  • Garfield County Sheriff's Office

    The Garfield County Sheriff’s Department portrays their motto “doing something to make a difference” seven days a week twenty four hours a day. The mission of the Garfield County Sheriff’s Office is to provide solutions for the people through professional, ethical and compassionate conduct by their personnel. The vision statement of the Garfield County Sheriff’s Department is to the future and prepare it for today and their motto is to “do something to make a difference.” KSUN Community Radio’s family is so proud to have the continued support of the Garfield County Sheriff’s Department as one of our dedicated corporate sponsors.

  • Grand Valley School District 16

    The volunteer members of the Grand Valley School District 16 are dedicated to the educational quality of the schools in the district. The school board is responsible to provide a setting and environment for the students and employees who promote a healthy and productive learning environment. This mission is guided by grounding our work in the four pillars of individual student success. KSUN Community Radio is very proud to have Grand Valley School District as a sponsor of the weekly programs Education Matters and to have the Grand Valley School District 16 and the Board of Education as corporate sponsor for KSUN Community Radio. You are a vital part of our KSUN Family.

  • Colorado River Valley Chamber of Commerce

    The Colorado River Valley Chamber of Commerce represents diverse communities in Western Colorado for over the past 67 years. Our mission is dedicated to serve our businesses, non-profits and government agencies. Our volunteer board and ambassadors consist of business owners and leaders in our communities who problems solve the issues of our local businesses and communities they face today. We are solution driven and advocate for your business. We strive to provide strategies that support our economy, economic development, housing, new businesses, workforce training, business support and collaboration with other member businesses. We are your business center for your business related items and we take it a step further by supporting our communities. We know that without “community”, you will not have a business. The Colorado River Valley Chamber is to help ensure your business success.

  • Alpine Bank

    Alpine Bank, a member of FDIC is celebrating their 75th Anniversary here in the State of Colorado. Alpine is dedicated to all of its community locations by making banking easier for their customers in large and small communities, being a trusted friend of individuals and businesses with their banking needs and being extremely supportive of its community activities and non-profit organizations including KSUN Community Radio. As a corporate sponsor for KSUN, Alpine Bank has assisted the station continuously to improve their equipment and broadcasting abilities. Welcome and a thank you to Alpine Bank as one of our very first and faithful corporate sponsors.

  • Battlement Mesa Service Association

    The Battlement Mesa Service Association consists of a Board of Directors who manage and direct the affairs of the Battlement Mesa Community. Presently the BMSA Board of Directors who manage and direct the affairs of the Battlement Mesa Community. Presently the BMSA Board consists of fifteen village Delegates/Directors and two Developer Delegates representing sixteen Delegate areas. Each Village within the Battlement Mesa PUD has the right to elect a Delegate to the Battlement Mesa Service Association and these elected Delegates are automatically elected to the BMSA Board of Directors. Village Delegates/Board Members are elected and serve a three year term, there are no limits. Delegates/Board Members often serve more than one term. An individual that owns property in more than one delegate area, is eligible to serve as a Delegate/Director for delegate areas where they own property. Therefore, an individual may serve as Delegate/Director for multiple delegate areas.

  • Remax Elite Realty

    Remax is a local and reliable real estate company in downtown Parachute. Their team is there to guide you throughout your next investment for a home, business, new construction or land. Amy Perrin and her team members are a part of the Parachute and Battlement Mesa Community and always available to guide you through your real estate purchase. From start to finish, answer your phone calls and keep you informed through your real estate journey leading you to a professional and timely conclusion.
