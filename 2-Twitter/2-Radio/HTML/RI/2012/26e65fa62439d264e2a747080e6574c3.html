Jazz After Dinner

Friday evenings at 8:00 PM join award winning pianist, Grammy nominated composer, Steinway Artist and your host Joe Parillo as he brings you an hour of some of the best Jazz on the planet.

WCRI’s Kids Hour

The first weekend of the month on Saturday at 9:00am and Sunday at 9pm, with hosts Jamie & Spencer. WCRI’s Kids Hour as we help capture your child’s attention and make them a lifelong fan of the arts! This program will explore the many ways music and the arts are a part of everyday life.

This Old Tree

The last weekend of the month on Saturday at 9:00am and Sunday at 9:00pm, with host Doug Still. Old trees are awe inspiring links to the past that fire our historical imagination. Ever wonder what their stories are? We'll find out in This Old Tree!

Conducting Conversations

Every Sunday evening from 7:00 to 8:00 PM join your host Mike Maino for an hour of music and conversation with notable people in the local arts community.
