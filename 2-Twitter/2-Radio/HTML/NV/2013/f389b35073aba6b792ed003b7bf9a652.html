About the NEW KJUL

Welcome to your new home for EASY FAVORITES! The NEW KJUL 104.7.  We’re now playing your all time favorite artists — Elton John, Madonna, Rod Stewart, Phil Collins, The Eagles, Backstreet Boys, Chicago, Air Supply, Lionel Ritchie, Fleetwood Mac, and hundreds more!

Our music is curated and custom tailored specifically for you. Perfect for cleaning the house, cruising the boulevard, pecking at a cell phone, or hammering away at the job. Take us along whenever and wherever you go. From home to work or play, we’ve got your best music every day.

Between the songs you’ll recognize the veteran Air Staff you have known and loved for years: Scott Gentry and David Allen. Companions you know and trust for warmth, good humor, and family safe listening enjoyment.

We’re playing more EASY FAVORITES than any other station, proven hit music by well established artists. No stiff played in any DJ shift.

DJ & Staff

At KJUL we have a very laid back approach to working. When you are having fun doing what you do for a living, it’s not really work at all. We all love being part of the KJUL family and want you to join in and learn more about us too.

Scott Gentry

DJ-GM

A 52-year veteran of broadcasting, Scott’s lengthily career was forged at a young age when he began in 1968 with Armed Forces Radio and Television during the Vietnam War. As he says, “That’s when I fell in love with Broadcasting. I was hooked the minute I opened the microphone.”

David Allen

DJ

I wanted to be a radio broadcaster my entire life, listening to radio stations constantly as a kid and then studying Radio and TV for a career at Michigan State University. As all radio people have, I’ve worked at lots of stations in all parts of the country but have found Las Vegas to be my favorite place of all!
