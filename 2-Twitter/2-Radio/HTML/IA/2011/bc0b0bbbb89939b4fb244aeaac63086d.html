About Us

Welcome to Classic KMRY!

Local voices on a local station. KMRY is your source for the greatest music, hourly news update from CBS Radio News & the KMRY Newsroom, and sports coverage from around the area. With chances to win on our "Sponsor Spin" to great deals throughout Eastern Iowa with our "Discount Dining". You won't want to miss a minute of the day with us!

On Air Schedule

  • Ricky Bartlett    6:00am - 12:00pm
  • Holly Penuel - 1:00pm - 5:00pm
  • News throughout the day from CBS News
  • KCRG TV9 First Alert Forecast throughout the day
