The Flagship Station for Clemson Athletics.

WCCP 105.5 FM The Roar is the premier local sports radio station in the state of South Carolina. We broadcast 13 hours of live and local content every single weekday starting at 6AM and ending at 7PM. WCCP is also the flagship station for Clemson Athletics.

If the Tigers are in action, we are on air. Listeners can hear every single Clemson football, baseball, men’s and women’s basketball game on our airwaves and right here on theroarfm.com.
