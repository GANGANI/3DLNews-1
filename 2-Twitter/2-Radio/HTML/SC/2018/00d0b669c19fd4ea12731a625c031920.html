Midlands Technical College Foundation

Prioritize academics and career training (PACT) for young adults in foster care

PACT@MTC runs Career Camps and events to enable foster youth to successfully complete coursework and to offer them guided college and career exploration opportunities with the support of dedicated faculty staff and college coaches. The objective is to provide access to college early and to enable 130 foster youth to obtain quality careers which provide family-sustainable wages.
