WHO WE ARE

New England’s Largest Local Media Company

Binnie Media is New England’s largest locally owned media company that reaches 1.5 million people each week, including over 425,400 radio listeners in New England, with digital and outdoor assets delivering millions of impressions to people in Maine, New Hampshire, Vermont and Massachusetts. Our team strives to achieve long term relationships with our clients that are mutually beneficial, cost effective, and successful!

OUR SERVICES

Radio Advertising
When getting your message out, you have to know that you’re correctly hitting your target audience. Binnie Media utilizes top-rated radio formats on their radio stations all across New England to make sure that your message is heard loud and clear. With hit stations all across Maine and New Hampshire, you will have access to a diverse audience at a competitive price.
Digital Marketing
Binnie Media Digital is the web development and digital marketing department of Binnie Media, and is focused on creating business value through the development and maintenance of websites, web applications and utilizing an array of digital marketing strategies. We work closely with our clients to figure out which marketing plan is best for their business, while keeping costs low and efficiency high.
I-95 BILLBOARDS
Reach a whole new audience by presenting your message on New Hampshire’s only Digital Billboard on I-95! Reach commuters traveling the Massachusetts, New Hampshire, and Maine corridor. With over 1,010,980 impressions per week, your message is guaranteed to get results.
EVENT MARKETING
From small to large-scale events, use the power of our radio stations to give your event the marketing boost it needs. Whether it’s a promotional partner to help get the word out to our massive audience, or on-site hosting and entertaining, our stations and on-air personalities are here to work with you on making your event a success.
RECRUITMENT
We at Binnie Media are here to get YOUR business back on its feet by recruiting those hardworking employees you desperately need. Take advantage of a free consultation from an expert in radio recruitment and join the businesses that are benefitting from these qualified leads. This consultation comes with no strings attached, no purchase is required. Our goal is to help local businesses like yours succeed!
