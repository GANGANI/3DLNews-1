About

Welcome To Word Up
We invite you to healing, Prosperity and the Power of God's word as you enter into the fellowship and presence of His Holy Spirit. You will experience complete ministry for your whole Spirit, Soul and Body. Word Up Ministries is a Non-Denominational Fellowship for those who desire the fullness of the Five Fold Ministry without traditional influence. The Liberty of Christ is ever present and God’s Word will lift you up. It is our prayer that you receive mightily all that the Holy Spirit has for you. We encourage you to enter into His presence with us as we praise, worship and receive the Word of God from each ministry gift that God has sent us. You will be blessed as you participate in the anointed services of God.
