GOD'S VOICE Conference

 GOD'S VOICE Conference will bring biblical clarity to the recent confusion surrounding the issues of "re-voiced" sexuality and "LGBTQ+ Christians." This year's GOD'S VOICE Conference will present a biblical response to LGBTQ+ tyranny of the culture, LGBTQ+  tyranny in the church and the continued queering* of the church of Jesus Christ. This conference is presented in an effort to teach, strengthen and encourage sincere followers of Jesus Christ and Christian leaders across all denominations to stand on the inerrant, unchanging and life-transforming power of the Word of God.

What the church needs now is GOD'S VOICE!
