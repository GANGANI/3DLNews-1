Brad Paisley Announces 2016 Life Amplified World Tour

Christina VinsonPublished: May 11, 2016
  • SHARE
  • TWEET

Brad Paisley has announced his 2016 Life Amplified World Tour, and he's bringing along Tyler Farr and Maddie & Tae for the ride.

The tour kicks off May 19 in Sacramento, Calif., at Toyota Amphitheatre, and it winds around the U.S. to Phoenix, Indianapolis, Pittsburgh, Dallas, Chicago, Charlotte and Las Vegas, and it also hits Quebec, Can. and Ontario, Can. with more dates to be added.

In addition to ampitheatres and arenas, Paisley and friends will also be stopping at festivals throughout the summer including Country Jam in Grand Junction, Colo. (which he's headlining); Pepsi Gulf Coast Jam in Panama City Beach, Fla.; Route 91 Harvest Country Music Festival in Las Vegas; Ottawa Bluefest in Canada and more.

There are a few dates on the Life Amplified World Tour that Chris Young will be opening as well.

The tour will go full force through the summer and fall, and fans can be sure he'll be playing a few of his 23 No. 1 hits as well as his most recent single "Perfect Storm." Plus, touring with Paisley always comes with a few hilarious tour pranks--who knows what will happen this year!
