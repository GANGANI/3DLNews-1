KFAN FM 100.3

The official radio flagship home of the Minnesota Wild.

Since the 2011-12 NHL season, KFAN has broadcast all Wild preseason, regular season and Stanley Cup Playoff games on the State of Hockey's top-rated sports talk station. Joe O'Donnell (play-by-play), Tom Reid (analyst) and Kevin Falness (studio host) capture all of the action and suspense. Coverage begins with a 15-minute pre-game show. Conflicting games are moved to KOOL 108 FM. Additional Wild-related programming on KFAN features the "Wild Weekly" show and "Wild Fanline," which airs after select Wild games.

iHeart Radio reaches more than 2.5 million listeners every week over the air, via the Internet and through iheartradio.com.

Skyview Networks

Skyview Networks is an audio technology, syndication and network audio sales solutions company based in Scottsdale, Arizona that has been serving the broadcast space for nearly 30 years. Its industry-leading services, including distribution and inventory management systems, are utilized by over 100 professional and collegiate sports organizations, major media companies, leading consumer brands, world-class entertainers, and state news networks. With more than 10,000 radio affiliate relationships, Skyview's nationwide coverage reaches over half of all Americans each week. For more information, visit skyviewnetworks.com.

Minnesota Wild Radio Network

The Minnesota Wild is committed to growing the sport of hockey throughout the state of Minnesota and upper Midwest as part of the Minnesota Wild Radio Network. Â 

Through a partnership with Skyview Networks, more than 50 radio affiliates will broadcast Wild hockey games to thousands of Minnesota fans. The Wild and its radio partners will create and execute a variety of fundraising, educational and inspiring promotional opportunities for youth hockey players in communities all across the region.
