Bringing Your Brain Back to the Game

A producer of old-school and modern supplements and adventures for your favorite roleplaying games. We specialize in low-priced, black and white products for B/X, Swords & Wizardry and Gamma World. Many of our books have a system neutral variant as well. Check the links below to see what products are available now.

Latest ThrowiGames Products

Back to BasiX

A fanzine from ThrowiGames. This fanzine celebrates, highlights and supports the Basic and Expert editions of Dungeons & Dragons. Each issue will provide or highlight content within the Holmes, Moldvay, Cook and Mentzer edited rules from TSR's Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson. 12+ Issues.

Gamma Zine

A new fanzine from ThrowiGames that provides post-apocalyptic content for old-school games such as Gamma World and metamorphosis Alpha. Two issues.

Off the Beaten Path: City Excursions

The fourth in a line of full-sized, 30+ page books of 1-2 page side quests. For Swords & Wizardry and System Neutral.

The Slumbering City

First of many Shadowdark RPG adventures. Based on the Slithering Shadow / Dusk of Xuthal (Conan) story by Robert E. Howard.

Five Torches Deep

Adventures to support the Five Torches Deep system.

Mork Borg

Adventures to support the grim system of Mork Borg.
