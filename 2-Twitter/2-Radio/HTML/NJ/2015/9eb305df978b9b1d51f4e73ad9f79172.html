WDVRFM.ORG

Good Music Good Conversation Good Company

WDVR is a listener-supported, community-oriented station, offering radio programming suitable for the entire family.

WDVR is a non-commercial radio station licensed to Penn-Jersey Educational Radio, a New Jersey, 501 (c) (3), Non-Profit Corporation. Our mission is to provide high quality, entertaining and educational programming to the Delaware and Lehigh Valley areas of Western and Central New Jersey and Eastern Pennsylvania.

Click here for our Pop-up Streaming Player!

Our unique mix of good music, good conversation and good company programming keeps you informed & entertained. Your financial support keeps us on the air.

Our staff of over 50 volunteers give of their time to provide the public airwaves and Internet a uniquely diversified format of programming, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. One hundred percent of every donation to WDVR goes directly into the operation of the station.

WDVR is a full-service station operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, on the FM dial at 89.7 in Delaware Township, NJ; 90.5 WPNJ in Easton, PA.; and 96.9 which serves the communities in and around Trenton, Princeton and Lawrenceville NJ.
