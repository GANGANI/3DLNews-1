About

WNTI.ORG is an internet radio station for Centenary University students and the greater Hackettstown community, with listeners all around the world. As the Sound of Centenary, WNTI.ORG supports student participation and learning in a collaborative atmosphere that celebrates Centenary University, regional arts and culture, community service and outreach.
