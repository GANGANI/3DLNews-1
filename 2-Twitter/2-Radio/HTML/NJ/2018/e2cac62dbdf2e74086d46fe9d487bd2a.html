Win Passes To Pier Village’s Outdoor Skating Rink This Week!

Justin LouisPublished: December 26, 2018
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Last month I told you about a number of places at the shore where you can take your family ice skating this winter, and this week we have your chance to win your way in to one!

Ice at the Pier is back at Pier Village in Long Branch, and all this week we're giving you a chance to win a pair of passes with skate rentals!

Just listen to the Ocean County Breakfast Show in the morning from 5 a.m.-10 a.m., and tune in to my show in the afternoon from 3 p.m. - 8 p.m. for your chance to call in and win!

Here's when you can hit the ice this winter at Pier Village through Presidents' Day (February 18th):

Thursday
3:30 PM - 9:00 PM

Friday
3:30 PM - 10:00 PM

Saturday
10:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Sunday
10:00 AM - 9:00 PM
