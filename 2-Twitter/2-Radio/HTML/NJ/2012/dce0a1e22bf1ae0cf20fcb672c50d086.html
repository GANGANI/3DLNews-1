3rd Annual Christmas Choir Contest Winners Revealed

Double DownPublished: December 21, 2012
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

For the past month New Jersey public and private elementary, middle and high schools have been uploading their recordings at nj1015.com, and we've downloaded and listened to them all!

We've been playing a choir a day for the last two weeks and you've been casting your votes for your favorites. Now it is time to announce the winners!

New jersey 101.5 congratulates the winners of our 3rd annual christmas choir contest!

 

With close to 100,000 total votes it was our best contest yet!

Here are all our winners:

The high school winner is Watchung Hills Regional High School from Warren…

The middle school winner is John Witherspoon Middle School of Princeton.

The High School and Middle School are each winners of $,1000! 

 

And the elementary winner and overall champion is Haddon Heights Elementary, who receive $1000 cash and  wins a brand new piano from the Great American Piano Company.  New jersey 101.5’s “3rd annual christmas choir contest” made possible by Raab coins, Value City Furniture and Enlow Recital hall.   Merry Christmas from New Jersey 101.5!
