Top 5 Greatest Electronic Music Artists

 

Daft Punk
Daft Punk is a French electronic music duo composed of producers Guy-Manuel de Homem-Cristo and Thomas Bangalter.

Daedmaus (DJ)
Joel Thomas Zimmerman (deadmau5) is a Canadian DJ, producer, and former web developer who produces music in electronic genres such as electro and dubstep.

Skrillex (musician)
Skrillex is an American (born 1988 in Los Angeles, California) EDM producer who is known for popularizing dubstep. He is part of the band Jack Ü (DJ) and was the lead singer from start to finish.

Aphex Twin (composer)
Richard David James (born August 18, 1971), known under the pseudonym Aphex Twin, is an Irish English electronic musician and composer. He is known for his work in electronic styles of music such as IDM and acid techno in the 1990s, for which he has received wide critical acclaim.

Kraftwerk
Kraftwerk is a German electronic music group formed by Ralf Hütter and Florian Schneider in 1970 in Düsseldorf.

Interesting Facts about the History of Electronic Music💫:

  • In 2016, 75% of the music released worldwide was created with computer programs, synthesizers, drum machines, and samplers.
  • Electronic music was created not by DJs and musicians, but by employees of various scientific laboratories, who experimented with sound waves (so any progress in electronic music is directly related to technical progress).
  • In 1870 the first electronic instruments appeared.
  • In 1897, the first full-fledged polyphonic electronic multi-octave instrument appeared.
