About RadioU

RadioU is where you go to find out what's NEXT — all with no ads!

Watch and listen live 24/7 for bands like twenty one pilots, Red, KB, Fit For A King, and Memphis May Fire. RadioU is your music channel with live streams, videos, artist interviews, The Hudson Show every morning, Weekdays With NayNay, plus playlists for hip hop, EDM, metal, throwback, and more!

RadioU isn't just the same ten songs you hear everywhere else. The music we play is different. Deeper. The stuff THEY don't play. It's where music is going.
