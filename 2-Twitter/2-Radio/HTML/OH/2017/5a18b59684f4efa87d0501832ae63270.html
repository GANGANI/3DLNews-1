welcome to z98fm.com

Delhi Public Radio, Inc. was established in Sept 2013 for the purpose of starting a radio station for the Delhi Township area.

In October 2013, we applied for the LPFM station on 98.1 during the FCC's latest LPFM filing window. In November 2013, we found out that we were a singleton application. Meaning that no one else in the area applied for 98.1

Dusty Rhodes "Day the Music Died" Special Starts at 6pm on Sunday February 2nd.
