Welcome to 88.3 WXUT! 
88.3 FM WXUT Toledo is owned by the University of Toledo Student Broadcasting Organization (SBO). Since 1990, we have been the home for the University of Toledo’s college student-run radio station. WXUT has a new home inside Rocket Hall! Be sure to spend some time hanging out on our website, and don’t forget to follow us on social media! We would also love to hear from you! Simply click on our Contact Us tab for more information and to get in touch with our station manager and various departments.
