Concerts & Events
Sep 26 Wed

CIM Orchestra & Guest Conductor Thomas Wilkins

Event Details

  • Start Time

    8:00 pm

  • Location

    Severance Music Center

  • Ticketing

    Free — Tickets Required

Thomas Wilkins’ appearance is made possible by the Kulas Visiting Artist Series funded by the Kulas Foundation.

This concert is made possible by generous support from Jean and Dick Hipple.

There will be a live broadcast on WCLV 104.9 FM and wclv.org with support from Audio-Technica

REPERTOIRE

  • SHOSTAKOVICH   Festive Overture, Op. 96
  • WALTON   Cello Concerto (1956)
  • TCHAIKOVSKY   Symphony No. 5 in E minor, Op. 64

PERFORMED BY

  • Cleveland Institute of Music Orchestra
  • Thomas Wilkins, guest conductor
  • Daniel Kaler, cello, student artist

TICKET INFORMATION

This concert is free, but tickets are required. To reserve your general admission tickets, please contact the Severance Box Office at 216.231.1111 or clevelandorchestra.com.

11001 Euclid Ave
Cleveland, OH 44106
