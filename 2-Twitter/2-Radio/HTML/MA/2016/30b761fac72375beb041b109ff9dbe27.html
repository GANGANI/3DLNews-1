617 Day: Celebrate All Local Music All Day on June 17

Join us as we celebrate 617 Day on June 17th (get it? 6-1-7.)  We’ll feature local music on-air all day, and host free music from 3-9 at Boston City Hall Plaza in partnership with the Mayor’s Office of Arts & Culture.  Help us celebrate the music, arts, people, and culture that make Boston awesome!
