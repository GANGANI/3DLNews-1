What is Sanrio outfit?

Sanrio is a company based in Japan that sells cute character goods and merchandise. Their products range from dolls and plushies to clothing, accessories, stationery, and food items. The most famous Sanrio character is Hello Kitty, but many others have become popular in recent years such as My Melody and Keroppi.

What should you wear to look Sanrio?

The key elements of a Sanrio-inspired aesthetic include pastel colors, polka dots, flowers, bows and ribbons, hearts, soft textures, Japanese characters, cute kawaii elements and of course Sanrio characters with scenes.

The Sanrio aesthetic clothing is not only for babies and kids, or for kawaii maid cosplay and Halloween costumes. It is also widely popular for adults who are into Japanese culture as everyday wear. Some fans are even wearing gowns of Sanrio themes for their weddings.

Sanrio is known to be a cute kawaii brand but you can actually mix and match Sanrio fashion pieces to give an alt aesthetic. Popular related outfit themes include Sanrio goth, Hello Kitty goth, pastel goth, pink goth, eGirl and grunge Hello Kitty aesthetic.

What are Sanrio outfit colors?

Sanrio outfit colors are usually pastel, lavender, mint green, sage, bright white, bright yellow, bubblegum pink and baby blue. For adults looking for an alt vibe, you can contrast these colors with black, burgundy, forest green and other dark shades.

What accessories to wear with Sanrio outfits?

There are a lot of kawaii Sanrio hair accessories (like bows and headbands), as well as temporary tattoos, leg warmers, arm warms and bags to compliment your Sanrio outfits. If you want to show off your Sanrio pride in a more subtle way, just hang a Sanrio plush or toy on the backpack or get a Sanrio tote bag. The brand even has stuff for your pets too.

✅ Sanrio bags are adorable, girly but yet cool eye-catchers. They add cuteness to any outfit and work for fans of any age. Click the images to see deals on Amazon!

How do you style your hair for Sanrio outfits?

If you have long hair, you could try tying your hair up into two buns on top of your head. If you have short hair, you could consider getting some bangs to make yourself look more like kawaii. Pastel hair dye, wigs and hair accessories are also popular for Sanrio outfits. There are many different types of headbands that you can use to accessorize your look with Sanrio characters such as Hello Kitty or My Melody!

How do you do Sanrio aesthetic nails?

Sanrio nail art can be the talking point for any gathering, there are many themes to try for Birthdays, Christmas and Halloween. If you’re looking to do a simple cute Japanese Sanrio nail design, here are a few easy things you’ll need to do.

First, you’ll need some nail polish. You can either go for a black base or something more girly to match your outfit. Second, you’ll need some Sanrio nail stickers, popular patterns will be Hello Kitty, Kuromi, My Melody, Little Twin Stars and Cinnamoroll. Finally, you’ll need some cotton balls and a q-tip.

To do the nails, start by applying your base color to your nails and then placing the stickers on top of it. Once they’re in place, use a cotton ball to dampen them and then apply your desired polish.

How do you do Sanrio aesthetic tattoos?

It may seem strange at first to get a tattoo of something so cute and cuddly! But there are many different reasons why people get tattoos: you might want something fun on your body that will remind you of your teenage life, you might want a small matching black and white Sanrio character outline tattoo with your best friend.

If you are getting a tattoo for the first time, get your favorite Sanrio character tattooed on the upper arms, forearms, thighs, and calves. Elbow and knee tattooing can be a little more difficult, but still doable. It is because the skin on the torso is softer and lighter, tattoos on the torso hurt more.

How to do makeup for Sanrio outfits?

Different Sanrio characters have different color schemes, for example, The Kuromi makeup look is dark and gothic, the Pom Pom Purin makeup look is more colorful with hearts and bows, while the My Melody and Hello Kitty makeup look is supposed to be apple red/pinky. In all cases, your Sanrio makeup look should look both clean and innocent. Black gel and/or liquid eyeliner, cream blush, sparkly lip gloss, removable tattoo stickers of Sanrio characters and light concealer/foundation are all recommended for your Sanrio aesthetic makeup.

Sanrio also had a lot of crossover with different makeup product brands. From makeup organizers, cosmetic bags, brushes, eyeshadow palettes, glitter lip gloss to other kits and sets, Sanrio fans can for sure find something fun to try.
