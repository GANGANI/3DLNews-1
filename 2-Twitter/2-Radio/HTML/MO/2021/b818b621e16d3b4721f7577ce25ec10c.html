    Glocal News in Social Artistry

    [su_animate type=”fadeInLeft”]

    Mondays: 5 – 6 p.m.

    Glocal News in Social Artistry is a weekly interview show with host Dick Dalton in conversation with local, national, and international guests working to build a more humane world from the inside out.
    If you have a story to tell about you and your project, contact Dick Dalton at <daltondster@gmail.com>. Please put Glocal News in the subject line.
    E-Mail Show Facebook Page

    Dick Dalton

    My father bought 915-17 East Broadway in the 1960s. I camped out on the 2nd floor for a time during 1970-72. KOPN moved in in 1973. I went from being an Integral Yoga teacher to a volunteer minister from 1975-2000; a PhD Health & Wellness teacher at Lincoln University from 1985-2015; a peace activist forever; author of I Am Not My Thoughts (2016); and Host of Glocal News in Social Artistry since 11/17. I am a Pod of Consciousness.
