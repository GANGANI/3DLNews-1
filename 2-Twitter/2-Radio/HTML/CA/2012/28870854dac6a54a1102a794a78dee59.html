Pet-O-Rama: Week of May 27th, 2024

Bill Nye!

Meet Bill Nye, the somewhat shy guy, this week's Pet-of-the-Week at the Santa Cruz County Animal Shelter. He came to the shelter as a stray so we don't have a lot of information about him. He's about 10 months old and a beautiful Shepherd mix. Bill has befriended staff and volunteers since his arrival. He leans in for pets and walks nicely on leash. For more info, read the full post, listen in to KPIG at 11am Thursdays, or visit the shelter, located at 1001 Rodriguez St. in Santa Cruz and open from 11am to 6pm every day.

Fresh Pork Chops: Week of May 27th, 2024

Sue Foley: "One Guitar Woman"

This week, Fresh Pork Chops features "One Guitar Woman," the new release from Sue Foley. This new record from Sue is a special tribute to the female pioneers of the guitar - including Memphis Minnie, Lydia Mendoza, Maybelle Carter, Ida Presti, and Sister Rosetta Tharpe. Tune in each weeknight at 7:00pm as Ramblin' Ror' plays the whole CD, two cuts at a time. And we'll give out a copy to a lucky winner each night! AND be sure to tune in on Wednesday at 9:00pm as Anthony plays and chats live in the sty on "Neighborhood Night!" Learn more about the new record at suefoley.com.  Fresh Pork Chops is sponsored by Felton Music Hall.
