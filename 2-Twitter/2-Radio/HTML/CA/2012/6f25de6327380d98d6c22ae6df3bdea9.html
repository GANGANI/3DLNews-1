About Park Family Insurance Inc.

Park Family Insurance and Financial Services, Inc. was founded in 1997 in Roseville, CA.   George and Victoria Park are a father daughter team.  In 2017, two things happened. We shortened our name to Park Family Insurance, Inc. and George returned home to his Native Reno where our corporate headquarters is located. Our specialties are personal and small business insurance, including life insurance, Nevada home insurance, Nevada auto insurance and all the extra coverage (like toys insurance, boats and umbrella insurance) families need.

Our name, Park Family Insurance was created because too often people are out hunting for the “Big Accounts” while ignoring all the hard working people that make things run. We wanted people to know that we were focused on family and family businesses insurance needs.

For 20 years, at Park Family Insurance, we have stuck to our focus family businesses and personal insurance. We bring dozens of carriers and a full range of options to the table for our insured clients. Our experience allows us to narrow a search for the right match to the needs of our clients out of the dizzying array of carriers available out there in the marketplace. We are members of PIIB (Pacific Interstate Insurance Brokers), a large Insurance Group that gives us access to all the insurance carriers and products the “big guys” have.

When you do business with Park Family Insurance you can count on a response within 24 business hours. We are also known to return emails all hours of most days. We take a vested interest in making sure our clients have the peace of mind and satisfaction of knowing their risks have been managed and that their insurance will cover when disaster strikes.

George was first licensed in 1993, Victoria in 2015. This means we have over 30 years of experience (and counting) in the insurance industry serving the needs of the “little guy”.

Park Family Insurance is currently licensed in 5 states: Arizona, California, Idaho, Nevada and Oregon.
