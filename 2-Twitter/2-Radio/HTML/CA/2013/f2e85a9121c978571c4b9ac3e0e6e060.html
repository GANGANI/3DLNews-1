KCEA-Home Of The Big Bands

The sounds of the Big Band Era swing on! At KCEA, we play the best music from “The Great American Songbook”- considered some of the most important and influential American popular songs and jazz standards from the early 20th century. KCEA collects, restores and preserves music recorded by a large number and wide range of singers, instrumental bands, and jazz musicians for the enjoyment and education of current and future generations. We hope our music serves as the sound track of your life.
