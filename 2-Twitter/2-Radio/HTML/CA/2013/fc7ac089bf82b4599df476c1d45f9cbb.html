To Be Determined Later

Wednesday, June 5 10:00pm — Thursday, June 6 2:00am

Join our educational airwaves on Wednesday, June 5th, from 10:00 p.m. to 2:00 a.m. Classic KFJC alumni are your DJ docents (Reject Girl, Mr. E, and Dominic Trix), guiding you on a punkified vision, forward, towards collusion confusion ballot collision, American Style. Chronic guestperson Ronald Redball provides heaping helpings of audio materials hastily assembled over the last few years, as you sit back in the comfort of your own private polling booth and enjoy the surreal subgenial college collage of music, talk, and the best boiling down of tough subjects since rawhide chews. We will inspect your rejection, and projectively inject you into every possible various perspective and position simultaneously. This will prepare you to make the most informed and educated decisions, if your skull holds. No matter how it ends, you'll revel in this PERFECTLY SAFE sonic bubble of voter-guiding, AI-curated, informational hand-crafted mayhem!

Er, Junehem.
