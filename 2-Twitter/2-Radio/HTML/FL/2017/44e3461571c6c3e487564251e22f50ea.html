Wave 94 – Christian Radio for Tallahassee!

Some people are new to the area and looking for a good, local Christian radio station in the Tallahassee, Crawfordville, Leon, Wakulla, Big Bend area of Florida.  Here we are!

If you are looking for a Christian radio station to advertise your business or church or event, we are a full time commercial Christian station!

If you are looking for a station where you can buy some air time and get help producing your own radio program and podcast, you’ve come to the right place!

If you’re looking for a radio station that plays the best music in the world (IOHO), we are affiliated with the Salem Radio Networks and they put forth considerable effort, resources and experience to make sure that each and every song on Wave 94 is the best of the best!

If you’re looking for the station that plays all the Wakulla High School War Eagle football games live on the radio – that’s us!  In fact, our call letters are WAKU, and you might guess why.

In our world today, the various forms of media spew out so much ungodly, unholy, untrue and unhelpful information and entertainment that degrades individuals and society, spreads darkness rather than light, and calls evil good and good evil.  Here at Wave 94, we are trying to do our part to spread God’s light and truth and hope and peace and love through Christian music and programming.

For more information, check out our About page.

If you have any questions or comments, just give us a call:  850-926-8000.  We are here to serve!
