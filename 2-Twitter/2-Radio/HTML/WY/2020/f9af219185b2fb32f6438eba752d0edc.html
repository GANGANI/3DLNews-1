    Somali & Amharic Now
    on RadioAfrica.fm

    YNOP launched the RadioAfrica.fm Podcast Network in August, 2023, to enable more and more people to have access to the Word of God in various African languages. Initially, we offered programs in Swahili, French, Fulani, and English.

    ​

    Since its launch, people from 34 nations around the world have streamed & downloaded programs, and on March 29th we added our sixth & seventh languages... AMHARIC and SOMALI!

    ​

    Please pray for our team as they are working on plans to use social media advertising to promote the ministry of RadioAfrica.fm, and they are also working on adding an EIGHTH LANGUAGE! As soon as arrangements can be finalized, RadioAfrica.fm will be providing content in the ARABIC LANGUAGE!

     

    PRAY that God will continue to draw people to RadioAfrica.fm!
