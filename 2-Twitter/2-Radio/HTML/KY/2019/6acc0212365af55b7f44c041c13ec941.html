A Christmas Story: A Sneak Preview of Gaylord Opryland’s 2019 ICE! Exhibit [Video]

Chadwick BenefieldPublished: November 22, 2019
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

Angel and I agree.  This is one of our favorite (if not THE favorite) ICE! theme ever.  Gaylord Opryland Resort in Nashville chose a brand new theme for this year's ICE! exhibit.  It's the classic Christmas movie, A Christmas Story, which has been brought to life with thousands of pounds of ice.  Here's a sneak preview of this year's Country Christmas celebration and some photos we snapped inside ICE!

And, inside ICE!, artisans have sculpted some of the most classic scenes from A Christmas Story.

And, of course, each year ICE! features an ice slide room.  And Angel couldn't help climbing the stairs and striking a pose.
