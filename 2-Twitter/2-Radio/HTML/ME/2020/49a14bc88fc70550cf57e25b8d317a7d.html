9 Maine Take-Out Places You Need To Try

Cooper FoxPublished: June 18, 2020
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on Twitter

In March, the spread of the coronavirus brought a temporary end to "dining in" at Maine restaurants.  Many continued to operate by doing take out throughout the course of the stay-at-home orders.

Based on your input, we've put together our list of the best take-out places in Maine.  We've decided to go really wide with this concept.  We're not just featuring establishments selling burgers and fish-n-chips.  We've got Thai places, pizza places, and more.

Since it looks like the summer of 2020 is going to be the summer of the "staycation", we hope you'll take the time to drive around and try the food from these amazing restaurants.

If there are any that we've missed, please send us a message through the 92 Moose app.
