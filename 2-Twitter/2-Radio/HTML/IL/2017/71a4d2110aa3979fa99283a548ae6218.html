Stormchasers

released 2017

In 2017, Lucky Boys Confusion released their highly anticipated album, Stormchasers. The album was a testament to the band's growth and maturity, while still retaining their signature sound that fans have come to love.

Stormchasers explored various themes, from love and relationships to the struggles of day-to-day life. The song "Stormchaser" set the tone for the rest of the album, with its electrifying guitar riffs and powerful vocals from lead singer Kaustubh "Stubhy" Pandav. The song spoke of perseverance and chasing your dreams, even in the face of adversity.

Other standout tracks from the album included "Good Luck", a catchy and upbeat song about taking risks for love, and "Insomniac", a haunting and introspective track exploring the struggles of dealing with mental health issues.

The band also experimented with different musical styles on Stormchasers. "Sun In My Eyes" featured a reggae-inspired beat, while "White Collar" had a funk-tinged sound. Lucky Boys Confusion's versatility and willingness to take risks helped make Stormchasers an instant classic.
